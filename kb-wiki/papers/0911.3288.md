# The optical model potential of the $Σ$ hyperon in nuclear matter

> We present our attempts to determine the optical model potential $U_Σ= V_Σ-iW_Σ$ of the $Σ$ hyperon in nuclear matter. We analyze the following sources of information on $U_Σ$: $ΣN$ scattering, $Σ^-$ atoms, and final state interaction of $Σ$ hyperons in the $(π,K^+)$ and $(K^-.π)$ reactions on nuclear targets. We conclude that $V_Σ$ is repulsive inside the nucleus and has a shallow a tractive pocket at the nuclear surface. These features of $V_Σ$ are consistent with the Nijmegen model F of the hyperon-nucleon interaction.

## Key claim

The real part of the Σ hyperon optical model potential is repulsive inside the nucleus and has a shallow attractive pocket at the nuclear surface, but this conclusion depends on using the Nijmegen model F of the ΣN interaction; all other Nijmegen models (D, SC, NSC) produce purely attractive potentials that would predict Σ hypernuclei which have not been observed.

## Method

The authors calculated the real potential V_Σ from the YNG effective ΣN interaction, which is the configuration-space representation of the G matrix in the low-order Brueckner approximation using four Nijmegen baryon-baryon interaction models (D, F, SC, NSC). For the absorptive potential W_Σ, they used a semi-classical expression based on total cross sections for ΣN scattering (including ΣΛ conversion and elastic scattering) modified by the exclusion principle. They then tested these optical potentials against three data sets: 23 data points from Σ^- atom strong interaction shifts and widths, the kaon spectrum from the (π^-,K^+) reaction on ²⁸Si at 1.2 GeV/c, and the pion spectrum from the (K^-,π^+) reaction on ⁹Be at 0.6 GeV/c.

## Key numbers

- Equilibrium nuclear density ρ₀ = 0.166 fm⁻³ used for calculating W_Σ components
- χ²(model F) = 38.1 for the fit to 23 Σ^- atom data points, compared to χ²(model D) > 130, χ²(model SC) = 55.0, and χ²(model NSC) > 904
- V_Σ(model F) = 17.25 MeV obtained from fitting the (π^-,K^+) kaon spectrum on ²⁸Si
- Proton separation energy radius R_P = 3.756 fm used for the ²⁸Si target shell model
- Pion momentum p_π = 1.2 GeV/c for the (π^-,K^+) reaction on ²⁸Si at θ_K = 6°
- Kaon momentum p_K = 0.6 GeV/c for the (K^-,π^+) reaction on ⁹Be at θ_π = 4°

## Context

This work extends the Nijmegen group's program of constructing baryon-baryon interaction models (models D, F, SC, NSC by de Swart and collaborators) by testing them against Σ hyperon data in nuclear matter. The analysis resolves an earlier discrepancy where kaon spectrum estimates without the elastic ΣN scattering contribution suggested a repulsive V_Σ of about 100 MeV; including W_e reduces the required repulsion to 17.25 MeV. The results enable future calculations of Σ hypernuclear structure and reactions using the Nijmegen model F as the preferred ΣN interaction, and the authors note that the observed bound state of ⁴_ΣHe (Harada and collaborators) requires a strong Lane component V_τ that is strongest in model F.
