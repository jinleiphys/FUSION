# Exclusive photoproduction of a di-meson pair with large invariant mass

> We consider the exclusive photoproduction of a di-meson pair with large invariant mass, $γN \rightarrow N' M_1M_2$, in the framework of collinear factorisation. The mesons considered $M_1$ and $M_2$ are either pions or rho mesons, charged or neutral. We consider the kinematic regime characterised by a large invariant mass of the two-meson system, and a small deflection of the nucleon in the centre-of-mass frame. In this kinematic domain, the amplitude factorises into a perturbative hard part and non-perturbative parts described by Generalised Parton Distributions (GPDs) and Distribution Amplitudes (DAs). We automate the calculation of the fully differential cross section at leading twist and leading order, and we present some numerical results at JLab 12 GeV kinematics. This class of processes provides yet more exclusive $2 \to 3$ channels that can be used to extract GPDs.

## Key claim

The authors claim that exclusive photoproduction of a di-meson pair with large invariant mass, $\gamma N \rightarrow N' M_1M_2$, can be described by collinear factorisation at leading twist and leading order, but only for processes that do not involve 2-gluon exchanges in the $t$-channel, in order to avoid factorisation breaking effects.

## Method

The authors generated all Feynman diagrams for the process $\gamma(q)\rightarrow u(k_1)\,\bar{u}(k_2)\,u(k_3)\,\bar{u}(k_4)\,u(k_5)\,\bar{u}(k_6)$ using FeynArts and FeynCalc in Mathematica, then projected each diagram onto 14 possible combinations of Dirac structures for the GPD and two DAs (vector, axial, or tensor). They performed the integration over the parton momentum fraction $x$, and the DA variables $v$ and $z$ numerically, using a partial fraction decomposition and Sokhotski-Plemelj formula, with a folding procedure to handle spurious divergences on the diagonals of the $(v,z)$ plane. The non-perturbative inputs used Radyushkin's Double Distribution Ansatz for GPDs and asymptotic forms for DAs.

## Key numbers

* Differential cross sections for $\pi^-\rho^0_L$ photoproduction reach values of order a thousand pb.GeV$^{-6}$ at $S_{\gamma N}=20$ GeV$^2$ and $M^2_{12}=3$ GeV$^2$, which is one to two orders of magnitude above the corresponding photon-meson pair photoproduction cross sections.
* The invariant mass $M^2_{12}$ of the di-meson system is taken as 3 GeV$^2$ for the numerical results, within the allowed range of 2 to 9 GeV$^2$ for JLab 12 GeV kinematics.
* The constant $C$ in the GPD parametrisation is fixed to $C=0.71$ GeV$^2$.
* The kinematic cuts $-u'>1$ GeV$^2$ and $-t'>1$ GeV$^2$ are applied to ensure the collinear factorisation regime.

## Context

This work extends the study of exclusive $2 \to 3$ processes from the photoproduction of a photon-meson pair (Boussarie 2016, Duplancic 2018, 2022, 2023) to the photoproduction of a di-meson pair, providing a broader class of channels for extracting both chiral-even and chiral-odd GPDs. The cross sections are found to be one to two orders of magnitude larger than those for the $\gamma N \rightarrow N'\gamma M$ processes, making them promising for experimental studies at JLab 12 GeV, LHC ultraperipheral collisions, and the future EIC. The work avoids factorisation breaking effects from 2-gluon exchanges in the $t$-channel, as discussed in Nabeebaccus (2023, 2024).
