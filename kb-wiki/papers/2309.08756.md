## Context

This work extends the theoretical framework of Feijoo (2023) who developed the first unitarized effective meson-baryon chiral Lagrangian including NLO contributions in the S=-2 sector, but could not constrain the NLO LECs due to insufficient experimental data. The authors use the ALICE measurement of KвБїќЫ correlations (2023) which provided the most precise data on this interaction. This methodology enables future constraints on effective chiral models for multi-strange and charm hadron interactions, where femtoscopic measurements at LHC will be the primary experimental input, particularly with the higher statistics expected from LHC Run 3 and Run 4 and dedicated experiments like ALICE 3.
