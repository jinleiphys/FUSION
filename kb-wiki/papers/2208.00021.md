# GTMDs and the factorization of exclusive double Drell-Yan

> Different exclusive processes have been proposed to access the generalized transverse momentum dependent distributions (GTMDs) with no proof of factorization, which allows to rigorously define the GTMDs. Using Soft Collinear Effective Theory we derive for the first time the factorization of the differential cross section for the exclusive double Drell-Yan process for the exclusive double Drell-Yan process pion N to N' gamma* gamma* to N' (l^+l^-)(l^+l^-), for small transverse momenta of the photons in terms of a perturbatively calculable hard factor, GTMDs and light-cone wave functions (LCWFs). We find that the hard factor of the process can be obtained from single inclusive Drell-Yan production so that one can resum logarithms at high orders in QCD. We also discuss the evolution of the GTMDs and the LCWFs.

## Key claim
Using Soft Collinear Effective Theory, the authors derive for the first time a factorization theorem for the exclusive double Drell-Yan process πN → N'γ*γ* → N'(ℓ⁺ℓ⁻)(ℓ⁺ℓ⁻) in terms of GTMDs and light-cone wave functions, but only under the specific kinematic condition that the transverse momenta of the photons are small compared to their invariant masses (|q_{i⊥}|/√(q_i²) ≪ 1).

## Method
The authors apply Soft Collinear Effective Theory (SCET) power counting to the hadronic tensor of the exclusive double Drell-Yan process, matching full QCD electromagnetic currents onto effective current operators. They perform a Fierz transformation to separate collinear and anti-collinear fermion fields, multipole expand the matrix elements in the leading regions of position space, and apply zero-bin subtraction to remove overlap between collinear, anti-collinear, and soft modes. The hard factor is shown to factorize as the product of two single-inclusive Drell-Yan hard coefficients |C(Q₁²,μ²)|²|C(Q₂²,μ²)|², verified by an explicit one-loop calculation in coordinate space.

## Key numbers
- The kinematic condition for factorization is |q_{i⊥}|/√(q_i²) ≪ 1, where q_i are the photon momenta with i=1,2.
- The hard coefficient C(Q²,μ²) for single inclusive Drell-Yan is known up to four loops in perturbation theory (Lee 2022, arXiv:2204.02422).
- The rapidity evolution kernel (Collins-Soper kernel) for Drell-Yan is known at 4 loops in perturbation theory (Echevarria 2015, Li 2016, Vladimirov 2016, Moult 2022, arXiv:2205.02242).
- The cusp anomalous dimension Γ_cusp and non-cusp anomalous dimension γ_V are known up to third order (Moch 1999, Moch 2005) and fourth order in α_s (Lee 2022, arXiv:2204.02422).

## Context
This work provides the first rigorous factorization proof for a process accessing generalized transverse momentum dependent distributions (GTMDs), which were introduced by Meissner (2008, 2009) and Lorce (2013). It extends the factorization proofs previously established for GPDs (Muller 1994, Radyushkin 1996, Ji 1996) and TMDs (Becher 2010, Collins 2011, Echevarria 2011, Chiu 2012) to the GTMD case, addressing a gap noted in proposals by Hatta (2016), Bhattacharya (2017, 2018), and Boer (2021). The result enables rigorous extraction of GTMDs from experimental data, with the evolution of both GTMDs and LCWFs shown to be spin-independent and governed by the same anomalous dimensions and rapidity evolution kernel as single inclusive Drell-Yan, allowing resummation of large logarithms at high accuracy.
