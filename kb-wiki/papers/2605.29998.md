# Higher Mellin Moments of the Unpolarized PDF of the Pion and the Kaon from Lattice QCD

> We present results on the Mellin moments of the unpolarized parton distribution function (PDF) of the pion and kaon up to the fourth order. The computation is done using one $N_f=2+1+1$ gauge ensemble of twisted mass fermions with quark masses tuned to approximately their physical values. We reconstruct the valence pion and kaon PDFs using the connected contributions to the three Mellin moments. We compare our results on the Mellin moments and the reconstructed PDFs with other lattice QCD and phenomenological determinations.

## Key claim

The paper presents lattice QCD results for the unpolarized PDF Mellin moments of the pion and kaon up to fourth order (⟨x³⟩), but only using connected contributions and a single gauge ensemble at a single lattice spacing, with quark masses tuned to approximately their physical values rather than exactly.

## Method

The computation uses one Nf=2+1+1 twisted mass clover-improved fermion ensemble (cB211.072.64) with lattice spacing a=0.0796 fm. The third and fourth Mellin moments ⟨x²⟩ and ⟨x³⟩ are extracted from ratios of three-point to two-point correlation functions using boosted frames to avoid operator mixing, with the ground-state energy determined from two-state fits. Renormalization is performed nonperturbatively in the RI'-MOM scheme with perturbative conversion to the MSbar scheme at 2 GeV, including subtraction of one-loop lattice artifacts.

## Key numbers

- Pion u-quark moments at μ=2 GeV in MSbar: ⟨x⟩=0.2194(22), ⟨x²⟩=0.1021(34), ⟨x³⟩=0.079(25)
- Kaon u-quark moments at μ=2 GeV in MSbar: ⟨x⟩=0.2151(12), ⟨x²⟩=0.0925(11), ⟨x³⟩=0.0557(54)
- Kaon s-quark moments at μ=2 GeV in MSbar: ⟨x⟩=0.30081(67), ⟨x²⟩=0.14312(86), ⟨x³⟩=0.0881(41)
- Renormalization factors at 2 GeV: Z_VDD = 1.4639(11)(03), Z_VDDD = 1.8831(19)(10)
- Ratio ⟨x²⟩/⟨x⟩ for pion u-quark: 0.466(16); for kaon u-quark: 0.430(5); for kaon s-quark: 0.475(3)
- SU(3) symmetry breaking ratio ⟨x⟩_u^K/⟨x⟩_s^K = 0.7151(46), indicating approximately 30% breaking

## Context

This work extends the previous ETMC study (Extended Twisted Mass Collaboration, 2024) that computed the first Mellin moment and momentum fractions for pion and kaon, now providing the third and fourth moments. The results enable comparison with phenomenological determinations such as the JAM global analysis (Barry, 2025) that incorporated lattice QCD input, and will provide theoretical input for the upcoming AMBER experiment at CERN and the future Electron-Ion Collider. The reconstructed PDFs using the standard functional form q(x)=N x^α (1-x)^β (1+γx) allow comparison with other lattice QCD and model calculations including Dyson-Schwinger studies and light-front approaches.
