# A relativistic model for neutrino pion production from nuclei in the resonance region

> We present a relativistic model for electroweak pion production from nuclei, focusing on the $Δ$ and the second resonance region. Bound states are derived in the Hartree approximation to the $σ-ω$ Walecka model. Final-state interactions of the outgoing pion and nucleon are described in a factorized way by means of a relativistic extension of the Glauber model. Our formalism allows a detailed study of neutrino pion production through $Q^2$, $W$, energy, angle and out-of-plane distributions.

## Key claim

The paper presents a fully relativistic formalism for neutrino-induced one-pion production on nuclei in the resonance region, but the model is currently limited to an intermediate Δ state and the second-resonance region, with final-state interactions treated in a factorized approximation rather than a full unfactorized calculation.

## Method

The authors adopt the impulse approximation, describing the bound nucleon with wave functions from the Hartree approximation to the σ-ω Walecka model. The Δ-production vertex uses phenomenological form factors constrained by CVC, PCAC, and electron-scattering data, while medium modifications of the Δ resonance are implemented via a density-dependent shift to its mass and width based on Oset's self-energy calculation. Final-state interactions of the outgoing nucleon and pion are computed using a relativistic multiple-scattering Glauber approximation (RMSGA), which calculates the nuclear transparency factor for fast nucleons and pions escaping the nucleus.

## Key numbers

- The formalism targets the region W < 2 GeV, where the majority of strength comes from quasi-elastic events and resonant one-pion production.
- Example calculations are shown for the process ν_μ + p → μ + Δ⁺⁺ on a carbon nucleus at E_ν = 1200 MeV (presented as two-fold distributions in figures).
- The eightfold differential cross section formula (Equation 2) includes explicit kinematic factors: m_l |k_l|/(2π)³ and M_N M_{A-1} |k_π| |k_N| / [2 (2π)⁵ |E_{A-1} + E_N + E_N k_N · (k_π - q)/|k_N|²|].

## Context

This work extends earlier relativistic multiple-scattering Glauber approximation calculations for neutrino-induced one-nucleon knockout (Martinez, year not specified) to resonant one-pion production. The formalism is motivated by new cross-section measurements from MiniBooNE and K2K collaborations, and is intended to support future experiments like Minerνa that aim for precise studies of exclusive channels. The framework enables calculations for different nuclei and resonances, producing predictions for Q², W, energy, and angular distributions of outgoing leptons and hadrons.
