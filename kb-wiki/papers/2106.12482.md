# $η^{(')}\toπ^+π^-γ^{(\ast)}$ in large-$N_c$ chiral perturbation theory

> We present a calculation of the decays $η^{(')}\toπ^+π^-γ^{(\ast)}$ at the one-loop level up to and including next-to-next-to-leading order (NNLO) in large-$N_c$ chiral perturbation theory. The numerical evaluation of the results is performed successively at LO, NLO, and NNLO, fitting the relevant low-energy constants to the available experimental data. We discuss the widths and decay spectra of $η^{(')}\toπ^+π^-γ$ as well as $η^{(')}\toπ^+π^-l^+l^-$, with $l=e,\ μ$.

## Key claim

The decays $\eta^{(')}\to\pi^+\pi^-\gamma^{(\ast)}$ are calculated at next-to-next-to-leading order (NNLO) in large-$N_c$ chiral perturbation theory, but the full NNLO result cannot describe the entire $\eta'$ spectrum because vector-meson degrees of freedom become important at high $\pi^+\pi^-$ invariant masses, and the $\mathcal{O}(p^8)$ Lagrangian has not been constructed, requiring an ansatz for the form factors.

## Method

The authors performed a one-loop calculation of the invariant amplitudes for $\eta^{(')}\to\pi^+\pi^-\gamma^{(\ast)}$ using large-$N_c$ chiral perturbation theory with a combined expansion in momenta, quark masses, and $1/N_c$ counted as $\mathcal{O}(\sqrt{\delta})$, $\mathcal{O}(\delta)$, and $\mathcal{O}(\delta)$, respectively. They employed the Wess-Zumino-Witten action at leading order and included NLO and NNLO Lagrangians from both even and odd intrinsic-parity sectors, fitting the relevant low-energy constants (LECs) to experimental decay spectra and widths for $\eta$ and $\eta'$ decays separately.

## Key numbers

* LO decay width for $\eta\to\pi^+\pi^-\gamma$: 36 eV (compared to experimental value $55.3\pm 2.4$ eV from Zyla (2020))
* LO decay width for $\eta'\to\pi^+\pi^-\gamma$: 3.4 keV (compared to experimental value $55.5\pm 1.9$ keV from Zyla (2020))
* NLO mixing angle $\theta^{[1]} = -11.1$ degrees, taken from the NLO analysis in Bickert (2016)
* NLO fit parameter $c_{15} = 5.78 \pm 0.23$ GeV$^{-2}$ (compared to a vector-meson-dominance estimate of 2.53 GeV$^{-2}$ from Hacker (2008))
* LO mixing angle $\theta^{[0]} = -19.6$ degrees

## Context

This work extends previous one-loop chiral perturbation theory calculations of $\eta\to\pi^+\pi^-\gamma$ by Bijnens (1989) and Hacker (2008), which found that $\mathcal{O}(p^6)$ corrections are crucial and contact terms dominate over loop corrections. The calculation enables improved dispersion-theoretical extraction of two-photon form factors for $\pi^0$, $\eta$, and $\eta'$ mesons, which enter the hadronic light-by-light scattering contribution to the anomalous magnetic moment of the muon, as discussed in reviews by Gan (2020) and Jegerlehner (2017). The results also provide a framework for testing P- and CP-violation and searching for axion-like particles beyond the Standard Model.
