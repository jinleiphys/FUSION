## Context

This work extends the successful NNLO HTLpt calculations of the QCD pressure and susceptibilities by Haque (2014) to the curvature of the QCD phase transition line, providing a resummed perturbative QCD alternative to lattice methods that suffer from the sign problem at finite baryon density. The results agree with lattice extractions using both the imaginary chemical potential method (Cea 2015, Bonati 2015, Borsanyi 2020) and Taylor expansion methods (Bonati 2018, Bazavov 2018), and provide predictions for Оєв‚„ and Оєв‚† that can be tested by future lattice calculations with improved precision.
