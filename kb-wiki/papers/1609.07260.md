## Context

This work extends the selection rule for electromagnetic transitions originally introduced by Koike et al. (2004) for the lowest chiral doublet bands in the 130 mass region to excited chiral doublet bands and to the 80 and 190 mass regions with identical symmetric proton-neutron configurations.
