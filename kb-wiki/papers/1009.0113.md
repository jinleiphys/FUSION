# Hadron physics with KLOE-2

> In the upcoming month the KLOE-2 data taking campaign will start at the upgraded DAFNE phi-factory of INFN Laboratori Nazionali di Frascati. The main goal is to collect an integrated luminosity of about 20 fb^(-1) in 3-4 years in order to refine and extend the KLOE program on both kaon physics and hadron spectroscopy. Here the expected improvements on the results of hadron spectroscopy are presented and briefly discussed.

## Key claim
The KLOE-2 experiment, starting data-taking in 2010 at the upgraded DAFNE phi-factory, aims to collect about 20 fb^(-1) of integrated luminosity over 3-4 years to refine and extend the KLOE program on hadron spectroscopy; the paper presents expected improvements on hadron spectroscopy results, but these are projections based on planned detector upgrades and assumed luminosities, not yet achieved measurements.

## Method
The KLOE-2 detector upgrades the existing KLOE setup (drift chamber and electromagnetic calorimeter in a 0.52 T solenoid) with small-angle tagging devices (Low Energy Tagger and High Energy Tagger) for detecting e+e- from gamma-gamma reactions, plus a planned Inner Tracker, crystal calorimeters (CCALT) covering down to 8 degrees, and a tile calorimeter (QCALT) for K_L decay photons. The paper evaluates expected sensitivities for hadron spectroscopy channels using simulations and extrapolations from KLOE data, assuming a peak luminosity of about 5x10^32 cm^(-2)s^(-1) and collecting 5 fb^(-1) in the first year.

## Key numbers
- Integrated luminosity goal: about 20 fb^(-1) over 3-4 years
- Peak luminosity: about 5x10^32 cm^(-2)s^(-1), a factor of 3 larger than previously obtained
- KLOE previously collected 2.5 fb^(-1) at the phi meson resonance plus 250 pb^(-1) off-peak (2000-2006)
- For gamma-gamma -> pi0 pi0 with 5 fb^(-1) at the phi peak: statistical uncertainty per energy bin reduced to 2%
- For eta -> pi0 gamma gamma: 1300 events expected from first year of KLOE-2 data-taking, allowing 3% accuracy on the branching fraction
- For eta' -> eta pi+ pi-: with O(10) fb^(-1), 80,000 fully reconstructed events expected
- For eta' total width: measurement to 1% level of cross section and BR(eta' -> gamma gamma) would bring fractional error on Gamma_eta' to about 1.4%

## Context
This paper extends the KLOE hadron spectroscopy program (2000-2006) to higher statistics and new channels at the upgraded DAFNE collider. It aims to improve on previous measurements from Mark II (Boyer 1990) for gamma-gamma -> pi+pi- near threshold, and from Crystal Ball (Marsiske 1990) for gamma-gamma -> pi0 pi0. The meson transition form factor measurements below 1 GeV^2 address poorly known regions relevant for the hadronic light-by-light contribution to the muon g-2 (Jegerlehner 2009). The eta-eta' mixing analysis builds on KLOE results (Ambrosino 2009) using the model of Rosner (1982) to extract gluonium content.
