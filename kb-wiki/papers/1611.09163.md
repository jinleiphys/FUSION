# Nucleon spin and quark content at the physical point

> We present results on the spin and quark content of the nucleon using $N_f=2$ twisted mass clover-improved fermion simulations with a pion mass close to its physical value. We use recently developed methods to obtain accurate results for both connected and disconnected contributions. We provide results for the axial charge, quark and gluon momentum fraction as well as the light, strange and charm $σ$-terms.

# Nucleon spin and quark content at the physical point

## Key claim

Using lattice QCD with N_f=2 twisted mass clover-improved fermions at a pion mass of 131 MeV, the authors obtain a nucleon spin sum of 0.51(5)(4) that satisfies the expected 1/2, but the axial charge g_A^{u-d}=1.21(3)(3) remains below the experimental value even after including disconnected contributions and excited-state analysis.

## Method

The authors compute nucleon matrix elements of scalar, axial-vector, and tensor operators at zero momentum transfer on a 48^3x96 lattice with lattice spacing a=0.093(1) fm and pion mass m_pi=131 MeV. Connected contributions are evaluated using three-point functions with sink-source separations up to 1.7 fm, while disconnected contributions are computed using deflation of low-lying eigenvalues combined with the truncated solver method, employing approximately 850,000 measurements for disconnected diagrams and up to 70,000 measurements for connected diagrams at the largest separations.

## Key numbers

- Axial charge isovector: g_A^{u-d} = 1.21(3)(3) (first error statistical, second systematic from fit method difference)
- Axial charge strange disconnected: g_A^s = -0.042(10)
- Scalar charge isovector: g_S^{u-d} = 0.93(25)(19)
- Tensor charge isovector: g_T^{u-d} = 1.004(21)(20)
- Light quark sigma term: sigma_{pi N} = 36(2) MeV
- Strange sigma term: sigma_s = 37(8) MeV
- Charm sigma term: sigma_c = 83(17) MeV
- Gluon momentum fraction (renormalized): <x>_g^R = 0.273(23)(24)
- Momentum sum rule: sum_q <x>_q + <x>_g = 1.01(10)(2)
- Quark intrinsic spin contributions at physical point: (1/2)Delta Sigma^u = 0.413(13), (1/2)Delta Sigma^d = -0.193(7), (1/2)Delta Sigma^s = -0.021(5)
- Total quark spin: J^u = 0.310(26), J^d = 0.056(26), J^s = 0.046(21)
- Gluon spin from sum rule: J^g = 0.09(6)

## Context

This work extends previous lattice QCD calculations by the same collaboration using twisted mass fermions at heavier pion masses (Abdel-Rehim et al., 2015; Alexandrou et al., 2013) to the physical pion mass, and provides the first direct computation of disconnected contributions to nucleon charges and moments at the physical point. The results enable direct comparison with experimental measurements from the European Muon Collaboration (Ashman, 1989) and ongoing programs at JLab, and the sigma terms reduce uncertainties in interpreting dark matter direct detection experiments through Higgs-nucleon coupling (Ellis, 2008). The gluon momentum fraction calculation builds on methodology tested at m_pi=373 MeV (Alexandrou et al., 2013).
