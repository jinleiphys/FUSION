## Context
This work extends the study of hadronic molecular states using the compositeness condition, previously applied to XYZ resonances and the deuteron by Dong (2008, 2017) and Branz (2009). It directly addresses the five narrow Î©*_c states observed by LHCb (Aaij 2017) in the Îž_c^+K^- mass spectrum. The results are compared with the constituent quark model calculations of Wang (2017) and with molecular interpretations by Montana (2017), Debastiani (2017), and Nieves (2017), as well as pentaquark-like interpretations by Kim (2017). The predicted radiative decay widths (order 0.1 eV) differ dramatically from those in the conventional three-quark picture (order 10^{-3} MeV), offering a clear experimental test to distinguish between molecular and quark-model interpretations.
