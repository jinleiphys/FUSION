# Multi-Pion Systems in Lattice QCD and the Three-Pion Interaction

> The ground-state energies of 2, 3, 4 and 5 π^+'s in a spatial volume V (2.5 fm)^3 are computed with lattice QCD. By eliminating the leading contribution from three-π^+ interactions, particular combinations of these n-π^+ ground-state energies provide precise extractions of the π^+π^+ scattering length in agreement with that obtained from calculations involving only two π^+'s. The three-π^+ interaction can be isolated by forming other combinations of the n-π^+ ground-state energies. We find a result that is consistent with a repulsive three-π^+ interaction for m_π< 352 MeV.

## Key claim

The paper reports the first lattice QCD calculation of systems with more than two hadrons, finding that the three-pion interaction is consistent with a repulsive interaction for pion masses below 352 MeV, but the authors caution that definitive statements about the physical value require further calculations with different lattice spacings and volumes.

## Method

The ground-state energies of 2, 3, 4, and 5 positively charged pions were computed in a spatial volume of approximately (2.5 fm)^3 using mixed-action lattice QCD with domain-wall valence quarks on rooted-staggered MILC gauge configurations at four pion masses. The energy shifts were extracted from ratios of n-pion correlation functions to the single-pion correlator, and the scattering length and three-body interaction were isolated using finite-volume energy shift formulas that depend on the number of pions and the volume.

## Key numbers

* Pion masses studied: 291.3(1.0)(1.0) MeV, 351.9(0.5)(0.2) MeV, 491.4(0.4)(0.3) MeV, and 590.5(0.8)(0.2) MeV
* Lattice spacing: b = 0.125 fm; spatial volume: V ~ (2.5 fm)^3 on a 20^3 x 64 lattice
* Renormalization-group invariant three-pion interaction m_pi f_pi^4 eta_3^L at L=2.5 fm: 1.3(2)(7) at m_pi = 291 MeV, 0.8(1)(2) at m_pi = 352 MeV, 0.4(2)(4) at m_pi = 491 MeV, and -0.4(3)(4) at m_pi = 591 MeV
* Scale-dependent three-pion interaction m_pi f_pi^4 eta_3(mu=1/b): 1.2(2)(7) at m_pi = 291 MeV, 0.7(1)(2) at m_pi = 352 MeV, -0.1(2)(4) at m_pi = 491 MeV, and -1.3(3)(4) at m_pi = 591 MeV
* Geometric constants used in finite-volume expansion: I = -8.9136329, J = 16.532316, K = 8.4019240

## Context

This work extends the finite-volume formalism for multi-boson systems developed by Luscher (1986, 1990) and Beane et al. (2007) to systems of three, four, and five pions, enabling the first direct lattice QCD extraction of the three-pion interaction. The results for the two-pion scattering length agree with previous calculations by Beane et al. (2007) using only two-pion systems, and the three-pion interaction magnitude is consistent with naive dimensional analysis expectations. Future calculations in different volumes and at finer lattice spacings are needed to determine the leading correction to the three-pion interaction and to eliminate finite-lattice spacing effects.
