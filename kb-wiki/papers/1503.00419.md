## Context

This work extends previous studies of О›(1405) photoproduction, particularly addressing the failure of a simple Born approximation model (Nam 2008) to reproduce CLAS data (Moriya 2013). It builds upon the chiral-unitary model (ChUM) calculations by Magas (2005), Jido (2003), and Hyodo (2011) that predicted the two-pole structure, and uses the theoretical framework of Nacher (1998) who studied the Dalitz process by gauging the Weinberg-Tomozawa interaction. The results enable future investigations of the О›(1405) internal structure, though the authors note that distinguishing between one-pole and two-pole scenarios remains challenging with current effective approaches.
