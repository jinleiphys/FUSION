# Study of the strong $Σ_c\to Λ_c π$, $Σ_c^{*}\to Λ_c π$ and $Ξ_c^{*}\to Ξ_c π$ decays in a nonrelativistic quark model

> We present results for the strong widths corresponding to the $Σ_c\to Λ_c π$, $Σ_c^{*}\to Λ_c π$ and $Ξ_c^{*}\to Ξ_c π$ decays. The calculations have been done in a nonrelativistic constituent quark model with wave functions that take advantage of the constraints imposed by heavy quark symmetry. Partial conservation of axial current hypothesis allows us to determine the strong vertices from an analysis of the axial current matrix elements. Our results \hbox{$Γ(Σ_c^{++}\to Λ_c^+ π^+)=2.41 \pm0.07\pm0.02 \mathrm{MeV}$}, {$Γ(Σ_c^{+}\to Λ_c^+ π^0)=2.79 \pm0.08\pm0.02 \mathrm{MeV}$}, {$Γ(Σ_c^{0}\to Λ_c^+ π^-)=2.37 \pm0.07\pm0.02 \mathrm{MeV}$}, {$Γ(Σ_c^{* ++}\to Λ_c^+ π^+)=17.52\pm0.74\pm0.12 \mathrm{MeV}$}, {$Γ(Σ_c^{* +}\to Λ_c^+ π^0)=17.31\pm0.73\pm0.12 \mathrm{MeV}$}, {$Γ(Σ_c^{* 0}\to Λ_c^+ π^-)=16.90\pm0.71\pm0.12 \mathrm{MeV}$}, {$Γ(Ξ_c^{* +}\to Ξ_c^0 π^++Ξ_c^+π^0)=3.18\pm0.10\pm0.01 \mathrm{MeV}$} and {$Γ(Ξ_c^{* 0}\to Ξ_c^+ π^-+Ξ_c^0π^0)=3.03\pm0.10\pm0.01 \mathrm{MeV}$} are in good agreement with experimental determinations.

## Key claim

The authors compute strong decay widths for charmed baryons using a nonrelativistic constituent quark model with heavy-quark-symmetry-constrained wave functions and the PCAC hypothesis, finding good overall agreement with experiment, though the results are sensitive to the precise hadron masses used and vary by 6-8% across five different quark-quark interactions.

## Method

The strong pion-emission amplitudes for the decays Sigma_c -> Lambda_c pi, Sigma_c* -> Lambda_c pi, and Xi_c* -> Xi_c pi were obtained via the PCAC relation from the non-pole part of the axial current matrix element between baryon states. The baryon wave functions were taken from a previous variational solution of the three-body problem using five different nonrelativistic quark-quark interactions (Bhaduri and four from Silvestre-Brac and Semay), and the matrix elements were evaluated in a spectator (one-quark emission) model using one-body current operators.

*   Gamma(Sigma_c^{0} -> Lambda_c^+ pi^-) = 2.37 +/- 0.07 +/- 0.02 MeV

## Context

This work extends the authors' previous nonrelativistic quark model studies of baryon static properties (Albertus 2004) and semileptonic Lambda_b and Xi_b decays (Albertus 2005) to strong pion decays of charmed baryons. It provides the first dynamical calculation of these decays within a nonrelativistic approach, contrasting with earlier CQM estimates by Rosner (1995) and Pirjol and Yan (1997) that did not evaluate full matrix elements, and with results from HHCPT (Yan 1992, Huang 1995, Cheng 1997), QCD sum rules (Grozin 1998, Zhu 1998), the light-front quark model (Tawfiq 1998), and the relativistic three-quark model (Ivanov 1999). The results enable improved tests of heavy quark symmetry and the nonrelativistic quark model in the charmed baryon sector, and the authors note that a new Lambda_c mass measurement by BaBar (2005) would reduce their calculated widths by 9% for Sigma_c decays and 3% for Sigma_c* decays.
