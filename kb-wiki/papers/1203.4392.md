# On timelike and spacelike hard exclusive reactions

> We show to next-to-leading order accuracy in the strong coupling alpha_s how the collinear factorization properties of QCD in the generalized Bjorken regime relate exclusive amplitudes for spacelike and timelike hadronic processes. This yields simple space--to--timelike relations linking the amplitudes for electroproduction of a photon or meson to those for photo- or meso-production of a lepton pair. These relations constitute a new test of the relevance of leading twist analyzes of experimental data.

## Key claim

The paper demonstrates to next-to-leading order (NLO) accuracy in the strong coupling α_s that analytic continuation and causality relate the hard coefficients for spacelike exclusive amplitudes to those for timelike exclusive amplitudes, yielding simple space-to-timelike relations that are modified by an additional -iπ term from factorization logarithms at NLO.

## Method

The authors start from the factorized form of deeply virtual Compton scattering (DVCS) amplitudes in terms of Compton form factors (CFFs) and generalized parton distributions (GPDs), writing the hard coefficients to NLO accuracy. They then apply the substitution rule ξ = +η → ξ = -η and Q² → -Q², combined with the +iε causality prescription, to analytically continue the spacelike hard coefficients into the timelike region for timelike Compton scattering (TCS). The same procedure is extended to deeply virtual meson production (DVMP) and exclusive Drell-Yan processes, where the amplitudes factorize into GPDs and meson distribution amplitudes (DAs).

## Key numbers

- The NLO space-to-timelike relation for Compton form factors is given by: ᵀℋ = ℋ* - iπ Q² ∂/∂Q² ℋ* (for ξ-symmetric CFFs) and ᵀℋ̃ = -ℋ̃* + iπ Q² ∂/∂Q² ℋ̃* (for ξ-antisymmetric CFFs)
- For the pion transition form factor, the NLO relation reads: ᵀℋ̃_{π±} ≈ ℋ̃_{π∓}* - iπ Q ∂/∂Q² [Q ℋ̃_{π∓}*]
- At the input scale μ² = Q² = 4 GeV², the NLO correction to the real part of the timelike CFF ℋ is approximately -400% in the small-ξ region (10⁻⁴ ≤ ξ ≤ 10⁻²), as illustrated using the Goloskokov-Kroll model for H GPDs
- The LO coefficient for DVMP is C₀(u,x,ξ) = 1/[u(ξ-x)], and the LO gluon coefficient for DVCS is C₀^g = 0

## Context

This work extends the classic analysis of spacelike and timelike meson form factors by Belitsky, Radyushkin, and Schäfer (BRS) to the case of generalized parton distributions and exclusive processes in the generalized Bjorken regime. The derived relations provide a new test of leading-twist factorization for experimental data, particularly for future Jefferson Lab experiments after the 12 GeV upgrade and for a proposed Electron-Ion Collider (Boer et al., 2011). The results also enable NLO predictions for lepton-pair angle asymmetries in TCS, governed by the real part of the timelike CFF, which was previously studied in a dipole model approach by Schäfer et al. (2010).
