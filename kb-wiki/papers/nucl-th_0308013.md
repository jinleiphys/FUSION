# Charm production from proton-proton collisions

> We evaluate the cross sections for charmed hadron production from proton-proton reactions $pp\to\bar D^0pΛ_c^+$ and $pp\to\bar D^{*0}pΛ_c^+$ using a hadronic Lagrangian. With empirical coupling constants and cutoff parameters in the form factors, sum of their cross sections at center-of-mass energy of 11.5 GeV is about 1 $μ$b and is comparable to measured inclusive cross section for charmed hadron production from proton-proton reactions. The cross section decreases to about 1 nb at 40 MeV above threshold.

## Key claim
The sum of the cross sections for the reactions \(pp\to\bar D^0p\Lambda_c^+\) and \(pp\to\bar D^{*0}p\Lambda_c^+\) at a center-of-mass energy of 11.5 GeV is about 1 \(\mu\)b, which is comparable to the measured inclusive cross section for charmed hadron production from proton-proton reactions, but only when using a cutoff parameter of 0.42 GeV in the form factors that was empirically adjusted to fit strange hadron production data.

## Method
The authors used a hadronic Lagrangian based on SU(4) flavor symmetry with empirical masses and coupling constants to evaluate the cross sections for the two reactions. They included contributions from pion, rho meson, \(D\), and \(D^*\) exchanges, and expressed the total cross sections in terms of off-shell subprocess cross sections. Form factors with a cutoff parameter of 0.42 GeV, calibrated by reproducing the measured cross section for the reaction \(pp\to K^+p\Lambda\), were introduced at interaction vertices involving virtual charmed mesons and baryons.

## Key numbers
- Sum of cross sections for \(pp\to\bar D^0p\Lambda_c^+\) and \(pp\to\bar D^{*0}p\Lambda_c^+\) at center-of-mass energy of 11.5 GeV: about 1 \(\mu\)b.
- Cross section at 40 MeV above threshold: about 1 nb.
- Cutoff parameter \(\Lambda\) in form factors \(F_2({\bf q}^2)\) at vertices involving virtual charmed mesons and baryons: 0.42 GeV.
- Coupling constants used: \(g_{\pi NN}=13.5\), \(g_{\rho NN}=3.25\), \(\kappa_\rho=6.1\), \(g_{\pi DD^*}=5.56\), \(g_{\rho DD}=g_{\rho D^*D^*}=2.52\), \(g_{D^{*}N\Lambda_c}=-5.6\), \(g_{DN\Lambda_c}\simeq 13.5\).
- Cutoff parameters for form factors at \(\pi NN\) and \(\rho NN\) vertices: \(\Lambda_{\pi NN}=1.3\) GeV and \(\Lambda_{\rho NN}=1.4\) GeV.

## Context
This work extends the same hadronic model previously used to study \(J/\psi\) absorption by nucleons (Liu et al., 2001) and charm photoproduction on nucleons (Liu et al., 2002) to charmed hadron production in proton-proton collisions. It improves upon earlier studies by Gasparyan et al. and Rekalo et al. by not making the on-shell approximation for the exchanged mesons. The results are intended to guide future experiments at the proposed accelerator facility at the German Heavy Ion Research Center (GSI).
