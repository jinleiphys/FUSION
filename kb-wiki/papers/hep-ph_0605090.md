# Color Skyrmions in the Quark-Gluon Plasma

> We consider the general formulation of nonabelian fluid dynamics based on symmetry considerations. We point out that, quite generally, this admits solitonic excitations which are the color analog of skyrmions. Some general properties of the solitons are discussed.

## Key claim

The paper argues that nonabelian fluid dynamics, formulated from general symmetry considerations, necessarily admits topological solitons that are the color analog of skyrmions, but the existence and properties of these solitons depend on the specific choice of Hamiltonian and the equation of state for the color degrees of freedom.

## Method

The authors develop a general formulation of nonabelian fluid dynamics by generalizing the Kostant-Kirillov-Souriau (KKS) symplectic form for particles carrying nonabelian charges to a continuum fluid description. They then construct an action for the fluid where the color degrees of freedom are described by a group-valued field \(g(\vec{x},t)\in SU(3)\) (or \(SU(2)\) for simplicity), and use a spherically symmetric ansatz for \(g\) together with a variational ansatz for the charge density \(j^0\) to estimate the energy of the soliton as a function of its color charge and spin.

## Key numbers

* The topological charge (soliton number) for maps \(g:S^3\rightarrow SU(3)\) is given by \(Q = -\frac{1}{24\pi^2}\int d^3x\; \epsilon^{ijk} \text{Tr}(g^{-1}\partial_i g\; g^{-1}\partial_j g\; g^{-1}\partial_k g)\), with homotopy group \(\Pi_3[SU(3)]=\mathbb{Z}\).
* For the spherically symmetric ansatz \(g_s(\vec{x}) = \cos\phi(r) + i\vec{\sigma}\cdot\hat{x}\sin\phi(r)\), the topological charge is \(Q = (\phi(0)-\phi(\infty))/\pi\).
* The variational estimate of the soliton energy is \(\mathcal{E} = \frac{8\mu\pi}{3}(30)^{1/4}\left[(m-s)^2 + \frac{(m+s)^2}{5}\right]^{1/4}\), where \(m\) is the color charge, \(s\) is the spin, and \(\mu\) is a scale parameter from the Hamiltonian.
* The minimum of the energy occurs at \((\mu R)^4 = \frac{15}{128}\frac{1}{\pi^4}\left[(m-s)^2 + \frac{(m+s)^2}{5}\right]\), where \(R\) sets the scale of the soliton.

## Context

This work extends the general formulation of nonabelian fluid dynamics developed by Jackiw, Nair, and collaborators (JNP, bistro) to include topological solitons, which are the color analog of the flavor skyrmions originally proposed by Skyrme and later applied to baryons by Witten. The paper enables future investigation of soliton production and detection in the quark-gluon plasma created at the Relativistic Heavy Ion Collider, and raises open questions about the equation of state for color degrees of freedom and the role of magnetic screening.
