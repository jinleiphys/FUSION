- No bound state found for JГјlich-Bonn-MГјnchen chiral EFT or HAL QCD in either (T, J^ПЂ) = (1/2, 1/2^+) or (1/2, 3/2^+) states

## Context

This work extends previous analyses of S = -2 three-baryon systems by Gar, Filikhin, and Hiyama (no year given in text) that used simplified interactions, by employing three modern coupled-channel interactions. The finding that JГјlich Ch-EFT and HAL QCD produce no bound state despite strong attraction in the ^1S_0, t=0 channel contrasts with the ESC08c result, and the authors note that repulsive effects from the isospin partner ^1S_0, t=1 state prevent binding. The results enable future realistic analyses of S = -2 hypernuclear systems using the Faddeev approach with coupled-channel T-matrices.
