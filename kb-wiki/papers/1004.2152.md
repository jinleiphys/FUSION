# Regge description of two pseudoscalar meson production in antiproton-proton annihilation

> A Regge-inspired model is used to discuss the hard exclusive two-body hadronic reactions (pbar p ----> pi+ pi-, pi0 pi0, K+ K-, Kbar0 K0) for the FAIR facility project at GSI with the Panda detector. The comparison between the differential cross sections predictions and the available data is shown to determine the values of the few parameters of the model.

## Key claim

A Regge-inspired model with saturating trajectories and hadronic form factors can describe both the soft (low momentum transfer) and hard (large momentum transfer) regimes of antiproton-proton annihilation into two pseudoscalar mesons, but the model reproduces data more successfully for invariant energies s ≥ 13 GeV² than at the lower energy s = 11.3 GeV², where discrepancies in the forward-backward asymmetry and a shoulder in the angular distribution remain.

## Method

The authors replaced Feynman propagators for nucleon and Δ baryon exchange in the t- and u-channels with Regge propagators using linear trajectories (α_N(t) = -0.37 + 0.98 t, α_Δ(t) = 0.1 + 0.93 t), and introduced saturating trajectories (α(t) → -1 as t → -∞) to extend the model to large momentum transfer. A smooth connection between soft and hard regimes was achieved by switching from dipole to monopole form factors at a transition parameter x₀ = -1.4 GeV², and a non-reggeized ρ-meson exchange in the s-channel was added for charged pion production. For kaon production, Λ and Σ baryon trajectories replaced the nucleon and Δ, with a Φ-meson exchange in the s-channel.

## Key numbers

- Nucleon Regge trajectory: α_N(t) = -0.37 + 0.98 t (dimensionless slope in GeV⁻²)
- Δ Regge trajectory: α_Δ(t) = 0.1 + 0.93 t (dimensionless slope in GeV⁻²)
- Saturation parameters: t_sat,N = -2.6 GeV², t_sat,Δ = -3.0 GeV²
- Form factor cutoff for NNπ vertex: Λ_NNπ = 0.85 GeV
- Form factor cutoff for NΔπ vertex: Λ_NΔπ = 0.55 GeV
- Transition parameter: x₀,N = x₀,Δ = -1.4 GeV²
- ρ-meson form factor cutoff: Λ_ρππ = 0.95 GeV; normalization λ_ρ = 0.9
- For kaon channels: Λ_NΛK = Λ_NΣK = 1.05 GeV; Λ_ΦKK = 1.3 GeV; λ_Φ = 1
- Expected PANDA counting rates for pbar p → π⁻ π⁺ at θ = 60° in one week at L = 2×10⁻³² cm⁻² s⁻¹: 10⁶ events at s = 13.5 GeV², 2×10⁵ events at s = 16 GeV², 4×10⁴ events at s = 20 GeV²

## Context

This work extends the Regge phenomenology previously applied to photoproduction and electroproduction of mesons (Guidal, Laget, and Vanderhaeghen, 1997; Guidal, 2008) to antiproton-proton annihilation channels relevant for the FAIR facility and the PANDA detector. The model contrasts with the quark interchange model (White, 1979) for charged pion production, predicting larger cross sections at forward and backward angles and in the large-angle region near cos θ = 0. The approach enables predictions for PANDA over the energy range 5.4 GeV² ≤ s ≤ 27 GeV², where high-statistics data could discriminate between Regge and perturbative QCD mechanisms.
