# The twist-three distribution $e^q(x,k_\perp)$ in a light-front model

> We discuss the twist-three, unpolarized, chiral-odd, transverse momentum dependent parton distribution (TMD) $e^q(x,k_\perp)$ within a light-front model. We review a model-independent decomposition of this TMD, which follows from the QCD equations of motion and is given in terms of a leading-twist mass term, a pure interaction-dependent contribution, and singular terms. The leading-twist and pure twist-three terms are represented in terms of overlap of light-front wave functions (LFWFs), taking into account the Fock states with three valence quark ($3q$) and three-quark plus one gluon ($3q+g$). The $3q$ and $3q+g$ LFWFs with total orbital angular momentum zero are modeled using a parametrization derived from the conformal expansion of the proton distribution amplitudes, with parameters fitted to reproduce available phenomenological information on the unpolarized leading-twist quark and gluon collinear parton distributions. Numerical predictions for both the quark TMD $e^q(x,k_\perp)$ and the collinear parton distribution $e^q(x)$ are presented, discussing the role of the quark-gluon correlations in the proton.

## Key claim

The twist-three TMD $e^q(x,k_\perp)$ can be decomposed into a leading-twist mass term, a pure interaction-dependent (tilde) term, and singular contributions; the authors compute these contributions explicitly in a light-front model with both three-quark and three-quark-plus-gluon Fock states, finding that the genuine twist-three (quark-gluon correlation) term is non-negligible compared to the Wandzura-Wilczek approximation.

## Method

The authors construct light-front wave functions (LFWFs) for the proton's $3q$ and $3q+g$ Fock states with zero total orbital angular momentum, parametrizing the longitudinal momentum dependence via conformal expansions of the proton's twist-three and twist-four distribution amplitudes (DAs) and the transverse momentum dependence via Gaussian forms modified by the Brodsky-Huang-Lepage prescription. They fit the DA coefficients, parton masses, and transverse-width parameters to reproduce the MMHT2014 unpolarized twist-two PDFs $f_1^q(x)$ and $f_1^g(x)$ at $Q^2 = 1$ GeV$^2$, then compute the overlap integrals giving the tilde term $\tilde e^q(x,k_\perp)$ and the mass term contribution to $e^q(x,k_\perp)$.

## Key numbers

* Quark mass: $m_q = 0.161$ GeV; gluon mass: $m_g = 0.050$ GeV (fitted values).
* Transverse-width parameters: $a_3 = 0.85$ GeV$^{-1}$ for the $3q$ state; $a_4 = 0.92 a_3$ for the $3q+g$ state.
* Fitted DA normalization: $f_N = 4.68 \times 10^{-3}$ GeV$^2$ (compared to lattice QCD value $5.0 \pm 0.5 \times 10^{-3}$ GeV$^2$).
* Fitted DA shape parameters: $A = 1.14$, $B = 0.50$ (lattice QCD ranges: $A \in [0.85,0.95]$, $B \in [0.23,0.33]$).
* Fitted twist-four DA coefficients: $\lambda_1^g = 2.79 \times 10^{-3}$ GeV$^2$, $\lambda_2^g = 1.33 \times 10^{-3}$ GeV$^2$, $\lambda_3^g = 0.36 \times 10^{-3}$ GeV$^2$ (QCD sum rule values: $\lambda_1^g = 2.6 \pm 1.2$, $\lambda_2^g = 2.3 \pm 0.7$, $\lambda_3^g = 0.54 \pm 0.2$, all in $10^{-3}$ GeV$^2$).

## Context

This work extends earlier light-front model calculations of twist-two PDFs and the twist-three polarized structure function $g_2^q(x)$ by Braun et al. (2011) to the unpolarized chiral-odd twist-three TMD $e^q(x,k_\perp)$, explicitly including gluonic degrees of freedom beyond the quark-model calculations of Lorce et al. (2014) and Avakian et al. (2010). The results enable quantitative studies of quark-gluon correlations in the proton and provide a benchmark for testing the Wandzura-Wilczek approximation in semi-inclusive deep-inelastic scattering (SIDIS) observables at JLab12 and a future Electron-Ion Collider.
