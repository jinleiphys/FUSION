# The off-shell electromagnetic form factors of pions and kaons in chiral perturbation theory

> The off-shell electromagnetic vertex of a (pseudo-) scalar particle contains, in general, two form factors F and G which depend, in addition to the squared momentum transfer, on the invariant masses associated with the initial and final legs of the vertex. Chiral perturbation theory to one loop is used to calculate the off-shell form factors of pions and kaons. The formalism of Gasser and Leutwyler, which was previously used to calculate the on-shell limit of the form factor F, is extended to accommodate the most general form for off-shell Green's functions in the pseudoscalar meson sector. We find that chiral symmetry predicts that the form factors F of the charged pions and kaons go off-shell in the same way, i.e., the off-shell slope at the real photon point is given by the same new phenomenological constant $β_1$. Furthermore, it is shown that at order $p^4$ the form factor F of the $K^0$ does not show any off-shell dependence. The form factors G are all related to the form factors F in the correct fashion as required by the Ward-Takahashi identity. Numerical results for different off-shell kinematics are presented.

## Key claim

Chiral perturbation theory to one loop predicts that the off-shell slope of the electromagnetic form factor F for charged pions and charged kaons is governed by the same new phenomenological constant β₁, meaning they go off-shell in the same way; at order p⁴, the form factor F of the neutral K⁰ shows no off-shell dependence at all.

## Method

The authors extended the Gasser and Leutwyler (1985) chiral perturbation theory formalism to accommodate the most general off-shell Green's functions in the pseudoscalar meson sector, identifying two additional operator structures at order p⁴ that vanish on-shell. They calculated the renormalized three-point Green's function for pions and kaons by summing tree-level contributions from the O(p²) and O(p⁴) Lagrangians, one-loop diagrams from the O(p²) Lagrangian, and applying wave function renormalization, then extracted the off-shell form factors F and G.

## Key numbers

- The pion decay constant used is F ≈ 93 MeV.
- The off-shell contribution to the unrenormalized F form factor of charged pions and kaons is proportional to 16β₁(p_f² + p_i² − 2M²)/F², where M is the meson mass.
- The tree-level G form factor for charged pions and kaons is (p_f − p_i)μ × 2L₉(p_i² − p_f²)/F².
- The one-loop function A(q², M², μ²) is given by −1/(192π²F²) × [ln(M²/μ²) + 1/3 + (1 − 4M²/q²)H(q²/M²)].

## Context

This work extends the on-shell chiral perturbation theory calculations of Gasser and Leutwyler (1984, 1985) for pion and kaon electromagnetic form factors to the off-shell case, which is required for consistent treatment of processes such as pion electroproduction and meson exchange currents where the intermediate meson is not on its mass shell. The results enable future calculations of off-shell effects in t-channel pion exchange diagrams and provide a framework for interpreting experiments at new electron accelerators that will probe kaon electromagnetic interactions.
