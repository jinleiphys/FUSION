# Note on "Vacuum stability of a general scalar potential of a few fields"

> The purpose of this letter is to point out that some conclusions in the paper (Eur. Phys. J. C {\bf 76}, 324(2016)) are incomplete, and to give complete and improved conclusions. The analytic necessary and sufficient conditions are given for the boundedness-from-below conditions of general scalar potentials of two real scalar fields $ϕ_1$ and $ϕ_2$ and the Higgs bonson $\mathbf{H}$.

# Note on "Vacuum stability of a general scalar potential of a few fields"

## Key claim
The analytic necessary and sufficient conditions for boundedness-from-below of general scalar potentials of two real scalar fields and the Higgs boson given by Kannike (2016) are incomplete; this note provides corrected and complete conditions, specifically correcting the positivity condition for the quadratic form M²(φ₁,φ₂) which requires positive semi-definiteness of its coefficient matrix via Sylvester's criterion rather than the conditions previously stated.

## Method
The author rewrites the scalar potential V(φ₁,φ₂,|H|) as a quadratic polynomial in |H|² with coefficients depending on φ₁ and φ₂, then applies the classical positivity conditions for a quadratic polynomial p(t)=at²+bt+c for all t≥0. The condition M²(φ₁,φ₂)≥0 is treated as positive semi-definiteness of a quadratic form in φ₁,φ₂, evaluated via Sylvester's criterion. The positivity of the quartic form V(φ₁,φ₂) and of the derived quartic form V'(φ₁,φ₂)=4λ_H V(φ₁,φ₂)−(M²(φ₁,φ₂))² are each analyzed using the analytic necessary and sufficient conditions for a binary quartic homogeneous polynomial to be positive definite, following the results of Wang-Qi (2005).

## Key numbers
- The corrected condition for M²(φ₁,φ₂)≥0 requires λ_H20 ≥ 0, λ_H02 ≥ 0, and λ_H20 λ_H02 − (1/4)λ_H11² ≥ 0 (Sylvester's criterion).
- The positivity condition for the quartic form V(φ₁,φ₂) requires λ₄₀ > 0, λ₀₄ > 0, and either (D=0, G=0, R=0 and Q>0) or (D>0 and Q≥0, or R>0), where D=I³−27J², Q=(1/6)λ₄₀λ₂₂−(1/16)λ₃₁², and R=λ₄₀²I−12Q².
- The positivity condition for V'(φ₁,φ₂) requires λ'₄₀ > 0, λ'₀₄ > 0, and analogous conditions on D', G', R', Q' constructed from the primed coefficients λ'₄₀=4λ₄₀λ_H−λ_H20², λ'₀₄=4λ₀₄λ_H−λ_H02², λ'₃₁=4λ_Hλ₃₁−2λ_H20λ_H11, λ'₁₃=4λ_Hλ₁₃−2λ_H02λ_H11, and λ'₂₂=4λ_Hλ₂₂−2λ_H20λ_H02−λ_H11².

## Context
This note corrects the boundedness-from-below conditions presented by Kannike (2016) for scalar potentials with two real scalar fields and the Higgs boson, specifically pointing out that the earlier work's Eqs.(54) and (55) are incomplete. The corrected analysis builds on the classical positivity theory for binary quartic forms tracing back to Rees (1922), Lazard (1988), Gadem-Li (1964), Ku (1965), and Jury-Mansour (1981), with the definitive analytic necessary and sufficient conditions provided by Wang-Qi (2005). The results enable precise vacuum stability analysis in multi-field scalar potentials relevant to beyond-Standard-Model physics, and the author notes that Qi-Song-Zhang (2022) have since given an alternative formulation of the positivity conditions.
