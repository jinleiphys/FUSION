# Associated jet + electroweak gauge boson production in hadronic collisions at forward rapidities in the color-dipole $S$-matrix framework

> The cross-section for the associated production of a jet with an electroweak gauge boson ($G = W^{\pm}, Z^0, γ$) at forward rapidities in $pp$ and $pA$ collisions is derived within the color - dipole $S$ - matrix framework. We present the full expressions for the differential cross-section of the $q p \rightarrow G q X$ process in the transverse momentum space, considering the longitudinal and transverse polarizations of the gauge boson. We demonstrate that the final formulae can be expressed in terms of the unintegrated gluon distribution and reproduce previous results for the associated jet + $γ$ and jet + $Z^0$ production, derived using other frameworks. Moreover, we derive the back - to - back correlation limit of the spectra and show that it can be expressed in terms of the unpolarized and linearly polarized transverse momentum gluon distributions. Our results improve the description of the inclusive jet plus color neutral particle production at forward rapidities, not far from the proton fragmentation region, in $pp$ or $pA$ collisions, and are the main ingredient to study the impact of nonlinear QCD effects in two - particle correlations.

## Key claim

The paper derives the full differential cross-section for associated jet plus electroweak gauge boson (W±, Z0, γ) production at forward rapidities in pp and pA collisions within the color-dipole S-matrix framework, claiming that the formalism reproduces previous results for jet+γ and jet+Z0 production in appropriate limits while providing the first derivation for jet+W± production; the key hedge is that the results are valid in the small-x limit and depend on the unintegrated gluon distribution which resums both initial and final state interactions.

## Method

The authors start from the color-dipole S-matrix framework, where the qp → GqX process is treated as the excitation of a perturbative |Gqk⟩ Fock state of the incoming quark by multiple gluon exchanges with the target. They derive the differential cross-section in transverse momentum space by expressing the scattering amplitude in terms of impact parameter integrals, then transform to momentum representation using the relation between the dipole-proton cross-section and the unintegrated gluon distribution f(x,k). The vector and axial contributions to the light-front wave functions for q → Gq' transitions are separated, and explicit expressions are obtained for transverse and longitudinal polarizations of the gauge boson.

## Context

This work extends the formalism of Bandeira, Goncalves, and Schafer (2024) for inclusive electroweak gauge boson production to the associated jet-plus-boson case, and reproduces the expressions used by Dominguez (2011), Stasto (2012), Basso (2015), Ducloue (2017), Goncalves (2020), Benic (2022), and Taels (2023) for jet+γ and jet+Z0 production in appropriate limits. The results enable studies of nonlinear QCD effects in two-particle correlations at forward rapidities at the LHC, particularly the depletion of the back-to-back peak predicted by collinear formalism due to the saturation scale Q_s, and provide the first derivation of the jet+W± cross-section in the hybrid factorization formalism.
