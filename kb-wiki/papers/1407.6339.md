# On higher twist chiral-odd pion generalized parton distributions

> We define in the framework of light-cone collinear factorization method, the chiral-odd generalized parton distributions (GPDs) of a pseudoscalar hadron (such as the $π^0$) up to twist 6. For that, we introduce the relevant matrix elements for 2-parton non-local operators, as well as matrix elements for 3-parton non-local correlators. Their detailed parametrization is fixed based on parity, charge conjugation and time reversal invariance. This leads to the introduction of 28 real GPDs, which are subject to constraints coming from the QCD equations of motion.

## Key claim

The paper defines the complete set of chiral-odd generalized parton distributions (GPDs) for a pseudoscalar hadron like the π⁰ up to twist 6, using light-cone collinear factorization, but explicitly excludes pion mass effects and notes that the 28 resulting GPDs are not independent and require further reduction via QCD equations of motion and n-invariance constraints.

## Method

The authors apply the light-cone collinear factorization method, generalizing the Ellis-Furmanski-Petronzio approach to exclusive processes, to expand hard scattering amplitudes around the dominant light-cone direction. They introduce 2-parton and 3-parton non-local correlators for the chiral-odd case, parametrize them using parity, charge conjugation, and time reversal invariance, and derive constraints from QCD equations of motion and n-invariance to relate the resulting GPDs.

## Key numbers

- 28 real GPDs are introduced from the 2-parton and 3-parton correlators up to twist 6
- The 2-parton correlators yield GPDs at twist 2, 3, and 4: H_T, H_T3, H_T4 (twist 2 and 4), H_S (twist 3), and zero for the γ⁵ structure (twist 3)
- The 3-parton and 2-parton (with transverse derivative) correlators for the σ^{αβ} structure introduce 6 functions T_i^T (i=1,...,6) for 2-parton and 6 functions T_i for 3-parton, spanning twist 3, 4, and 5
- The 3-parton and 2-parton (with longitudinal derivative) correlators for the σ^{αβ} structure introduce 3 functions M_1^-, M_2^-, M_3^- for 2-parton and 3 functions M_1, M_2, M_3 for 3-parton, spanning twist 4, 5, and 6
- Four independent equations (I, II, III, IV) are derived from the QCD equations of motion, with a second set of four equations from the antiquark Dirac equation

## Context

This work extends the higher-twist analysis of generalized parton distributions, previously developed for chiral-even cases such as the ρ meson (Anikin 2009, Anikin 2011) and for nucleon GPDs (Braun 2011), to the chiral-odd sector of the pion. It builds on the light-cone collinear factorization method (Ellis 1982, Efremov 1983, Teryaev 1995) and the n-invariance technique (Anikin 2009, Anikin 2009). The results enable future calculations of exclusive processes involving pion GPDs beyond leading twist, such as deeply virtual Compton scattering or hard exclusive pion production, and provide the foundation for reducing the 28 GPDs to an independent set through combined QCD equations of motion and n-invariance constraints.
