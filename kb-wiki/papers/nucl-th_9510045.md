## Method

They then solved the gap equation for proton pairing in the ¬πS‚ÇÄ state at zero temperature, and used the resulting critical temperatures together with the formalism of Yakovlev and Levenfish (1995) to compute neutrino energy loss rates for both neutron-branch and proton-branch modified URCA processes with electrons and muons, including suppression factors from proton superconductivity.
