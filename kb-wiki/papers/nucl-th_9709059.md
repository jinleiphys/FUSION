# Sign convention of residues in QCD sum rules

> We show that signs of pole residues $λ_N, λ_Λ, λ_Σ, λ_Ξ$ for ${1\over2}^+$ octet baryons are identical in the QCD sum rule approach. To do this we compare signs of meson-baryon coupling constants $ g_{KN Λ}, g_{KN Σ}$, $g_{πΛΣ}$ and $g_{KΛΞ}$ each other.

# Sign convention of residues in QCD sum rules

## Key claim
The signs of the pole residues λ_N, λ_Λ, λ_Σ, and λ_Ξ for the 1/2^+ octet baryons are identical in the QCD sum rule approach, but the absolute sign (whether they are all positive or all negative) remains unknown.

## Method
The author compares the signs of meson-baryon coupling constants g_{KNΛ}, g_{KNΣ}, g_{πΛΣ}, and g_{KΛΞ} calculated from three-point QCD sum rules, using Ioffe's interpolating fields. By matching the sign patterns from the sum rule expressions to the known sign conventions of de Swart (1990), the relative signs of the baryon residues are deduced.

## Key numbers
- g_{πΛΣ} = 7.53, computed with ⟨q̄q⟩ = −(0.230 GeV)^3 and ⟨(α_s/π) G^2⟩ = (0.340 GeV)^4
- g_{KΣΞ} = −7.02, computed with ⟨q̄q⟩ = −(0.230 GeV)^3 and f_K = 160 MeV

## Context
This work extends the QCD sum rule calculations of meson-baryon coupling constants by Choe, Cheoun, and Lee (1996) to determine the relative signs of baryon residues, which are not accessible from standard two-point sum rules that only yield λ_B^2. The results are consistent with the sign convention of de Swart (1990) and resolve an ambiguity in the sign of residues for the 1/2^+ octet baryons. The author notes that the transition moment μ(Σ^0Λ) could provide an independent check, but no direct QCD sum rule calculation of this moment exists yet.
