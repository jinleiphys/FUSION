# State mixing and masses of the $π^0$, $η$ and $η^\prime$ mesons from $n_f=1+1+1$ lattice QCD+QED

> We present a lattice analysis of the light pseudoscalar mesons with consideration for the mixing between the flavour-neutral states $π^0$, $η$ and $η^\prime$. We extract the masses and flavour compositions of the pseudoscalar meson nonet in $n_f=1+1+1$ lattice QCD+QED around an SU(3)-flavour symmetric point, and observe flavour-symmetry features of the extracted data, along with preliminary extrapolation results for the flavour compositions at the physical point. A key result of this work is the observed mass splitting between the $π^0$ and $η$ on our ensembles, which is found to exhibit behaviour that is simply related to the corresponding flavour compositions.

## Key claim

The paper extracts the masses and flavour compositions of the π⁰, η and η′ mesons from lattice QCD+QED at an SU(3)-flavour symmetric point, but the extrapolation to physical quark masses is preliminary and does not include continuum or infinite-volume extrapolations, so the quoted physical-point mixing angles carry large uncertainties.

## Method

The authors use n_f=1+1+1 lattice QCD+QED on 24³×48 ensembles with a tree-level Symanzik improved gauge action and O(a)-improved SLiNC fermions at a single lattice spacing a=0.068(2) fm. They construct a 6×6 correlation matrix from three quark-flavour basis operators (ūγ⁵u, d̄γ⁵d, s̄γ⁵s) each with two levels of Gaussian smearing, solve a generalized eigenvalue problem to extract masses and overlaps, and fit the results to leading-order flavour-breaking expansions in mass and charge parameters.

## Key numbers

* Lattice spacing: a = 0.068(2) fm, with QCD coupling β_QCD = 5.5 and QED coupling β_QED = 0.8 (giving α_QED ≈ 0.1, roughly 10× the physical value)
* Ensemble 1 (U-spin symmetric): M_π⁰ = 457(5) MeV, M_η = 473(5) MeV, M_η′ = 1234(51) MeV, with mass splitting M_η − M_π⁰ = 15(1) MeV
* Ensemble 4 (U-spin symmetric, different quark masses): M_π⁰ = 430(11) MeV, M_η = 521(9) MeV, M_η′ = 1519(127) MeV, with mass splitting M_η − M_π⁰ = 86(3) MeV
* Ensemble 6 (lightest up/down quarks): M_π⁰ = 404(7) MeV, M_η = 503(5) MeV, M_η′ = 1058(50) MeV, with mass splitting M_η − M_π⁰ = 97(2) MeV
* Extrapolated η–η′ mixing angle: θ_ηη′ = (−15.1^{+5.9}_{−6})°
* Extrapolated π⁰ flavour composition: |π⁰⟩ = 0.85(14)|π_3⟩ − 0.27(25)|η_8⟩ + 0.29(22)|η_1⟩

## Context

This work extends previous lattice studies of η–η′ mixing (Christ 2010, Dudek 2011, Ottnad 2017, Bali 2021) by including QED and resolving π⁰–η mass splitting for the first time in n_f=1+1+1 lattice QCD+QED, whereas prior studies worked in the isospin limit and excluded π⁰ admixture. The results enable future calculations at physical quark masses with continuum and infinite-volume extrapolations, and provide a framework for disentangling QCD and QED contributions to flavour-neutral meson mixing.
