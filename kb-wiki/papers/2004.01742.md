# Threshold $π^-$ Photoproduction on the Neutron

> Recent data from the PIONS$@$MAX-lab Collaboration, measuring the total cross section of the pion incoherent photoproduction $γd\toπ^-pp$ near threshold, have been used to extract the E$_{0+}$ multipole and total cross section of the reaction $γn\!\to\!π^-p$, also near threshold. These are the first measurements of the reaction $γd\toπ^-pp$ in the threshold region. The value of E$_{0+}$ is extracted through a fit to the deuteron data in a photoproduction model accounting for final-state interactions. The model takes an $S$-wave approximation for the elementary reaction $γn\!\to\!π^-p$ with E$_{0+}\! = $ const in the threshold region. The obtained value E$_{0+} = -31.86\pm 0.8$ (in $10^{-3}/m_{π^+}$ units) is in agreement with other existing results. Model predictions for the total cross section $σ(γn\!\to\!π^-p)$ are also given.

## Key claim

The paper extracts the E$_{0+}$ multipole for the reaction $\gamma n\to\pi^-p$ from new deuteron data, obtaining a value consistent with previous analyses, but the extraction relies on an S-wave approximation with constant E$_{0+}$ in the threshold region, and the model shows systematic disagreement with data above E$_\gamma \sim 156$ MeV, suggesting energy-dependent corrections may be needed.

## Method

The authors fit the total cross section data for $\gamma d\to\pi^-pp$ from the PIONS@MAX-lab Collaboration using a photoproduction model that includes impulse approximation, nucleon-nucleon final-state interactions, pion-nucleon final-state interactions, and a two-loop term. The elementary $\gamma n\to\pi^-p$ amplitude is taken as an S-wave with constant E$_{0+}$ multipole, and the fit extracts E$_{0+}$ as a free parameter from six data points near threshold.

## Key numbers

- E$_{0+} = -31.86 \pm 0.8$ (in $10^{-3}/m_{\pi^+}$ units) from fitting all six MAX-lab data points
- E$_{0+} = -31.75 \pm 0.8$ (same units) when excluding the first data point at E$_\gamma = 147$ MeV
- E$_{0+} = -33.70 \pm 1.2$ (same units) when fitting only the first four data points (excluding points at 157.6 and 159.8 MeV)
- Total cross section $\sigma(\gamma n\to\pi^-p) = 31.9 \pm 9.5$ $\mu$b at E$_\gamma = 149.7 \pm 0.4$ MeV
- Total cross section $\sigma(\gamma n\to\pi^-p) = 100.7 \pm 11.0$ $\mu$b at E$_\gamma = 162.5 \pm 0.4$ MeV

## Context

This work provides the first measurements of $\gamma d\to\pi^-pp$ in the threshold region, extending the sparse experimental database for neutron-target pion photoproduction. The extracted E$_{0+}$ value agrees with the earlier result of Drechsel (1992) and with predictions from the SAID MA19 and MAID2007 partial-wave analyses. The results enable improved determination of the isospin $I=1/2$ amplitudes in $\gamma N\to\pi N$, which require both proton and neutron target data to separate from the $I=3/2$ components that can be determined from proton targets alone.
