# $x$-dependence reconstruction of pion and kaon PDFs from Mellin moments

> We present a calculation of the connected-diagram contributions to the first three non-trivial Mellin moments for the pion and kaon, extracted using local operators with up to 3 covariant derivatives. We use one ensemble of gauge configurations with two degenerate light, a strange and a charm quark ($N_f$=2+1+1) of maximally twisted mass fermions with clover improvement. The ensemble has a pion mass $\sim$260 MeV, and a kaon mass $\sim$530 MeV. We reconstruct the $x$-dependence of the PDFs via fits to our results, and find that our lattice data favor a $(1-x)^2$-behavior in the large-$x$ region for both the pion and kaon PDFs. We integrate the reconstructed PDFs to extract the higher moments, $\langle x^n \rangle$, with $4 \leq n \leq 6$. Finally, we compare the pion and kaon PDFs, as well as the ratios of their Mellin moments, to address the effect of SU(3) flavor symmetry breaking.

## Key claim
The lattice data favor a $(1-x)^2$ behavior in the large-$x$ region for both the pion and kaon PDFs, but this conclusion is based on connected-diagram contributions only, using a single ensemble with a pion mass of approximately 260 MeV and a kaon mass of approximately 530 MeV.

## Method
The authors computed the first three non-trivial Mellin moments (⟨x⟩, ⟨x²⟩, ⟨x³⟩) for pion and kaon PDFs using local operators with up to three covariant derivatives on a single Nf=2+1+1 maximally twisted mass clover-improved fermion ensemble. They performed two-state fits to control excited-state contamination and then reconstructed the x-dependence by fitting the moments to a standard functional form q(x) = N x^α (1-x)^β (1+γ x), using a 2-parameter fit with γ=0 as the final method. The reconstructed PDFs were evolved to 5.2 GeV for comparison with other results.

## Key numbers
- Pion mass: ~260 MeV; kaon mass: ~530 MeV
- Lattice spacing: a = 0.093 fm; volume: 32³ × 64; L = 3.0 fm
- Pion up-quark moment: ⟨x⟩ = 0.261(3); ⟨x²⟩ = 0.110(7); ⟨x³⟩ = 0.024(18) (two-state fit results)
- Kaon up-quark moment: ⟨x⟩ = 0.246(2); ⟨x²⟩ = 0.096(2); ⟨x³⟩ = 0.033(6)
- Kaon strange-quark moment: ⟨x⟩ = 0.317(2); ⟨x²⟩ = 0.139(2); ⟨x³⟩ = 0.073(5)
- Fit parameter β for pion: 2.20(64); for kaon up: 2.59(28); for kaon strange: 2.27(22)
- Distribution peaks: x q_π^u(x=0.30) = 0.42(5); x q_K^u(x=0.27) = 0.43(2); x q_K^s(x=0.35) = 0.52(2)
- Higher moments from reconstruction at 27 GeV²: ⟨x⁴⟩_π^u = 0.023(5)(7); ⟨x⁵⟩_π^u = 0.014(4)(6); ⟨x⁶⟩_π^u = 0.009(3)(4)

## Context
This work extends earlier lattice calculations of pion and kaon Mellin moments by Alexandrou et al. (2020, 2021) to reconstruct the full x-dependence of PDFs, addressing the longstanding challenge that PDF reconstruction from moments was considered unfeasible. The results support the reanalysis of E615 Drell-Yan data by Aicher (2010) suggesting (1-x)² behavior rather than the original (1-x)¹ finding, and agree with DSE calculations by Chen (2016). The reconstructed PDFs enable calculation of higher moments (n=4-6) without the operator mixing problems that plague direct lattice calculations of those moments, and provide a lattice-QCD basis for studying SU(3) flavor symmetry breaking by comparing pion and kaon structure.
