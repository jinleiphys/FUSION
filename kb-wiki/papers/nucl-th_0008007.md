# Improvement of the extended P+QQ interaction by modifying the monopole field

> The extended pairing plus $QQ$ interaction with the $J$-independent isoscalar proton-neutron force as an average monopole field, which has succeeded in describing collective yrast states of $N \approx Z$ even-$A$ nuclei, is improved. The improvement is accomplished by adding small monopole terms (relevant to spectroscopy) to the average monopole field (indispensable to the binding energy). This modification extends the applicability of the interaction to nuclei with $N \approx 28$ such as $^{48}$Ca, and moreover improves energy levels of noncollective states. The modified interaction successfully describes not only even-$A$ but also odd-$A$ nuclei in the $f_{7/2}$ shell region. Results of exact shell model calculations in the model space ($f_{7/2}$, $p_{3/2}$, $p_{1/2}$) (its usefulness has been demonstrated previously) are shown for $A$=47, 48, 49, 50 and 51 nuclei.

## Key claim

The extended P+QQ interaction, which previously succeeded for collective yrast states of N≈Z even-A nuclei, is improved by adding small isovector and isoscalar monopole terms to the average monopole field, thereby extending its applicability to nuclei with N≈28 (such as ⁴⁸Ca) and improving energy levels of noncollective states, though the additional monopole terms are noted to be possibly too strong for A=50 and A=51 nuclei with large isospin.

## Method

Exact shell model calculations were performed in the model space (f₇/₂, p₃/₂, p₁/₂) using the P₀+P₂+QQ+V⁰πν interaction with added monopole corrections. The isovector monopole correction ΔV¹ was parameterized by Δk¹(f₇/₂f₇/₂) = -0.14 MeV and Δk¹(f₇/₂r) = 0.05 MeV, and the isoscalar correction ΔV⁰ by Δk⁰(f₇/₂f₇/₂) = 0.18 MeV and Δk⁰(f₇/₂r) = -0.07 MeV, with the strength of the J-independent isoscalar force reduced to k⁰ = 2.15(42/A) MeV. Parameters were determined by fitting to ⁴⁸Ca and ⁴⁷Ti.

## Key numbers

- Ground-state energy of ⁴⁸Ca: original model gave -3.72 MeV; modified model gave -7.04 MeV (experimental value).
- Single-particle energies in Set B: ε₇/₂ = 0.0 MeV, ε₃/₂ = 2.1 MeV, ε₁/₂ = 3.9 MeV.
- Isovector monopole corrections: Δk¹(f₇/₂f₇/₂) = -0.14 MeV, Δk¹(f₇/₂r) = 0.05 MeV.
- Isoscalar monopole corrections: Δk⁰(f₇/₂f₇/₂) = 0.18 MeV, Δk⁰(f₇/₂r) = -0.07 MeV.
- Reduced strength of V⁰πν: k⁰ = 2.15(42/A) MeV.
- B(E2) values in ⁵⁰Cr for J=2: 197 e²fm⁴ (modified model D) vs. 215 e²fm⁴ (original model A); for J=4: 277 vs. 302 e²fm⁴; for J=6: 122 vs. 211 e²fm⁴; for J=8: 121 vs. 200 e²fm⁴.
- In ⁴⁶V, the lowest 3⁺ state with T=0 moved from 0.24 MeV (model A) to 0.59 MeV (model D), approaching the observed 0.80 MeV.
- Spectroscopic quadrupole moment of ⁴⁷Ti ground state (5/2⁻₁): 23.6 e fm² (present), compared to 30.3 e fm² (experiment) and 22.7 e fm² (Martínez-Pinedo et al.).
- B(E2: 7/2₁ → 5/2₁) in ⁴⁷Ti: 145 e²fm⁴ (present) vs. 252(50) e²fm⁴ (experiment) and 140 e²fm⁴ (Martínez-Pinedo et al.).

## Context

This work extends the extended P+QQ model previously applied to even-A N≈Z nuclei in the f₇/₂ shell region (Hasegawa et al., 1999, referred to as (I)), which succeeded for collective yrast states but failed for noncollective states and nuclei with N≈28. The modification addresses flaws analogous to those found when the Kuo-Brown (KB) interaction was applied to many-valence-nucleon systems like ⁴⁸Ca, which were cured by modifying monopole components (Pasquini, 1977; Poves, 1981) leading to the KB3 interaction (Caurier, 1995; Poves, 1995). The results are compared with full fp shell model calculations using the KB3 interaction by Martínez-Pinedo et al. (1996, 1997) for A=47 and 49 nuclei. The modified interaction enables description of both even-A and odd-A nuclei in the f₇/₂ shell region, though the authors note that fine improvement remains possible and the parameters may need A-dependence.
