# Renormalization group improvement of the spectrum of Hydrogen-like atoms with massless fermions

> We obtain the next-to-next-to-leading-log renormalization group improvement of the spectrum of Hydrogen-like atoms with massless fermions by using potential NRQED. These results can also be applied to the computation of the muonic Hydrogen spectrum where we are able to reproduce some known double logs at O(mα^6). We compare with other formalisms dealing with log resummation available in the literature.

## Key claim

The paper obtains the next-to-next-to-leading-log (NNLL) renormalization group improvement of the spectrum of Hydrogen-like atoms with massless fermions using potential NRQED (pNRQED), and shows that this result agrees with known double-logarithmic corrections at O(mα⁶) for muonic Hydrogen, while a comparison with the vNRQED formalism reveals a disagreement that is later resolved by a correction to the vNRQCD computation.

## Method

The author constructs the pNRQED Lagrangian including light (massless) fermions, derives the renormalization group (RG) equations for the matching coefficients at one loop up to O(1/m²), and solves them to obtain the scale-dependent potentials. The RG-improved potentials are then inserted into the Schrödinger equation, and the energy spectrum is obtained by setting the ultrasoft scale ν_us ∼ mα² to minimize large logarithms, yielding the NNLL correction to the NNLO energy expression.

## Key numbers

- The NNLL correction to the energy is given by δE_{n,l,j}^{pot}(ν_us) = E_n α² (Z²δ_{l0}/(3n)) [ - (16/β₀) log(α(ν_us)/α) - 3(c_D - 1) ], where E_n = -mZ²α²/(2n²).
- The running of the coefficient c_D(ν_s) is c_D(ν_s) = 1 + (16/3) ln z, with z = [α(ν_s)/α(m)]^(1/β₀) and β₀ = - (4/3) T_F n_f (T_F = 1).
- The RG equation for the 1/m² potential coefficient D_d^(2) is ν_{us} d/dν_{us} D_d^(2) = - (4/3) (α(ν_{us})/π) V_A² c_k² α(r⁻¹).
- The expanded result for D_d^(2) at O(α³) yields terms proportional to (α²/π) ln(mv²/m) and (α³/π²) ln²(mv²/m), matching the structure of Pachucki's O(mα⁶ ln²) corrections for muonic Hydrogen.

## Context

This work extends the pNRQCD formalism of Pineda and Soto (2001) for heavy quarkonium to the simpler QED case of Hydrogen-like atoms with massless fermions, enabling a direct comparison with the vNRQED approach of Hoang, Manohar, and Stewart (2001). The results reproduce the known double-logarithmic O(mα⁶ ln²) corrections for muonic Hydrogen computed by Pachucki (1999), and the disagreement with vNRQED is later resolved by a correction reported in Hoang (2002). This formalism can be applied to muonic atoms and similar systems where light fermions contribute to logarithmic enhancements.
