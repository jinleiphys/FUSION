# Heavy and Strange Holographic Baryons

> We extend the $D4$-$D8$ holographic construction to include three chiral and one heavy flavor, to describe heavy-light baryons with strangeness and their exotics. At strong coupling, the heavy meson always binds to the bulk instanton in the form of a flavor zero mode in the fundamental representation. We quantize the ensuing bound states using the collective quantization method, to obtain the spectra of heavy and strange baryons with both explicit and hidden charm and bottom. Our results confirm the existence of two low-lying charmed penta-quark states with $\frac 12^-,\frac 32^-$ assignments, and predict many new ones with both charm and bottom. They also suggest a quartet of low-lying neutral $Ω_c^0$ with assignments $\frac 12^\pm ,\frac 32^\pm$ that are heavier than the quintuplet of neutral $Ω_c^0$ recently reported by LHCb.

## Key claim

The paper extends the D4-D8 holographic construction to three light flavors plus one heavy flavor, claiming that heavy mesons bind to a bulk instanton as a flavor zero mode in the fundamental representation, and that the resulting spectra confirm two low-lying charmed pentaquark states with 1/2- and 3/2- assignments while predicting a quartet of neutral Omega_c^0 states that are heavier than the quintuplet recently reported by LHCb.

## Method

The authors embed N_f-1 light D8-branes and one heavy D8-brane in the Sakai-Sugimoto holographic setup, then identify baryons with small-size SU(3) flavor instantons in the bulk. They introduce heavy-light meson fields Phi_M that couple to the instanton background, derive the zero-mode equation in the double limit of large 't Hooft coupling lambda followed by large heavy meson mass m_H, and quantize the bound system using collective quantization of the instanton moduli and the heavy spinor zero-mode chi.

## Key numbers

- The mass splitting between the SU(3) sextet and antitriplet single-heavy baryons is 0.334 M_KK, where M_KK is the Kaluza-Klein mass scale (approximately 1 GeV from rho meson fits).
- The mass of the antitriplet single-heavy baryons relative to the nucleon (p=q=1, N_Q=0, j=1/2) is M_antitriplet - M_nucleon - m_H = -0.570 M_KK.
- The mass of the sextet single-heavy baryons relative to the nucleon is M_sextet - M_nucleon - m_H = -0.236 M_KK.
- For double-heavy baryons with QQ content (N_Q=2), the mass relative to the nucleon is M_3-plet - M_nucleon - 2m_H = -0.844 M_KK.
- The parameters eta and mu in the heavy-light effective action are eta = (2*sqrt(6)+1)/3 + 1 approximately 2.966 and mu = 13/12 approximately 1.083.

## Context

This work extends the holographic bound-state approach previously developed for two light flavors (Liu and Zahed, 2016) to three light flavors with strangeness, incorporating the Chern-Simons term corrections from the three-flavor case (CSTHREE). It builds on the Sakai-Sugimoto D4-D8 construction (Sakai and Sugimoto, 2005) and the identification of baryons as bulk instantons (SSXB, SSXBB). The results enable predictions for heavy baryon spectra with charm and bottom, including pentaquark states and Omega_c^0 excitations, extending the Skyrme model bound-state approach (PENTARHO, SKYRMEHEAVY) to holography.
