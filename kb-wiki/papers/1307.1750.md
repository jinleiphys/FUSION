# Comparative study of the production of scalar and tensor mesons in $e^+e^-$ collisions

> The intensity of scalar $a_0(980)$, $f_0(980)$ and tensor $a_2(1320)$, $f_2(1270)$ mesons production at VEPP-2000 (BINP, Novosibirsk) and the upgraded DA$Φ$NE (Frascati, Italy) in the processes $e^+e^- \to a_0 (f_0$, $a_2$, $f_2) γ$ is calculated. The calculation was made with the help of VDM and the kaon loop model (for scalar mesons case). Note that the processes $e^+e^-\to a_2(f_2)γ$ have not been studied in the energy region of VEPP-2000. It turned out that in the VEPP-2000 energy region $1.7-2.0$ GeV $σ(e^+e^-\to a_2(f_2)γ)\sim 10$ pb, and $σ(e^+e^-\to a_0(f_0)γ)\sim 0.1$ pb. Photon angle distribution and the spin density matrices of $a_2$ and $f_2$ were calculated also.

## Key claim

The cross sections for scalar meson production (a0(980), f0(980)) in e+e- collisions at VEPP-2000 energies (1.7-2.0 GeV) are predicted to be about 0.1 pb, which is two orders of magnitude smaller than the tensor meson cross sections (a2(1320), f2(1270)) of about 10 pb; this large difference, if confirmed experimentally, would support the four-quark nature of the light scalar mesons over the conventional qqbar interpretation.

## Method

The authors calculated cross sections for e+e- → Sγ (S = a0, f0) using the vector dominance model (VDM) combined with the kaon loop model, where the process proceeds through intermediate vector mesons (ρ, ρ', ρ'', ω, ω', ω'', φ, φ', φ'') decaying to K+K- pairs that then form the scalar meson plus a photon. For tensor mesons (a2, f2), they used VDM with an effective Lagrangian describing the T → VV coupling, assuming the dominant contribution comes from ρ, ρ', ρ'' intermediate states for f2 and from ρ, ω, ρ', ω', ρ'', ω'' for a2, with the tensor meson subsequently decaying to π0π0 or ηπ0 final states. Photon angular distributions and spin density matrices for the tensor mesons were also computed.

## Key numbers

- σ(e+e- → a2(f2)γ) ∼ 10 pb in the VEPP-2000 energy region 1.7-2.0 GeV
- σ(e+e- → a0(f0)γ) ∼ 0.1 pb in the VEPP-2000 energy region 1.7-2.0 GeV
- Γ(f2 → γγ) = 3.03 ± 0.35 keV (from PDG 2012)
- Γ(a2 → γγ) = 1.00 ± 0.06 keV (from PDG 2012)
- m_a2 = 1322 MeV, Γ_a2^tot = 116 MeV, r_a2 = 1.9 GeV^{-1} (from Ann. Sgn. 2011)
- m_f2 = 1272 MeV, Γ_f2^tot = 196 MeV, B(f2 → ππ) = 0.848, r_f2 = 8.2 GeV^{-1} (from Ann. Sgn. 2011)

## Context

This work extends the kaon loop model previously applied to V → K+K- → a0(f0)γ decays (Achasov 1989, 1997) to the e+e- collider environment at VEPP-2000 and upgraded DAΦNE, and provides the first calculations of e+e- → a2(f2)γ in this energy region. The predicted factor-of-100 suppression of scalar versus tensor production would discriminate between the four-quark interpretation of light scalars (Jaffe 1977, Z. Phys. 1982) and the conventional qqbar picture; observation of tensor signals and non-observation of scalar signals would support the four-quark nature. The results enable experimental searches at VEPP-2000 after reaching design luminosity and at DAΦNE after its planned full upgrade.
