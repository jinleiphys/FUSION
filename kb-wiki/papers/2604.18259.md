# Questioning MAMI's recent determination of $B_Λ({_Λ^3}{\rm H})$

> A recent report on ${^7{\rm Li}}(e,e'K^+)$ electroproduction runs by the A1 collaboration at the Mainz Microtron (MAMI) assigns a sharp pion-momentum line at $p_{π^-}\approx 113.8\pm 0.1$ MeV/c to ${_Λ^3}{\rm H}\toπ^-+{^3{\rm He}}$ weak decay, resulting in exceptionally large ${_Λ^3}{\rm H}$ binding-energy $B_Λ({_Λ^3}{\rm H})=0.523\pm 0.013\pm 0.075$ MeV. Here I suggest an alternative interpretation of the observed sharp line in terms of ${_Λ^7}{\rm He}_{\rm g.s.}\toπ^-+{^7{\rm Li}}(E_{\rm x}=478$ keV) weak decay, discussing also the model dependence of $B_Λ({_Λ^7}{\rm He})$.

## Key claim

The sharp pion-momentum line at \(p_{\pi^-}\approx 113.8\pm 0.1\) MeV/c recently reported by the MAMI A1 collaboration as evidence for hypertriton (\({_\Lambda^3}{\rm H}\)) weak decay is instead argued to arise from \({_\Lambda^7}{\rm He}_{\rm g.s.}\to\pi^-+{^7}{\rm Li}(E_{\rm x}=478\ {\rm keV})\) weak decay, provided the binding energy of \({_\Lambda^7}{\rm He}\) is \(5.84\pm 0.07\) MeV rather than the value assumed by MAMI.

## Method

The author examines the \({^7{\rm Li}}(e,e'K^+)\) electroproduction kinematics and the expected weak-decay pion momenta from all possible \({_\Lambda^7}{\rm He}\to\pi^-+{^7}{\rm Li}\) decays, considering both ground-state and excited levels of the initial hypernucleus and final nucleus. The observed sharp line is compared to calculated \(p_{\pi^-}\) values using two different experimental inputs for \(B_\Lambda({_\Lambda^7}{\rm He})\) from JLab experiments, and the consistency of the resulting binding energy with isospin-triplet relations among A=7 hypernuclei is evaluated.

## Key numbers

- MAMI's sharp pion-momentum line: \(p_{\pi^-}=113.789\pm 0.020({\rm stat.})\pm 0.112({\rm syst.})\) MeV/c, assigned by MAMI to \({_\Lambda^3}{\rm H}\to\pi^-+{^3}{\rm He}\) decay.
- MAMI's resulting hypertriton binding energy: \(B_\Lambda({_\Lambda^3}{\rm H})=0.523\pm 0.013\pm 0.075\) MeV, more than \(4\sigma\) above the pre-MAMI weighted average \(\bar{B}_\Lambda({_\Lambda^3}{\rm H})=0.108\pm 0.027\) MeV.
- The alternative interpretation as \({_\Lambda^7}{\rm He}_{\rm g.s.}\to\pi^-+{^7}{\rm Li}(E_{\rm x}=0.478\ {\rm MeV})\) decay yields \(B_\Lambda({_\Lambda^7}{\rm He})=5.84\pm 0.07\) MeV.
- JLab's most recent \(B_\Lambda({_\Lambda^7}{\rm He})\) value: \(5.55\pm 0.15\) MeV (Gogami 2016); earlier JLab value: \(5.68\pm 0.25\) MeV (Nakamura 2013).
- Isospin analysis using emulsion \(B_\Lambda({_\Lambda^7}{\rm Li})=5.58\pm 0.05\) MeV gives \(B_\Lambda({_\Lambda^7}{\rm He})=5.38\pm 0.10\) MeV; using KEK-SKS value \(B_\Lambda({_\Lambda^7}{\rm Li})=5.82\pm 0.11\) MeV gives \(B_\Lambda({_\Lambda^7}{\rm He})=5.86\pm 0.14\) MeV.
- Predicted \({_\Lambda^7}{\rm He}_{\rm g.s.}\to\pi^-+{^7}{\rm Li}_{\rm g.s.}\) decay line: \(p_{\pi^-}\approx 114.5\pm 0.1\) MeV/c, with about twice the strength of the 113.8 MeV/c line.

## Context

This work challenges the assignment by the MAMI A1 collaboration (MAMI 2026) of a sharp pion line to hypertriton decay, which would imply a binding energy in severe conflict with the pre-existing weighted average from KPH (2026). The alternative interpretation builds on known \({_\Lambda^7}{\rm He}\) levels established in JLab electroproduction experiments (Gogami 2016; Nakamura 2013) and uses isospin relations among A=7 hypernuclei, including emulsion data (Davis 2005) and KEK-SKS results (GHM 2016; BBF 2017). If confirmed, future \({^7{\rm Li}}(e,e'K^+)\) runs should observe a stronger companion line at \(p_{\pi^-}\approx 114.5\) MeV/c from ground-state-to-ground-state decay, enabling a definitive resolution.
