# Systematic study on production of hidden-bottom pentaquark via $Оіp$ and $ПҖ^{-}p$ scatterings

> The production of hidden-bottom pentaquark $P_{b}$ states via $Оіp$ and $ПҖ^{-}p$ scatterings is studied within an effective Lagrangian approach and the vector-meson-dominance mechnism. For the $P_{b}$ production in the process $Оіp\rightarrow ОҘp$, the dipole Pomeron model is employed to calculate the background contribution, and the experimental data can be well described. For the process $ПҖ^{-}p\rightarrow ОҘn$, the Reggeized $t$-channel with $ПҖ$ exchange is considered as the main background for the $P_{b}$ production. Near the threshold, two-peak structure from the states $P_{b (11080)$ and $P_{b}(11125)$ can be observed if energy bin width is chosen as 0.01 GeV, and the same result is obtained in the $ПҖ^{-}p$ scattering. Moreover, by taking the branching ratio of Br$[{P_{b}\rightarrow ПҖN}]\simeq 0.05\%$, the numerical result shows that the average value of cross section from the $P_{b}(11080)$ state produced in the $Оіp$ or $ПҖ^{-}p$ scattering reaches at least 0.1 nb with a bin of 0.1 GeV. Even if we reduce the branching ratio of the $P_{b}$ state into $ПҖN$ channel by one order, the theoretical average of the cross section from $P_{b}(11080)$ production in $ПҖ^{-}p$ scattering can reach the order of 0.01 nb with a bin of 0.1 GeV, which means that the signal can still be clearly distinguished from the background. The experimental measurements and studies on the hidden-bottom pentaquark $P_{b}$ state production in the $Оіp $ or $ПҖ^{-}p$ scattering near-threshold energy region around $W\simeq 11$ GeV are strongly suggested, which are accessible at COMPASS and JPARC. Particularly, the result of the photoproduction suggests that it is very promising to observe the hidden-bottom pentaquark at proposed EicC facility in China.

## Method
The authors employed an effective Lagrangian approach combined with the vector-meson-dominance (VMD) mechanism to model the production of P_b states through s-channel exchange. For the Оіp вҶ’ ОҘp reaction, the dipole Pomeron model was used to calculate the background contribution, with parameters tuned to reproduce existing experimental data. For the ПҖвҒ»p вҶ’ ОҘn reaction, the background was described by Reggeized t-channel pion exchange, using the Regge propagator with trajectory Оұ_ПҖ(t) = 0.7(t вҲ’ m_ПҖВІ). The coupling constants for the P_b states were derived from predicted decay widths from Gutsche (2019) and the assumed branching ratio Br[P_b вҶ’ ПҖN] вүҲ 0.05%.

## Context
This work extends the study of hidden-charm pentaquark production (P_c states observed by LHCb in 2019) to the bottom sector, using the heavy quark symmetry that relates ОЈ_b(*)B interactions to ОЈ_c(*)D interactions. The authors build on the molecular state predictions of Gutsche (2019) for hidden-bottom pentaquark masses and decay widths, and on their own previous work on P_c photoproduction (Wang 2019) and pion-induced production (Wang 2019). The study enables experimental searches at existing facilities (COMPASS, J-PARC) and the proposed EicC in China, providing specific cross section predictions and optimal energy windows (W вүҲ 11 GeV) for observing these states.
