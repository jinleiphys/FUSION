## Context

This work extends the analysis of Hall et al. (2013) on ОіZ box corrections to parity-violating elastic scattering, which used the Adelaide-Jefferson Lab-Manitoba (AJM) parametrization with a higher QВІ boundary (2.5 GeVВІ) for the DIS region. The new constraint comes from the first direct observation of quark-hadron duality in parity-violating deep-inelastic scattering from the deuteron by the E08-011 experiment at Jefferson Lab (Wang et al., 2013, 2014), and from the demonstration of approximate isospin independence of duality in electromagnetic structure functions (Niculescu et al., 2015). The result enables more precise extraction of the proton's weak charge from the Q_weak experiment and provides predictions for future experiments such as MOLLER at Jefferson Lab and MESA at Mainz, with the uncertainty well within the 4% accuracy goal of Q_weak.
