# $D^{*}D ρ$ vertex from QCD sum rules

> We calculate the form factors and the coupling constant in the $D^{*}D ρ$ vertex in the framework of QCD sum rules. We evaluate the three point correlation functions of the vertex considering both $ D $ and $ ρ$ mesons off--shell. The form factors obtained are very different but give the same coupling constant: $g_{D^{*}D ρ} = 4.1 \pm 0.1$ GeV$^{-1}$.

## Key claim

The form factors for the $D^{*}D\rho$ vertex, computed with one meson off-shell, are very different in shape (monopole versus exponential), but when extrapolated to the respective meson mass poles they yield the same coupling constant \(g_{D^{*}D\rho} = 4.1 \pm 0.1\) GeV\(^{-1}\) after averaging and accounting for isospin factors.

## Method

The authors evaluated the three-point correlation functions of the \(D^{*}D\rho\) vertex using QCD sum rules, considering both the \(D\) meson and the \(\rho\) meson off-shell. The OPE side included the perturbative diagram and the quark condensate contribution, while the phenomenological side was built from hadronic parameters and an effective Lagrangian. After double Borel transformation, the sum rules were matched to extract the \(Q^2\)-dependent form factors, which were then extrapolated to the meson mass poles to obtain the coupling constant.

## Key numbers

- Coupling constant from the \(D\) off-shell form factor: \(g_{D^{*}D\rho}^{(D)} = 5.70 \pm 0.06\) GeV\(^{-1}\) (extrapolated to \(Q^2 = -m_D^2\)).
- Coupling constant from the \(\rho\) off-shell form factor: \(g_{D^{*}D\rho}^{(\rho)} = 5.90 \pm 0.08\) GeV\(^{-1}\) (extrapolated to \(Q^2 = -m_\rho^2\)).
- Final isospin-averaged coupling constant: \(g_{D^{*}D\rho} = 4.13 \pm 0.08\) GeV\(^{-1}\) (after dividing by \(\sqrt{2}\)).
- Monopole parametrization for the \(D\) off-shell form factor: \(g_{D^{*}D\rho}^{(D)}(Q^2) = 234.38 / (Q^2 + 44.14)\) (units of GeV\(^{-1}\)).
- Exponential parametrization for the \(\rho\) off-shell form factor: \(g_{D^{*}D\rho}^{(\rho)}(Q^2) = 5.12 \, e^{-Q^2/4.33}\) (units of GeV\(^{-1}\)).
- Borel mass chosen for the \(D\) off-shell case: \(M^2 = 30\) GeV\(^2\); for the \(\rho\) off-shell case: \(M^2 = 3\) GeV\(^2\).

## Context

This work extends a series of QCD sum rule calculations of vertices involving charmed mesons, such as \(D^{*}D\pi\) (Nielsen et al., 2000; 2002), \(DD\rho\) (Bracco et al., 2001), and \(D^{*}D^{*}\rho\) (Bracco et al., 2008). The obtained coupling constant is compatible with results from Light Cone Sum Rules (Wang, 2006; Dai et al., 2002) but somewhat larger than estimates from Vector Dominance Model (Su et al., 2005) and SU(4) symmetry relations. The form factors can be used to compute final state interactions in \(B\) decays and decays of charmonium-like states such as \(X(3872) \rightarrow J/\psi + \rho\).
