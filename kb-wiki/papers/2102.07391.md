# Strong decays of the low-lying bottom strange baryons

> In this work, the strong decay behaviors of the $λ-$mode low-lying $Ξ_{b}$ and $Ξ_{b}^\prime$ baryons are investigated within the $^3P_0$ model. Our results suggest that all of the low-lying $\bar 3_F$ states $Ξ_b(2S)$, $Ξ_b(1P)$, and $Ξ_b(1D)$ have small decay widths of less than 20 MeV, and these states have good potentials to be observed in the $Ξ_b^\prime π$ and $Ξ_b^{\prime *} π$ invariant masses. Further, most of the $6_F$ multiplets are relatively narrow and may decay into the $Ξ_b π$, $Ξ_b^\prime π$, $Ξ_b^{\prime *} π$, and $Λ_b \bar K$ final states. Considering the masses and strong decay behaviors, we can assign the newly observed $Ξ_{b}(6100)$ resonance as the $Ξ_b(1P)$ state with $J^P=3/2^-$ and interpret the $Ξ_{b}(6227)$ structure as the $J^P=3/2^-$ $Ξ_b^\prime(1P)$ state with a proper mixing angle. We expect that our predictions for these excited bottom strange baryons will provide helpful information for future experimental research.

# Strong decays of the low-lying bottom strange baryons

## Key claim
The newly observed Ξ_b(6100) resonance can be assigned as the Ξ_b(1P) state with J^P = 3/2^- and the Ξ_b(6227) structure can be interpreted as the J^P = 3/2^- Ξ_b'(1P) state with a mixing angle in the range 12° to 29° (or -12° to -29°), but the authors caution that the ^3P_0 model cannot give precise results due to relatively small phase space and that predictions contain uncertainties of about 30%.

## Method
The strong decay behaviors of λ-mode low-lying Ξ_b and Ξ_b' baryons were calculated using the ^3P_0 model, where a quark-antiquark pair with quantum numbers J^PC = 0^{++} is created from the vacuum and the initial baryon rearranges into two final hadrons. The masses of the initial states were taken from the relativistic quark model predictions of Ebert (2011), while final ground state masses were taken from the Review of Particle Physics. The same model parameters previously used successfully for Λ_c, Σ_c, Ξ_c, Ξ_c', Λ_b, Σ_b, and Ω_b systems were adopted.

## Key numbers
- All low-lying Ξ_b(2S), Ξ_b(1P), and Ξ_b(1D) states have total decay widths less than 20 MeV.
- The Ξ_b(2S) state (predicted mass 6266 MeV) has a total decay width of 6.99 MeV, with branching ratios Br(Ξ_b' π, Ξ_b'* π) = 37.9%, 62.1%.
- The Ξ_b(6100) assigned as the J^P = 3/2^- Ξ_b(1P) state has a total decay width of 6.76 MeV, dominated by the Ξ_b'* π mode.
- The pure Ξ_b0'(1/2^-) state has a total decay width of 370.75 MeV, while the pure Ξ_b2'(3/2^-) and Ξ_b2'(5/2^-) states have widths of 6.93 MeV and 7.12 MeV respectively.
- For the Ξ_b(6227) interpreted as a mixed J^P = 3/2^- Ξ_b'(1P) state with mixing angle θ = 22°, the branching ratios are Br(Ξ_b π, Ξ_b' π, Ξ_b'* π, Λ_b K̅) = 21.8%, 2.3%, 65.1%, 10.8%.
- The Ξ_b'(2S) and Ξ_b'*(2S) states have total decay widths of 33.89 MeV and 35.64 MeV respectively.
- The six Ξ_b'(1D) states have total widths ranging from 2.23 MeV (J^P = 7/2^+) to 39.82 MeV (J^P = 3/2^+).

## Context
This work extends the systematic ^3P_0 model studies of singly heavy baryons previously applied to Λ_c, Σ_c, Ξ_c, Ξ_c', Λ_b, Σ_b, and Ω_b systems by Liang (2019, 2020) and Lu (2018, 2019, 2020). It addresses the fact that unlike the Λ_b and Ω_b systems, the spectra of bottom strange baryons are far from established, with only two excited structures (Ξ_b(6227) and Ξ_b(6100)) observed experimentally. The results provide assignments for these two observed resonances and predict narrow widths and specific decay channels for most low-lying Ξ_b and Ξ_b' states, enabling future experimental searches in the Ξ_b' π, Ξ_b'* π, Ξ_b π, and Λ_b K̅ final states. The authors note that the mixing angle obtained for Ξ_b(6227) is close to that found for the non-strange partner Σ_b(6097) in their previous work (Liang 2019).
