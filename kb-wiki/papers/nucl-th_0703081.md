# The one-particle momentum transfer in point-form spectator approximation

> The one-particle momentum transfer operator is derived in point-form spectator approximation for $NN$ system. General expression is applied to elastic electron-deuteron scattering and to deuteron photodisintegration.

## Key claim

The one-particle momentum transfer operator for the NN system is derived in point-form spectator approximation, generalizing the earlier result for elastic electron-deuteron scattering to arbitrary NN processes; the operator depends on internal variables and the relative "position" of initial and final center-of-momentum frames, and reduces to a number only when acting on plane-wave states with a fixed parameter **h**.

## Method

The author constructs the operator \(Q^2_i\) for the momentum transfer to the i-th particle in point-form dynamics by introducing a special Lorentz frame (Lev frame) where the sum of initial and final system 4-velocities has zero spatial component. Using hyperbolic transformations between the initial center-of-momentum frame, the Lev frame, and the final center-of-momentum frame, the squared momentum transfer to the struck particle is expressed in terms of internal momentum variables and the parameter \(h\) characterizing the relative boost. The general expression is then specialized to elastic electron-deuteron scattering (where the Lev frame coincides with the Breit frame) and to deuteron photodisintegration with a real photon.

## Key numbers

* For elastic electron-deuteron scattering, \(\sinh^2 \Delta/2 = Q^2 / (4 m_D^2)\), where \(m_D\) is the deuteron mass and \(Q^2\) is the squared four-momentum transfer.
* For deuteron photodisintegration with a real photon of energy \(E_\gamma\) in the laboratory system (which coincides with the initial center-of-momentum frame), \(\sinh^2 \Delta = E_\gamma^2 / [(m_D + E_\gamma)^2 - E_\gamma^2]\).
* The momentum transfer operator acting on a plane-wave final state in deuteron photodisintegration yields \(Q_1^2 = E_\gamma^2 - (w_n - w_p)^2 = (2w_n - m_D)(2w_p - m_D)\), where \(w_n\) and \(w_p\) are the final neutron and proton energies in the initial center-of-momentum frame.

## Context

This work extends the point-form spectator approximation treatment of elastic electron-deuteron scattering by Allen et al. (2000) to a general one-particle momentum transfer operator applicable to any NN system. The derivation follows the approach of Lev (1993) for constructing electromagnetic current operators in relativistic composite systems. The result enables consistent treatment of momentum transfer in deuteron photodisintegration and, by extension, in NN bremsstrahlung and electrodisintegration processes within the point-form framework.
