# Meson-baryon scattering lengths without annihilation diagrams to order $p^4$ in heavy baryon chiral perturbation theory

> We calculate the threshold $T$ matrices of the meson and baryon processes that have no annihilation diagrams: $π^{+}Σ^{+}$, $π^{+}Ξ^0$, $K^+p$, $K^+n$, and $\bar{K}^0Ξ^0$ to the fourth order in heavy baryon chiral perturbation theory. By performing least squares and Bayesian fits to the non-physical lattice QCD data, we determine the low-energy constants through both perturbative and non-perturbative iterative methods. By using these low-energy constants, we obtain the physical scattering lengths in these fits. The convergence behavior is not good across all channels in the perturbative method. The scattering lengths for the five channels, obtained by taking the median values from four different fitting approaches, are $a_{π^+Σ^+}=-0.16\pm 0.07\,\text{fm}$, $a_{π^+Ξ^0}=-0.04\pm0.04\,\text{fm}$, $a_{K^+p}=-0.41\pm 0.11\,\text{fm}$, $a_{K^+n}=-0.19\pm 0.10\,\text{fm}$, and $a_{\bar{K}^0Ξ^0}=-0.30\pm 0.07\,\text{fm}$, where the uncertainties are conservatively estimated by taking the maximum deviation between the median and extreme values of the statistical errors.

## Key claim

The paper calculates meson-baryon scattering lengths for five channels without annihilation diagrams to fourth order in heavy baryon chiral perturbation theory, but the convergence behavior is not good across all channels in the perturbative method, and the final results are obtained by taking median values from four different fitting approaches with conservatively estimated uncertainties.

## Method

The authors compute threshold T-matrices for π⁺Σ⁺, π⁺Ξ⁰, K⁺p, K⁺n, and K̄⁰Ξ⁰ to fourth order (p⁴) in heavy baryon chiral perturbation theory (HBChPT). They determine low-energy constants (LECs) by performing least-squares fits and Bayesian fits to non-physical lattice QCD data from Torok (2009), using both perturbative and non-perturbative iterative methods, then extrapolate to physical scattering lengths.

## Key numbers

- Scattering length for π⁺Σ⁺: a = -0.16 ± 0.07 fm (median from four fitting approaches)
- Scattering length for π⁺Ξ⁰: a = -0.04 ± 0.04 fm (median from four fitting approaches)
- Scattering length for K⁺p: a = -0.41 ± 0.11 fm (median from four fitting approaches)
- Scattering length for K⁺n: a = -0.19 ± 0.10 fm (median from four fitting approaches)
- Scattering length for K̄⁰Ξ⁰: a = -0.30 ± 0.07 fm (median from four fitting approaches)
- χ²/d.o.f. = 0.4 for the perturbative least-squares fit (Fit LSp) with 20 data points and 11 parameters
- χ²/d.o.f. = 0.2 for the non-perturbative iterative least-squares fit (Fit LSu) with 20 data points and 12 parameters

## Context

This work extends previous third-order (p³) SU(3) HBChPT calculations by Liu (2006, 2007) and Huang (2015, 2017, 2019), which suffered from unnaturally large LECs and poor convergence for kaon-baryon channels as noted by Torok (2009). The fourth-order calculation with iterative resummation follows the approach used by Huang (2021) for light meson-heavy meson scattering lengths. The results enable improved predictions for meson-baryon scattering lengths at the physical point and call for more precise lattice QCD data to resolve remaining discrepancies, particularly for the K̄⁰Ξ⁰ channel.
