## Method
The authors use an algebraic approach based on the trace invariance of the oscillation probability under unitary transformations of the mixing matrix. They express the Maiani parameterization of the three-neutrino mixing matrix in terms of SO(3) rotation generators and a CP-phase-dependent transformation, then derive equivalence relations among parameter sets that produce identical oscillation probabilities for all flavor transitions and all L/E values.

## Context
This work extends the analysis of bounds on neutrino mixing angles previously considered by Gluza (2002) for propagation through matter, confirming their results for vacuum propagation while providing an additional alternative conclusion via symmetry relations. The algebraic approach used here was developed by the same authors (Latimer and Ernst, 2003) for vacuum neutrino oscillations. The results enable phenomenologists to correctly explore the full parameter space when analyzing neutrino oscillation data, particularly when restricting the CP phase to the commonly used interval [0,ŌÄ).
