# Covariant tetraquark equations in quantum field theory

> We derive general covariant coupled equations of QCD describing the tetraquark in terms of a mix of four-quark states $2q2\bar q$, and two-quark states $q\bar q$. The coupling of $2q2\bar q$ to $q\bar q$ states is achieved by a simple contraction of a four-quark $q\bar q$-irreducible Green function down to a two-quark $q\bar q$ Bethe-Salpeter kernel. The resulting tetraquark equations are expressed in an exact field theoretic form, and are in agreement with those obtained previously by consideration of disconnected interactions; however, despite being more general, they have been derived here in a much simpler and more transparent way.

## Key claim

The paper derives general covariant coupled equations for the tetraquark that couple four-quark (2q2q̅) states to two-quark (qq̅) states, achieving agreement with previous derivations that relied on disconnected interactions, but through a simpler and more transparent method that avoids introducing explicit disconnected contributions to two-body interactions.

## Method

The authors first formulate four-body tetraquark equations for the qq̅-irreducible part of the 2q2q̅ Green function, G⁽⁴⁾_ir, using Faddeev-like equations from Khvedelidze and Kvinikhidze (1991). They then generate the qq̅ kernel K⁽²⁾ through a contraction of four-body to two-body states, expressing the 4q-reducible part of the two-body Bethe-Salpeter kernel as K⁽²⁾_4q-red = A⁽²⁻⁴⁾ G⁽⁴⁾_ir A⁽⁴⁻²⁾. The tetraquark equations are derived under the approximation where two-body t-matrices are expressed in the bound state pole approximation and only meson-meson (MM) and diquark-antidiquark (DD̅) components are retained.

## Key numbers

* The tetraquark bound state pole occurs at P² = M², where M is the tetraquark mass, with G⁽⁴⁾ → i ΨΨ̅/(P²-M²) and G⁽²⁾ → i G₀⁽²⁾Γ*Γ̅*G₀⁽²⁾/(P²-M²) as P² approaches M².
* The two-body t-matrix in the bound state pole approximation is T⁽²⁾_a = i Γ_a D_a Γ̅_a, where D_a(P_a) = 1/(P_a² - m_a²) is the propagator for the bound particle (diquark, antidiquark, or meson) with mass m_a.
* The coupled channel kernel matrix V for MM-DD̅ space is V = [[-(1+P)V₁₂, 2V₁D], [2V_D₁, 0]], where P is the permutation operator of meson state labels.

## Context

This work extends the covariant few-body approach to tetraquarks previously formulated by Heupel et al. (2012) and Kvinikhidze and Blankleider (2014), and addresses discrepancies with the formulation of Santowsky and Eichmann (2020). The derivation resolves issues of overcounting that first arose in the analogous πNN system (Kvinikhidze and Blankleider, 1993), and provides a foundation for describing tetraquarks in terms of simultaneous poles in the full 2q2q̅ and qq̅ Green functions.
