# Breit - Wigner parameters of nucleon resonance S11(1535)

> The result of partial - wave analysis of angular distributions for the process gamma+p -> eta +p at the energies upto 2 GeV are given. From the energy dependence of the regression coefficient a0(W) the reliable estimates of Breit - Wigner parameters of S11(1535) - resonance and energy dependence of real and imagenery parts of electric dipol amplitude E0+ and its phase were obtained

## Key claim

The paper provides reliable estimates of the Breit-Wigner parameters of the S11(1535) resonance from photoproduction data, but the extracted total width and other parameters vary significantly depending on which experimental dataset is used, with some datasets yielding widths consistent with hadroproduction values and others not.

## Method

The authors performed a partial-wave analysis of angular distributions for the reaction gamma + p -> eta + p up to 2 GeV, expanding differential cross sections in Legendre polynomials and keeping only the first three terms (a0, a1, a2) based on statistical justification. The s-wave dominance allowed them to fit the energy dependence of the regression coefficient a0(W) (equal to |E0+|^2) using a Breit-Wigner parametric model with three fitted parameters: the peak cross section sigma(W_R), the resonance mass W_R, and the total width Gamma_R, with fixed branching ratios for eta, pi, and pipi decay channels.

## Key numbers

- From the Krusche (krus1) dataset fitted to 4pi a0(W) with b_eta=0.55, b_pi=0.35: W_R = 1558.73 +/- 4.79 MeV, Gamma_R = 244.09 +/- 20.32 MeV, sigma(W_R) = 34.63 +/- 0.82 microbarn, A_{1/2} = 122.96 +/- 3.51 x 10^{-3} GeV^{-1/2}, chi^2/nu = 0.52
- From the GRAAL (graal1) dataset fitted to 4pi a0(W) with b_eta=0.55, b_pi=0.35: W_R = 1537.33 +/- 1.47 MeV, Gamma_R = 139.7 +/- 5.76 MeV, sigma(W_R) = 41.06 +/- 1.24 microbarn, A_{1/2} = 93.43 +/- 1.54 x 10^{-3} GeV^{-1/2}, chi^2/nu = 15.27
- From the Soez (soez) dataset fitted to 4pi a0(W) with b_eta=0.55, b_pi=0.35: W_R = 1559.18 +/- 2.83 MeV, Gamma_R = 199.61 +/- 13.86 MeV, sigma(W_R) = 29.83 +/- 0.76 microbarn, A_{1/2} = 103.35 +/- 2.45 x 10^{-3} GeV^{-1/2}, chi^2/nu = 2.3
- From the JLab (jlab1) dataset fitted to 4pi a0(W) with b_eta=0.55, b_pi=0.35: W_R = 1530.63 +/- 12.13 MeV, Gamma_R = 152.4 +/- 18.7 MeV, sigma(W_R) = 43.18 +/- 9.13 microbarn, A_{1/2} = 96.86 +/- 8.32 x 10^{-3} GeV^{-1/2}, chi^2/nu = 11.22
- Noncentrality parameter delta^2 = 46.8 for the GRAAL dataset, yielding an expected noncentral chi^2 per degree of freedom of 1.05

## Context

This work extends the partial-wave analysis of eta photoproduction on protons previously reported by Balandina (bal3eng) and addresses the long-standing discrepancy between total widths extracted from photoproduction and hadroproduction of eta mesons, as noted in the Review of Particle Physics (RPP). The results from the Soez and JLab datasets yield total widths (around 150-200 MeV) that are more consistent with hadronic determinations, removing the earlier disagreement, while the Krusche and GRAAL datasets give significantly different values. The analysis enables future extraction of higher multipole amplitudes (p-wave, d-wave) but requires more precise polarization observable data to achieve reliable estimates.
