# Chiral theory of $ρ$-meson gravitational form factors

> The low-energy chiral effective field theory of vector mesons and Goldstone bosons in external gravitational field is considered. The energy-momentum tensor is obtained and the gravitational form factors of the $ρ$-meson are calculated up to next-to-leading order in the chiral expansion. This amounts to considering tree-level and one-loop order diagrams. The chiral expansion of the form factors at zero momentum transfer as well as of the slope parameters is worked out. Also, the long-range behaviour of the energy and internal force distributions is obtained and analysed.

## Key claim

The paper computes the gravitational form factors (GFFs) of the rho meson using chiral effective field theory up to next-to-leading order, but the results are limited to low-energy, long-distance behavior and rely on the complex-mass renormalization scheme to handle the unstable nature of the rho meson; the chiral expansions contain singular terms in the pion mass that complicate direct comparison with lattice QCD results.

## Method

The authors construct a chiral effective Lagrangian for rho and omega mesons and pions coupled to an external gravitational field, derive the energy-momentum tensor, and compute the six gravitational form factors (A0, A1, J, D0, D1, E) of the rho meson at tree level and one-loop order. They apply the complex-mass renormalization scheme to handle the unstable rho meson and use dimensional regularization with FeynCalc, expanding loop integrals via the method of dimensional counting to obtain chiral expansions at zero momentum transfer and for slope parameters.

## Key numbers

- A0(0) = 1 (exact, no chiral corrections)
- J(0) = 1 (exact, no chiral corrections)
- D1(0) contains a term proportional to 1/M_pi: (g_{omega rho pi}^2 M_R^3)/(60 pi F^2) * (1/M_pi), leading to D1(0) ≈ 4 at the physical pion mass
- The slope s_{A1} contains a term proportional to 1/M_pi: -(g_{omega rho pi}^2 M_R)/(480 pi F^2) * (1/M_pi)
- The slope s_{D1} contains a term proportional to 1/M_pi^3: (g_{omega rho pi}^2 M_R^3)/(560 pi F^2) * (1/M_pi^3)
- In the chiral limit, D1(t) contains a term proportional to 1/sqrt(-t): (3 g_{omega rho pi}^2 M_R^3)/(256 F^2) * (1/sqrt(-t))

## Context

This work extends the chiral EFT approach previously applied to nucleon GFFs (Polyakov 2002, Polyakov 2018) to the spin-1 rho meson, following the formalism for spin-1 GFFs defined by Holstein (2006) and the multipole decomposition for arbitrary spin by Cotogno (2019). It complements light-cone constituent quark model calculations (Sun 2020) and lattice QCD results for the gluon part of rho-meson GFFs (Pefkou 2021), providing chiral extrapolation formulas needed to connect lattice simulations at unphysical pion masses to the physical point. The results enable future studies of the long-range behavior of energy and force distributions inside vector mesons and can be used to test large-N_c predictions for baryon structure (Panteleeva 2020, Kim 2020).
