# Magnetic dipole moment of the S11(1535) from the γp \to γηp reaction

> The γp \to γηp reaction in the S11(1535) resonance region is investigated as a method to access the S11(1535) magnetic dipole moment. To study the feasibility, we perform calculations of the γp \to γηp process within an effective Lagrangian approach containing both the S11 resonant mechanism and a background of non-resonant contributions. Predictions are made for the forthcoming experiments. In particular, we focus on the sensitivity of cross sections and photon asymmetries to the S11(1535) magnetic dipole moment.

## Key claim

The paper proposes that the magnetic dipole moment of the S11(1535) resonance can be accessed via the γp → γηp reaction, and performs feasibility calculations showing that cross sections and photon asymmetries are sensitive to this moment, but only within an effective Lagrangian approach that includes both the resonant S11 mechanism and non-resonant background contributions.
