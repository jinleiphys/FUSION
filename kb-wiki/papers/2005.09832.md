# T-odd generalized and quasi transverse momentum dependent parton distribution in a scalar spectator model

> Generalized transverse momentum dependent parton distributions (GTMDs), as mother funtions of transverse momentum dependent parton distributions (TMDs) and generalized parton distributions (GPDs), encode the most general parton structure of hadrons. We calculate four twist-two time reversal odd GTMDs of pion in a scalar spectator model. We study the dependence of GTMDs on the longitudinal momentum fraction $x$ carried by the active quark and the transverse momentum $|\vec k_T|$ for different values of skewness $ξ$ defined as the longitudinal momentum transferred to the proton as well as the total momentum $|\vecΔ_T|$ transferred to the proton. In addition, the quasi-TMDs and quasi-GPDs of pion have also been investigated in this paper.

## Key claim

The paper calculates four twist-two time-reversal odd (T-odd) generalized transverse momentum dependent parton distributions (GTMDs) of the pion in a scalar spectator model, finding that these distributions only have nonvanishing analytical results in the DGLAP region (x greater than the skewness xi), contrary to the abstract's implication that the full x-dependence is studied without this restriction.

## Method

The authors compute the T-odd GTMDs, quasi-TMDs, and quasi-GPDs of the pion using a scalar spectator model with a pseudo-scalar pion-quark coupling, including one-loop corrections from the Wilson line to generate T-odd effects. The model parameters are fixed by fitting the unpolarized parton distribution f1π(x) to the GRV parametrization, adopting values gπ'=6.316, λ=0.855, α=0, β=1, with quark mass m=0.3 GeV and strong coupling αs≈0.3. Analytical results are derived for the four T-odd GTMDs (F1o, G̃1o, H1k,o, H1Δ,o) in both the |ΔT|=0 and |ΔT|≠0 cases, and for the quasi-TMD h1π,Q(3)⊥ and quasi-GPD H1,Q(3) with their Pz dependence.

## Key numbers

- Model parameters fitted to GRV parametrization: gπ'=6.316, λ=0.855, α=0, β=1
- Quark mass adopted: m=0.3 GeV
- Strong coupling adopted: αs≈0.3
- For T-odd GTMD F1o at ξ=0.1 and |ΔT|=0, the maximum kT yielding nonvanishing values at x=0.1 is about 0.8 GeV
- For T-odd GTMD F1o at ξ=0.1 and |ΔT|=0, the minimum value occurs when kT is about 0.3 GeV
- The maximum absolute value of T-odd GTMD H1k,o can reach 0.56

## Context

This work extends previous model calculations of quasi-distributions by Bhattacharya (2018, 2019) and Ma (2019) to T-odd GTMDs of the pion, and provides analytical results that complement earlier T-odd GTMD studies by Meissner (2008, 2009) and Lorce (2013). The results enable future lattice QCD calculations of quasi-TMDs and quasi-GPDs via the large-momentum effective theory (LaMET) approach of Ji (2013), and provide a model benchmark for extracting the Collins-Soper evolution kernel as computed by Shanahan (2019, 2020).
