# Character Expansions, Itzykson-Zuber Integrals, and the QCD Partition Function

> A combinatorial formula to generate U(N) character expansions is presented. It is shown that the resulting character expansion formulas greatly simplify a number of problems where integrals over the group manifolds need to be calculated. Several examples are given, including direct and very quick calculations of the Itzykson-Zuber integral and the finite volume effective partition function of QCD in the sector with a given topological charge.

## Key claim

The paper presents a combinatorial formula to generate U(N) character expansions that greatly simplifies calculations of integrals over group manifolds, including the Itzykson-Zuber integral and the finite-volume effective QCD partition function, but the method is restricted to representations where the number of boxes in the last row of the Young Tableau is zero, with an additional summation over a parameter that can take both positive and negative values.

## Method

The author extends a previously given combinatorial formula (Balantekin, 1984) for U(N) character expansions by starting from a power series G(x,t) = sum_n A_n(x) t^n and constructing determinants that, after transformation of indices and division by the Vandermonde determinant, yield an expansion in terms of U(N) characters. The resulting formulas are then applied to evaluate group integrals by expanding the integrand in characters, using orthogonality relations for group integration, and re-summing the series using determinantal identities.

## Key numbers

* The Itzykson-Zuber integral is evaluated as: ∫ dU exp[β Tr(M₁ U M₂ U†)] = (∏_{p=0}^{N-1} p!) β^{-N(N-1)/2} [det(exp(β λ_i ν_j))/(Δ(λ)Δ(ν))], where λ and ν are eigenvalues of M₁ and M₂ respectively.
* The finite-volume QCD partition function for equal masses and topological charge ν is: Z_ν = det(I_{ν+i-j}(V Σ m)), where V is volume, Σ is the quark condensate constant, and m is the quark mass.
* For unequal masses, the QCD partition function becomes: Z_ν = (1/2)^{-N(N-1)/2} [∏_{i=1}^{N} (N-i)!] (1/Δ(μ₁²,…,μ_N²)) det[μ_i^{j-1} I_{ν+j-1}(μ_i)], where μ_i are eigenvalues of the mass matrix.
* The generalized Itzykson-Zuber integral I = ∫ dU ∫ dV exp[i Re Tr(U x V† y)] evaluates to: I = (2i)^{N(N+1)} [∑_{i=0}^{N-1} i!]² det(J₀(x_i y_j))/(Δ(x²)Δ(y²)).

## Context

This work extends the author's earlier combinatorial formula for U(N) character expansions (Balantekin, 1984) and provides a much simpler derivation of the Itzykson-Zuber integral originally calculated by Itzykson and Zuber (1980) and Harish-Chandra (1958). The method enables direct calculation of the finite-volume QCD partition function previously studied by Leutwyler and Smilga (1992) for equal masses and proven for unequal masses by Brower, Rossi, and Tan (1981), Verbaarschot (1997), and Akuzawa and Wadati (1998). The technique opens the door to applications in random matrix theory and extensions to supergroups U(N/M) and orthogonal/symplectic groups.
