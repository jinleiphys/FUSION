# Threshold photoproduction of $η_c$ and $η_b$ using holographic QCD

> We discuss the possibility that threshold photoproduction of $η_{c,b}$ may be sensitive to the pseudovector $1^{+-}$ glueball exchange. We use the holographic construction to identify the pseudovector glueball with the Kalb-Ramond field, minimally coupled to bulk Dirac fermions. We derive the holographic C-odd form factor and its respective charge radius. Using the pertinent Witten diagrams, we derive and analyze the differential photoproduction cross section for $η_{c,b}$ in the threshold regime, including the interference from the dual bulk photon exchange with manifest vector dominance. The possibility of measuring this process at current and future electron facilities is discussed.

## Key claim

Threshold photoproduction of $\eta_c$ and $\eta_b$ may be sensitive to the exchange of a pseudovector $1^{+-}$ glueball (the Odderon), but the cross section is expected to be suppressed compared to charmonium photoproduction, and the process includes interference from a dual bulk photon exchange with manifest vector dominance.

## Method

The authors use a holographic QCD construction in which the $1^{+-}$ glueball is identified with the Kalb-Ramond 2-form field in a 5D anti-de Sitter (AdS) background with a non-constant dilaton. They minimally couple this field to bulk Dirac fermions representing the nucleon, and derive the C-odd form factor and charge radius from the holographic 3-point functions. Using Witten diagrams, they compute the differential photoproduction cross section for $\eta_c$ and $\eta_b$ near threshold, including both the Odderon exchange and the photon Primakoff exchange contributions.

## Key numbers

* The $\eta_c$ mass is 2.984 GeV, and the $J/\Psi$ mass is 3.097 GeV, providing the kinematic context for threshold photoproduction.
* The 5D Chern-Simons coupling is fixed as $g_{CS} = N_c / (24\pi^2)$.
* The 5D Newton constant is given by $\tilde{g}_5^2 = 8\pi^2 / N_c^2$.
* The proton magnetic moment normalization fixes the Pauli parameter $\eta_P$ via $F_2(0) = (\mu_p - 1) = 1.7928$, yielding $\eta_P = 1.7928 / [4(\tau - 1)]$.
* The C-odd Odderon form factor is given by $F_b(K) = 16 (\tau - 1) \Gamma(\tau + 1) \Gamma(a_K + 1) \times {}_2\tilde{F}_1(\tau + 1, a_K + 1; \tau + a_K + 1; -3)$.

## Context

This work extends the holographic approach previously applied to threshold charmonium photoproduction by Mamo and Zahed (2019, 2022), which successfully extracted the mass and scalar radii of the gluonic component of the nucleon and was compared to JLab data by Duran et al. (2022). The present paper extends that framework to the C-odd channel, identifying the Odderon with the Kalb-Ramond field following the earlier holographic proposal by Brower et al. (2008). The results enable predictions for future measurements at electron facilities such as JLab and the Electron-Ion Collider, potentially providing a direct probe of the C-odd gluonic structure of the nucleon.
