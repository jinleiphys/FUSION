# On the Multiboson Coherent State in Deformed Boson Scheme

> The treatment for the multiboson coherent state is extended for the completeness of the formulation. The basic idea for the extension is presented in terms of expanding the boson space. The MYT boson mapping plays a central role. The resultant multiboson coherent state includes the state obtained in our previous treatment and the form suggests various multiboson coherent states are possible in the deformed boson scheme.

## Key claim

The paper extends the previously limited treatment of the multiboson coherent state, which was restricted to a special boson subspace, to the full boson space, thereby completing the formulation; the extension shows that various multiboson coherent states are possible within the deformed boson scheme, with the previous result appearing as a special case when the additional parameter is set to zero.

## Method

The authors expand the boson space from a subspace (spanned by states with occupation numbers that are integer multiples of \(m\)) to the whole space (including all occupation numbers \(mn+r\) with \(r=0,1,\dots,m-1\)). They use the MYT boson mapping to map this enlarged space onto a product space of two independent boson operators \((\hat{c},\hat{c}^*)\) and \((\hat{d},\hat{d}^*)\). A new multiboson coherent state \(\kket{c}\) is defined in the full space with two complex parameters \(\gamma\) and \(\delta\), and its image \(\ket{c}\) in the mapped boson space is derived, along with the operators \(\hat{\gamma}\) and \(\hat{\delta}\) for which \(\ket{c}\) is an eigenstate.

## Key numbers

- The multiboson coherent state is characterized by an integer \(m\) (\(m=2,3,\dots\)) that defines the multiboson building block \(({\tilde c}^*)^m\).
- The normalization constant for the extended state is \(\Gamma = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \sum_{r=0}^{m-1} [(mn+r)!]^{-1} (|\gamma|^2)^n (|\delta|^2)^r\).
- The mapping operator from the full boson space to the product space is \(\mathbf{U} = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \sum_{r=0}^{m-1} \ket{n,r}\langle\!\langle mn+r|\).
- The deformation function in the mapped state is \(f_m(n,r) = \sqrt{n!}\sqrt{r!} / \sqrt{(mn+r)!}\).
- The operator \(\hat{\gamma}\) satisfies \(\hat{\gamma}\ket{c} = \gamma\ket{c}\) and \(\hat{\delta}\ket{c} = \delta\hat{P}_{m-1}\ket{c}\), where \(\hat{P}_{m-1} = \sum_{r=0}^{m-2} \rket{r}\rbra{r}\).

## Context

This work completes the treatment of the multiboson coherent state initiated in the authors' previous papers (Kuriyama et al., 2001, Papers I–III) and extends the approach of Penson and Solomon (1999) by going beyond a restricted boson subspace. The MYT boson mapping (Marumori, Yamamura, and Tokunaga, 1964) plays a central role. The results enable the construction of various deformed coherent states within the deformed boson scheme and are related to a new boson realization in the Lipkin model (Kuriyama et al., 2001). The formalism also connects to the Dirac canonical theory for constraint systems (Dirac, 1964) via an alternative operator satisfying a modified commutation relation.
