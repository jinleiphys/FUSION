# Nucleon charges and $σ$-terms in lattice QCD

> We determine the nucleon axial, scalar and tensor charges and the nucleon $σ$-terms using twisted mass fermions. We employ three ensembles with approximately equal physical volume of about 5.5~fm, three values of the lattice spacing, approximately 0.06~fm, 0.07~fm and 0.08~fm, and with the mass of the degenerate up and down, strange and charm quarks tuned to approximately their physical values. We compute both isovector and isoscalar charges and $σ$-terms and their flavor decomposition including the disconnected contributions. We use the Akaike Information Criterion to evaluate systematic errors due to excited states and the continuum extrapolation. For the nucleon isovector axial charge we find $g_A^{u-d}=1.250(24)$, in agreement with the experimental value. Moreover, we extract the nucleon $σ$-terms and find for the light quark content $σ_{πN}=41.9(8.1)$~MeV and for the strange $σ_{s}=30(17)$~MeV.

## Key claim

The authors determine nucleon charges and σ-terms using lattice QCD with twisted mass fermions at three lattice spacings and physical quark masses, finding the isovector axial charge g_A^{u-d}=1.250(24) in agreement with experiment, but note that the quoted uncertainties include systematic errors evaluated via the Akaike Information Criterion for excited-state and continuum extrapolation systematics.

## Method

The authors compute two- and three-point nucleon correlation functions on three N_f=2+1+1 twisted mass fermion ensembles with lattice spacings of approximately 0.06 fm, 0.07 fm, and 0.08 fm and physical pion masses near 140 MeV. They extract ground-state matrix elements using simultaneous two-state fits to the two-point functions and ratios of three- to two-point functions, then perform model averaging with the Akaike Information Criterion over variations in fit ranges and number of states. Renormalization factors are computed nonperturbatively in the RI'/MOM scheme and converted to the MSbar scheme at 2 GeV, including both connected and disconnected contributions for isoscalar and flavor-diagonal quantities.

## Key numbers

- Isovector axial charge: g_A^{u-d} = 1.250(24)
- Light quark σ-term (pion-nucleon sigma term): σ_{πN} = 41.9(8.1) MeV
- Strange σ-term: σ_s = 30(17) MeV
- Three lattice spacings used: 0.07957(13) fm (B64), 0.06821(13) fm (C80), 0.05692(12) fm (D96)
- Physical volume approximately 5.5 fm for all ensembles
- Pion masses: 140.2(2) MeV (B64), 136.7(2) MeV (C80), 140.8(2) MeV (D96)

## Context

This work extends lattice QCD calculations of nucleon structure by Alexandrou et al. (2018, 2019) and the Extended Twisted Mass Collaboration (2021, 2022) to include all three lattice spacings at the physical point with both connected and disconnected contributions, avoiding chiral extrapolations. The results provide inputs for beyond Standard Model searches in neutrino experiments such as DUNE and COHERENT, for dark matter detection cross sections, and for phenomenological analyses of transversity parton distribution functions as discussed by Cocuzza (2023).
