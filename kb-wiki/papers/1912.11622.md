## Context

This work extends the method used for the NN interaction by Durso (1980) and Kim (1994), where correlated 2ПЂ exchange was used to determine Пѓ and ПЃ coupling constants in the full Bonn potential, to the heavy meson sector. It provides coupling constants needed for molecular interpretations of exotic states such as X(3872) discovered by Belle (2003), where Пѓ exchange provides medium-range attraction for DD and D*D* bound states. The results enable more quantitative studies of heavy meson molecules and can be compared with lattice QCD results from Can (2012) and Bracco (2001), as well as with quark model estimates by Liu (2008, 2010, 2019) and Lee (2009).
