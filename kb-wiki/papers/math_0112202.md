# A q-Analogue of the embedding chain $U(6) \supset G \supset SO(3)$

> A q-analogue of the embedding chains of the Arima-Iachello model is proposed. The generators of the deformed U(6)-subalgebras are written in terms of the generators of gl_{q}(6), using q-bosons.

## Key claim

A $q$-analogue of the three embedding chains of the Arima-Iachello model (vibrational, rotational, and $\gamma$-unstable) can be constructed by replacing $U(6)$ with $gl_q(6)$ and expressing the generators of the deformed subalgebras in terms of $gl_q(6)$ generators using $q$-bosons, but the generators of $so_q(3)$ cannot be written in terms of the generators of the intermediate deformed subalgebra $L_q$, only in terms of the "grand-mother" algebra $gl_q(6)$.

## Method

The author constructs $q$-deformed analogues of the three Arima-Iachello embedding chains by starting from $gl_q(6)$ realized with $q$-bosons. For each chain, the Cartan-Chevalley generators of the deformed subalgebras ($gl_q(5)$, $so_q(5)$, $sl_q(3)$, $so_q(6)$) are written explicitly as bilinears of $q$-bosons, using previously established deforming maps between $sl(2)$ and $sl_q(2)$ (Curtright-Zachos functional) and between $Sp(4)$ and $sp_q(4)$. The $so_q(3)$ generators are then expressed in terms of the generators of $gl_q(6)$ (or $gl_q(5)$ for the vibrational chain) rather than in terms of the intermediate subalgebra.

## Key numbers

- The $q$-boson commutation relation is $b_i b_j^+ - q^{\delta_{ij}} b_j^+ b_i = \delta_{ij} q^{-N_i}$.
- The $q$-number is defined as $[x]_q = (q^x - q^{-x})/(q - q^{-1})$.
- The $so_q(3)$ generator $L_0$ for the vibrational chain is $L_0 = 2N_1 + N_2 - N_4 - 2N_5 = 2H_1 + \frac{3}{2}H_2$, where $H_1, H_2$ are Cartan generators of $so_q(5)$.
- The $so_q(3)$ generator $L_0$ for the rotational chain is $L_0 = 2N_1 + N_2 - N_5 - 2N_6 = H_1 + H_2$, where $H_1, H_2$ are Cartan generators of $sl_q(3)$.
- The $so_q(3)$ generator $L_0$ for the $\gamma$-unstable chain is $L_0 = 2N_1 + N_2 - N_5 - 2N_6 = \frac{3}{2}(H_1 + H_3) + 2H_2$, where $H_1, H_2, H_3$ are Cartan generators of $so_q(6)$.

## Context

This work extends the program of applying $q$-deformed algebras as generalized dynamical symmetries in nuclear physics, building on the Arima-Iachello model (Arima and Iachello, 1970s) and on earlier studies of $q$-deformed embedding chains for $SU(3) \supset SO(3)$ (Sciarrino, 2001; various authors reviewed by Bonatsos and Daskaloyannis). It provides explicit $q$-boson realizations for the deformed subalgebras of $gl_q(6)$, going beyond the partial results of Navratil and Stoitsov (1999) who showed that $sl_q(3)$ and $so_q(6)$ could be written in terms of $gl_q(6)$. The construction enables writing Hamiltonians as sums of Casimir operators of the $q$-algebras, which in the limit $q \to 1$ reduce to the undeformed Arima-Iachello Hamiltonians, and opens the possibility of applying the formalism to other physically relevant models including supersymmetric extensions.
