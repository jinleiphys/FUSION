# Off-forward gluonic structure of vector mesons

> The spin-independent and transversity generalised form factors (GFFs) of the $ϕ$ meson are studied using lattice QCD calculations with light quark masses corresponding to a pion mass $m_π\sim450(5)$ MeV. One transversity and three spin-independent GFFs related to the lowest moments of leading-twist spin-independent and transversity gluon distributions are obtained at six non-zero values of the momentum transfer up to 1.2 GeV$^2$. These quantities are compared with the analogous spin-independent quark GFFs and the electromagnetic form factors determined on the same lattice ensemble. The results show quantitative distinction between the spatial distribution of transversely polarised gluons, unpolarised gluons, and quarks, and point the way towards further investigations of the gluon structure of nucleons and nuclei.

## Key claim
The paper presents the first lattice QCD determination of gluon generalized form factors (GFFs) for a hadron, specifically the spin-1 φ meson, but only a subset of the seven unpolarized and eight transversity GFFs can be extracted due to technical limitations, with the results obtained at unphysical quark masses corresponding to a pion mass of 450(5) MeV.

## Method
The authors performed lattice QCD simulations on a single ensemble of 24³ × 64 isotropic gauge configurations with N_f = 2+1 flavors of dynamical quarks, using a clover-improved quark action and Lüscher-Weisz gauge action at lattice spacing a = 0.1167(16) fm. They constructed two-point and three-point correlation functions from 1042 configurations with 96 source locations each, extracted matrix elements of lowest-n twist-2 gluon operators via ratio methods, and solved systems of linear equations to determine the dominant GFFs at six non-zero momentum transfers up to 1.2 GeV².

## Key numbers
- Pion mass: m_π ~ 450(5) MeV; φ meson mass: m_φ ~ 1040(3) MeV
- Lattice spacing: a = 0.1167(16) fm; lattice dimensions: L³ × T = 24³ × 64, corresponding to L = 2.801(29) fm and T = 7.469(77) fm
- Six non-zero momentum transfers in the range 0 < |Δ²| < 1.2 GeV² were studied
- 1042 gauge configurations and 10⁵ measurements (N_meas) were used in the analysis
- Three spin-independent GFFs (B^{(2)}_{1,0}, B^{(2)}_{2,0}, B^{(2)}_{4,0}) and one transversity GFF (A^{(2)}_{1,0}) were extracted

## Context
This work extends lattice QCD studies of hadron structure from quark distributions to gluonic structure, providing the first LQCD determination of gluon GFFs for any hadron. The results complement prior calculations of quark GFFs and electromagnetic form factors on the same lattice ensemble (Detmold, 2016), and the techniques developed here enable future investigations of gluon structure in nucleons and nuclei, which will be relevant for interpreting data from the planned Electron-Ion Collider (Accardi, 2012).
