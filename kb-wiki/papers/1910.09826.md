# Four-Body Faddeev-Type Calculation of the $\bar{K}NNN$ System: Preliminary Results

> The paper is devoted to the $\bar{K}NNN$ system, consisting of an antikaon and three nucleons. Four-body Faddeev-type AGS equations are being solved in order to find possible quasi-bound state in the system.

## Key claim
The paper reports preliminary evidence for a quasi-bound state in the four-body \(\bar{K}NNN\) system (specifically \(K^- ppn\)), but the results are provisional because bugs were found in the program code after the conference talk, and tests and checks are still in progress.

## Method
The author solved four-body Faddeev-type Alt-Grassberger-Sandhas (AGS) equations in momentum representation, using separable two-body \(\bar{K}N\) and \(NN\) potentials. The three-body \(\bar{K}NN\), \(NNN\), and \(2+2\) \(\bar{K}N+NN\) amplitudes were separabilized via the Energy Dependent Pole Approximation (EDPA), taking only one separable term. Exact optical versions of two phenomenological \(\bar{K}N\) potentials (with one-pole and two-pole structure of the \(\Lambda(1405)\) resonance) were used, and the homogeneous system of 18 antisymmetrized channels was solved to find the pole position of the quasi-bound state.

## Key numbers
- Preliminary pole position for the \(K^- ppn\) quasi-bound state using the one-pole phenomenological \(\bar{K}N\) potential (SIDD1): \(z = -75 - i\,14\) MeV.
- Preliminary pole position for the \(K^- ppn\) quasi-bound state using the two-pole phenomenological \(\bar{K}N\) potential (SIDD2): \(z = -71 - i\,8\) MeV.
- Binding energy of the three-nucleon system \(NNN\) (\({}^3\)H or \({}^3\)He) with the new two-term \(NN\) potential: 9.95 MeV (Coulomb not included).
- Singlet \(NN\) scattering length: \(a_s = 16.32\) fm; triplet \(NN\) scattering length: \(a_t = -5.40\) fm; deuteron binding energy: \(E_{deu} = 2.225\) MeV.
- For comparison, the three-body \(K^- pp\) pole positions from coupled-channel calculations: SIDD1: \(-53.3 - i\,32.4\) MeV; SIDD2: \(-47.4 - i\,24.9\) MeV; Chiral: \(-32.2 - i\,24.3\) MeV.

## Context
This work extends the author's previous series of dynamically exact three-body Faddeev-type calculations of the \(\bar{K}NN\) and \(\bar{K}\bar{K}N\) systems (Shevchenko, 2015-2017) to the four-body \(\bar{K}NNN\) system. Prior calculations of the \(\bar{K}NNN\) system exist (Akaishi et al., 2002; Barnea et al., 2012; Ikeda et al., 2013), but the author notes that more accurate calculations are needed. The present approach uses the four-body AGS equations with separable potentials and the EDPA method, which the author claims can treat energy-dependent \(\bar{K}N\) potentials exactly. The preliminary results enable future refined calculations with the chirally motivated \(\bar{K}N\) potential and inclusion of more separable terms for improved accuracy.
