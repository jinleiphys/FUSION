# Radiative decays of П•-meson and nature of light scalar resonances

> Based on gauge invariance, we show that the new threshold phenomenon is discovered in the П•radiative decays П•\toОіa0\toОі\pi0О·and П•\toОіf0\toОі\pi0\pi0. This enables to conclude that production of the lightest scalar mesons a0(980) and f0(980) in these decays is caused by the four-quark transitions, resulting in strong restrictions on the large Nc expansions of the decay amplitudes. The analysis shows that these constraints give new evidences in favor of the four-quark nature of a0(980) and f0(980) mesons.

## Context

This work extends the author's earlier proposal (Achasov and Ivanchenko, 1989) that ПҶ radiative decays could reveal the nature of a0(980) and f0(980) mesons, and builds on the four-quark bag model states suggested by Jaffe (1977). The analysis uses experimental data from the SND and CMD-2 detectors at Novosibirsk, confirmed by DAОҰNE, to argue against the conventional quark-antiquark interpretation of these scalar mesons. The results enable future studies of the OZI rule and large Nc expansions in hadron spectroscopy, and provide constraints on models of light scalar mesons that must reproduce the K+K- loop mechanism.
