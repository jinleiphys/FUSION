## Method
The authors calculated branching ratios, differential distributions, and forward-backward leptonic asymmetries for the $\bar B_d \to \mu^+ \mu^- e^+ e^-$ decay. They included resonance contributions from $\rho(770)$, $\omega(782)$, four charmonium resonances ($\psi(3770)$, $\psi(4040)$, $\psi(4160)$, $\psi(4415)$), tails of $J/\psi$ and $\psi(2S)$, plus non-resonant contributions from virtual photon emission by the $b$-quark, bremsstrahlung, and weak annihilation, using the vector meson dominance (VMD) model.

## Context
This work provides the first Standard Model predictions for the $\bar B_d \to \mu^+ \mu^- e^+ e^-$ decay, extending studies of rare $B$ meson decays into four charged leptons. The inclusion of multiple resonance contributions using VMD goes beyond simpler treatments that might omit the $\omega(782)$ contribution. These predictions enable future comparisons with experimental data from LHCb or Belle II, potentially revealing new physics if deviations from the Standard Model are observed.
