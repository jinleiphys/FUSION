# Ab initio no-core properties of 7Li and 7Be with JISP16 and NNLO_opt interactions

> We investigate the properties of 7Li with the JISP16 and chiral NNLO_opt nucleon-nucleon interactions and 7Be with the JISP16 interaction in the ab initio no-core full configuration (NCFC) approach. We calculate selected observables that include energy spectra, point proton root-mean-square radii, electromagnetic moments and transitions. We compare our results with experimental results, where available, as well as with results obtained using nucleon-nucleon plus three-nucleon interactions. We obtain reasonable agreement between theory and experiment for low-lying states that are dominated by p-shell configurations.

## Key claim

The JISP16 and NNLO_opt nucleon-nucleon interactions, without three-nucleon forces, produce reasonable agreement with experiment for low-lying states of 7Li and 7Be that are dominated by p-shell configurations, though the results with JISP16 or AV18+IL7 are in better agreement with experimental data than those with NNLO_opt.

## Method

The authors performed ab initio no-core full configuration (NCFC) calculations for 7Li and 7Be using a harmonic oscillator basis with truncation parameter N_max up to 16 and HO energies from 10 MeV to 40 MeV. They diagonalized the Hamiltonian including the two-nucleon interaction and Coulomb interaction, neglecting three-nucleon forces, using the MFDn code. Ground state energies were extrapolated to the infinite basis space using an exponential form with three successive N_max values, and the numerical uncertainty was taken as the difference between extrapolated results from two consecutive sets of N_max values.

## Key numbers

- Ground state energy minima for both 7Li and 7Be with JISP16 and NNLO_opt are near HO energy of 20 MeV
- Extrapolated ground state energies lie below the first breakup thresholds (3H+4He for 7Li, 3He+4He for 7Be) for all cases
- Magnetic dipole moment of 7Be converges to a little below -1.08 mu_N
- Magnetic dipole moment of 7Li with NNLO_opt converges close to 2.97 mu_N; with JISP16 a little less than 2.96 mu_N
- Quadrupole moment visually estimated to converge near -3.5 e*fm^2 for 7Li and -5.5 e*fm^2 for 7Be
- B(E2) transitions from the 7/2- state to ground state are about half those from the 1/2- state to ground state for both 7Li and 7Be

## Context

This work extends prior NCFC calculations for 7Li with JISP16 (Vary et al., 2013) to higher N_max values (up to 16) and presents the first NCFC results for 7Li with the chiral NNLO_opt interaction, as well as more extensive properties for 7Be than previously reported. The results are compared with experimental data and with calculations using AV18+IL7 (Shirokov et al., 2014; Pastore et al., 2014) and chiral NN+NNN (Maris et al., 2015). The decomposition of magnetic moment contributions into proton and neutron orbital and spin components enables identification of level crossing in the J=5/2 states with JISP16, which is not observed with NNLO_opt.
