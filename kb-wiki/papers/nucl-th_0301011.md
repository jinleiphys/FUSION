# Vector meson photoproduction processes near threshold

> We discuss the photoproduction processes of light vector mesons ($ρ$, $ω$, and $ϕ$) from the nucleon near threshold. We first develop a simple model based on meson exchanges which is modified by the nucleon pole terms. We then extend this model to study other physically interesting topics. As examples, we discuss the missing nucleon resonances problem in $ω$ photoproduction and the direct $ϕNN$ coupling constant in $ϕ$ photoproduction. The calculated cross sections are compared with the recent experimental data. Various spin observables are discussed, which may be measured at current photon/electron facilities such as TJNAF and SPring-8 of RCNP. Precise measurements of such quantities would provide very useful information to understand the production mechanism.

## Key claim

The paper argues that a simple model based on Pomeron, meson, and nucleon exchanges can describe the non-resonant background for vector meson photoproduction near threshold, and that this background must be well understood before extracting nucleon resonance parameters or the direct φNN coupling constant from experimental data.

## Method

The author develops a model for ρ, ω, and φ photoproduction using three mechanisms: Pomeron exchange (described by a Regge form with trajectory α_P(t) = 1.08 + 0.25 t), one-meson exchange (π, η, σ), and s- and u-channel nucleon exchange. The model is then extended to include 22 nucleon resonances (12 positive parity, 10 negative parity up to spin-9/2) from the Capstick and Roberts quark model predictions for ω photoproduction, and to study the φNN coupling constant in φ photoproduction using effective Lagrangians.

## Key numbers

- The Pomeron trajectory is α_P(t) = 1.08 + α'_P t with α'_P = 0.25 GeV^{-2}
- For the form factors: Λ_π = 0.7 GeV, Λ_{Vγπ} = 0.77 GeV, Λ_η = 1.0 GeV, Λ_{Vγη} = 0.9 GeV
- The nucleon exchange form factor uses Λ_{VNN} ≈ 0.8 GeV
- The φNN coupling constant g_{φNN} = -3.0 reproduces recent CLAS data, consistent with a range of g_{φNN} = -2.3 to -4.7 from baryon-baryon scattering analyses
- The OZI-rule-consistent φNN coupling from kaon loops is estimated as g_{φNN} ≈ -0.24

## Context

This work extends the meson-exchange model of Friman and Soyeur (1996) for ρ photoproduction to ω and φ channels, and contrasts with the SU(6)×O(3) constituent quark model calculations of Zhao et al. (1998, 1999) by using the Capstick and Roberts (1992, 1994) quark model predictions with configuration mixing. The results enable experimental tests at TJNAF and SPring-8 to resolve the missing nucleon resonance problem and to determine the strangeness content of the nucleon through φ photoproduction.
