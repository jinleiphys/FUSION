# Deuteron wave function for Reid93 potential and polarization observables in elastic lepton-deuteron scattering

> Elastic lepton-deuteron scattering was considered within the limit of zero lepton mass. The deuteron wave function in the coordinate representation for the Reid93 potential was applied to numerical calculations. The angular-momentum dependence of values spin correlation coefficients $C_{xz}^{(0)}$, $C_{zz}^{(0)}$ and tensor asymmetries $A_{xx}^{(0)}$, $A_{xz}^{(0)}$, $A_{zz}^{(0)}$ have been evaluated in 3D format for Reid93 potential. These polarization observables are analyzed for different energies and scattering angles. Application of the obtained values allows better explanation and illustration of the laws of elastic lepton-deuteron scattering.

# Deuteron wave function for Reid93 potential and polarization observables in elastic lepton-deuteron scattering

## Key claim
Within the limit of zero lepton mass, the Reid93 nucleon-nucleon potential yields specific numerical values for spin correlation coefficients and tensor asymmetries in elastic lepton-deuteron scattering, but these results are conditional on the chosen analytical form of the deuteron wave function and the particular parametrization of deuteron electromagnetic form factors.

## Method
The author used the analytical form of the deuteron wave function in coordinate representation for the Reid93 potential, expressed as expansions in exponentials of r³. Numerical calculations were performed for three sets of polarization observables: spin correlation coefficients C_{xz}^{(0)} and C_{zz}^{(0)} (for longitudinally polarized lepton beam on vector-polarized deuteron target), and tensor asymmetries A_{xx}^{(0)}, A_{xz}^{(0)}, A_{zz}^{(0)} (for unpolarized lepton beam on tensor-polarized deuteron target), evaluated as functions of muon beam energy and scattering angle in the zero lepton mass limit.

## Key numbers
- Spin correlation coefficients C_{xz}^{(0)} and C_{zz}^{(0)} vanish at zero scattering angle and at small energies, becoming negative and forming a "tray" as angle and energy increase (no explicit numerical values given for these coefficients).
- For tensor asymmetries A_{xx}^{(0)} and A_{zz}^{(0)}, a characteristic hump (peak) appears near 2 fm⁻¹ in the range of scattering angles 0-180 degrees.
- For tensor asymmetry A_{xz}^{(0)}, a pit (negative feature) appears instead of a peak.
- A characteristic plane for tensor asymmetries appears at energy increase above 4 fm⁻¹.

## Context
This work extends the formalism of Gakh (2014) for elastic lepton-deuteron scattering with finite lepton mass to the zero-mass limit, applying the Reid93 potential from the Nijmegen group. The deuteron wave function coefficients for Reid93 were previously published by Zhaba (2016, MPLA2016). The spin correlation coefficients C_{Zz}^{(0)} and C_{Xz}^{(0)} (labeled A_B^L and A_B^T) were previously calculated for Reid93 in Zhaba (WSN114). The results enable future comparison with calculations that include finite lepton mass, and may inform studies of proton-antiproton annihilation into massive leptons as discussed by Dbeyssi (2012).
