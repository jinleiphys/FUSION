# Contribution of the "hanged" diagrams into the reaction $np \to np ѕА^+ ѕА^-$

> The contribution of "hanged" diagrams into the reaction $np \rightarrow np ѕА^+ ѕА^-$ was considered. It was shown that taking into account of these diagrams permits to get better description of the effective mass spectrum of $ѕА^+ѕА^-$-combinations.

## Key claim

The inclusion of so-called "hanged" diagrams (ѕА-meson and pomeron exchange) improves the description of the ѕАвБЇѕАвБї effective mass spectrum in the reaction np вЖТ np ѕАвБЇѕАвБї, particularly for masses near 300 MeV/c¬≤, but the authors note that at PвВА = 5.20 GeV/c the contribution of these diagrams did not exceed 2%, and interference between diagrams was neglected.

## Method

The matrix elements for "hanged" diagrams involving ѕА-meson exchange and pomeron (P) exchange were constructed, using formfactors from prior work (Jerusalimov, OPERnp2pi), off-shell elastic ѕАѕА-scattering amplitudes, and vertex functions with parameters from Nikitin. The squared matrix element was written as the sum of the squared amplitudes for ѕАвБ∞ exchange, ѕА¬± exchange, and pomeron exchange, neglecting interference terms, and calculations were performed for the reaction np вЖТ np ѕАвБЇѕАвБї at two kinematic conditions.

## Key numbers

- At PвВА = 5.20 GeV/c, the contribution of "hanged" diagrams did not exceed 2%.
- The constant of strong interaction used was G¬≤/4ѕА = 14.6.
- Calculations were performed for the reaction at PвВА = 1.73 GeV/c and at T_kin = 1.25 GeV (the latter using ~10вБЈ events from the HADES setup).
- The improved description of the ѕАвБЇѕАвБї effective mass spectrum was noted for masses close to 300 MeV/c¬≤.

## Context

This work extends the earlier study of the reaction np вЖТ np ѕАвБЇѕАвБї at intermediate energies (1.73 < PвВА < 5.2 GeV/c) by Jerusalimov (OPERnp2pi), which considered reggeized ѕА-meson exchange and one-baryon exchange diagrams but omitted "hanged" diagrams. The present analysis was motivated by statistically significant bumps observed in HADES data at T_kin = 1.25 GeV (np2piH, HADES), which were previously attributed to fluctuations at low statistics. The inclusion of "hanged" diagrams enables a better description of the low-mass ѕАвБЇѕАвБї region and suggests a dynamical origin for the observed structure.
