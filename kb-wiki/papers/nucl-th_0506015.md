# Incoherent pion photoproduction on the deuteron with polarization observables I: Formal expressions

> Formal expressions are developed for the general five-fold differential cross section of incoherent $π$-photoproduction on the deuteron including beam and target polarization. The polarization observables of the cross section are described by various beam, target and beam-target asymmetries for polarized photons and/or polarized deuterons. They are given as bilinear hermitean forms in the reaction matrix elements divided by the unpolarized cross section. In addition, the corresponding observables for the semi-exclusive reaction $\vec d(\vec γ,π)NN$ are also given.

## Key claim

The paper derives formal expressions for the five-fold differential cross section of incoherent pion photoproduction on the deuteron with beam and target polarization, correcting errors in prior work (Dar04a, Dar05a, Dar05b, Dar05c, DaS05) where incorrect expressions for polarization observables were used, particularly those related to linearly polarized photons and the claim that all circularly polarized photon asymmetries vanish identically.

## Method

The authors develop the general form of the T-matrix for the reaction γ + d → π + N₁ + N₂ using a partial wave decomposition with multipole expansions (electric and magnetic multipoles E^L_M and M^L_M). They derive the density matrices for polarized photons (circular and linear polarization) and polarized deuterons (vector and tensor polarization), then evaluate the trace over spin degrees of freedom to obtain the differential cross section expressed as bilinear hermitean forms in the reaction matrix elements. The symmetry properties of the T-matrix under parity are used to simplify the expressions, and the results are specialized to the semi-exclusive reaction d(γ,π)NN where only the pion is detected.

## Key numbers

- The five-fold differential cross section is given by d⁵σ/(dq dΩ_q dΩ_p) with kinematic factor c(ω,q,Ω_q,Ω_p) = (1/(2π)⁵) × (E_d/(E_d+p_d)) × (m_N²/(4ωω_π)) × (p* q²/W_NN)
- The relative momentum of the two final nucleons in their c.m. system is p* = (1/2)√(W_NN² - 4m_N²)
- The unpolarized semi-exclusive differential cross section is d³σ₀/(dq dΩ_q) = V¹₀₀(q, θ_q)
- For forward and backward pion emission (θ_q = 0 and π), the asymmetries Σ̃ˡ, T̃⁰̅ᵢₘ for M≠0, and T̃ˡᵢₘ for M≠2 vanish identically
- The total cross section expression includes terms with P^d₂ T̄⁰₂₀ × (1/2)(3cos²θ_d - 1), P^γ_c P^d₁ T̄ᶜ₁₀ cosθ_d, and P^γ_l P^d₂ T̄ˡ₂₂ cos(2φ_d) × (√6/4) sin²θ_d

## Context

This work extends the formal treatment of polarization observables from deuteron photodisintegration (Are88) to incoherent pion photoproduction, correcting errors in the series of papers by Darwish et al. (2004-2005) who incorrectly applied symmetry properties from photodisintegration. It complements the work of Blaazer et al. (1994) who derived polarization observables for coherent pion photoproduction on the deuteron. The formalism enables subsequent numerical calculations (FiA05) of the influence of NN- and πN-rescattering on the various asymmetries, and provides a correct basis for experimental studies at facilities like LEGS and the A2 collaboration at Mainz.
