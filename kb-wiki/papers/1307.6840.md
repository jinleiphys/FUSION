# What is the role of the meson cloud in the $Σ^{*0} \to γΛ$ and $Σ^\ast \to γΣ$ decays?

> We study the effect of the meson cloud dressing in the octet baryon to decuplet baryon electromagnetic transitions. Combining the valence quark contributions from the covariant spectator quark model with those of the meson cloud estimated based on the flavor SU(3) cloudy bag model, we calculate the transition magnetic form factors at $Q^2=0$ ($Q^2=-q^2$ and $q$ the four-momentum transfer), and also the decuplet baryon electromagnetic decay widths. The result for the $γ^\ast Λ\to Σ^{\ast 0}$ decay width is in complete agreement with the data, while that for the $γ^\ast Σ^+ \to Σ^{\ast +}$ is underestimated the data by 1.4 standard deviations. This achievement may be regarded as a significant advance in the present theoretical situation.

## Key claim

The meson cloud dressing, including contributions from kaon and eta mesons in addition to pions, and from photon coupling to intermediate baryon states, can bring the calculated \(\Sigma^{*0} \to \gamma \Lambda\) decay width into complete agreement with data, while the \(\Sigma^{*+} \to \gamma \Sigma^{+}\) width remains underestimated by 1.4 standard deviations.

## Method

The authors combined valence quark contributions from the covariant spectator quark model with meson cloud contributions estimated using the flavor SU(3) cloudy bag model (CBM) in the static approximation. The meson cloud contributions included both direct photon coupling to the meson (diagram a) and photon coupling to intermediate baryon states (diagram b), for pion, kaon, and eta meson clouds. The CBM results were normalized to the pion cloud contribution for the \(\gamma^\ast N \to \Delta\) transition from the covariant spectator quark model, using a strength parameter \(\lambda_\pi = 0.441\).

## Key numbers

*   The pion cloud strength parameter for the \(\gamma^\ast N \to \Delta\) reaction is \(\lambda_\pi = 0.441\), defining the normalization \(G_M^{{\rm MC} \pi}(0) = 3 \lambda_\pi\).
*   The CBM bare core contribution for the \(\gamma^\ast N \to \Delta\) reaction is \(G_M^B(0) = 1.633\) (in the CBM framework).
*   The total pion cloud contribution for the \(\gamma^\ast N \to \Delta\) reaction in the spectator model is \(G_M^{{\rm MC}\pi}(0) = 1.323\), giving a total \(G_M(0) = 2.956\).
*   The bag radius used in the CBM calculations is \(R = 1\) fm.

## Context

This work extends the authors' previous study (Ramalho and Tsushima, 2013) which included only pion cloud effects and direct photon-meson coupling, and found only partial agreement with data for the \(\Sigma^{*0} \to \gamma \Lambda\) and \(\Sigma^{*+} \to \gamma \Sigma^{+}\) decays. The present inclusion of kaon and eta meson clouds, plus photon coupling to intermediate baryon states, improves the agreement. The results enable more precise predictions for octet-to-decuplet electromagnetic transitions and decay widths, and clarify the role of meson cloud dressing beyond the well-studied \(\gamma^\ast N \to \Delta\) reaction.
