# Interactions of B = 4 Skyrmions

> It is known that the interactions of single Skyrmions are asymptotically described by a Yukawa dipole potential. Less is known about the interactions of solutions of the Skyrme model with higher baryon number. In this paper, it is shown that Yukawa multipole theory can be more generally applied to Skyrmion interactions, and in particular to the long-range dominant interactions of the B = 4 solution of the Skyrme model, which models the alpha-particle. A method that gives the quadrupole nature of the interaction a more intuitive meaning in the pion field colour picture is demonstrated. Numerical methods are employed to find the precise strength of quadrupole and octupole interactions. The results are applied to the B = 8 and B = 12 solutions and to the Skyrme crystal.

## Key claim

The paper shows that Yukawa multipole theory can be applied to describe the long-range interactions of higher-baryon-number Skyrmions, specifically that the dominant asymptotic interaction of two B=4 Skyrmions is quadrupole-quadrupole, with a subleading octupole contribution, provided the separation is large enough that the linearized product ansatz is valid.

## Method

The author derives a general formula for the interaction energy of two Yukawa multipoles in a massive scalar field theory, then shows that the asymptotic interaction of Skyrmions reduces to this scalar theory via a planar integral derived from the product ansatz and the Skyrme field equations. The symmetry of the B=4 Skyrmion (cubic O_h symmetry) is used to determine which multipoles are allowed: the pion fields (F1,F2) transform as E_g and support a quadrupole, while F3 transforms as A_{2u} and supports an octupole. Numerical shooting methods are employed to solve the radial profile equations and extract the multipole strengths C2 and C3.

## Key numbers

- The hedgehog dipole parameter C1 = 2.16 at pion mass m = 0 (previously known from Manton (2004)).
- The quadrupole strength C2 and octupole strength C3 for the B=4 Skyrmion are determined numerically (exact numerical values not provided in the truncated text).
- The interaction energy formula for two Yukawa multipoles is given by E_int = (-1)^l Q^{(2)}_{i1...im} Q^{(1)}_{j1...jl} (∂_{i1}...∂_{im}∂_{j1}...∂_{jl} φ)(X), where φ(X) = -e^{-m|X|}/(4π|X|).

## Context

This work extends the known Yukawa dipole description of single Skyrmion interactions (Jackson (1985)) to the B=4 Skyrmion, which models the alpha-particle. The results enable computation of interaction energies for B=8, B=12, and the Skyrme crystal, where B=4 subunits are in face-to-face contact, building on the cluster approach of Battye (2006, 2009). The method provides an intuitive interpretation of the quadrupole interaction in the pion field colour picture.
