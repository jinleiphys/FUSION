# Doubly Heavy Baryon Xi_cc Production in Upsilon (1S) Decay

> Upsilon (1S) decay to Xi_cc +anything is studied. It is shown that the branching ratio can be as significant as that of Upsilon (1S) decay to J/Psi +anything. The non-relativistic heavy quark effective theory framework is employed for the calculation on the decay width. Measurements on the production of Xi_cc and likely production characteristic of the partonic state with four charm quarks at BELLE2 are suggested.

## Key claim

The branching ratio of Upsilon(1S) decay to the doubly heavy baryon Xi_cc plus anything can be as significant as that of Upsilon(1S) decay to J/Psi plus anything, provided that the non-perturbative matrix elements h1 and h3 are of comparable magnitude; the quoted numerical result assumes h1 = h3 as a simplifying assumption.

## Method

The authors employ the non-relativistic heavy quark effective theory (NRQCD) framework to calculate the partial width for the process Upsilon(1S) -> Xi_cc + anti-c + anti-c + g at leading order in perturbative QCD (tree level). The initial Upsilon(1S) bound state is treated via the color-singlet b-bbar pair with vanishing relative momentum, using the radial wave function at the origin. The final-state Xi_cc baryon is described by a factorization formalism within heavy quark effective theory, with two non-perturbative parameters h1 and h3 representing the probability for a cc pair in a 1S0 or 3S1 state, respectively, in specific color configurations to form the baryon. Six Feynman diagrams for the hard-scattering amplitude are evaluated, and the decay width and momentum distributions are computed numerically.

## Key numbers

* Branching ratio for Upsilon(1S) -> Xi_cc + anything: 1.34 x 10^-4, obtained with h1 = h3 and using the given input parameters.
* Partial decay width: 7.256 eV, with h1 = h3.
* The perturbative part of the width is Gamma = (0.0126 h1 + 0.240 h3) keV, with h3 = 0.0287 GeV^3 from a potential model and h1 taken equal to h3 for the numerical result.
* Input parameters: Upsilon(1S) mass = 9.46 GeV, Xi_cc mass = 3.621 GeV, bottom quark mass = 4.73 GeV, Upsilon radial wave function at origin Psi_Upsilon(0) = 2.194 GeV^(3/2), alpha_s(m_c) = 0.253.
* The dependence of the branching ratio on the ratio m_c/m_b is studied, with the nominal value taken as m_c/m_b = 0.25.

## Context

This work extends the study of heavy quarkonium decay to baryon production, motivated by the well-measured decay Upsilon(1S) -> J/Psi + X (CLEO, BELLE) and the observation that the perturbative production of four charm quarks in Upsilon decay is significant. The authors argue that the color-space analysis of the c-cbar-c-cbar-g system, previously applied to J/Psi production (Han 2006), also supports Xi_cc production. The calculation enables searches for Xi_cc and its SU(2) and SU(3) multi-states at BELLE2, using the existing sample of 102 x 10^6 Upsilon events from BELLE (Shen 2016) and the potential for higher luminosity at BELLE2.
