# Nondiffractive feature of $ОіN\to ПҒ^\pm N$ with $ПҒ$-meson electromagnetic multipole moments

> We investigate photoproduction of charged $ПҒ$ off the nucleon using $ПҒ(770)+ПҖ(140)$ Regge pole exchanges by considering the $ПҒ$-meson electromagnetic multipole moments. The significance of the Ward identity at the $ОіПҒПҒ$ vertex is emphasized for current conservation in the process. Given $ПҖ$ exchange with the well-known coupling constants for $ОіПҖПҒ$ and $ПҖNN$, we analyze the role of the $ПҒ$ exchange in the $Оіp\to ПҒ^+n$ and $Оіn\toПҒ^-p$ processes without model-dependences except for the magnetic moment $Ој_{ПҒ^\pm}=\pm2.01$ and electric quadrupole moment ${\cal Q}_{ПҒ^\pm}=\pm0.027$ fm$^2$ taken from theoretical estimates. The nondiffractive feature of both cross sections is reproduced with a rapid decrease beyond the resonance region by the dominance of $ПҖ$ exchange over the $ПҒ$\,. % Cross sections for differential and density matrix elements are presented to compare with existing data. The parity and photon polarization asymmetries are predicted to demonstrate the apparent roles of the $ПҒ$-meson electromagnetic multipole moments.

# Nondiffractive feature of ОіN вҶ’ ПҒВұN with ПҒ-meson electromagnetic multipole moments

## Key claim
The nondiffractive rapid decrease of the charged ПҒ photoproduction cross sections beyond the resonance region is reproduced by ПҖ exchange dominating over ПҒ exchange, but only if exchange degeneracy (EXD) between the ПҒ and a2 trajectories is broken by assigning the canonical phase to ПҒ, rather than using the usual EXD phase or a cutoff function.

## Method
The authors constructed a Regge-pole model for Оіp вҶ’ ПҒ+n and Оіn вҶ’ ПҒ-p using ПҒ(770) and ПҖ(140) exchanges in the t-channel, with the ОіПҒПҒ vertex constrained by the Ward identity to ensure current conservation. The ПҒ exchange amplitude was gauge-invariant by including nucleon pole terms and a contact term, while the ПҖ exchange used known coupling constants from the ПҒВұ вҶ’ ПҖОі decay width (68 keV) and ПҖNN coupling. The ПҒ trajectory was taken as ОұПҒ(t) = 0.9(t - mПҒВІ) + 1 and the ПҖ trajectory as ОұПҖ(t) = 0.7(t - mПҖВІ), with the ПҒ exchange assigned the canonical phase ВҪ(-1 + e^{-iПҖОұПҒ(t)}) rather than the EXD phase.

## Context
This work extends the Regge model treatment of charged ПҒ photoproduction beyond earlier studies by Clark and Donnachie (using EXD breaking) and Laget (using a cutoff function), while incorporating the Ward identity for the ОіПҒПҒ vertex that was not properly satisfied in earlier work by Clark, Joos, and Reka. The authors note that unlike the diffractive ПҒвҒ° process dominated by Pomeron exchange (Laget, 2001), charged ПҒ production proceeds via nonresonant meson exchanges. The results enable future experimental determination of the ПҒ-meson electromagnetic multipole moments through measurements of parity asymmetry PПғ and photon polarization asymmetry ОЈ, which the authors predict to be sensitive to these moments.
