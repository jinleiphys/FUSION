## Context

This work extends the JΟΦlich group's previous calculations (Hanhart 1998a, 1998b) for total cross sections and analyzing powers to spin-dependent observables, and is one of only two model calculations (alongside the Osaka group's work by Mae 1997 and Tam 1998) that consider all relevant pion production channels with higher partial waves. The predictions enable comparison with newly available data from polarized beam and polarized target experiments (Mey 1998, Mey 1999, Sah 1999, Prz 1999, Jan 2000), and the results suggest that spin correlation coefficients are well suited for testing the treatment of Έî resonance contributions at energies far below the resonance regime.
