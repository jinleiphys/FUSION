# Predictions of charged charmonium-like structures with hidden-charm and open-strange channels

> We propose the initial single chiral particle emission mechanism, with which the hidden-charm di-kaon decays of higher charmonia and charmonium-like states are studied. Calculating the distributions of differential decay width, we obtain the line shape of the $J/ψK^+$ invariant mass spectrum of $ψ_i\to J/ψK^+K^-$, where $ψ_i=ψ(4415), Y(4660)$, and $ψ(4790)$. Our numerical results show that there exist enhancement structures with both hidden-charm and open-strange decays, which are near the $D\bar{D}_s^*/D^*\bar{D}_s$ and $D^*\bar{D}_s^*/\bar{D}^*{D}_s^*$ thresholds. These charged charmonium-like structures predicted in this Letter can be accessible in future experiments, especially BESIII, BelleII, and SuperB.

## Key claim
The authors propose that charged charmonium-like structures with both hidden-charm and open-strange decays should exist near the \(D_s^{(*)}\bar{D}^{*}/\bar{D}_s^{(*)}{D}^{*}\) and \(D^*\bar{D}_s^*/\bar{D}^* D_s^*\) thresholds, observable in the \(J/\psi K^+\) invariant mass spectra of \(\psi_i\to J/\psi K^+K^-\) decays. However, the claim is conditional: these structures appear only when the initial charmonium mass exceeds 4331 MeV, and no such peak is predicted near the \(D\bar{D}_s/D_s\bar{D}\) threshold.

## Method
The authors extend the Initial Single Pion Emission (ISPE) mechanism to the Initial Single Chiral Particle Emission (ISChE) mechanism, applying it to hidden-charm di-kaon decays. They calculate triangle-loop diagrams at the hadron level using an effective Lagrangian approach with coupling constants determined in the heavy quark limit and chiral symmetry, including a form factor \(\mathcal{F}(q^2,m_E^2) = (m_E^2-\Lambda^2)/(q^2-\Lambda^2)\) with \(\Lambda = \alpha \Lambda_{QCD} + m_E\) and \(\alpha=1\). The differential decay widths \(d\Gamma/dm_{J/\psi K^+}\) are computed for \(\psi(4415)\), \(Y(4660)\), and \(\psi(4790)\) decaying to \(J/\psi K^+K^-\), considering intermediate \(D\bar{D}_s+H.C.\), \(D^{*}\bar{D}_s+D\bar{D}_s^\ast+H.C.\), and \(D^*\bar{D}_s^*+H.C.\) contributions separately.

## Key numbers
- The minimum sum of masses of the kaon, \(D^{(*)}\), and \(D_s^{(*)}\) is about 4331 MeV, setting the threshold for initial charmonium mass required for the ISChE mechanism.
- For \(Y(4660)\to J/\psi K^+K^-\) with intermediate \(D^{*}\bar{D}_s+D\bar{D}_s^\ast+H.C.\), a sharp peak structure appears near the \(D_s\bar{D}^{*}/\bar{D}_s^{*}{D}\) thresholds, with a broad reflection structure; for the \(D^*\bar{D}_s^*+H.C.\) intermediate state, a small sharp peak appears near the \(D^*\bar{D}_s^*/\bar{D}^* D_s^*\) threshold, with an obscure reflection raised at 3.7 GeV.
- For \(\psi(4790)\to J/\psi K^+K^-\) with intermediate \(D^{*}\bar{D}_s+D\bar{D}_s^\ast+H.C.\), a sharp peak near the \(D_s\bar{D}^{*}/\bar{D}_s^{*}{D}\) threshold and its reflection are found; with \(D^*\bar{D}_s^*+H.C.\) intermediate state, a sharp peak near the \(D^*\bar{D}_s^*/\bar{D}^* D_s^*\) threshold and a broad reflection are more clear than for \(Y(4660)\).
- The coupling constants used include \(g_{\psi DD}=7.44\), \(g_{\psi D^\ast D}=2.49\ \mathrm{GeV}^{-1}\), \(g_{\psi D^\ast D^\ast}=8.01\), and \(g_{D_s^\ast D K}=17.76\), derived from \(f_\psi=416\) MeV (from \(\Gamma_{J/\psi\to e^+e^-}=5.55\) keV), \(f_\pi=132\) MeV, and \(g=0.59\).

## Context
This work extends the authors' prior ISPE mechanism, previously applied to hidden-charm dipion decays (Chen et al., 2011) and used to predict the \(Z_c(3900)\) structure later observed by BESIII, to the kaon sector via the ISChE mechanism. It specifically predicts charged charmonium-like structures with both hidden-charm and open-strange decays in \(\psi(4415)\), \(Y(4660)\), and \(\psi(4790)\) decays, which are accessible at BESIII, BelleII, and SuperB. The results contrast with the smooth line shape found for the \(D\bar{D}_s\) intermediate state, and the predicted structures near \(D_s^{(*)}\bar{D}^{*}\) thresholds offer a test of the universality of the ISChE mechanism for heavy quarkonia.
