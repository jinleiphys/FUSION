# Strong CP violation in spin-1/2 singly charmed baryons

> We report on the calculation of the CP-violating form factor $F_3$ and the corresponding electric dipole moment for charmed baryons in the spin-1/2 sector generated by the QCD $θ$-term. We work in the framework of covariant baryon chiral perturbation theory within the extended-on-mass-shell renormalization scheme up to next-to-leading order in the chiral expansion.

## Key claim

The QCD θ-term induces CP-violating electric dipole moments (EDMs) in spin-1/2 singly charmed baryons, with predictions that carry a significant spread due to undetermined low-energy constants; the results are exploratory and depend on fixing several LECs from lattice QCD data for the neutron and proton EDMs.

## Method

The authors performed a one-loop calculation of the CP-violating form factor F₃(q²) and the corresponding electric dipole moments for the spin-1/2 singly charmed baryons (antitriplet and sextet multiplets) using covariant baryon chiral perturbation theory within the extended-on-mass-shell renormalization scheme up to next-to-leading order in the chiral expansion. The CP-violating source was the QCD θ-term, and the chiral Lagrangian included mesonic and baryonic sectors with the θ-term incorporated via external field methods. Tree-level and one-loop diagrams were evaluated, with unknown low-energy constants fixed from lattice QCD results for neutron and proton EDMs (Dragos 2019) and from earlier studies of baryon masses, magnetic moments, and meson-baryon couplings (Jiang 2014, Jiang 2015, Xiabng 2018).

## Key numbers

- Neutron EDM lattice data (Dragos 2019) used to fix β w₁₅ = 0.00175 GeV⁻¹
- Proton EDM lattice data used to fix β w₁₃/₁₄ = -0.00435 GeV⁻¹
- Pion decay constant Fπ = 92.2 MeV
- Symmetry-breaking LECs: bD = -0.606 GeV⁻¹, bF = -0.209 GeV⁻¹ (Borasoy 1996, Guo 2012)
- Meson-baryon couplings: g₁ = 0.98, g₂ = -0.60, g₃ = 0.85, g₄ = 1.04 (Jiang 2014, Jiang 2015)
- Magnetic moment coupling w₁₆/₁₇ = 0.40 (Kubis 2000, Meissner 1997)
- V₀⁽²⁾ = -5 × 10⁻⁴ GeV⁴, V₃⁽¹⁾ = 3.5 × 10⁻⁴ GeV² (HerreraSiklody 1997)
- Electric dipole moments for Set 2 (w'₁₀ = w'₁₃/₁₄ = w'₁₅ = 0): Λc⁺ = 0.0011 e θ₀ fm, Ξc⁺ = 0.0005 e θ₀ fm, Ξc⁰ = -0.0015 e θ₀ fm, Σc⁺⁺ = -0.0090 e θ₀ fm, Σc⁺ = -0.0049 e θ₀ fm, Σc⁰ = -0.0010 e θ₀ fm, Ξc'⁺ = -0.0097 e θ₀ fm, Ξc'⁰ = 0.0015 e θ₀ fm, Ωc⁰ = -0.0003 e θ₀ fm

## Context

This work extends earlier studies of CP-violating effects from the QCD θ-term in light baryons (Baluni 1978, Crewther 1979) and of magnetic moments of spin-1/2 singly charmed baryons in covariant chiral perturbation theory (Xiabng 2018). It also builds on the heavy baryon approach calculations for charmed and bottomed baryon masses, axial charges, and electromagnetic decays (Jiang 2014, Jiang 2015). The results provide first predictions for EDMs of singly charmed baryons induced by strong CP violation, which could be tested by future measurements at LHCb and other facilities (Aiola 2020), and may help constrain the QCD θ-term from a new sector with different systematic uncertainties than neutron and proton EDM experiments.
