# Two Contrastive Boson-Pair Coherent States in Deformed Boson Scheme

## Key claim

Although the two boson-pair coherent states $\ket{c_2(+)}$ and $\ket{c_2(-)}$ are superposed contrastively in terms of boson-pairs, the expectation values of the boson-pair operators for these states are the same as one another with high accuracy, but only for the leading terms with respect to $|c|^2$; the state $\ket{c_2(+)}$ can be extended to $m=3,4,5,\cdots$ while $\ket{c_2(-)}$ cannot.

## Method

The authors construct two types of boson-pair coherent states in a deformed boson scheme: a multiboson coherent state $\kket{c_m(+)}$ and a formally contrastive state $\kket{c_m(-)}$, both defined in a space $\tilde{B}$ spanned by orthonormal states $\kket{mn+r}$. For the case $m=2$, they map these states via a unitary operator $\mathbf{U}$ into a new space $\hat{B}$ built from a standard boson $({\hat c},{\hat c}^*)$ and a truncated operator $({\maru d},{\maru d}^*)$ obeying $({\maru d})^2=0$. They then compute expectation values of the $su(1,1)$ generators ${\hat{\mathbf{T}}}_{\pm,0}$ and the mapped boson operator ${\hat{\mathbf{c}}}^*$ for both states, deriving exact results for $\ket{c_2(-)}$ and approximate expressions in the limits $|\gamma_+|\rightarrow 0$ and $|\gamma_+|\rightarrow \infty$ for $\ket{c_2(+)}$.

## Key numbers

- The normalization factor for $\ket{c_2(+)}$ is $\Gamma_2(+) = \cosh|\gamma_+| + |\delta_+|^2 \cdot |\gamma_+|^{-1} \sinh|\gamma_+|$ (Eq. 21a).
- The normalization factor for $\ket{c_2(-)}$ is $\Gamma_2(-) = (1-4|\gamma_-|^2)^{-1/2} + |\delta_-|^2 \cdot (1-4|\gamma_-|^2)^{-3/2}$ (Eq. 21b), convergent only for $m=2$ in the region $|\gamma_-|^2 < 1/4$.
- The expectation value of ${\hat{\mathbf{T}}}_0$ for both states is $\bra{c_2(\pm)}{\hat{\mathbf{T}}}_0\ket{c_2(\pm)} = |c|^2 + |d|^2/2 + 1/4$ (Eq. 44).
- For $\ket{c_2(-)}$, the exact expectation value of ${\hat{\mathbf{T}}}_+$ is $\bra{c_2(-)}{\hat{\mathbf{T}}}_+\ket{c_2(-)} = c^*\sqrt{|c|^2+|d|^2+1/2}$ (Eq. 42).
- For $\ket{c_2(+)}$ in the region $|c|\rightarrow 0$, $\bra{c_2(+)}{\hat{\mathbf{T}}}_+\ket{c_2(+)} = c^*\sqrt{(1/3)(|c|^2+|d|^2)+1/2}$ (Eq. 43a).
- For $\ket{c_2(+)}$ in the region $|c|\rightarrow \infty$, $\bra{c_2(+)}{\hat{\mathbf{T}}}_+\ket{c_2(+)} = c^*\sqrt{|c|^2+|d|^2+|d|^4/4|c|^2}$ (Eq. 43b).

## Context

This work extends the authors' previous investigations of squeezed boson coherent states and multiboson coherent states within the $q$-deformed boson scheme (Kuriyama et al., 2001; Penson and Solomon, 1999). The state $\ket{c_2(-)}$ was previously shown (Kuriyama et al., 2001) to provide the classical counterpart of the boson-pair via the $su(1,1)$ algebra, yielding a Holstein-Primakoff type boson representation upon requantization. The present paper demonstrates that $\ket{c_2(+)}$, despite its contrastive superposition structure, reproduces the same expectation values with high accuracy and has the advantage of being extendable to higher $m$ values where $\ket{c_2(-)}$ fails. The authors suggest applying $\ket{c_2(+)}$ to the Lipkin model, building on their earlier prototype work (Kuriyama et al., 2001).
