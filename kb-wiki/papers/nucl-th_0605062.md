# Strangeness s = -6 dibaryon

> The structure of $(ΩΩ)_{0^+}$ dibaryon with strangeness $s=-6$ is studied in the extended chiral SU(3) quark model, in which vector meson exchange dominates the short range interaction. The resonating group method (RGM) is adopted, in which the $ΩΩ$ and $CC$ (hidden color) channels are involved. The color screening effect and the effects of mixing of scalar mesons on $(ΩΩ)_{0^+}$ are also investigated.

## Key claim

The paper claims that the $(\Omega\Omega)_{0^+}$ dibaryon with strangeness $s=-6$ is always a bound state in the extended chiral SU(3) quark model, regardless of which parameter set is used, due to its unique symmetry property; however, the binding energy varies substantially (from about 15 MeV to 158 MeV) depending on the choice of scalar meson mixing angle and model parameters.

## Method

The authors employed the resonating group method (RGM) to solve a coupled-channel calculation for the $(\Omega\Omega)_{0^+}$ system, including both the $\Omega\Omega$ channel and the hidden color (CC) channel. The extended chiral SU(3) quark model was used, in which vector meson exchange (rather than one gluon exchange) dominates the short-range quark-quark interaction, with parameters fitted to reproduce the deuteron binding energy and baryon mass splittings. The effects of color screening (using an error-function-like confining potential) and different scalar meson mixing angles were also investigated.

## Key numbers

- Binding energy of $(\Omega\Omega)_{0^+}$ in the chiral SU(3) quark model: 170.9 MeV, with rms radius 0.62 fm.
- Binding energy in the extended chiral SU(3) quark model, set I: 135.6 MeV, rms radius 0.60 fm.
- Binding energy in the extended chiral SU(3) quark model, set II: 158.0 MeV, rms radius 0.59 fm.
- With ideal scalar meson mixing ($\theta^{S}=35.264^\circ$) in the extended chiral SU(3) quark model: binding energy 15.9 MeV, rms radius 0.85 fm.
- With scalar meson mixing $\theta^{S}=-18^\circ$ in the extended chiral SU(3) quark model: binding energy 75.6 MeV, rms radius 0.65 fm.
- With color screening (error-function confinement) in the extended chiral SU(3) quark model, set I: binding energy 126.1 MeV; set II: 149.1 MeV.
- Hidden color channel coupling changes the binding energy by at most 0.1 MeV (e.g., from 135.6 to 135.7 MeV for set I).

## Context

This work extends prior studies by Zhang et al. (2000) who first predicted the $(\Omega\Omega)_{0^+}$ dibaryon as a deeply bound state in the chiral SU(3) quark model. The authors previously extended that model to include vector meson exchange (Dai et al., 2003) and here apply it to the dibaryon system, comparing with earlier work on the d* dibaryon by Yuan et al. and Dai et al. (2005) where hidden color channels had significant effects. The results enable future experimental searches for this dibaryon in heavy ion collisions, as previously suggested by Zhang et al., and motivate further theoretical work to fit KN, NN, and YN scattering data simultaneously with a unified parameter set.
