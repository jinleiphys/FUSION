# Dilepton production in pp and np collisions at 1.25 GeV

> The inclusive reactions $pp \rightarrow e^+ e^- X$ and $np \rightarrow e^+ e^- X$ at the laboratory kinetic energy of 1.25 GeV are investigated in a model of dominance of nucleon and $Œî$ resonances. Experimental data for these reactions have recently been reported by the HADES Collaboration. ## Context
