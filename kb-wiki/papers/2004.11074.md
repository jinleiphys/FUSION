# Thresholds of ultraperipheral processes

> Threshold behavior of the cross sections of ultraperipheral nuclear interactions is studied. Production of $e^+e^-$ and $μ^+μ^-$ pairs as well as $π^0$ and parapositronium is treated. The values of corresponding energy thresholds are presented and the total cross sections of these processes at the newly constructed NICA and FAIR facilities are estimated.

# Thresholds of ultraperipheral processes

## Key claim
Ultraperipheral production of muon pairs and dimuons is energetically impossible at the NICA and FAIR facilities because their thresholds exceed the available collision energies; only electron-positron pairs (including parapositronium) and neutral pions may be observable, but the cross-section estimates depend on the ultraperipherality parameter \(u\), which is uncertain to within a factor of about 1.5.

## Method
The author uses the equivalent photon approximation, convolving the photon flux from each nucleus (Eq. 1) with the two-photon cross section for the final state (e.g., Eq. 3 for muon pairs, Eq. 5 for resonances). The ultraperipherality parameter \(u\), which sets the maximum transverse momentum and thus the threshold, is taken from prior estimates for Pb and Au ions (\(u \approx 0.02\)) and for protons (\(u \approx 0.2\)). Total cross sections are then evaluated at the design energies of NICA and FAIR.

## Key numbers
- Threshold for unbound muon pair production: \(\sqrt{s_{nn,\text{thr}}} \approx 15\) GeV (from Eq. 7 with \(u \approx 0.02\) and \(m_\mu\)).
- Total cross section for parapositronium (\(m_{Ps} = 1.022\) MeV, \(\Gamma_{Ps} = 5.1 \times 10^{-12}\) MeV): about 22 mb at NICA and 19 mb at FAIR.
- Total cross section for \(\pi^0\) (\(m_{\pi^0} = 135\) MeV, \(\Gamma_{\pi^0} = 7.5 \times 10^{-6}\) MeV): about 2.3 \(\mu\)b at NICA, and very close to threshold at FAIR.
- Ultraperipherality parameter for PbPb (and AuAu): \(u_{PbPb} \approx 0.02\); for pp: \(u_{pp} \approx 0.2\).
- Design energies: NICA AuAu-mode \(\sqrt{s_{nn}} = 9\) GeV, pp-mode 12.5 GeV; FAIR \(\sqrt{s_{nn}} = 7\) GeV.

## Context
This work extends the study of ultraperipheral processes, previously measured at RHIC and LHC (e.g., Adam et al., Chatrchyan et al., Abbas et al., Khachatryan et al., Aaboud et al., ATLAS and CMS collaborations), to the lower-energy facilities NICA and FAIR. It builds on the equivalent photon approximation formulated by Fermi (1924) and Weizsacker and Williams (1934), and on prior threshold estimates by Vysotsky et al. (2017) for the ultraperipherality parameter. The results enable experimental searches for monochromatic photons from parapositronium decay (0.511 MeV) and \(\pi^0\) decay (67.5 MeV) at these colliders, testing the validity of the theoretical approach at low energies.
