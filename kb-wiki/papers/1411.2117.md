# The masses and axial currents of the doubly charmed baryons

> The chiral dynamics of the doubly heavy baryons is solely governed by the light quark. In this work, We have derived the chiral corrections to the mass of the doubly heavy baryons up to N$^3$LO. The mass splitting of $Ξ_{cc}$ and $Ω_{cc}$ at the N$^2$LO depends on one unknown low energy constant $c_7$. With the experimental mass of $Ξ_{cc}(3520)$ as the input, we estimate the mass of $Ω_{cc}$ to be around 3.678 GeV. Moreover, we have also performed a systematical analysis of the chiral corrections to the axial currents and axial charges of the doubly heavy baryons. The chiral structure and analytical expressions will be very useful to the chiral extrapolations of the future lattice QCD simulations of the doubly heavy baryons.

# The masses and axial currents of the doubly charmed baryons

## Key claim

The mass of the doubly charmed baryon Ωcc is estimated to be around 3.726 GeV, but this prediction depends on assuming a specific value of the unknown low energy constant c7 = -0.2 GeV⁻¹, which cannot be uniquely determined from current lattice data; any c7 in the range (-6.0, 0.6) fits the available lattice data equally well.

## Method

The authors construct chiral effective Lagrangians for doubly charmed baryons (Ξcc⁺⁺, Ξcc⁺, Ωcc⁺) interacting with pseudoscalar mesons up to O(p⁴), then perform a non-relativistic reduction using heavy baryon chiral perturbation theory. They calculate chiral corrections to baryon masses up to next-to-next-to-next-to-leading order (N³LO) and to axial currents up to next-to-next-to-leading order (N²LO), fitting the unknown low energy constants to lattice QCD data from Alexandrou (2012) for Ξcc masses at various pion masses and charm quark masses.

## Key numbers

- The mass of Ξcc is predicted to be 3.665 GeV with errors +0.093/-0.097 GeV, using the best fit with c7 = -0.2 GeV⁻¹
- The mass of Ωcc is predicted to be 3.726 GeV with errors +0.093/-0.097 GeV, using the same fit with c7 = -0.2 GeV⁻¹
- The axial charge g^{1+i2}_{Ξcc⁺⁺Ξcc⁺} is 1.15 and g^{4+i5}_{Ξcc⁺⁺Ωcc⁺} is 1.18, taking λ = 3.6 GeV for the unknown constants h₂ and h₃
- The pion, kaon, and eta decay constants used are Fπ0 = 0.092 GeV, FK0 = 0.112 GeV, and Fη0 = 0.110 GeV
- The axial coupling gA is taken as 0.6, determined from fitting the D*⁺ width by Hu and Mehen (2005)

## Context

This work extends chiral perturbation theory calculations previously applied to singly heavy baryons by Guo (2008) and Jiang (2014) to the doubly charmed baryon sector. The mass predictions for Ξcc (3.51-3.67 GeV) and Ωcc (3.68-3.76 GeV) from lattice QCD groups such as Alexandrou (2012), Lewis (2001), and Na (2008) are used as comparison and fitting input. The derived chiral expressions are intended to enable future chiral extrapolations of lattice QCD simulations of doubly heavy baryons, and the axial current results may be tested through future semileptonic decay measurements of doubly charmed baryons.
