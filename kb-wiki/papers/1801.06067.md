# The radiative corrections to double-Dalitz decays revisited

> In this study, we revisit and complete the full next-to-leading order corrections to pseudoscalar double-Dalitz decays within the soft-photon approximation. Comparing to the previous study, we find small differences, which are nevertheless relevant for extracting information about the pseudoscalar transition form factors. Concerning the latter, these processes could offer the opportunity to test them-for the first time-in their double-virtual regime

## Key claim

The authors revisit and complete the full next-to-leading order (NLO) radiative corrections to pseudoscalar double-Dalitz decays within the soft-photon approximation, finding small differences compared to the previous study by Barker et al. (2002) that are nevertheless relevant for extracting information about the pseudoscalar transition form factors (TFFs), particularly because these processes could offer the first opportunity to test TFFs in their double-virtual regime.

## Method

The authors compute the full NLO corrections to the decays \(P\to \bar\ell\ell \bar\ell'\ell'\) for pseudoscalar mesons, including vacuum polarization, vertex corrections, and the previously missing three-point, four-point, and five-point loop amplitudes, using the soft-photon approximation for bremsstrahlung to cancel infrared divergencies. The results are expressed in terms of Passarino-Veltman scalar integrals, with the transition form factor effects reduced to a sum of massive-photon propagators, and numerical integration is performed using the CUBA library and checked with Mathematica's NIntegrate routine.

## Key numbers

- \(\Gamma_{4\ell}/\Gamma_{2\gamma}\) for \(\pi^0\to 4e\) (constant TFF, total): \(3.4196(4) \times 10^{-5}\)
- \(\Gamma_{4\ell}/\Gamma_{2\gamma}\) for \(K_L\to 4e\) (constant TFF, total): \(6.2219(7) \times 10^{-6}\)
- \(\Gamma_{4\ell}/\Gamma_{2\gamma}\) for \(K_L\to 2e2\mu\) (constant TFF, total): \(2.8589(3) \times 10^{-6}\)
- \(\Gamma_{4\ell}/\Gamma_{2\gamma}\) for \(K_L\to 4\mu\) (constant TFF, total): \(0.9375(1) \times 10^{-9}\)
- \(\Gamma_{4\ell}/\Gamma_{2\gamma}\) for \(\eta\to 4e\) (constant TFF, total): \(6.4610(7) \times 10^{-5}\)
- \(\Gamma_{4\ell}/\Gamma_{2\gamma}\) for \(\eta\to 2e2\mu\) (constant TFF, total): \(3.9961(4) \times 10^{-6}\)
- \(\Gamma_{4\ell}/\Gamma_{2\gamma}\) for \(\eta\to 4\mu\) (constant TFF, total): \(6.0739(10) \times 10^{-9}\)

## Context

This work extends the partial NLO analysis of Barker et al. (2002) by including the previously missing three-point, four-point, and five-point loop amplitudes, completing the full NLO radiative corrections to pseudoscalar double-Dalitz decays. The results enable more precise extraction of double-virtual transition form factors from experimental data, which are relevant for predicting the hadronic light-by-light contribution to the anomalous magnetic moment of the muon, and could provide the first experimental test of TFFs in their double-virtual regime.
