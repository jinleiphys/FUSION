# Exploring the chiral magnetic effect in isobar collisions through Chiral Anomaly Transport

> We investigate the signal of the chiral magnetic effect (CME) in Au+Au collisions and isobar collisions of $_{44}^{96}\text{Ru}+\rm{} _{44}^{96}Ru$ and $_{40}^{96}\text{Zr}+\rm{}_{40}^{96}Zr$ in the newly developed chiral anomaly transport (CAT) module based on the state-of-the-art model a multiphase transport (AMPT). Our numerical simulation results for the ratio charge correlation $Δγ$ in Ru+Ru and Zr+Zr collisions are close to the latest experimental data. The simulation shows that the CME signal is larger in Ru+Ru collisions than that in Zr+Zr collisions, while the background is smaller, and the upper limit of the CME signal is $15\%$ in the isobar collisions.

## Key claim

The chiral anomaly transport (CAT) module, based on the AMPT model, shows that the chiral magnetic effect (CME) signal is larger in Ru+Ru collisions than in Zr+Zr collisions while the background is smaller, and the upper limit of the CME signal in isobar collisions is 15%. This result is conditional on the specific Woods-Saxon parameter sets used to model nuclear structure, and the centrality definition used in the simulation.

## Method

The authors developed a chiral anomaly transport (CAT) module that replaces the ZPC parton cascade in the AMPT model with a chiral kinetic theory framework incorporating magnetic fields and chirality imbalance. The initial magnetic field from spectator protons is calculated using the HIJING initial conditions, and its time evolution follows a Lorentzian decay with lifetime tau = 0.4 fm/c. The chiral chemical potential is set as mu_5 = +/- (2.1T + sqrt(eB)) GeV, and the parton cascade dynamically generates charge separation along the magnetic field, which is then transferred to hadrons through quark coalescence and hadron rescattering.

## Key numbers

* The initial magnetic field ratio between Ru+Ru and Zr+Zr collisions is approximately eB_y,Ru ≈ 1.1 eB_y,Zr for all cases of isobar collisions at 200 GeV.
* The upper limit of the CME signal in isobar collisions is 15%.
* The magnetic field results in a 1-3% difference in elliptic flow v2 between Ru+Ru and Zr+Zr collisions.
* The magnetic field lifetime is tau = 0.4 fm/c.
* The chiral chemical potential is parameterized as mu_5 = +/- (2.1T + sqrt(eB)) GeV at 200 GeV collision energy.

## Context

This work extends the AMPT-based approach of Ma (2011) by replacing the ad hoc initial charge separation with a self-consistent chiral kinetic theory evolution. It directly addresses the STAR Collaboration (2021) blind analysis results from isobar collisions, which were inconsistent with earlier predictions by Voloshin (2010) and Deng (2016). The CAT module enables future studies of CME signals in other collision systems and can be used to disentangle nuclear structure effects from the CME signal in isobar collisions.
