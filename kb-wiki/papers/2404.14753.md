# Investigating $Ξ$ resonances from pentaquark perspective

> We have investigated the $qss\bar{q}q$ ($q = u$ or $d$) system to find possible pentaquark explanations for the $Ξ$ resonances. The bound state calculation is carried out within the framework of the quark delocalization color screening model. The scattering processes are also studied to examine the possible resonance states. The current results indicate that the $Ξ(1950)$ can be interpreted as $Λ\bar{K}^*$ state with $J^P = 1/2^-$. Three states are identified that match the $Ξ(2250)$, which are $Σ^* \bar{K}^*$ state with $J^P = 3/2^-$, $Σ^* \bar{K}^*$ state with $J^P =5/2^-$, and $Ξ^* ρ$ state with $J^P =5/2^-$. This may explain the conflicting experimental values for the width of the $Ξ(2250)$. A new $Ξ$ resonance is predicted, whose mass and width are 2066--2079 MeV and 186--189 MeV, respectively. These results contribute to understanding the nature of the $Ξ$ resonances and to the future search for new $Ξ$ resonances. Moreover, it is meaningful to further investigate the $Ξ$ resonances from an unquenched picture on the basis of pentaquark investigation.

## Key claim

The paper proposes that the $\Xi(1950)$ resonance can be interpreted as a $\Lambda \bar{K}^*$ pentaquark state with $J^P = 1/2^-$, and that three distinct states (two $\Sigma^* \bar{K}^*$ states and one $\Xi^* \rho$ state) may all correspond to the $\Xi(2250)$, potentially explaining its conflicting experimental width values.

## Method

The authors performed bound-state and scattering calculations for the $qss\bar{q}q$ ($q = u$ or $d$) pentaquark system using the quark delocalization color screening model (QDCSM). They employed the resonating group method (RGM) and generating coordinates method to solve the five-quark Schrödinger equation, and studied hadron-hadron scattering phase shifts to identify resonance states by locating where phase shifts rise through $\pi/2$.

## Key numbers

- $\Xi(1950)$ interpreted as $\Lambda \bar{K}^*$ state with $J^P = 1/2^-$
- Three states identified matching $\Xi(2250)$: $\Sigma^* \bar{K}^*$ with $J^P = 3/2^-$, $\Sigma^* \bar{K}^*$ with $J^P = 5/2^-$, and $\Xi^* \rho$ with $J^P = 5/2^-$
- Predicted new $\Xi$ resonance with mass range 2066--2079 MeV and width range 186--189 MeV

## Context

This work extends previous pentaquark studies using the QDCSM that successfully described $\Lambda_c$ baryons (Yan 2022) and $\Omega_c$ baryons (Yan 2023). It contrasts with traditional three-quark interpretations of $\Xi$ resonances from quark models (Capstick 1986, Glozman 1997) and with molecular state explanations from the one-boson-exchange model (Chen 2019) and chiral unitary approaches (Ramos 2002, Gamermann 2011). The results enable future experimental searches for new $\Xi$ resonances and further investigation of $\Xi$ states from an unquenched pentaquark perspective.
