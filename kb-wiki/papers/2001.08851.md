# Comparative analysis of $Ï‰p$, $Ï•p$, and $J/Ïˆp$ scattering lengths from A2, CLAS, and GlueX threshold measurements

> The high accuracy $Ï•$-meson photoproduction data from the CLAS experiment in Hall~B of Jefferson Laboratory allow us to determine the near-threshold total cross section of the $Î³p\toÏ•p$ reaction and use it for evaluating the $Ï•p$ scattering length $Î±_{Ï•p}$. These data result in an absolute value of $|Î±_{Ï•p}| = (0.063\pm 0.010)$~fm, which is smaller than the typical hadron size. A comparative analysis of $Î±_{Ï•p}$ with the previously determined scattering lengths for $Ï‰p$ and $J/Ïˆp$ from the A2 and GlueX experiments is performed.

## Key claim

The absolute value of the $\phi p$ scattering length extracted from CLAS near-threshold photoproduction data using the vector-meson dominance (VMD) model is $|\alpha_{\phi p}| = (0.063\pm 0.010)$~fm, which is smaller than the typical hadron size and intermediate between the much larger $\omega p$ scattering length and the much smaller $J/\psi p$ scattering length.

## Method

The authors determined the total cross section $\sigma_t$ for $\gamma p\to\phi p$ near threshold by fitting the CLAS differential cross sections from Dey (2014) with a Legendre polynomial expansion up to $j=6$ over $\cos\theta$ from -0.80 to 0.93. They then fit the resulting $\sigma_t$ as a function of the center-of-mass momentum $q$ using a series of odd powers ($b_1 q + b_3 q^3 + b_5 q^5$) and extracted the linear coefficient $b_1$. Using the VMD model, the scattering length was obtained as $|\alpha_{Vp}| = R_V \sqrt{b_1}$, where $R_V$ is a kinematic factor derived from the vector-meson electromagnetic decay width.

## Key numbers

- $|\alpha_{\phi p}| = (0.063 \pm 0.010)$~fm, extracted from CLAS $\gamma p\to\phi p$ data near threshold using VMD
- $|\alpha_{\omega p}| = (0.820 \pm 0.030)$~fm, from A2 $\omega$-meson photoproduction data (Strakovsky 2014)
- $|\alpha_{J/\psi p}| = (0.00308 \pm 0.00055)$~fm (statistical) $\pm 0.00042$~fm (systematic), from GlueX $J/\psi$ data (Strakovsky 2019)
- Linear coefficient $b_1 = (3.40 \pm 1.15) \times 10^{-4}$~$\mu$b/(MeV/c) from the fit of CLAS $\phi$ total cross sections
- $\Gamma_{\phi\to e^+e^-} = (1.27 \pm 0.04)$~keV used for the VMD coupling constant
- $R_\phi = (343.0 \pm 5.4)$~MeV$^{1/2}$ and $h_{\phi p} = (0.000184 \pm 0.000032)$~fm/MeV$^{1/2}$

## Context

This work extends the method previously applied to $\omega p$ scattering from A2 data (Strakovsky 2014) and $J/\psi p$ scattering from GlueX data (Strakovsky 2019) to the $\phi$ meson using CLAS data (Dey 2014). The resulting hierarchy $|\alpha_{J/\psi p}| \ll |\alpha_{\phi p}| \ll |\alpha_{\omega p}|$ shows a strong exponential increase of the scattering length with inverse vector-meson mass, reflecting a weakening of the interaction from light-quark to strange-quark to charm-quark configurations. The extracted $|\alpha_{\phi p}|$ is much smaller than the value of 0.15~fm from LEPS coherent $\phi$ photoproduction from deuterons (Chang 2007) and the value of $\simeq$2.37~fm from a QCD van der Waals potential model (Gao 2000), indicating the proton is more transparent to $\phi$ mesons than predicted by that model.
