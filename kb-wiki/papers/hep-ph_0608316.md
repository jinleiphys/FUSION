# Dynamical study of ${\bf QQ-\bar u \bar d}$ mesons

> It has been recently conjectured by Selem and Wilczek \cite{Sel06} the existence of a $ss-[\bar u \bar d]$ meson due to strong correlations between the two light antiquarks. We make a detailed study of this system within a dynamical quark model which has proven to be successful in reproducing the most important features of low-energy hadron phenomenology. Our results, obtained within a parameter-free calculation, show that the antidiquark component of the $ss \bar u \bar d$ system indeed entails the stronger attraction, and drives its energy much lower than the $\bar{N}Ξ$ threshold, but still above the $\bar{K^0} {K^*}^-$ or $\bar{{K^*}^0} K^-$ thresholds. We have also studied the $cc \bar u \bar d$ and $bb \bar u \bar d$ systems. Exotic mesons are only expected to exist in the limit of large mass for the two-quark subsystem, $bb\bar u \bar d$, since the calculated mass is below the $\bar{B^0} {B^*}^-$ or $\bar{{B^*}^0} B^-$ thresholds.

# Dynamical study of ${\bf QQ-\bar u \bar d}$ mesons

## Key claim

The antidiquark component of the $ss\bar u \bar d$ system provides the strongest attraction and drives its energy much lower than the $\bar N \Xi$ threshold, but the system remains above the $\bar K^0 K^{*-}$ or $\bar K^{*0} K^-$ thresholds; exotic mesons of this type are only expected to exist for the $bb\bar u \bar d$ system, where the calculated mass falls below the $\bar B^0 B^{*-}$ or $\bar B^{*0} B^-$ thresholds.

## Method

The authors performed a parameter-free variational calculation within a constituent quark model that includes chiral boson-exchange potentials (sigma, pion, kaon, eta) for light quarks, one-gluon-exchange (OGE) potential, and a screened confining potential. They constructed a trial wave function as a linear combination of generalized Gaussians in Jacobi coordinates, including all relevant color-flavor-spin components, and solved for the ground-state energy of the $QQ\bar u \bar d$ four-body system for $Q = s, c, b$ by comparing the eigenvalues with the corresponding two-meson and two-baryon physical thresholds.

- The $ss\bar u \bar d$ system in the full calculation (antidiquark plus nondiquark mixing) lies above the $\bar K^0 K^{*-}$ or $\bar K^{*0} K^-$ thresholds (explicit numerical mass values not given in the truncated text).

## Context

This work extends the conjecture by Selem and Wilczek (2006) of a strongly correlated $ss-[\bar u \bar d]$ meson by providing a dynamical calculation within a constituent quark model previously successful for baryon spectra and baryon-baryon interaction (Rep05) and meson spectra (Vij05). The results contradict the expectation of a bound $ss\bar u \bar d$ state, finding it unbound relative to two-meson thresholds, and support stability only for the $bb\bar u \bar d$ system. The study enables future investigation of heavy pentaquark systems such as $[ud][ud]\bar b$, and suggests that if a light $ss\bar u \bar d$ state were observed decaying to baryon-antibaryon channels, it would indicate a dynamical correlation mechanism beyond the present model.
