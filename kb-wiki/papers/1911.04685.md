## Context

This work extends the long-standing program of testing CKM unitarity using semileptonic decays, following the dispersion relation approach of Seng (2018) and the CMS calculation of Czarnecki (2019) that found 2-3Пѓ deviations from unitarity in super-allowed nuclear beta decays. The paper introduces R_V as a vector-current analog to the well-established axial-vector ratio R_A from Marciano (2004), providing a complementary probe that cancels different systematic uncertainties. The results call for new experimental measurements of pion beta decay with factor 2-3 improvement in precision, and encourage lattice gauge theory studies to resolve the 2.2Пѓ tension between the vector and axial-vector determinations of Vus/Vud.
