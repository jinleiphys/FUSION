# Soft-collinear resummation in deeply virtual Compton scattering

> We derive an all order resummation formula for the deeply virtual Compton scattering (DVCS) amplitude, which takes into account soft gluon exchanges in the non-singlet quark coefficient function. We identify the ladder diagrams responsible in light-like gauge for [alphaS log^2 (x +/- xi)]^n contributions. The resummed series results in a simple closed expression.

## Key claim
The paper derives an all-order resummation formula for the deeply virtual Compton scattering (DVCS) amplitude that accounts for soft gluon exchanges in the non-singlet quark coefficient function, but the final resummed expression is not uniquely determined because the matching condition used to fix the argument of the logarithms requires going beyond double logarithmic accuracy.

## Method
The authors work in a light-like gauge where the amplitude is dominated by ladder diagrams. They expand momenta in the Sudakov basis and identify the dominant kinematics through strong ordering in both longitudinal and transverse momenta. Using dimensional regularization and Cauchy integration over Sudakov parameters, they compute the nested integrals that yield the leading double-logarithmic contributions at each order, then sum the series to obtain a closed form.

## Key numbers
- The one-loop coefficient function contains terms of order \(\frac{\alpha_S C_F}{4\pi(x-\xi+i\varepsilon)} \log^2\left(\frac{\xi-x}{2\xi}-i\epsilon\right)\) (from Eq. C1)
- The n-loop ladder contribution to the coefficient function is \(I_n = -\frac{4}{x-\xi+i\epsilon}\frac{(2\pi i)^n}{(2n)!}\log^{2n}\left(\frac{\xi-x}{2\xi}-i\epsilon\right)\) (Eq. In-final)
- The resummed series yields \(\sum_{n=0}^\infty K_n = \frac{e_q^2}{x-\xi+i\epsilon} \cosh\left[ D \log\left( \frac{\xi-x}{2\xi} - i\epsilon \right) \right]\) with \(D = \sqrt{\frac{\alpha_s C_F}{2\pi}}\) (Eq. Sumn-final)
- Numerical plots use \(\xi=0.05\), \(\alpha_s=0.33\) (corresponding to \(Q=2\) GeV and \(\Lambda_{QCD}=0.2\) GeV)

## Context
This work extends the collinear factorization framework for DVCS established by the authors cited as "historyofDVCS" and "review", building on the NLO coefficient function derived by Pire et al. (2011). The resummation addresses the region near \(x = \pm \xi\) where double-logarithmic terms \(\alpha_s \log^2(x\pm\xi)\) become large. The result enables stabilized perturbative predictions for DVCS observables such as Compton form factors, and the authors note that a related expression should apply to exclusive meson electroproduction and timelike Compton scattering (MPSW). Future work is needed to include running \(\alpha_s\) effects, gluon GPD contributions important at small \(\xi\), and to reformulate the resummation in terms of conformal Mellin moments.
