# Chiral Lagrangians with decuplet baryons to one loop

> We construct the relativistic chiral Lagrangians with decuplet baryons up to the order $\mathcal{O}(p^4)$ (one loop). For the meson-decuplet-decuplet couplings, there are 1, 13, 55, and 548 terms in the $\mathcal{O}(p^1)$-$\mathcal{O}(p^4)$ order Lagrangians, respectively. For the meson-octet-decuplet Lagrangians, the number of independent terms from $\mathcal{O}(p^1)$ to $\mathcal{O}(p^4)$ are 1, 5, 67, and 611, respectively. For convenience of applications, the $πΔΔ$ and $πNΔ$ chiral Lagrangians are picked out. This new form of $Δ$ Lagrangians is equivalent to the original isovector-isospinor one and we establish relations between these two forms.

## Key claim

The paper constructs the complete and minimal set of relativistic chiral Lagrangians with decuplet baryons up to order O(p⁴) (one loop), covering both SU(3) (full decuplet) and SU(2) (Δ only) cases, and establishes explicit equivalence relations between the new tensor-representation Lagrangians and the earlier isovector-isospinor form for Δ baryons.

## Method

The authors use the Rarita-Schwinger vector-spinor formalism for spin-3/2 fields, representing decuplet baryons as a totally symmetric flavour tensor T_{abc} (a,b,c = 1,2,3 for SU(3); a,b,c = 1,2 for SU(2)). They construct chirally invariant monomials from building blocks (meson fields, external sources, baryon fields and their covariant derivatives) and reduce them to a minimal independent set using linear relations from partial integration, equations of motion, Bianchi identity, Cayley-Hamilton relations, Schouten identities in flavour space, and Fierz transformations for SU(2) and SU(3). The parameter A in the free Rarita-Schwinger Lagrangian is absorbed via a point transformation to obtain A-independent low-energy constants.

## Key numbers

- For meson-decuplet-decuplet couplings: 1 term at O(p¹), 13 terms at O(p²), 55 terms at O(p³), and 548 terms at O(p⁴).
- For meson-octet-decuplet couplings: 1 term at O(p¹), 5 terms at O(p²), 67 terms at O(p³), and 611 terms at O(p⁴).
- The lowest-order πΔΔ coupling constant e₁⁽¹⁾ is related to the earlier formalism constant g₁ by e₁⁽¹⁾ = (1/2) g₁.
- The lowest-order πNΔ coupling constant f₁⁽¹⁾ is related to g_{πNΔ} by f₁⁽¹⁾ = (1/√2) g_{πNΔ}.

## Context

This work extends the systematic construction of chiral Lagrangians with decuplet baryons, which had previously been done only fragmentarily for specific problems (e.g., Jenkins (1991), Hemmert (1997), Jiang (2017) for Δ only). It provides the complete one-loop-order Lagrangians needed for consistent calculations of octet/decuplet baryon masses, electromagnetic form factors, meson-baryon scattering, and transitions between decuplet and octet states, including applications to dibaryon studies as in Haidenbauer (2017). The new tensor form for Δ Lagrangians is equivalent to the earlier isovector-isospinor form of Jiang (2017), and the explicit relations between the two formalisms are established.
