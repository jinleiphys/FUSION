# Production of charmed baryon $Λ_c(2940)^+$ at PANDA

> In this work we evaluate the production rate of the charmed baryon $Λ_c(2940)^+$ at PANDA. For possible assignments of $Λ_c(2940)^+$: $J^P=1/2^\pm$, $3/2^\pm$ and $5/2^\pm$, the total cross section of $p\bar{p}\to \barΛ_c Λ_c(2940)^+$ is estimated, which may exceed 1 nb. With the designed luminosity ($2\times10^{-32}$cm$^{-2}$/s) of PANDA, our estimate indicates that ten thousand events per day if $Λ_c(2940)^+$ is of $J^P=1/2^+$ or $10^8$ per day if it is of $J^P=5/2^+$ can be expected. Those values actually set the lower and upper limits of the $Λ_c(2940)^+$ production. In addition, we present the Dalitz plot and carry out a rough background analysis of the $Λ_c(2940)^+$ production in the $p\bar{p}\to D^0 p\barΛ_c$ and $p\bar{p}\to Σ_c^{0,++}π^{+,-}\barΛ_c$ processes, which would provide valuable information for accurate determination of the $Λ_c(2940)^+$ identity.

## Key claim

The production rate of the charmed baryon Λ_c(2940)^+ at PANDA is estimated for all possible spin-parity assignments (J^P = 1/2^±, 3/2^±, 5/2^±), with the total cross section of p anti-p → anti-Λ_c Λ_c(2940)^+ potentially exceeding 1 nb, but the actual event rate depends strongly on the unknown J^P assignment, ranging from 10^4 events per day (for J^P = 1/2^+) to 10^8 events per day (for J^P = 5/2^+).

## Method

The authors used an effective Lagrangian approach to calculate the cross section for p anti-p → anti-Λ_c Λ_c(2940)^+ via D^0 meson exchange, with coupling constants g_{J^P} determined by assuming a partial decay width Γ(Λ_c(2940)^+ → D^0 p) = 1.5 MeV. They considered all six possible J^P assignments for Λ_c(2940)^+ (1/2^±, 3/2^±, 5/2^±) and included a monopole form factor with cutoff Λ = 3 GeV, as well as an initial-state interaction (ISI) suppression factor of about 10. They also performed Dalitz plot and background analyses for the sequential decays p anti-p → anti-Λ_c D^0 p and p anti-p → anti-Λ_c π^- Σ_c^{++}.

## Key numbers

- Mass of Λ_c(2940)^+: m = 2939.8 ± 1.3 (stat) ± 1.0 (syst) MeV (BaBar) and m = 2938.0 ± 1.3 +2.0/-4.0 MeV (Belle)
- Width of Λ_c(2940)^+: Γ = 17.5 ± 5.2 (stat) ± 5.9 (syst) MeV (BaBar) and Γ = 13 +8+27/-5-7 MeV (Belle)
- Designed luminosity of PANDA: 2 × 10^32 cm^-2/s, corresponding to integrated luminosity ~10^4 nb^-1 per day
- Coupling constants for Λ_c(2940)^+ → D^0 p (assuming Γ = 1.5 MeV): g_{1/2^-} = 0.26; g_{1/2^+} = 1.25; g_{3/2^-} = 5.26 GeV^-1; g_{3/2^+} = 1.10 GeV^-1; g_{5/2^-} = 4.23 GeV^-2; g_{5/2^+} = 20.19 GeV^-2
- Cross section of p anti-p → anti-Λ_c Λ_c(2940)^+ for J^P = 5/2^+ is larger than for J^P = 1/2^- by a factor of ~10^4
- Background cross section of p anti-p → Λ_c anti-Λ_c → anti-Λ_c D^0 p is about 10 nb, far larger than the signal for J^P = 1/2^+ assignments
- Expected events per day at PANDA: 10^4 (for J^P = 1/2^+) to 10^8 (for J^P = 5/2^+), assuming 50% overall efficiency and branching ratio of Λ_c(2940)^+ → D^0 p ~ 0.1

## Context

This work extends previous theoretical studies of Λ_c(2940)^+ structure, which have proposed it as either a D^*N molecular state (He et al. 2006; Dong et al. 2009, 2010, 2011) or a conventional charmed baryon (Capstick et al. 1986; Cheng et al. 2006; Ebert et al. 2007; Valcarce et al. 2008), by evaluating its production in the antiproton-proton collisions at PANDA rather than in e^+e^- collisions. The results enable future PANDA experiments to distinguish between different J^P assignments of Λ_c(2940)^+ through the measured production rates and Dalitz plot distributions, and complement parallel studies of charmonium-like states such as X(3872) and Z^+(4430) at PANDA (Chen et al. 2008; Ke et al. 2008).
