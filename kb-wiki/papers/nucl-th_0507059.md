# Charm Production in $\bar pA$-Collisions at the Charmonium Threshhold

> We discuss the production of charmonium states in antiproton-nucleus collisions at the $ψ'$ threshold. It is explained that measurements in $\bar p A$ collisions will allow to get new information about the strengths of the inelastic $J/ψN$ and $ψ'N$ interaction, on the production of $Λ_c$ and $\bar{D}$ in charmonium-nucleon interactions and for the first time about the nondiagonal transitions $ψ' N\to J/ψN$. The inelastic $J/ψ$-nucleon cross section is extracted from the comparison of hadron-nucleus collisions with hadron-nucleon collisions. Predictions for the ratio of $J/ψ$ to $ψ'$ yields in antiproton-nucleus scatterings close to the threshold of $ψ'$ production for different nuclear targets are presented.

## Key claim

Measurements of charmonium production in antiproton-nucleus collisions at the ψ′ threshold will allow new information about the inelastic J/ψN and ψ′N interaction strengths, the production of Λc and D̄ in charmonium-nucleon interactions, and for the first time the nondiagonal transition ψ′N → J/ψN, provided that formation time effects are negligible at this low energy.

## Method

The author uses a semiclassical Glauber-approximation to compute cross sections for ψ′ production and subsequent J/ψ production in antiproton-nucleus collisions at the ψ′ threshold. The model accounts for the absorption of the antiproton, the production of ψ′ only on protons (not neutrons) near threshold, the inelastic rescattering of the ψ′ and J/ψ in the nucleus, and the nondiagonal transition ψ′N → J/ψN. Predictions are made for the ratio of J/ψ to ψ′ yields for different nuclear targets (O, S, Cu, W, Pb) using five parameter sets that vary the inelastic and nondiagonal cross sections.

## Key numbers

- In the “normal” parameter set: inelastic antiproton-nucleon cross section σp̄N inel = 50 mb; inelastic ψ′ cross section σψ′N inel = 7.5 mb; inelastic J/ψ cross section σψN inel = 0 mb; nondiagonal transition cross section σ(ψ′N → ψN) = 0.2 mb.
- In the “ψ-absorption” set: σψN inel = 3.1 mb.
- In the “large ψ′ absorption” set: σψN inel = 15 mb.
- In the “small nondiagonal” set: σJ/ψN→ψ′ = 0.1 mb.
- In the “large nondiagonal” set: σJ/ψN→ψ′ = 0.4 mb.
- The Λc energy is below the threshold for the process p + Λc → N + N + D, which would require 4.2 GeV in the proton rest frame, while the Λc has on average less than 3 GeV.

## Context

This work extends the author’s previous study (Gerland, 2005) and is motivated by the future antiproton-nucleus experiment at GSI (GSI, 2005). At higher SPS energies, formation time effects complicate the measurement of genuine charmonium-nucleon cross sections (Spieles, 1998; Bratkovskaya, 2004), and at RHIC and LHC energies charmonium states are produced only far outside the nucleus (Gerland, 2002). The proposed low-energy measurements at the ψ′ threshold avoid these formation-time complications and enable the first extraction of the nondiagonal ψ′N → J/ψN cross section, as well as providing a method to form charmed hypernuclei via Λc production.
