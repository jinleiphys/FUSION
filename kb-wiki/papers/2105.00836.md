# On the structure in the $О›N$ cross section at the $ОЈN$ threshold

> The complexity of threshold phenomena is exemplified on a prominent and long-known case - the structure in the $О›p$ cross section (invariant mass spectrum) at the opening of the $ОЈN$ channel. The mass splitting between the $ОЈ$ baryons together with the angular momentum coupling in the $^3S_1$-$^3D_1$ partial wave imply that, in principle, up to six channels are involved. ## Context

This work extends the long-standing study of threshold phenomena in the О›p system, first observed by Dahl (1961) and Tan (1969), and later investigated in reactions such as pp в†’ KвЃєО›p by ElSamad (2013), Roder (2013), Hauenstein (2017), and Munzer (2018), and in О›p correlation functions by the ALICE Collaboration (2021).
