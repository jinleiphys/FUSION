## Context

This work extends the chiral coupled-channel approach of Lutz (2004) for K‚ĀĽp scattering to photoproduction reactions, offering a way to test K‚ĀĽp amplitudes below the K‚ĀĽN threshold where the ő£(1385) and őõ(1405) resonances dominate. The approach contrasts with earlier electroproduction data from DESY (Azemoon, 1970s) that could not separate the two resonances, and with the model of Nacher (1999) which neglected the ő£(1385) resonance.
