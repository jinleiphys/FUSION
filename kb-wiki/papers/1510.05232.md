# Anatomy of molecular structures in $^{20}$Ne

> We present a beyond mean-field study of clusters and molecular structures in low-spin states of $^{20}$Ne with a multireference relativistic energy density functional, where the dynamical correlation effects of symmetry restoration and quadrupole-octupole shapes fluctuation are taken into account with projections on parity, particle number and angular momentum in the framework of the generator coordinate method. Both the energy spectrum and the electric multipole transition strengths for low-lying parity-doublet bands are better reproduced after taking into account the dynamical octupole vibration effect. Consistent with the finding in previous studies, a rotation-induced dissolution of the $α+^{16}$O molecular structure in $^{20}$Ne is predicted.

## Key claim

The rotation-induced dissolution of the α+¹⁶O molecular structure in ²⁰Ne is predicted, and the authors conjecture that this phenomenon is partially attributed to a special deformation-dependent moment of inertia governed by the underlying shell structure, though a further dedicated study of the moment of inertia is required to address this issue.

## Method

The authors performed a beyond-mean-field calculation using a multireference relativistic energy density functional (PC-PK1) with the generator coordinate method, mixing quantum-number projected quadrupole-octupole shaped configurations. Symmetry restoration was implemented via projections onto parity, particle number, and angular momentum, and the reference states were generated from deformation-constrained self-consistent relativistic mean-field calculations in the (β₂, β₃) deformation plane. The method included dynamical correlations from shape fluctuations and used Pfaffian techniques to avoid sign problems in norm overlaps.

## Key numbers

- The mean-field global energy minimum for ²⁰Ne is found at a reflection-symmetric prolate shape with deformation parameters (β₂ = 0.5, β₃ = 0).
- On the projected 0⁺ energy surface, the minimum occurs at deformation parameters (β₂ = 0.7, β₃ = 0.5).
- The GCM calculation used N_GCM = 54 reference states covering the (β₂, β₃ ≥ 0) deformation plane.
- The numbers of mesh points for the rotation angle β and gauge angle φ were N_β = 14 and N_φ = 7, respectively.
- The strength parameters of the pairing force were V_n = −349.5 MeV fm³ for neutrons and V_p = −330.0 MeV fm³ for protons.

## Context

This work extends previous studies of cluster structures in ²⁰Ne using angular-momentum projected Hartree-Fock (Lee 1972), resonating group method (Matsuse 1975), 5α generator coordinate method (Nemoto 1975), and antisymmetrized molecular dynamics (Kanada-En'yo 1995; Taniguchi 2004; Kimura 2004), which all predicted that the reflection-asymmetric α+¹⁶O molecular structure weakens with increasing spin. The present study provides a fully microscopic beyond-mean-field treatment that reproduces both the energy spectrum and electric multipole transition strengths for low-lying parity-doublet bands, and it enables future investigations of nuclear α-decay dynamics in a full microscopic framework.
