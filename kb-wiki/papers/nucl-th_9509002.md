# Nuclear muon capture by 3He: meson exchange currents for the triton channel

> Exchange current corrections are calculated using currents found from the hard-pion model and AV14+3BF wavefunctions. Results are given for the rate and spin observables. Their sensitivity to g_P, the nucleon pseudoscalar form factor, is reported.

## Key claim

Exchange current corrections to nuclear muon capture by ³He leading to the ³H final state, calculated using the hard-pion model and AV14+3BF wavefunctions, bring the theoretical capture rate into agreement with experiment; without these currents the rate is 12% smaller than observed.

## Method

The authors calculated meson exchange current (MEC) corrections to the weak nuclear current using the hard-pion model, employing non-relativistic operators that satisfy the nuclear continuity equation (PCAC) at order 1/M² for the axial part and CVC at order 1/M for the vector part. They used realistic three-nucleon wavefunctions from the coupled rearrangement channel (CRC) method with the AV14 NN potential plus three-body force, and parameterized the ³H → ³He weak nuclear current by six Q-dependent current amplitudes (ρ₀, ρ₁, j_Q, j_σ, j_×⁽¹⁾, j_×⁽²⁾) from which the statistical capture rate and analyzing powers were derived.

## Key numbers

- Capture rate with MEC included: 1502 ± 32 per second
- Without exchange currents, the rate is 12% smaller than experiment
- Analyzing power A_v = 0.515 ± 0.005
- Analyzing power A_t = -0.375 ± 0.004
- Analyzing power A_Δ = -0.110 ± 0.006
- Three-momentum transfer Q = 103.22 MeV
- Energy transfer Q₀ = 2.44 MeV
- Gamow-Teller matrix element contributions from Δ-excitation currents ANP4 and ANP5: +0.052 and -0.022 respectively (using parameters matching Carlson et al.)
- The Gamow-Teller matrix element comparison used f_πNN²/4π = 0.079, f_πΔN²/4π = 0.2275, g_A(0) = -1.262, g_ρNN²/4π = 0.5, κ_V = 6.6, G₁ = 3.06, with Λ_π = 0.90 GeV and Λ_ρ = 1.35 GeV
- The ratio of the trion isovector magnetic moment matrix element ⟨³H||3I₃⁻||³He⟩ = 0.9998, indicating good isospin symmetry
- D-state probability in trion wavefunctions: ~9%
- P-state probability: <0.2%

## Context

This work extends the elementary particle model (EPM) analysis of Congleton and Fearing (1994) for muon capture on ³He by incorporating explicit meson exchange current effects using the hard-pion model currents of Truhlik and Khanna (1992), which were previously applied to muon capture on deuterons by Adam et al. (1991). The results show that including weak axial and vector π-MEC in microscopic calculations yields predictions closely matching the EPM, and the sensitivity of observables to the induced pseudoscalar coupling g_P makes this reaction a potential precision probe of g_P comparable to capture on a free proton. The calculation enables improved extraction of g_P from future experimental data on ³He muon capture.
