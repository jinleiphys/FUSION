# Anti-collinear resummation in JIMWLK evolution in the linear regime

> The recently-proposed resummation procedure for anti-collinear logarithms in the JIMWLK kernel~\cite{Kovner:2023vsy} is studied in the linear (BFKL) regime in the fixed-coupling approximation. Simple closed form expressions for the resummed momentum space kernel and characteristic function $χ(n,γ)$ are found. We find that the anti-collinear pole in the leading order characteristic function at $γ=1$ disappears, and instead $χ(γ=1)=\frac{12}{11}\fracπ{α_sN_c}$ for $n_F=0$. Comparison with the known NLO BFKL eigenvalue, with the target-Bjorken limit ($Q_T\gg Q_P$) of the $γ^*(Q_P)+γ^*(Q_T)$-scattering amplitude and with the ``all-poles'' resummation prescription are presented.

## Key claim

The anti-collinear resummation procedure for the JIMWLK kernel, previously proposed in Kovner (2023), is studied in the linear (BFKL) regime under the fixed-coupling approximation; the leading-order BFKL pole at γ=1 disappears and is replaced by a finite value χ(γ=1) = (12/11)(π/α_s N_c) for n_F=0, though this result depends on the specific choice of the transverse resolution scale and the fixed-coupling approximation.

## Method

The authors linearize the resummed JIMWLK Hamiltonian to quadratic order in the Reggeized gluon field, obtaining a resummed BFKL kernel in coordinate space. They then Fourier transform this kernel to momentum space and derive a closed-form expression for the characteristic function χ(n,γ) of the anti-collinearly resummed BFKL equation, using the scale choice Q²_⋆(x,y,z) = max(|x−z|⁻², |y−z|⁻²) and a smooth variant thereof. The resummed characteristic function is compared with the known NLO BFKL eigenvalue, with the target-Bjorken limit of γ*γ* scattering, and with the "all-poles" resummation prescription.

## Key numbers

- The anti-collinear pole at γ=1 in the leading-order characteristic function disappears; instead χ(γ=1) = (12/11)(π/α_s N_c) for n_F=0.
- The resummation does not affect χ(γ) close to the collinear pole at γ=0.
- The fixed-coupling approximation is employed, with α_s taken at a fixed scale μ² unrelated to coordinates; this is justified as running coupling in the resummation equations is formally an NNLO effect.

## Context

This work extends the anti-collinear resummation procedure introduced in Kovner (2023) for the JIMWLK kernel, which resums DGLAP-type transverse logarithms in the anti-collinear regime (Q_T ≫ Q_P). The present paper studies this resummation in the dilute (weak-field) limit where the evolution becomes linear and is given by the BFKL equation, building on the foundational BFKL works (BFKL1, BFKL2, BFKL3) and NLO BFKL corrections (NLO-BFKL, NLO Ciafaloni 1, NLO Ciafaloni 2). The results enable future work on running-coupling effects in the resummed JIMWLK kernel and on the generalization to resum both collinear and anti-collinear logarithms simultaneously.
