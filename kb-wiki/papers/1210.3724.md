# Diffractive and deeply virtual Compton scattering in holographic QCD

> We further analyze the holographic dipole-dipole scattering amplitude developed in arXiv:1202.0831, 1205.3223. Gribov diffusion at strong coupling yields the scattering amplitude in a confining background. We compare the holographic result for the differential cross section to proton-proton and deeply virtual Compton scattering data.

## Key claim
The holographic dipole-dipole scattering amplitude, based on Gribov diffusion at strong coupling in a confining background, can describe both proton-proton diffraction and deeply virtual Compton scattering data, but only after fitting the effective proton size and IR cutoff parameters to the pp data; the hard-wall cutoff is a crude approximation that requires a smooth-wall background for full consistency.

## Method
The authors use the holographic dipole-dipole scattering amplitude from Basar and Zahed (2012) and Stoffers and Zahed (2012), where a non-critical closed string exchange in transverse AdS$_3$ with a hard-wall IR cutoff yields an eikonalized amplitude. The differential cross section for elastic pp scattering and DVCS is computed via a Fourier transform of the eikonal amplitude, with the proton and photon wave functions approximated as delta functions in the holographic coordinate $u = -\ln(z/z_0)$. The parameters $z_p$ (effective proton dipole size) and $z_0$ (IR cutoff) are fitted to CERN ISR pp data at $\sqrt{s} = 20-60$ GeV, then tested against LHC TOTEM data at $\sqrt{s}=7$ TeV and HERA DVCS data at $\sqrt{s}=40-100$ GeV.

## Key numbers
- t'Hooft coupling $\lambda = 23$, fitted through the slope of the proton structure function $F_2$ from DIS data.
- For ISR pp data fit: $z_0 = 2$ GeV$^{-1}$, $z_p = 3.3$ GeV$^{-1}$, normalization $\mathcal{N}_p = 0.16$.
- For LHC TOTEM data fit: $\kappa = 3.75$, $z_p = 3.1$ GeV$^{-1}$, $z_0 = 1.5$ GeV$^{-1}$ (compared to ISR values $\kappa=2.5$, $z_p=3.3$ GeV$^{-1}$, $z_0=2$ GeV$^{-1}$).
- For DVCS fit: $z_p = 3.3$ GeV$^{-1}$, $z_0 = 2$ GeV$^{-1}$, normalization $\mathcal{N}_{\gamma^*\gamma} = 0.00016$.
- Pomeron intercept $\alpha_{\bf P} = 1 + D_\perp/12$ with $D_\perp = 3$, diffusion constant ${\bf D} = 1/(2\sqrt{\lambda})$.
- Slope parameter $B(s) \sim {\bf D} \chi (z_p^2 + z_0^2)$ at $|t|=0$ GeV$^2$.

## Context
This work extends the holographic dipole-dipole scattering model of Basar and Zahed (2012) and Stoffers and Zahed (2012), which was previously compared only to inclusive DIS data, to exclusive diffractive processes (pp and DVCS). It builds on the dipole picture used by Shoshi (2002), Kowalski (2006), and others, and on holographic pomeron studies by Brower (2006, 2007), Hatta (2007), and Cornalba (2008, 2010). The results enable further tests of holographic QCD in exclusive processes and motivate the use of smooth-wall backgrounds (Karch, 2006) to improve the IR description.
