## Context

This work extends the NLO shear viscosity calculation of Ghiglieri et al. (2018) from zero chemical potential to finite baryon chemical potential, building on the leading-log finite-Œº results of Danhoni and Moore (2022). The results cannot be directly compared to lattice QCD at finite Œº due to the sign problem, but can be used phenomenologically to model the (Œº,T) behavior of Œ∑/s, as was recently done using leading-log results by Danhoni and Moore (2024). The perturbative results serve as a limiting behavior for interpolation between high-density perturbative, low-density hadron-resonance gas, and intermediate density data-driven estimates.
