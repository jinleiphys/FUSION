# Updated three-body model of $^6$He $ОІ$ decay into the $Оұ$ + $d$ continuum

> The $ОІ$-decay process of the $^6$He halo nucleus into the $Оұ+d$ continuum is studied in an updated three-body model. The $^6$He nucleus is described as an $Оұ+n+n$ system in hyperspherical coordinates on a Lagrange-mesh. The shape and absolute values of the transition probability per time and energy units of new experiments are reproduced with a modified $Оұ+d$ potential. ## Context

This work updates the three-body model of Tursunov, Baye, and Descouvemont (2006) to describe new experimental data from the ISOLDE collaboration (Raabe 2009) and the REX-ISOLDE facility (Pfutzner 2015), which showed a 20% larger total transition probability than the earlier theoretical prediction. The paper confirms the cancellation mechanism between internal and halo parts of the Gamow-Teller matrix element first explained by Baye, Suzuki, and Descouvemont (1994), and suggests that the effective integral I_E(R) can provide a link between phenomenological three-body models and future ab initio calculations, which have studied вҒ¶He (Quaglioni 2016) and Оұ + d scattering (Hupin 2015) but may struggle with the delicate cancellation and the extended halo up to about 20 fm.
