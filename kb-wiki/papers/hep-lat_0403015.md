# Nucleon Masses and Magnetic Moments in a Finite Volume

> We compute finite-size corrections to nucleon masses and magnetic moments in a periodic, spatial box of size L, both in QCD and in partially-quenched QCD.

## Key claim

The paper computes the leading finite-size corrections to nucleon masses and magnetic moments in a periodic spatial box of size L using baryon chiral perturbation theory (χPT) including the Δ resonance as an explicit degree of freedom, with the caveat that the effective field theory may be reaching the limits of its validity when the bound L ≳ 2 fm is saturated, particularly when pions are light.

## Method

The author uses baryon chiral perturbation theory with explicit Δ degrees of freedom to compute one-loop corrections to nucleon masses and magnetic moments, then generalizes the loop integrals from infinite volume to a finite periodic box of size L by quantizing momenta as p = 2πn/L. The finite-size corrections are expressed as differences between the summed and integrated loop contributions, evaluated using a master formula involving modified Bessel functions, with results given for both QCD and partially-quenched QCD including strong isospin breaking.

## Key numbers

- The hierarchy requires fL ≫ 1 and (mπL)²(fL)² ≫ 1, implying mπL ≳ 1; for pion masses 139 MeV < mπ < 300 MeV, this gives L ≳ 2 fm
- Finite-volume effects tend to be small for L > 4 fm, so the relevant range is 2 fm < L ≤ 4 fm
- The asymptotic formula for the nucleon mass finite-size correction is δ^A_L M_N = (9g_A² mπ²)/(8πf²) · (1/L) exp(-mπ L) + (4g_ΔN² mπ^{5/2})/((2π)^{3/2} f²) · (1/(Δ L^{1/2})) exp(-mπ L)
- The asymptotic formula for the magnetic moment finite-size correction is δ_L μ = -[ (M_N g_A² mπ)/(2π f²) (1 - 2/(mπ L)) + (4 M_N g_ΔN² mπ^{3/2})/(9(2π)^{3/2} f²) · (1/(Δ L^{1/2})) ] exp(-mπ L) τ̂₃
- The asymptotic formula is not accurate for L < 10 fm even with heavy pions; for mπ L ≳ 1 one must include corrections of O(exp(-|n| mπ L)) with |n| > 1
- Parameters used: f = 132 MeV, g_A = 1.26, g_ΔN = 1.4

## Context

This work extends previous calculations of finite-volume dependence in the baryon sector, which had computed the nucleon mass dependence to O(mπ⁴) in baryon χPT without including the Δ as an explicit degree of freedom (Khan 2003), and where some discussion of Δ effects appeared in AliKhan (2002). The paper also provides the first complete expressions for finite-size corrections to nucleon magnetic moments and extends all results to partially-quenched QCD, enabling lattice QCD simulations to extrapolate simultaneously in quark masses, lattice spacing, and lattice size when they encounter the chiral regime.
