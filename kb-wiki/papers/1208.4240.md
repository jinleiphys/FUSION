# Line shape of ПҲ(3770) in e^+e^-\to D\bar D

> Interference phenomena observed in the $ПҲ(3770)$ resonance region in the reactions $e^+e^-$ $\to$ $ D\bar D$ are analyzed. To avoid ambiguities in the determination of the $ПҲ(3770)$ resonance parameters, when analyzing data between the $D\bar D$ and $D\bar DПҖ$ thresholds, the amplitudes satisfying the elastic unitarity requirement should be used. In the lack of information on the $P$ wave of $D\bar D$ elastic scattering, the $ПҲ(3770)$ parameters, determined by fitting the $e^+e^-$ $\to $ $D\bar D$ data, can essentially depend on the model used for the total contribution of the resonance and background. The selection of the models can be toughened by comparing their predictions with the relevant data on the shape of the $ПҲ(3770)$ peak in the non-$D\bar D$ channels $e^+e^-$ $\to $ $ОіПҮ_{c0}$, $J/ПҲО·$, $П•О·$, etc.

# Line shape of ПҲ(3770) in eвҒәeвҒ» вҶ’ D DМ…

## Key claim

The ПҲ(3770) resonance parameters extracted from eвҒәeвҒ» вҶ’ D DМ… data depend strongly on the model used for the sum of resonance and background contributions because information on the P-wave D DМ… elastic scattering amplitude is unavailable; models that satisfy elastic unitarity can describe the data but yield very different resonance parameters, and the selection between models can be tightened by comparing predictions for non-D DМ… channels such as eвҒәeвҒ» вҶ’ ОіПҮc0, J/ПҲО·, and ПҶО·.

## Method

The authors constructed models for the isoscalar part of the D meson electromagnetic form factor FвҒ°D that satisfy elastic unitarity in the region between the D DМ… and D DМ…ПҖ thresholds. They fitted these models to the combined eвҒәeвҒ» вҶ’ D DМ… cross section data from BES, CLEO, BABAR, and Belle (87 data points between 3.72 and 3.9 GeV), using a resonance-plus-background model and a model including both ПҲ(3770) and ПҲ(2S) resonances with their mutual mixing through D DМ… intermediate states. The resulting form factors were then used to predict cross sections for eвҒәeвҒ» вҶ’ ОіПҮc0, J/ПҲО·, and ПҶО· at a single energy point for comparison with CLEO measurements.

## Context

This work extends earlier analyses by the KEDR Collaboration (2011-2012) who found two very different solutions for interfering resonance and background amplitudes that are indistinguishable by ПҮВІ, and by BES, CLEO, BABAR, and Belle who observed an unusual ПҲ(3770) peak shape with a deep dip near 3.81 GeV. The authors build on the coupled-channel model of Eichten, Gottfried, Kinoshita, Lane, and Yan (1978) which predicted a zero in the form factor. The analysis enables future studies using high-statistics data from CLEO-c (818 pbвҒ»В№) and BES III (2.9 fbвҒ»В№) to discriminate between models, and suggests that measurements of ПҲ(3770) in non-D DМ… channels and in weak B decays or photoproduction can further constrain the resonance parameters.
