# Phenomenology of the polarized cross-sections of the rho meson leptoproduction at high energy

> We present a model for the polarized cross-sections of the hard diffractive leptoproduction of rho meson in the high energy limit. Our model is based on the light-cone collinear factorization of the virtual photon to rho meson impact factor when using the impact factor representation of the helicity amplitudes of the rho meson leptoproduction. This gauge invariant treatment when expressed in impact parameter space, leads to the factorization on one hand of the color dipole scattering amplitude and on the other hand of the distribution amplitudes of the rho meson up to twist 2 and 3. We show that the results of this approach are in good agreement with HERA data for virtualities above ~5 GeV^2.

## Key claim

The model, based on light-cone collinear factorization of the virtual photon to rho meson impact factor expressed in impact parameter space, reproduces HERA data for polarized cross-sections of rho meson leptoproduction only for virtualities above approximately 5 GeV², with discrepancies at lower Q² attributed to higher twist corrections.

## Method

The authors compute helicity amplitudes T₀₀ and T₁₁ for hard diffractive rho meson leptoproduction in the perturbative Regge limit (W² ≫ Q² ≫ Λ²_QCD) using an impact factor representation that factorizes the color dipole scattering amplitude from the rho meson distribution amplitudes up to twist 2 and 3. The impact factors for longitudinally and transversely polarized rho mesons are expressed in impact parameter space, where the dipole cross-section is taken from a model fitted to inclusive deep inelastic scattering data (Albacete 2010), and the rho meson distribution amplitudes are taken from Ball, Braun, Koike and Tanaka (1998). The b-slope parameter for the t-dependence of the differential cross-section is taken from H1 experimental measurements.

## Key numbers

- The model agrees with H1 data for virtualities above approximately 5 GeV².
- The dipole cross-section model used has a χ²/dof of approximately 1.2 when fitted to inclusive and longitudinal structure functions of deep inelastic scattering.
- The renormalization scale for the full twist 3 result for σ_T and the twist 2 result for σ_L is chosen as μ² = (Q² + m²_ρ)/4.

## Context

This work extends the light-cone collinear factorization approach for rho meson leptoproduction developed in Anikin (2009, 2010) and Anikin (2011) by expressing the impact factors in impact parameter space, which allows inclusion of saturation effects via the color dipole picture. The model enables future generalization to other vector mesons (e.g., phi meson) and to studies of the transverse distribution of gluons in the target by incorporating impact parameter dependence in the dipole models.
