## Context

This work extends previous covariant and gauge-invariant calculations of nucleon-nucleon bremsstrahlung by Kaptari and Kampfer (2008) to include low-lying nucleon resonances beyond the О” isobar. The results are relevant for interpreting di-electron data from the DLS and HADES collaborations, and the authors note that earlier transport models (e.g., Bratkovskaya et al., 2008) that used their bremsstrahlung cross sections successfully described the DLS and HADES data, resolving the so-called "DLS puzzle." The paper enables more accurate modeling of di-electron production in heavy-ion collisions by providing elementary cross sections that include resonance contributions and their interference with bremsstrahlung.
