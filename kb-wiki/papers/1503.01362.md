## Context

This work addresses the "proton radius puzzle" arising from the discrepancy between the proton charge radius extracted from muonic hydrogen Lamb shift measurements (Pohl 2010, Antognini 2013) and from electronic hydrogen spectroscopy (Mohr 2012) or electron-proton elastic scattering (Bernauer 2010, Bernauer 2013). It proposes a new experimental approach complementary to the MUSE experiment (Gilman 2013), which plans simultaneous őľp and ep elastic scattering measurements, and to ongoing low-Q¬≤ ep scattering experiments (Mihovilovic 2014, Gasparian 2014).
