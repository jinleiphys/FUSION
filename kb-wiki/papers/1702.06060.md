# Photoproduction of $η$ and $η'$ Mesons on Proton

> New high-precision total and differential cross sections for $η$ and $η'$ photoproduction on the proton obtained by the A2 Collaboration at the Mainz Microtron are presented. The data for $η$ photoproduction demonstrate a cusp at the energy W$\sim$1.9~GeV. Furthermore, we present a new version of the $η$MAID model for $η$ and $η'$ photoproduction. The model includes 23 nucleon resonances parametrized with Breit-Wigner shapes. The background is described by vector and axial-vector meson exchanges in the $t$ channel using the Regge phenomenology. Parameters of the resonances were obtained from a fit to preliminary data of the A2 Collaboration at MAMI and available data from other collaborations. The cusp is explained as a threshold effect due to the opening $η' p$ decay channel of the $N(1895)1/2^-$ resonance.

## Key claim

The cusp observed in the total cross section of $\eta$ photoproduction on the proton at W~1.9 GeV is explained as a threshold effect from the opening of the $\eta' p$ decay channel of the $N(1895)1/2^-$ resonance, which has only a 2-star overall status according to the PDG review.

## Method

The authors performed a phenomenological partial-wave analysis using the updated $\eta$MAID-2016 isobar model, which includes 23 nucleon resonances parametrized with Breit-Wigner shapes and a background described by vector and axial-vector meson exchanges in the $t$ channel using Regge phenomenology with Regge cuts. The model was fitted to published data from CBELSA/TAPS, CLAS, GRAAL, and preliminary high-statistics differential cross section data from the A2 Collaboration at MAMI for both $\gamma p \to \eta p$ and $\gamma p \to \eta' p$ reactions.

## Key numbers

- Total and differential cross sections for $\gamma p \to \eta p$ and $\gamma p \to \eta' p$ cover the energy region from threshold up to W = 2.8 GeV.
- Polarization observables for $\gamma p \to \eta p$ cover up to W = 1.85 GeV for T and F, 2.13 GeV for E, and 1.91 GeV for $\Sigma$.
- The model includes 23 nucleon resonances, with 5 giving only small contributions.
- The Regge background uses a mass scale parameter $s_0 = 1\;\mathrm{GeV}^2$.
- The obtained solution describes data up to $E_{\gamma} = 8$ GeV.
- The cusp in the $\gamma p \to \eta p$ total cross section appears at W ~ 1.9 GeV, corresponding to the $\eta'$ threshold.

## Context

This work extends the $\eta$MAID-2003 model (MAID, 2003) and the $\eta$MAID-2015 version by replacing pole-like Feynman propagators with Regge propagators and increasing the number of nucleon resonances from 8 to 23. The new model improves upon previous analyses such as SAID-GE09 and BG2014-2 (BnGa, 2012) by explaining the cusp structure in $\eta$ photoproduction and providing a unified description of both $\eta$ and $\eta'$ photoproduction channels. The results enable improved baryon spectroscopy for the $N(1895)1/2^-$ resonance and provide a framework for analyzing future polarization data.
