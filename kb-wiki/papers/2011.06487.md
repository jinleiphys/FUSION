# Charmonium decay in the C3P0 Model

> In this work, we use the $C^3P_{\,0}$ model to calculate the decay widths of the low lying charmonium $J^{PC}=1^{--}$ states, nominally $J/ПҲ(1S)$ and $ПҲ(2S)$, in the following common channels: $ПҒ\,ПҖ$, $Пү\,О·$, $Пү\,О·^\prime$, $K^{\ast +}\,K^-$, $K^{\ast 0}\,\bar{K}^0$, $П•\,О·$, $П•\,О·^\prime$.

## Key claim

The CВіPвӮҖ model, which incorporates bound-state corrections to the leading-order ВіPвӮҖ pair-creation mechanism via the Fock-Tani formalism, can reproduce the OZI-suppressed decay widths of J/ПҲ(1S) and ПҲ(2S) into light meson channels, but only when a flavor-mixing scheme is introduced to account for the small cМ„c components in the light vector and pseudoscalar mesons.

## Method

The authors start from a microscopic qqМ„ pair-creation Hamiltonian and apply the Fock-Tani unitary transformation to obtain an effective meson decay Hamiltonian. The leading-order term reproduces the standard ВіPвӮҖ model, while next-to-leading-order terms introduce bound-state corrections via the bound-state kernel О”, which modifies the quark-antiquark interaction strength. Decay widths are computed from the resulting CВіPвӮҖ amplitudes combined with relativistic phase space, using harmonic-oscillator spatial wave functions and a flavor-mixing scheme for Пү, ПҶ, О·, О·вҖІ, and J/ПҲ.

## Context

This work extends the CВіPвӮҖ model, previously applied to ПҶ meson decays (da Silva et al., 2020, Physical Review C), into the charmonium sector. The approach improves upon the leading-order ВіPвӮҖ model (Barnes et al., various) by including bound-state corrections that modify the pair-creation strength; for ПҶ mesons these corrections reduced the fit quality parameter R. The results serve as a baseline for future studies of charmed meson spectroscopy at the PANDA experiment at FAIR, particularly for searches for exotic states such as the cМ„cg charmonium hybrid and multiquark states like ПҮc1(3872) and Zc(4430).
