# $蠅- 蟺纬^*$ transition form factor in proton-proton collisions

> Dalitz decays of $蠅$ and $蟻$ mesons, $蠅\to 蟺^0纬^*\to蟺^0 e^+e^-$ and $蟻^0\to 蟺^0纬^*\to蟺^0 e^+e^-$, produced in $pp$ collisions are calculated within a covariant effective meson-nucleon theory. We argue that the $蠅$ transition form factor $F_{蠅\to 蟺^0纬^*}$ is experimentally accessible in a fairly model independent way in the reaction $pp \to pp 蟺^0 e^+ e^-$ for invariant masses of the $蟺^0 e^+ e^-$ subsystem near the $蠅$ pole. Numerical results are presented for the intermediate energy kinematics of envisaged HADES experiments.

## Key claim

The authors argue that the 蠅 meson transition form factor \(F_{\omega\to\pi^0\gamma^*}\) can be extracted from the reaction \(pp \to pp \pi^0 e^+ e^-\) in a fairly model-independent way, but only for invariant masses of the \(\pi^0 e^+ e^-\) subsystem near the 蠅 pole, where the contribution from 蟻 meson Dalitz decays is sufficiently small.

## Method

The cross section for di-electron production from Dalitz decays of 蠅 and 蟻 mesons in \(pp\) collisions was calculated within a covariant effective meson-nucleon theory using phenomenological Lagrangians for \(NN\sigma\), \(NN\pi\), \(NN\rho\), and \(NN\omega\) interactions. The amplitude was factorized into a part describing vector meson production in \(NN\) interactions and a part describing the Dalitz decay vertex, allowing partial analytic integration over the leptonic and decay phase spaces. The calculations were performed for a beam kinetic energy of 2.2 GeV, corresponding to the kinematics of the HADES experiment, with final state interactions among nucleons treated via the Jost function formalism.

## Key numbers

- Beam kinetic energy: \(T_{beam} = 2.2\) GeV, chosen to match the HADES proposal.
- 蠅 meson total width: \(\Gamma_\omega = (8.49 \pm 0.08)\) MeV; 蟻 meson total width: \(\Gamma_\rho = (146.5 \pm 1.5)\) MeV.
- Branching ratios: \(\omega \to \pi^0\gamma\) = \((8.9^{+0.27}_{-0.23}) \times 10^{-2}\); \(\rho \to \pi^0\gamma\) = \((6.1 \pm 0.8) \times 10^{-4}\).
- Coupling constants extracted: \(f_{\omega\pi^0\gamma} \simeq 0.72\) GeV\(^{-1}\); \(f_{\rho\pi^0\gamma} \simeq 0.25\) GeV\(^{-1}\).
- The extracted form factor via the proposed ratio method differs from the input form factor by less than 0.5% when only resonant 蠅 and 蟻 decays contribute.
- Background contributions dialed to \(\sim 10\%\) at the 蠅 peak can cause interference corrections up to 55% in the maximum cross section.

## Context

This work extends previous studies of vector meson production in nucleon-nucleon reactions (Kaptari and Kampfer, earlier works on 蠅 and 蠁 production) and addresses the discrepancy between earlier experimental measurements of the 蠅 transition form factor (LandPhysRep, omegaFrmf1, omegaFrmf2, omegaFrmf3) and theoretical predictions from vector meson dominance models (Faessler, Thomas, Meissner) and dispersion relation calculations (anisovich_IE, omegaFormfNormaliz). The paper enables a direct experimental determination of the 蠅 transition form factor using the HADES detector at SIS18/GSI Darmstadt, which can detect di-electrons in \(pp\) collisions with high efficiency over a wide kinematical range.
