# Exclusive $П•$ production in proton-proton collisions in the resonance model

> The exclusive $П•$ meson production in proton-proton reactions is calculated within the resonance model. ## Context

This work extends the resonance model, previously applied to ПЂ, О·, ПЃ, П‰, and ПЂПЂ production in NN collisions (Teis; Fuchs03), to П† meson production. It contradicts the naive OZI rule estimate (OZI) and explains the large discrepancy observed experimentally (DISTO; Blo75; Bal77; Are82; Gol97) without additional parameters. The model enables further tests by comparison of invariant mass distributions of the proton-П† system once experimental data become available.
