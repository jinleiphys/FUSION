# Deformed Boson Scheme in Time-Dependent Variational Method. II

> In this paper as a continuation of Part I, the case of two kinds of boson operators is treated. The deformation of the coherent states for the su(2)- and the su(1,1)-algebra and their related deformed algebras are discussed in various forms including the most popular form.

## Key claim

This paper extends the deformed boson scheme from Part I to systems with two kinds of boson operators, showing that the deformation of coherent states for the su(2)- and su(1,1)-algebra can be characterized by at most three independent functions (algebraically two), and that by choosing these functions appropriately one can derive various deformed algebras including the standard su(2)_q-algebra with the form [x]_q = (q^x - q^{-x})/(q - q^{-1}).

## Method

The authors start from the Schwinger boson representation of su(2)- and su(1,1)-spin generators in terms of two boson operators (â, â*) and (b̂, b̂*). They deform the conventional coherent states |c_±⁰⟩ by inserting three functions f̃(N_a), g̃(N_b), and h̃(N_b) into the exponential operators, then show that the term involving h̃ commutes with all generators and can be set to unity, leaving two independent deformation functions f(x) and g(x). The deformed operators γ̂ and δ̂ are defined via similarity transformation by Ω_±(N_a, N_b) = f(N_a) g(N_b)^(∓1), and their commutation relations are derived and shown to reduce to the classical Poisson brackets under the replacement of finite differences by derivatives.

## Key numbers

- The normalization constant for the deformed coherent state is given by Γ = Σ_{m,n} (|γ|^(2m) |δ|^(2n))/(m! n!) (n!/(n∓m)!)^(±1) f(m)² g(n∓m)^(-2) (g(n)·h(n)^(-1))^(±2)
- The expectation values of boson number operators are N_a = |x|² and N_b = |y|² ∓ |x|² for the su(2) (upper sign) and su(1,1) (lower sign) cases
- The deformed generators satisfy [Ŝ_+, Ŝ_-] = +[2Ŝ_0]_q and [T̂_+, T̂_-] = -[2T̂_0]_q, where [2Ŝ_0]_q = [N̂_a]_f [N̂_b+1]_g - [N̂_a+1]_f [N̂_b]_g and [2T̂_0]_q = [N̂_a+1]_f [N̂_b+1]_g - [N̂_a]_f [N̂_b]_g, with [x]_f = x f(x)^(-2) f(x-1)² and [x]_g = x g(x)^(-2) g(x-1)²

## Context

This paper is Part II of a series by Kuriyama, Providencia, da Providencia, Tsue, and Yamamura, extending the deformed boson scheme from the single-boson case treated in Part I to two-boson systems. It builds on the Schwinger boson representation of su(2) and su(1,1) algebras (Schwinger) and the authors' previous work on coherent states for these algebras (Kuriyama et al., 1999). The work enables the description of damped and amplified oscillations in conservative form using su(1,1)-spin systems (Tsue et al., 1999) and provides a framework for deriving Holstein-Primakoff representations for su(2)_q- and su(1,1)_q-algebras within the deformed boson scheme.
