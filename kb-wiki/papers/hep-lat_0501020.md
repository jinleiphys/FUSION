# Baryon masses at O(a^2) in chiral perturbation theory

> The chiral Lagrangian for the Symanzik action through O(a^2) for baryons is obtained. We consider two flavor unquenched and partially quenched lattice theories, allowing for mixed actions in the latter. As an application, we calculate masses to O(a^2) for the nucleons and deltas, and investigate the corrections due to the violation of O(4) rotational invariance. These results are contrasted with those in the meson sector for lattice simulations using mixed and unmixed actions of Wilson and Ginsparg-Wilson quarks.

## Key claim

The paper derives the chiral Lagrangian for baryons at O(a^2) in the Symanzik action for two-flavor unquenched and partially quenched lattice theories with mixed actions, finding that unlike the meson sector, the baryon sector requires operators that break O(4) rotational symmetry at this order in the chiral expansion, though the number of independent parameters entering mass expressions remains relatively small.

## Method

The author constructs the Symanzik Lagrangian through O(a^2) for a partially quenched, mixed lattice action where valence and sea quarks are either Wilson or Ginsparg-Wilson fermions, then matches these operators onto heavy baryon partially quenched chiral perturbation theory (PQchiPT) at O(epsilon^4) in a power counting where epsilon^2 ~ m_q/Lambda_QCD ~ a Lambda_QCD. The effective Lagrangian includes operators at O(m_Q a) and O(a^2) for both spin-1/2 baryons (in the 70-dimensional supermultiplet) and spin-3/2 baryons (in the 44-dimensional supermultiplet), with the O(4)-breaking operators treated using hypercubic invariants constructed from the velocity vector v_mu and spin operator S_mu.

## Key numbers

- The power counting scheme uses epsilon^2 ~ m_q/Lambda_QCD and epsilon^2 ~ a Lambda_QCD as the small dimensionless expansion parameters
- The pion decay constant f = 132 MeV is used in the meson Lagrangian
- For spin-1/2 baryons, there are 11 independent operators at O(m_Q a) involving the combination W_+ x M_+
- For spin-3/2 baryons, there are 6 independent operators at O(m_Q a) involving W_+ x M_+
- For spin-1/2 baryons, there are 8 operators from class four (four-quark chiral symmetry breaking) operators at O(a^2) involving W_+ x W_+
- For spin-3/2 baryons, there are 5 operators from class four operators at O(a^2) involving W_+ x W_+
- The mass splitting Delta between the 70 and 44 multiplets in the chiral limit scales as epsilon

## Context

This work extends the finite lattice spacing corrections in chiral perturbation theory from the meson sector (Sharpe 1998, Lee 1999, Rupak 2002, Bar 2002, Bar 2003, Aoki 2003) to the baryon sector at O(a^2), building on earlier O(a) baryon work by Beane (2003) and Arndt (2004). The results enable lattice practitioners to perform simultaneous chiral and continuum extrapolations of nucleon and delta masses using expressions that account for both quark mass dependence and lattice spacing artifacts, including the violation of O(4) rotational symmetry that appears uniquely in the baryon sector at this order.
