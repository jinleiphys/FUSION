# Level density of 2+ states in 40Ca from high energy-resolution (p,p') experiments

> The level density of 2+ states in 40Ca has been extracted in the energy region of the isoscalar giant quadrupole resonance (ISGQR) from a fluctuation analysis of high energy-resolution p,p') data taken at incident energies of 200 MeV at the K600 magnetic spectrometer of iThemba LABS, South Africa. Quasi-free scattering cross sections were calculated to estimate their role as a background contribution to the spectra and found to be small. The shape of the background was determined from the discrete wavelet transform of the spectra using a biorthogonal wavelet function normalized at the lowest particle separation threshold. The experimental results are compared to widely used phenomenological and microscopic models.

## Key claim

The level density of 2+ states in 40Ca in the ISGQR region (approximately 10-20 MeV) can be extracted from a fluctuation analysis of high energy-resolution (p,p') data, provided that the non-resonant background is properly subtracted using a discrete wavelet transform and that the quasi-free scattering contribution is small, as confirmed by DWIA calculations.

## Method

High energy-resolution (p,p') spectra of 40Ca were measured at 200 MeV incident proton energy using the K600 magnetic spectrometer at iThemba LABS at scattering angles of 7°, 11°, and 15°. The background from quasi-free scattering was calculated using the DWIA code THREEDEE for (p,2p) and (p,pn) reactions and found to be small. The shape of the remaining non-resonant background was determined using a discrete wavelet transform (DWT) with a biorthogonal BIOR6.8 wavelet, and the level density of 2+ states was extracted via an autocorrelation fluctuation analysis of the background-subtracted spectra.

## Key numbers

* Level density at Ex = 11 MeV: 17.5 (+2.9/-3.1) MeV^-1 (11° data) and 18.1 (+3.0/-1.9) MeV^-1 (15° data)
* Level density at Ex = 13 MeV: 64.1 (+10.6/-10.7) MeV^-1 (11°) and 57.3 (+9.5/-6.6) MeV^-1 (15°)
* Level density at Ex = 15 MeV: 213 (+35/-38) MeV^-1 (11°) and 224 (+37/-32) MeV^-1 (15°)
* Level density at Ex = 17 MeV: 217 (+36/-43) MeV^-1 (11°) and 236 (+39/-37) MeV^-1 (15°)
* Level density at Ex = 19 MeV: 1007 (+166/-226) MeV^-1 (11°) and 705 (+116/-122) MeV^-1 (15°)
* Experimental energy resolution: 35-40 keV FWHM
* Incident proton energy: 200 MeV
* Quasi-free contribution at 25 MeV represents not more than about half of the experimental cross section value

## Context

This work extends the fluctuation analysis method previously applied to giant resonances in 58Ni and 90Zr (Kalmykov et al., 2007) to the doubly magic nucleus 40Ca, where shell closure makes phenomenological level density models particularly uncertain. The extracted spin- and parity-resolved level densities are compared to the back-shifted Fermi gas model (Rauscher et al., 1997; von Egidy and Bucurescu, 2005), HF-BCS calculations (Demetriou and Goriely, 2001), and HFB calculations (Goriely et al., 2008). The results enable tests of spin distribution models and parity dependence of level densities with astrophysical consequences for nucleosynthesis calculations.
