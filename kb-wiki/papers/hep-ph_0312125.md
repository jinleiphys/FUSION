# Probing the partonic structure of pentaquarks in hard electroproduction

> Exclusive electroproduction of a K or K* meson on the nucleon can give a Theta+ pentaquark in the final state. This reaction offers an opportunity to investigate the structure of pentaquark baryons at parton level. We discuss the generalized parton distributions for the N-->Theta+ transition and give the leading order amplitude for electroproduction in the Bjorken regime. Different production channels contain complementary information about the distribution of partons in a pentaquark compared with their distribution in the nucleon. Measurement of these processes may thus provide deeper insight into the very nature of pentaquarks.

## Key claim

Exclusive electroproduction of a K or K* meson on the nucleon can produce a Theta+ pentaquark in the final state, and the leading-order amplitude for this process in the Bjorken regime can be expressed in terms of transition generalized parton distributions (GPDs) from the nucleon to the Theta+, provided that Q^2 is large enough for the factorization theorem to apply and that the Theta+ has spin 1/2 and isospin 0.

## Method

The authors define the transition GPDs for the N to Theta+ process using light-cone coordinates and express the leading-order scattering amplitudes for longitudinal photon and meson polarization in terms of these GPDs and the distribution amplitudes of the produced kaon or K* meson. They derive the cross section formula for the gamma* p to meson + Theta reaction and evaluate the contribution from kaon exchange in the t-channel, relating it to the Theta+ decay width and the kaon form factor.

## Key numbers

- Theta+ mass taken as m_Theta = 1540 MeV for numerical evaluations (results do not change significantly for 1525 MeV or 1555 MeV)
- Kaon decay constant f_K = 160 MeV; K* decay constant f_K* = (218 +/- 4) MeV
- Gegenbauer coefficients for the kaon distribution amplitude at factorization scale mu = 1 GeV: a_1^{K-} = -0.18 +/- 0.09, a_2^{K-} = 0.16 +/- 0.10; for K*-: a_1^{K*-} = -0.4 +/- 0.2, a_2^{K*-} = 0.09 +/- 0.05
- For an indicative Theta+ width Gamma_Theta = 10 MeV, the squared coupling g^2_{Theta NK}/(4pi) = 0.77 for positive parity (eta_Theta = 1) and 0.015 for negative parity (eta_Theta = -1)
- Decay nucleon momentum in Theta+ rest frame k approximately 268 MeV
- Couplings for hyperon production: g^2_{Sigma NK}/(4pi) = 1.2 and g^2_{Lambda NK}/(4pi) = 14 (from Frankfurt et al. 1999)

## Context

This work extends the formalism of generalized parton distributions, previously applied to nucleon and hyperon transitions, to the case of pentaquark production, following experimental evidence for the Theta+ resonance reported by Nakano (2003) and Barth (2003). The approach enables comparison of Theta+ production with hyperon channels such as gamma* p to K+ Lambda or K+ Sigma0, where SU(3) flavor symmetry relates the transition GPDs to those of the nucleon itself, potentially revealing the partonic structure of pentaquarks. The authors note that higher-order corrections in alpha_s and 1/Q can be substantial at moderate Q^2, as seen in rho0 electroproduction, and suggest that ratios of cross sections may cancel some theoretical uncertainties.
