# Nucleon isovector charges and twist-2 matrix elements with $N_f=2+1$ dynamical Wilson quarks

> We present results from a lattice QCD study of nucleon matrix elements at vanishing momentum transfer for local and twist-2 isovector operator insertions. Computations are performed on gauge ensembles with non-perturbatively improved $N_f=2+1$ Wilson fermions, covering four values of the lattice spacing and pion masses down to $M_π\approx200$MeV. Several source-sink separations (typically ~1.0fm to ~1.5fm) allow us to assess excited-state contamination. Results on individual ensembles are obtained from simultaneous two-state fits across all observables and all available source-sink separations with the energy gap as a common fit parameter. Renormalization has been performed non-perturbatively using the Rome-Southampton method for all but the finest lattice spacing for which an extrapolation has been used. Physical results are quoted in the $\overline{MS}$ scheme at a scale of $μ=2$GeV and are obtained from a combined chiral, continuum and finite-size extrapolation. For the nucleon isovector axial, scalar and tensor charges we find physical values of $g_A^{u-d}=1.242(25)_\text{stat}(\genfrac{}{}{0pt}{2}{+00}{-31})_\text{sys}$, $g_S^{u-d}=1.13(11)_\text{stat}(\genfrac{}{}{0pt}{2}{+07}{-06})_\text{sys}$ and $g_T^{u-d}=0.965(38)_\text{stat}(\genfrac{}{}{0pt}{2}{+13}{-41})_\text{sys}$, respectively, where individual systematic errors in each direction from the chiral, continuum and finite-size extrapolation have been added in quadrature. Our final results for the isovector average quark momentum fraction and the isovector helicity and transversity moments are given by $\langle x\rangle_{u-d}=0.180(25)_\text{stat}(\genfrac{}{}{0pt}{2}{+14}{-06})_\text{sys}$, $\langle x\rangle_{Δu-Δd}=0.221(25)_\text{stat}(\genfrac{}{}{0pt}{2}{+10}{-00})_\text{sys}$ and $\langle x\rangle_{δu-δd}=0.212(32)_\text{stat}(\genfrac{}{}{0pt}{2}{+20}{-10})_\text{sys}$, respectively.

## Key claim

The paper presents lattice QCD results for nucleon isovector charges and twist-2 matrix elements, claiming physical values that are obtained from a combined chiral, continuum and finite-size extrapolation, with systematic errors that include contributions from the extrapolation added in quadrature; the axial charge result of \(g_A^{u-d}=1.242(25)_\text{stat}(^{+00}_{-31})_\text{sys}\) is slightly below the experimental value of 1.2724(23), with the systematic error asymmetric and only in the negative direction.

## Method

The authors performed lattice QCD calculations on eleven gauge ensembles with \(N_f=2+1\) non-perturbatively improved Wilson fermions at four lattice spacings and pion masses down to approximately 200 MeV. They computed two-point and three-point correlation functions for local and twist-2 isovector operator insertions at vanishing momentum transfer, using multiple source-sink separations (typically 1.0 fm to 1.5 fm) to assess excited-state contamination. Results on individual ensembles were obtained from simultaneous two-state fits across all observables and all available source-sink separations with a common energy gap as a fit parameter, and renormalization was performed non-perturbatively using the Rome-Southampton method for all but the finest lattice spacing where an extrapolation was used.

## Key numbers

* Axial charge: \(g_A^{u-d} = 1.242(25)_\text{stat}(^{+00}_{-31})_\text{sys}\) in the \(\overline{\mathrm{MS}}\) scheme at \(\mu = 2\) GeV
* Scalar charge: \(g_S^{u-d} = 1.13(11)_\text{stat}(^{+07}_{-06})_\text{sys}\) in the \(\overline{\mathrm{MS}}\) scheme at \(\mu = 2\) GeV
* Tensor charge: \(g_T^{u-d} = 0.965(38)_\text{stat}(^{+13}_{-41})_\text{sys}\) in the \(\overline{\mathrm{MS}}\) scheme at \(\mu = 2\) GeV
* Isovector average quark momentum fraction: \(\langle x \rangle_{u-d} = 0.180(25)_\text{stat}(^{+14}_{-06})_\text{sys}\) in the \(\overline{\mathrm{MS}}\) scheme at \(\mu = 2\) GeV
* Isovector helicity moment: \(\langle x \rangle_{\Delta u - \Delta d} = 0.221(25)_\text{stat}(^{+10}_{-00})_\text{sys}\) in the \(\overline{\mathrm{MS}}\) scheme at \(\mu = 2\) GeV
* Isovector transversity moment: \(\langle x \rangle_{\delta u - \delta d} = 0.212(32)_\text{stat}(^{+20}_{-10})_\text{sys}\) in the \(\overline{\mathrm{MS}}\) scheme at \(\mu = 2\) GeV
* Pion masses on ensembles ranged from approximately 200 MeV to 352 MeV, with lattice spacings from 0.04981 fm to 0.08636 fm

## Context

This work extends previous lattice QCD calculations of nucleon charges and parton distribution moments, such as those by Capitani (2017) and Bhattacharya (2016), by employing a simultaneous multi-state fit across all observables to control excited-state contamination. The results for the axial charge are compared to the experimental value from the Particle Data Group (Tanabashi, 2018) and to the FLAG review (Aoki, 2019), while the scalar and tensor charges provide input for searches for beyond-Standard-Model physics and dark matter searches. The study enables future comparisons with phenomenological determinations of the tensor charge from experiments such as those discussed by Ye (2016), and the authors plan to extend the work to isoscalar observables.
