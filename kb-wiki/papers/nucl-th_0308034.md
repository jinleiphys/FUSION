# Cross sections for pentaquark baryon production from protons in reactions induced by hadrons and photons

> Using hadronic Lagrangians that include the interaction of pentaquark $Θ^+$ baryon with $K$ and $N$, we evaluate the cross sections for its production from meson-proton, proton-proton, and photon-proton reactions near threshold. With empirical coupling constants and form factors, the predicted cross sections are about 1.5 mb in kaon-proton reactions, 0.1 mb in rho-nucleon reactions, 0.05 mb in pion-nucleon reactions, 20 $μ$b in proton-proton reactions, and 40 nb in photon-proton reactions.

## Key claim

Using hadronic Lagrangians with empirical coupling constants and monopole form factors, the predicted cross sections for pentaquark Θ⁺ production are about 1.5 mb in kaon-proton reactions, 0.1 mb in rho-nucleon reactions, 0.05 mb in pion-nucleon reactions, 20 μb in proton-proton reactions, and 40 nb in photon-proton reactions; these values depend on the assumed Θ⁺ width of 20 MeV and a cutoff parameter Λ = 0.5 GeV for meson-nucleon vertices.

## Method

The authors constructed a hadronic model based on SU(3) flavor symmetry with empirical hadron masses and monopole form factors at interaction vertices, determining the KNΘ coupling constant from the Θ⁺ width (Γ_Θ = 20 MeV, m_Θ = 1.54 GeV). They evaluated spin- and isospin-averaged cross sections for Θ⁺ production from meson-nucleon reactions (πN → K̄Θ, KN → πΘ, ρN → K̄Θ), proton-proton reactions (pp → Σ⁺Θ⁺, pp → π⁺ΛΘ⁺, pp → K̄⁰pΘ⁺), and photon-proton reactions (γp → K̄⁰Θ⁺, γp → K*⁻π⁺Θ⁺, γp → K⁻ρ⁺Θ⁺) using the amplitudes from the interaction Lagrangians and integrating over phase space.

## Key numbers

- Peak cross section for KN → πΘ: about 1.6 mb (center-of-mass energy near threshold)
- Peak cross section for πN → K̄Θ: about 0.05 mb
- Peak cross section for ρN → K̄Θ: about 0.1 mb
- Peak cross section for pp → Σ⁺Θ⁺: about 2 μb at center-of-mass energy around 3.3 GeV
- Peak cross section for pp → π⁺ΛΘ⁺: about 3 μb at center-of-mass energy 4.2 GeV
- Peak cross section for pp → K̄⁰pΘ⁺: about 13 μb
- Total cross section for Θ⁺ production from proton-proton reactions: about 20 μb
- Peak cross section for γp → K̄⁰Θ⁺: about 36 nb (low center-of-mass energies)
- Peak cross section for γp → K*⁻π⁺Θ⁺: about 8 nb
- Peak cross section for γp → K⁻ρ⁺Θ⁺: about 6 nb
- Coupling constant g_KNΘ = 4.4 (from Γ_Θ = 20 MeV, m_Θ = 1.54 GeV)

## Context

This work extends the hadronic model approach previously applied to charmed hadron production (Liu et al., 2002) to the newly discovered pentaquark Θ⁺ baryon, providing the first systematic evaluation of its production cross sections in hadron- and photon-induced reactions. The results enable quantitative studies of Θ⁺ production in relativistic heavy ion collisions, where Randrup (2003) had estimated Θ⁺/Λ ratios of 10-20% using a statistical model, and Chen (2003) had studied quark coalescence from quark-gluon plasma; the cross sections reported here are needed to assess whether final-state hadronic interactions significantly alter the Θ⁺ yield from the initial plasma phase.
