# Matter effects on $η$ and $η'$ mesons

> We show how the nuclear medium affects the masses of the $η$ and $η'$ mesons. The change should be easily detectable for dense matter and/or strong $η(η')N\bar N$ coupling. We also find that the $η-η'$ mixing angle is less in magnitude in the nuclear matter than in vacuum.

## Key claim

The paper shows that the nuclear medium shifts the masses of the η and η' mesons and reduces the magnitude of their mixing angle, but these effects are significant only for dense matter (several times normal nuclear density) and/or strong η(η')N\(\bar N\) pseudoscalar couplings, which are poorly constrained.

## Method

The authors compute the one-loop self-energy of the η and η' mesons in symmetric nuclear matter using a pseudoscalar coupling Lagrangian for the ηN\(\bar N\) and η'N\(\bar N\) interactions. The nucleon propagator is split into a free part and a density-dependent part arising from Pauli blocking, and the polarization tensor is evaluated in the long-wavelength limit to obtain the in-medium masses and the matter-induced η–η' mixing angle.

## Key numbers

- Vacuum η mass: 547.75 MeV; vacuum η' mass: 957.78 MeV (horizontal solid lines in figures).
- Vacuum η–η' mixing angle: \((-21.3 \pm 1.5)^\circ\) inferred from the γγ decay mode.
- For couplings \(g_{\eta N\bar N}=g_{\eta' N\bar N}=2\) at nuclear density \(\rho/\rho_0=1\) (normal nuclear density), the mass shifts are small; at \(\rho/\rho_0=6\), the η' mass shift becomes significant (figure 1, dot-dashed lines).
- For \(g_{\eta N\bar N}=4\), \(g_{\eta' N\bar N}=3\) at \(\rho/\rho_0=6\), the η mass drops by roughly 50–100 MeV and the η' mass rises by roughly 50–100 MeV (figure 2b, short-dashed lines with mixing).
- The matter-induced mixing angle is negative and its magnitude is smaller than the vacuum value; for \(g_{\eta N\bar N}=g_{\eta' N\bar N}=2\) at \(\rho/\rho_0=6\), the angle is approximately \(-5^\circ\) (figure 3, dot-dashed line).
- Vacuum branching ratios for clean decay channels: η→2γ (0.39), η→e⁺e⁻γ (5×10⁻³), η→μ⁺μ⁻γ (3×10⁻⁴), η'→ρ⁰γ (0.30), η'→ωγ (0.03), η'→2γ (0.021).

## Context

This work extends the study of matter-induced mass shifts and mixing, previously applied to vector mesons ρ and ω (e.g., by Abhee et al.), to the pseudoscalar η and η' mesons. It uses the same one-loop Dyson equation approach as in earlier mean-field and relativistic Hartree models for nucleon mass shifts. The results enable experimental tests via two-photon decay widths and other clean decay channels at future hadronic colliders, and they predict that the medium rotates η and η' back toward the SU(3) gauge basis (η₁, η₈).
