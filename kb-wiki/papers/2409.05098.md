# Heavy flavor-asymmetric pseudoscalar mesons on the light front

> We extract the leading Fock-state light front wave functions (LF-LFWFs) of heavy flavor-asymmetric pseudoscalar mesons $D$, $B$ and $B_c$ from their Bethe-Salpeter wave functions based on Dyson-Schwinger equations approach, and study their leading twist parton distribution amplitudes, generalized parton distribution functions and transverse momentum dependent parton distributions. The spatial distributions of the quark and antiquark on the transverse plane are given, along with their charge and energy distributions on the light front. We find that in the considered mesons, the heavier quarks carry most longitudinal momentum fraction and yield narrow $x$-distributions, while the lighter quarks play an active role in shaping the transverse distributions within both spatial and momentum space, exhibiting a duality embodying characteristics from both light mesons and heavy quarkonium.

## Key claim

The authors extract leading Fock-state light front wave functions (LF-LFWFs) of heavy flavor-asymmetric pseudoscalar mesons (D, B, B_c) from Bethe-Salpeter wave functions based on the Dyson-Schwinger equations approach, finding that the heavier quarks carry most longitudinal momentum fraction and yield narrow x-distributions while the lighter quarks actively shape the transverse distributions, but this result is obtained under the leading Fock-state truncation and the rainbow-ladder truncation of the Dyson-Schwinger equations, with model parameters fine-tuned to reproduce the physical meson spectrum and decay constants.

## Method

The authors first solve the Dyson-Schwinger equation for dressed quark propagators and the Bethe-Salpeter equation for meson amplitudes under the rainbow-ladder truncation, using the Qin-Chang model for the dressed-gluon propagator with flavor-dependent parameters (Dω) and current quark masses renormalized at 19 GeV. They then extract the leading Fock-state light front wave functions ψ₀ and ψ₁ by projecting the covariant Bethe-Salpeter wave functions onto the light front at x⁺=0, computing Mellin moments up to m_Max=5-8, and reconstructing the x-dependence at each discretized |k_T| using a parameterization form x^α (1-x)^β (c₀ + c₁ x + c₂ x²) with fitting quality χ ≲ 1%.

## Key numbers

- Current quark masses used: m_{u/d}=0.0033 GeV, m_c=0.854 GeV, m_b=3.682 GeV, renormalized at 19 GeV
- Model parameters: (Dω)_{u/d}=(0.82 GeV)³, (Dω)_c=(0.66 GeV)³, (Dω)_b=(0.48 GeV)³
- Mixing parameters for flavor-asymmetric kernel: α_{c[ū/ḏ]}=0.689, α_{b[ū/ḏ]}=0.751, α_{b\bar{c}}=0.585
- Normalization contributions (N_{↑,↓}, N_{↑,↑}): (0.25, 0.08) for D, (0.30, 0.08) for B, (0.46, 0.04) for B_c mesons
- Mean squared impact parameters: ⟨b_T²⟩^c_D=(0.13 fm)², ⟨b_T²⟩^{ū}_D=(0.40 fm)², ⟨b_T²⟩^b_B=(0.056 fm)², ⟨b_T²⟩^{ū}_B=(0.47 fm)², ⟨b_T²⟩^b_{B_c}=(0.077 fm)², ⟨b_T²⟩^{\bar{c}}_{B_c}=(0.22 fm)²
- Light-cone charge radii: ⟨r²_{c,LC}⟩_{D⁺}=(0.25 fm)², ⟨r²_{c,LC}⟩_{B⁻}=(0.38 fm)², ⟨r²_{c,LC}⟩_{B_c⁻}=-(0.19 fm)²
- Light-cone mass radii: ⟨r²_{E,LC}⟩_D=(0.21 fm)², ⟨r²_{E,LC}⟩_B=(0.14 fm)², ⟨r²_{E,LC}⟩_{B_c}=(0.13 fm)²
- Mean transverse momenta of valence quarks: ⟨|k_T|⟩_D=0.43 GeV

## Context

This work extends the authors' previous DS-BSEs studies of light and heavy meson LF-LFWFs (pion, ρ, η_c, J/ψ) to flavor-asymmetric systems, and complements basis light front quantization (BLFQ) studies of D, B, and B_c mesons by Tang et al. (2019). The results for parton distribution amplitudes are compared with earlier DSE predictions by Binosi et al. (2018) and Serna et al. (2020), as well as lattice QCD quasi-DA results by Han et al. (2024) using large-momentum effective theory (LaMET). The work enables further study of three-dimensional parton structure of heavy-light mesons, including generalized parton distributions and transverse momentum dependent distributions, which are relevant for understanding CP violation effects in D and B meson decays.
