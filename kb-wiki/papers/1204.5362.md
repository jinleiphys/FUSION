# Angular momentum decomposition of chiral multiplets in front form

> In this article we derive the unitary transformation that relates the $q\bar q$ chiral basis $\{R; I J^{PC} \}$ to the $\{I; ^{2S+1}L_J\}$-basis in a front-form framework. From the most general expression for the Clebsch-Gordan coefficients of the Poincaré group one can see that the chiral limit brings the angular momentum coupling into a simple form that permits the relation in terms of SU(2) Clebsch-Gordan coefficients. We demonstrate that such a transformation is identical to the one was obtained for canonical spin in the instant form.

## Key claim

The unitary transformation that relates the $q\bar q$ chiral basis $\{R; I J^{PC}\}$ to the $\{I; ^{2S+1}L_J\}$-basis, previously derived in the instant form of relativistic quantum mechanics, is shown to be identical in the front form, but only in the chiral limit where the constituent masses go to zero and the generalized Melosh rotation becomes the identity.

## Method

The author starts from the most general expression for the Clebsch-Gordan coefficients of the Poincaré group in the front form, which includes mass-dependent Melosh rotations that transform canonical spins to front-form spins. By taking the chiral limit (m → 0), the Melosh rotations become the identity and front-form spins coincide with helicity spins, allowing the product of Wigner D-functions with the same argument to be combined using SU(2) Clebsch-Gordan coefficients, exactly as in the instant-form derivation. The resulting decomposition is then expressed in terms of the same matrix elements as in the instant form.

## Key numbers

- The $\rho$-like state $|(0,1)+(1,0);11^{--}\rangle$ decomposes as $\sqrt{\frac{2}{3}}|1;^3S_1\rangle + \sqrt{\frac{1}{3}}|1;^3D_1\rangle$.
- The $\rho$-like state $|(1/2,1/2)_b;11^{--}\rangle$ decomposes as $\sqrt{\frac{1}{3}}|1;^3S_1\rangle - \sqrt{\frac{2}{3}}|1;^3D_1\rangle$.
- The matrix element $\langle JM\lambda_1\lambda_2 |^{2S+1}L_JM\rangle = \sqrt{\frac{2L+1}{2J+1}}C^{S\Lambda}_{s_1 \lambda_1 s_2 -\lambda_2} C^{J\Lambda}_{L0S\Lambda}$ is identical in both instant and front forms.

## Context

This work extends the unitary transformation between chiral and LS bases, originally derived by Glozman (2007) in the instant form, to the front-form framework used in light-front quantization and front-form relativistic quantum mechanics. The result enables the use of chiral basis decompositions in front-form calculations, such as those previously applied by Glozman (2009) to study chiral symmetry breaking in the $\rho$ meson, where the $q\bar q$ component in the infrared was found to be a superposition of chiral states yielding a nearly pure $^3S_1$ partial wave. The derivation relies on the property that the generalized Melosh rotation relating front-form spins to helicity spins becomes the identity for massless particles, as established by Diehl (2003), Soper (1972), and Lepage (1980).
