# Polarization Tensors for the fuzzy Grassmannians $Gr_{2;n}^F$ and Grassmannian harmonics on $Gr_{2;n}$

> We explicitly construct the eigenfunctions of the Laplacian for the fuzzy Grassmannian spaces $Gr_{2;n}^F$. We calculate the spectrum and find it be a truncation of the continuum case. As a byproduct of our approach we find a novel expression for the Grassmannian harmonics in terms of Plucker coordinates which can be interpreted as free Schrodinger particle wave functions on $Gr_{2;n}$.

## Key claim

The paper explicitly constructs the eigenfunctions of the Laplacian for the fuzzy Grassmannian spaces \(Gr_{2;n}^F\) and finds that the spectrum is a truncation of the continuum case, but this result holds only for the specific family \(Gr_{2;n}\) with \(r=2\) and under the condition that the fuzzy function algebra is a \(U(2)\) singlet.

## Method

The authors use a Schwinger-Fock type construction with two-index pseudo-oscillators corresponding to fuzzy Plücker coordinates, acting on a double Fock vacuum. The \(U(n)\)-invariant Laplacian is built from left action derivatives of \(su(n)\), and the polarization tensors (eigenfunctions) are defined via representation theory decomposition of the tensor product of irreducible representations of \(su(n)\). The spectrum is computed by evaluating the quadratic Casimir operator on these representations, and the normalisation factors are found using a recurrence relation derived from trace identities.

## Key numbers

* The dimension of the \(su(n)\) irreducible representation carried by \(\mathcal{F}_L\) is \(d_n(L) = \frac{(L+n-1)!(L+n-2)!}{L!(L+1)!(n-1)!(n-2)!}\).
* The eigenvalue of the Laplacian on the representation \((p,l,0,\cdots,0,l,p)\) is \(C_n(l,p) = 2(l+p)^2 + 2(n-p-2)l + (n-p-1)p\).
* The recurrence relation for the normalisation factor \(Q_n(l,p,L)\) is \(Q_n(l,p,L) = \frac{(L-l+1)(L-l-p)(L+l+n-2)(L+n+l+p-1)}{L^2 (L+1)^2} Q_n(l,p,L-1)\), valid down to \(L = l+p\).
* The volume of the Grassmannian is \(\text{Vol}(Gr_{2;n}) = \frac{\pi^{2(n-2)}}{(n-1)!(n-2)!}\).
* For the special case \(n=3\) (\(Gr_{2;3} = \mathbb{CP}^2\)), the normalisation factor for the Grassmannian harmonics is \(q_3(l,p) = \frac{\text{Vol}(Gr_{2;n}) ((l+p)! (l+p+1)!)^2 }{2^{2l+2p-1} (2l+2p+1)! (2l+2p+2)!}\).

## Context

This work extends the Schwinger-Fock construction previously applied to fuzzy complex projective spaces by Dolan (2006) and to flag and super-flag manifolds by Murray (2006). It provides the first explicit construction of Grassmannian harmonics on \(Gr_{2;n}\) in terms of Plücker coordinates, which can be interpreted as free Schrödinger particle wave functions. The results enable future study of a fuzzy Dirac operator and eigenspinors on \(Gr_{2;n}^F\) and \(Gr_{2;n}\), particularly for even \(n\) where a spin structure exists, and suggest generalisation to other Grassmannians.
