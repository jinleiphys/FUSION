## Context

This work extends the Bonn-Gatchina group's previous analyses (Anisovich 2010, 2011) by comparing their transition amplitudes for hyperon and О· production with other partial-wave analysis models, including those from Candlin et al. (1983), the Bonn-Juelich group (Doring 2010), and KAON-MAID (Bennhold 1999). The paper reveals severe discrepancies between different analyses, attributed to the lack of polarization data, and enables future improvements as ongoing experiments with polarized photon beams, polarized targets, and recoil polarization measurements at Bonn, Mainz, and JLab are completed and analyzed.
