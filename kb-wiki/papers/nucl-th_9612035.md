# A Precise Determination of the Pion-Nuclear Coupling Parameter from Weak Processes in ^3He

> We utilize precise weak interaction experiments on atomic muon capture and beta decay in the A = 3 nuclei, and take into account the effects of nuclear "anomalous thresholds" to extract the pseudoscalar pi-^3He-^3H coupling parameter, G^{eff}(m_π^2) = 45.8+- 2.4. This is an order of magnitude improvement in precision over that from the use of pion-nuclear scattering data and dispersion relations.

# A Precise Determination of the Pion-Nuclear Coupling Parameter from Weak Processes in ^3He

## Key claim
The pseudoscalar π-^3He-^3H coupling parameter G^{eff}(m_π^2) = 45.8 ± 2.4 can be extracted from weak interaction experiments (muon capture and beta decay in A=3 nuclei) with an order of magnitude improvement in precision over values from pion-nuclear scattering data and dispersion relations, provided one accounts for nuclear "anomalous thresholds" and assumes nuclear PCAC and the Goldberger-Treiman relation.

## Method
The authors used the precisely measured muon capture rate on ^3He from the PSI experiment (Λ_c = 1496 ± 4 s^{-1}) and the ^3H beta decay axial form factor F_A(0) = 1.212 ± 0.004 to determine the effective pseudoscalar form factor F_P at the muon capture kinematics. They then applied the nuclear PCAC relation and a dispersion relation incorporating anomalous cuts from deuteron-nucleon and three-nucleon breakup thresholds to extract G^{eff} at t = 0 and t = t_cap, and performed a linear extrapolation to the pion pole at t = m_π^2.

## Key numbers
- Muon capture rate on ^3He: Λ_c = 1496 ± 4 s^{-1}
- Axial form factor from ^3H beta decay: F_A(0) = 1.212 ± 0.004
- Effective coupling at t = 0 from beta decay: G^{eff}_{π^3He^3H}(0) = 36.81 ± 0.15
- Pseudoscalar form factor from muon capture: F_P = 20.80 ± 1.6
- Effective coupling at muon capture kinematics (t ≈ -0.954 m_π^2): G^{eff}(-0.954 m^2) = 31.9 ± 1.3
- Ratio G^{eff}(0)/G^{eff}(t_cap) = 1.15 ± 0.05, confirming anomalous cut effects
- Pion-nuclear coupling at the pion pole: G^{eff}(m_π^2) = 45.8 ± 2.4

## Context
This work extends the approach of Jarlskog and Yndurain (1970s) and Kopeliovich (1970s) on anomalous thresholds in the A=3 system, and the dispersion relation analyses of Ericson and Locher (1969) for pion-nuclear scattering. The authors use the precise PSI muon capture experiment to improve upon earlier strong-interaction determinations of the π-^3He-^3H coupling from scattering data by authors such as those summarized in Table I of the paper. The result enables a test of nuclear PCAC and the Goldberger-Treiman relation at the few-percent level, and motivates future polarization measurements in muon capture and improved pion-nuclear scattering experiments at pion factories.
