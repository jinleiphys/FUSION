# Evaluation of overlaps between arbitrary Fermionic quasiparticle vacua

> We derive an expression that allows for the unambiguous evaluation of the overlap between two arbitrary quasiparticle vacua, including its sign. Our expression is based on the Pfaffian of a skew-symmetric matrix, extending the formula recently proposed by [L. M. Robledo, Phys. Rev. C 79, 021302(R) (2009)] to the most general case, including the one of the overlap between two different blocked n-quasiparticle states for either even or odd systems. The powerfulness of the method is illustrated for a few typical matrix elements that appear in realistic angular-momentum-restored Generator-Coordinate Method calculations when breaking time-reversal invariance and using the full model space of occupied single-particle states.

## Key claim

The paper derives an expression for the unambiguous evaluation of the overlap between two arbitrary quasiparticle vacua, including its sign, by extending the Pfaffian-based formula of Robledo (2009) to the most general case. This includes overlaps between different blocked n-quasiparticle states for both even and odd systems, and cases where the two vacua are expressed in different single-particle bases that do not span the same subspace.

## Method

The authors use a generalized Thouless parameterization of quasiparticle vacua that handles fully occupied single-particle states and blocked states for odd particle numbers. They insert Fermionic coherent state resolutions of the identity in the respective single-particle bases of the two vacua, evaluate the resulting Grassmann integrals, and express the final overlap as the Pfaffian of a reduced skew-symmetric matrix multiplied by a determinant and sign factors. The method reduces the matrix dimension by removing rows and columns corresponding to fully occupied states in each vacuum.

## Key numbers

- The final expression for the overlap is given by ⟨φ_a|φ_b⟩ = s_n s_{r_a} σ det(R) pf(M_r), where s_n = (-1)^{n(n+1)/2}, s_{r_a} = (-1)^{r_a(r_a-1)/2}, and σ = (-1)^{∑(n+i_{k_a})+∑i_{k_b}} depends on the indices of fully occupied states.
- The reduced matrix M_r has dimension N_r = 2n - (r_a + r_b), where n is the number of single-particle states and r_a, r_b are the numbers of fully occupied states in each vacuum.
- When N_r is odd, the overlap is automatically zero, consistent with the Pfaffian of an odd-rank skew-symmetric matrix being zero by definition.

## Context

This work extends the Pfaffian-based overlap formula of Robledo (2009) from fully-paired quasiparticle vacua to arbitrary blocked n-quasiparticle states, including odd-particle-number systems that were not covered by Robledo (2009, 2011). The method also generalizes the special case of symmetry-restoration overlaps treated by Bertsch and Robledo (2011). The formula enables practical evaluation of overlap kernels in angular-momentum-restored Generator Coordinate Method calculations that break time-reversal invariance and use the full model space of occupied single-particle states, avoiding the cumbersome phase-tracking techniques previously employed by groups such as Hara and Sun (1982) or Enami et al. (1999).
