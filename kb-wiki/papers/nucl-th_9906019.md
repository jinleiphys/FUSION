# Medium Effects in Coherent Pion Photo- and Electroproduction on 4He and 12C

> Coherent pi0 photo- and electroproduction on 4He and 12C nuclei is investigated in the framework of a distorted wave impulse approximation in momentum space. The elementary process is described by the recently developed unitary isobar model. Medium effects are considered by introducing a phenomenological Delta self-energy. The recent experimental data for 4He and 12C can be well described over a wide range of energies and emission angles by the assumption that the Delta-nuclear interaction saturates.

# Medium Effects in Coherent Pion Photo- and Electroproduction on 4He and 12C

## Key claim

The Delta-nucleus interaction saturates already for 4He, meaning the same phenomenological Delta self-energy extracted from 4He data can describe coherent pion production on 12C without adjustment, provided a form-factor-type parametrization is used rather than a density-dependent one.

## Method

Coherent pi0 photo- and electroproduction on 4He and 12C was calculated using a distorted wave impulse approximation (DWIA) in momentum space, with the elementary amplitude described by the unitary isobar model (UIM). Medium effects were introduced by modifying the Delta propagator with a phenomenological Delta self-energy, tested in two forms: a momentum-space form factor parametrization (F-type) and a local density approximation (rho-type). The F-type parametrization assumed saturation of the Delta-nucleus interaction for 4He, using the same complex strength V1 for both nuclei.

## Key numbers

- At photon lab energy E_gamma = 290 MeV, standard DWIA calculations overestimate measured differential cross sections by approximately a factor of 2 at the maximum.
- The extracted complex strength of the Delta-nucleus interaction from 4He data is Re V1 = 19 MeV and Im V1 = -33 MeV.
- The F-type parametrization uses a harmonic oscillator form factor with beta = 0.54 fm^2.
- The rho-type parametrization uses nuclear saturation density rho_0 = 0.17 fm^-3.
- The Roper resonance contribution is of the order of 2-3% at photon lab energies below 400 MeV.
- Predictions are given for equivalent photon energies E_gamma = 228 MeV and 289 MeV at Q^2 values of 0, 0.054, 1.81 (GeV/c)^2 and 0, 0.074, 1.61 (GeV/c)^2 respectively.

## Context

This work extends the unitary isobar model of Drechsel et al. (1999) from the nucleon to nuclear targets, applying it for the first time to coherent pion production on nuclei. It builds on earlier DWIA momentum-space calculations by Eramzhyan et al. (1983) and Chumbalov et al. (1987), and on Delta-hole model studies by Koch and Moniz (1983) and Koch et al. (1984). The finding that the Delta-nucleus interaction saturates for 4He enables future studies of heavier nuclei and systematic investigation of the Q^2 dependence of medium effects via electroproduction, with first NIKHEF results already in agreement.
