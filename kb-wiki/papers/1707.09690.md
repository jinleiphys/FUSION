# Chiral Lagrangians with $Δ(1232)$ to one loop

> We construct the Lorentz-invariant chiral Lagrangians up to the order $\mathcal{O}(p^4)$ by including $Δ(1232)$ as an explicit degree of freedom. A full one-loop investigation on processes involving $Δ(1232)$ can be performed with them. For the $πΔΔ$ Lagrangian, one obtains 38 independent terms at the order $\mathcal{O}(p^3)$ and 318 independent terms at the order $\mathcal{O}(p^4)$. For the $πNΔ$ Lagrangian, we get 33 independent terms at the order $\mathcal{O}(p^3)$ and 218 independent terms at the order $\mathcal{O}(p^4)$. The heavy baryon projection is also briefly discussed.

## Key claim

The paper constructs the complete Lorentz-invariant chiral Lagrangians including the Δ(1232) baryon as an explicit degree of freedom up to order O(p⁴), enabling full one-loop calculations for processes involving Δ, but the construction relies on the small scale expansion scheme where the expansion parameter p includes the pion mass, the Δ-nucleon mass difference δ, and three-momenta of baryons, and the off-shell parameters in the Δ interactions are shown to be removable.

## Method

The authors adopt the Rarita-Schwinger vector-spinor formalism for the spin-3/2 Δ field and use the small scale expansion scheme of Hemmert (1997) for power counting. They construct chirally invariant monomials for πΔΔ and πNΔ interactions by combining building blocks (uμ, χ±, f±μν, and their isospin components) with Clifford algebra elements, covariant derivatives, and Levi-Civita tensors, then eliminate redundant terms using subsidiary conditions, Schouten identities, partial integration, and the equations of motion for the Δ field. The arbitrary parameter A in the free Rarita-Schwinger Lagrangian and the off-shell parameters zn in the πNΔ interaction are absorbed into redefined fields, yielding Lagrangians with A-independent and zn-independent low-energy constants.

## Key numbers

- For the πΔΔ Lagrangian: 38 independent terms at order O(p³) and 318 independent terms at order O(p⁴)
- For the πNΔ Lagrangian: 33 independent terms at order O(p³) and 218 independent terms at order O(p⁴)
- The mass gap between Δ(1232) and the nucleon is approximately 300 MeV, which sets the scale for the expansion parameter δ/Λχ with Λχ ∼ 1 GeV

## Context

This work extends the chiral Lagrangian with Δ baryons beyond the leading O(p²) Lagrangian of Hemmert (1997) to the one-loop level (O(p⁴)), completing the systematic construction needed for precision calculations. It builds on the methodology for baryon chiral Lagrangians without Δ developed by Gasser (1988), Krause (1990), Ecker (1994), and Jiang (2016), and on the off-shell parameter analysis of Krebs (2009). The results enable full one-loop studies of processes such as πN scattering, nucleon electromagnetic form factors, and nucleon-Δ transitions, which previously used only partial higher-order terms as in the works of Pascalutsa (1999, 2005), Procura (2008), and Bernard (2007, 2009).
