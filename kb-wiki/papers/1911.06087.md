# Statistical double $őõ$ hypernuclear formation from $őě^-$ absorption at rest in light nuclei

> We investigate double $őõ$ hyperfragment formation from the statistical decay of double $őõ$ compound nuclei produced in the $őě^-$ absorption at rest in light nuclei, $^{12}\mathrm{C}$, $^{14}\mathrm{N}$ and $^{16}\mathrm{O}$. We examine the target and the $őõőõ$ bond energy dependence of the double $őõ$ hyperfragment formation probabilities, especially of those double hypernuclei observed in experiments.
