# Neutral hidden charm pentaquark states $P_c^0(4380)$ and $P_c^0(4450)$ in $π^-p \to J/ψn$ reaction

> We investigate the neutral hidden charm pentaquark states $P_c^0(4380)$ and $P_c^0(4450)$ in $π^-p \to J/ψn$ reaction within an effective Lagrangian approach. The background contributions for the process mainly come from $t$-channel $π$ and $ρ$ meson exchanges. The contributions of $P_c^0(4380)$ and $P_c^0(4450)$ states give clear peak structures in the magnitude of 1 $μ$b at center of mass energy 4.38 GeV and 4.45 GeV in the total cross sections. Hence, this reaction may provide a new good platform to search for neutral $P_c$ states. It is expected that our estimated total cross sections together with the angular distributions can be tested by future experiments at J-PARC.

## Key claim

The paper proposes that the neutral hidden charm pentaquark states \(P_c^0(4380)\) and \(P_c^0(4450)\) can be produced in the \(\pi^- p \to J/\psi n\) reaction, producing clear peak structures in the total cross section of about 1 \(\mu\)b at center-of-mass energies of 4.38 GeV and 4.45 GeV. However, the claim is conditional on the assumed branching ratios of 10% for \(P_c \to J/\psi N\) and 1% for \(P_c \to \pi N\), and the results depend on the unknown spin-parity assignments of the two states.

## Method

The authors used an effective Lagrangian approach to calculate the total and differential cross sections for the \(\pi^- p \to J/\psi n\) reaction. The \(s\)-channel contributions from the neutral \(P_c^0(4380)\) and \(P_c^0(4450)\) states were included for four possible spin-parity assignments, while the background was modeled via \(t\)-channel \(\pi\) and \(\rho\) meson exchanges. Coupling constants for the \(P_c\) vertices were derived from their partial decay widths, assuming branching ratios of 10% for \(P_c \to J/\psi N\) and 1% for \(P_c \to \pi N\), and phenomenological form factors were applied to account for the non-point-like nature of the hadrons.

## Key numbers

- The total cross section for \(\pi^- p \to J/\psi n\) shows peak structures of about 1 \(\mu\)b at center-of-mass energies of 4.38 GeV and 4.45 GeV, corresponding to the \(P_c^0(4380)\) and \(P_c^0(4450)\) states.
- The \(P_c^0(4380)\) state has a mass of \(4380 \pm 8 \pm 29\) MeV and a width of \(205 \pm 18 \pm 86\) MeV, as reported by LHCb.
- The \(P_c^0(4450)\) state has a mass of \(4449.8 \pm 1.7 \pm 2.5\) MeV and a width of \(39 \pm 5 \pm 19\) MeV, as reported by LHCb.
- The product branching fractions from LHCb are: \(\mathcal{B}(\Lambda_b^0 \to P_c^+(4380) K^-)\mathcal{B}(P_c^+(4380) \to J/\psi p) = 2.56 \pm 0.22 \pm 1.28^{+0.46}_{-0.36} \times 10^{-5}\) and \(\mathcal{B}(\Lambda_b^0 \to P_c^+(4450) K^-)\mathcal{B}(P_c^+(4450) \to J/\psi p) = 1.25 \pm 0.15 \pm 0.33^{+0.22}_{-0.18} \times 10^{-5}\).
- The coupling constants for \(P_c N J/\psi\) and \(P_c N \pi\) vertices are tabulated for different spin-parity assignments; for example, for \(P_c(4380)\) with \(J^P = 3/2^+\), the \(J/\psi N\) coupling is 1.09 and the \(\pi N\) coupling is \(8.56 \times 10^{-3}\).

## Context

This work extends the search for hidden charm pentaquarks from the charged \(P_c^+\) states observed by LHCb (2015) in \(\Lambda_b^0 \to J/\psi K^- p\) decays to their neutral partners, \(P_c^0\). It builds on prior theoretical studies of \(P_c\) production in \(\gamma p \to J/\psi p\) reactions (Wang 2015, Karliner 2015) and in \(\Lambda_b \to J/\psi \bar{K}^0 n\) decays (Lebed 2015, Cheng 2015). The paper enables future experimental searches at J-PARC, where pion beams with energies up to 20 GeV and high luminosity could test the predicted total cross sections and angular distributions, potentially distinguishing between different spin-parity assignments for the \(P_c\) states.
