# Skin values and matter radii of $^{208}$Pb and $^{58,60,64}$Ni based on reaction cross section of $^{3,4}$He scattering (published in Results in Physics)

> The PREX group reported a new skin value, $r_{\rm skin}^{208}({\rm PREX2}) = 0.283 \pm 0.071{\rm fm}$. Using the chiral (Kyushu) $g$-matrix folding model with the proton and neutron densities determined with D1S+GHFB+AMP, we determined neutron skin thickness $r_{\rm skin}^{208}({\rm exp})$ from reaction cross sections $σ_{\rm R}({\rm exp})$ of p+$^{208}$Pb scattering. The method also yielded $r_{\rm skin}^{208}({\rm exp})$ from $σ_{\rm R}({\rm exp})$ of $^{4}$He+$^{208}$Pb scattering. We accumulated the 206 EoSs and determined a sloop parameter from the 206 EoSs. The value yields $r_{\rm skin}^{208}=0.102 \sim 0.354~{\rm fm}$. As the first aim, we first determine $r_{\rm skin}^{208}({\rm exp})$ from $σ_{\rm R}({\rm exp})$ of $^{3}$He scattering on $^{208}$Pb target and take the weighted mean and its error for $r_{\rm skin}^{208}({\rm PREX2})$, three skin values of p+$^{208}$Pb, $^{3, 4}$He+$^{208}$Pb scattering and the $r_{\rm skin}^{208}$ based on the 206 EoSs. As the second aim, we determine matter radii $r_{m}({\rm exp})$ of $^{58,60,64}$Ni from $σ_{\rm R}({\rm exp})$ of $^{3,4}$He scattering on $^{58,60,64}$Ni targets. Our result is $r_{\rm skin}^{208}({\rm exp}) =0.512 \pm 0.268~{\rm fm}$ for $^{3}$He+$^{208}$Pb scattering. Our conclusion is $r_{\rm skin}^{208} =0.285 \pm 0.030~{\rm fm}$. It is determined from the 5 skin values mentioned above.

## Key claim

The paper determines a combined neutron skin thickness for \(^{208}\)Pb of \(r_{\rm skin}^{208} = 0.285 \pm 0.030\) fm by taking the weighted mean of five independent skin values, including a new extraction from \(^{3}\)He+\(^{208}\)Pb scattering and a theoretical constraint from 206 equations of state (EoSs), but the individual \(^{3}\)He result has a large uncertainty of \(\pm 0.268\) fm.

## Method

The authors use the chiral (Kyushu) \(g\)-matrix folding model for scattering, combined with proton and neutron densities calculated using the Gogny-D1S Hartree-Fock-Bogoliubov method with angular momentum projection (D1S-GHFB+AMP). For \(^{208}\)Pb, they extract the neutron skin thickness from experimental reaction cross sections \(\sigma_{\rm R}({\rm exp})\) of \(^{3}\)He+\(^{208}\)Pb scattering by scaling the D1S-GHFB+AMP densities to reproduce the data, under the constraint that the proton radius matches the electron scattering value \(r_{\rm p}({\rm exp}) = 5.444\) fm. For \(^{58,60,64}\)Ni, they determine matter radii \(r_{\rm m}({\rm exp})\) from \(\sigma_{\rm R}({\rm exp})\) of both \(^{3}\)He and \(^{4}\)He scattering using the same folding model and density scaling procedure.

## Key numbers

*   Neutron skin thickness of \(^{208}\)Pb from \(^{3}\)He+\(^{208}\)Pb scattering: \(r_{\rm skin}^{208}({\rm exp}) = 0.512 \pm 0.268\) fm, extracted from data in the energy range \(32 \leq E_{\rm lab} \leq 56\) MeV per nucleon.
*   Combined neutron skin thickness of \(^{208}\)Pb from five skin values: \(r_{\rm skin}^{208} = 0.285 \pm 0.030\) fm.
*   Matter radius of \(^{58}\)Ni from \(^{4}\)He scattering: \(r_{\rm m}^{58}({\rm exp}) = 3.734 \pm 0.091\) fm.
*   Matter radius of \(^{60}\)Ni from \(^{4}\)He scattering: \(r_{\rm m}^{60}({\rm exp}) = 3.815 \pm 0.094\) fm.
*   Matter radius of \(^{64}\)Ni from \(^{4}\)He scattering: \(r_{\rm m}^{64}({\rm exp}) = 4.001 \pm 0.093\) fm.
*   Matter radius of \(^{58}\)Ni from \(^{3}\)He scattering: \(r_{\rm m}^{58}({\rm exp}) = 3.709 \pm 0.098\) fm.
*   Matter radius of \(^{60}\)Ni from \(^{3}\)He scattering: \(r_{\rm m}^{60}({\rm exp}) = 3.753 \pm 0.112\) fm.
*   Matter radius of \(^{64}\)Ni from \(^{3}\)He scattering: \(r_{\rm m}^{64}({\rm exp}) = 3.822 \pm 0.142\) fm.
*   Theoretical constraint on \(r_{\rm skin}^{208}\) from 206 EoSs: \(r_{\rm skin}^{208} = 0.102 \sim 0.354\) fm, derived from the slope parameter range \(L = 5.020 \sim 161.05\) MeV.

## Context

This work extends the authors' previous determinations of neutron skin thickness for \(^{208}\)Pb from p+\(^{208}\)Pb scattering (Tagami 2020) and \(^{4}\)He+\(^{208}\)Pb scattering (Matsuzaki 2021) by adding a new extraction from \(^{3}\)He+\(^{208}\)Pb scattering. It also provides matter radii for \(^{58,60,64}\)Ni from both \(^{3}\)He and \(^{4}\)He scattering, which are consistent with the GSI storage-ring measurement for \(^{58}\)Ni (Zamora 2017). The combined result \(r_{\rm skin}^{208} = 0.285 \pm 0.030\) fm, which includes the PREX2 value (Adhikari 2021), provides a more precise constraint on the symmetry energy slope parameter \(L\) and enables improved tests of nuclear equation-of-state models.
