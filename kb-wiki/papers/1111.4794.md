# Strangeness Production in Hadron Reactions

> The paper gives an overview of strangeness-production experiments at the Cooler Synchrotron COSY. Results on kaon-pair and $ϕ$ meson production in $pp$, $pd$ and $dd$ collisions, hyperon-production experiments and $Λp$ final-state interaction studies are presented as well as a search for a strangeness $S=-1$ resonance in the $Λp$ system.

## Key claim

The paper provides an overview of strangeness-production experiments at the COSY accelerator, but the abstract glosses over the fact that many results are preliminary, model-dependent, or limited by statistics and systematic uncertainties; for example, the extracted scattering lengths for the K⁻p system have large uncertainties and the search for a strangeness S=-1 dibaryon yielded only upper limits, not a discovery.

## Method

The experiments were performed at the Cooler Synchrotron COSY using internal (ANKE, COSY-11) and external (TOF, BIG KARL) spectrometers with unpolarized proton and deuteron beams up to about 3.7 GeV/c. Total and differential cross sections for kaon-pair, phi meson, and hyperon production in pp, pd, and dd collisions were measured as functions of excess energy, and final-state interactions were analyzed via scattering length extraction and Dalitz plot techniques. The search for a strangeness S=-1 resonance in the Λp system used high-resolution missing-mass spectroscopy and chi-squared analysis to set upper limits.

## Key numbers

- Total cross section for kaon-pair production parameterized as σ_tot = 0.00137 ε^2.376 nb (ε in MeV) for most channels except dd → K⁺K⁻α.
- K⁻p scattering length from FSI analysis: a_{K⁻p} = (0 + 1.5i) fm (Ref. Mae08).
- K⁺K⁻ scattering length: a_{K⁺K⁻} = [0.5_{-0.5}^{+4} + (3 ± 3)i] fm (Ref. Sil09).
- Chiral SU(3) unitary approach gives a₀ = (-1.64 + 0.75i) fm and a₁ = (-0.06 + 0.57i) fm.
- Effective range parameters for pp → K⁺Λp: a_s = -2.43^{+0.16}_{-0.25} fm, r_s = 2.21^{+0.16}_{-0.36} fm.
- Ratio of phi to omega production: R_{φ/ω} = 8 × R_OZI for pp reactions and R_{φ/ω} = 20 × R_OZI for pd reactions, with R_OZI = 4.2 × 10⁻³.
- Resonance mass in K⁺Λ channel: m(N*(1650)) = 1.65 ± 0.15 GeV/c² with width Γ = 0.15 GeV/c².
- Upper limit for resonance production cross section in pp → K⁺D_s: dσ^r/dΩ_K = 42 nb/sr at M_r = 2096.5 MeV, Γ = 500 keV (not observed; upper limits given for Γ = 100 keV, 500 keV, 1.0 MeV).

## Context

This review summarizes a decade of COSY experiments on strangeness production, extending earlier work at SATURNE (Bal01) and KEK (Ito98) and providing new data on final-state interactions and resonance searches. The results on K⁻p scattering lengths are compared with chiral SU(3) calculations by Borasoy et al. (2006) and with elastic scattering data from KEK and DEAR (Gua04), showing discrepancies that motivate further theoretical work. The search for a strangeness S=-1 dibaryon, predicted by Aerts and Dover (1985), sets upper limits that constrain future searches at higher-intensity facilities.
