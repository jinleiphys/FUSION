# Nucleon form factors with Nf=2 dynamical twisted mass fermions

> We present results on the electromagnetic and axial nucleon form factors using two degenerate flavors of twisted mass fermions on lattices of spatial size 2.1 fm and 2.7 fm and a lattice spacing of about 0.09 fm. We consider pion masses in the range of 260-470 MeV. We chirally extrapolate results on the nucleon axial ch arge, the isovector Dirac and Pauli root mean squared radii and magnetic moment to the physical point and co mpare to experiment.

## Key claim

The authors present lattice QCD results for nucleon electromagnetic and axial form factors using two degenerate flavors of twisted mass fermions, finding that the lattice data show a flatter dependence on momentum transfer \(Q^2\) than experiment, and that chiral extrapolations of the axial charge and Dirac radius are sensitive to the inclusion of data from other collaborations.

## Method

The authors compute nucleon two-point and three-point correlation functions on gauge configurations with two degenerate flavors of twisted mass fermions at a single lattice spacing of about 0.09 fm, using spatial lattice sizes of 2.1 fm and 2.7 fm. They extract the isovector electromagnetic form factors (electric \(G_E\) and magnetic \(G_M\)) and axial form factors (\(G_A\) and \(G_p\)) from ratios of three-point to two-point functions, using sequential inversions through the sink with the nucleon at rest. The lattice scale is set using the nucleon mass in the physical limit, and results are chirally extrapolated to the physical pion mass using heavy baryon chiral perturbation theory.

## Key numbers

- Pion masses considered: 260–470 MeV.
- Lattice spacing: about 0.09 fm (specifically \(a_{\beta=3.9}=0.089(1)(5)\) fm).
- Spatial lattice sizes: 2.1 fm and 2.7 fm.
- Number of gauge configurations used: 667 at \(m_\pi=258\) MeV on a \(32^3\times 64\) lattice; 944 at \(m_\pi=302\) MeV on a \(24^3\times 48\) lattice; 477 at \(m_\pi=465\) MeV on a \(24^3\times 48\) lattice.
- Axial charge at the physical point from chiral extrapolation using only TMF data: \(g_A = 1.13(10)\).
- Axial charge at the chiral point from fit: \(g_A(0)=1.10(13)\).
- Scale parameter from nucleon mass fit to \({\cal O}(p^3)\) HB\(\chi\)PT: \(r_0=0.462(5)\) fm.
- Scale parameter from \({\cal O}(p^4)\) HB\(\chi\)PT: \(r_0=0.489(11)\) fm.
- Renormalization constant for the axial current: \(Z_A=0.76(1)\) (from RI'-MOM scheme) or \(Z_A=0.768(3)\) (from perturbative subtraction of \({\cal O}(a^2)\) terms).

## Context

This work extends lattice QCD calculations of nucleon form factors using twisted mass fermions, building on earlier results from the same collaboration for baryon masses (ETMC-baryons) and meson properties (ETMC-mesons). The results are compared to those from dynamical domain wall fermions (DWF) by the RBC-UKQCD collaborations (Ohta 2008) and to experimental data. The authors note that their results for the isovector magnetic moment and Dirac and Pauli radii agree with DWF results, and that the axial charge at the physical point is close to the experimental value but with a large error from the chiral extrapolation. The analysis at a finer lattice spacing (\(\beta=4.05\)) is stated to be underway to check for cutoff effects.
