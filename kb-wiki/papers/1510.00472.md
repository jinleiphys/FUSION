# Non-perturbative collisional energy loss of heavy quarks in quark-gluon plasma

> We suggest a new mechanism for the energy loss of fast heavy quarks in quark-gluon plasma. This mechanism is based on pion production caused by the anomalous chromomagnetic quark-gluon-pion interaction induced by strong topological fluctuations of the gluon fields represented by instantons. We found that this mechanism gives a considerable contribution to the collisional energy loss of a heavy quark in quark-gluon plasma, which shows a nontrivial role of nonperturbative phenomena in strongly interacting quark-gluon plasma.

## Key claim

The paper proposes that a nonperturbative mechanism based on instanton-induced chromomagnetic quark-gluon-pion interactions gives a contribution to heavy quark collisional energy loss in quark-gluon plasma that is comparable to or larger than the perturbative QCD contribution, with the caveat that the result is sensitive to the effective quark mass in the instanton vacuum.

## Method

The authors compute the collisional energy loss of a charm quark in quark-gluon plasma using Bjorken's formula with cross sections derived from an effective Lagrangian that includes instanton-generated chromomagnetic quark-gluon and quark-gluon-pion interactions. They evaluate two diagrams: one with a quark-gluon vertex only and one with an additional pion emission, integrating over parton densities at temperature T and using the instanton form factor with a cutoff at the sphaleron energy. The results are compared with the standard perturbative QCD collisional energy loss formula from Peigne and Peshier (2008).

## Key numbers

- The ratio of nonperturbative to perturbative collisional energy loss, R = dE^np(b)/dx divided by dE^pert/dx, is 1.2 to 1.3 for charm quarks in the energy range 10–30 GeV at T = 2 T_c with effective quark mass M_q = 86 MeV.
- Using a higher effective quark mass M_q = 170 MeV, the ratio R becomes approximately 4.
- The average instanton size is taken as rho_c = 1/3 fm, giving alpha_s(rho_c^{-2}) ≈ 0.5.
- The deconfinement temperature is T_c = 150 MeV, and the charm quark mass is M_c = 1.3 GeV.
- The Debye mass is M_D(T) ≈ 3 T, following lattice QCD calculations.
- The sphaleron energy is E_sph = 3π/(4 rho_c alpha_s(rho_c^{-2})).

## Context

This work extends the perturbative collisional energy loss framework of Bjorken (1982) and Peigne and Peshier (2008) by incorporating nonperturbative effects from instantons, which are known to generate effective interactions in QCD (Shuryak 1983; Diakonov 2002). The mechanism is related to the anomalous chromomagnetic quark-gluon interaction of Kochelev (1998) and the quark-gluon-pion interaction used in studies of inclusive pion production in proton-proton collisions (Kochelev et al. 2015). The results suggest that the nonperturbative contribution could reduce the need for a large radiative energy loss factor (K ≈ 3.5) to explain heavy quark suppression data from RHIC and LHC, and open the question of how similar mechanisms affect light quark energy loss.
