# Erratum: Eur. Phy. J. C 10 (1999) 487 (Photon production in relativistic heavy-ion collisions using rates with two-loop calculations from quark matter)

> It has been shown recently shown that the values of $J_T$ and $J_L$ which appear in Eq.(2) and (5) of the above paper and which are taken from the work of Aurenche et al are too large by a factor of 4. Correcting for this changes the Fig.~1--4. The basic result of the paper, that the emissions from the quark matter can outshine those from the hadronic matter when the photon rates upto two-loop level are used, remains valid though the range of $p_T$ over which it happens is reduced to $p_T <$ 0.5, 1, and 2 GeV/$c$ respecitively at SPS, RHIC, and LHC energies.

## Key claim

The central claim of the original paper, that two-loop photon emission rates from quark matter can exceed those from hadronic matter, remains valid after correcting a factor-of-4 error in the quantities \(J_T\) and \(J_L\) taken from Aurenche et al., but the transverse-momentum range over which this occurs is reduced to \(p_T < 0.5\), 1, and 2 GeV/\(c\) at SPS, RHIC, and LHC energies, respectively.

## Method

The erratum corrects a factor-of-4 overestimate in the values of \(J_T\) and \(J_L\) that appear in Eqs. (2) and (5) of the original paper, which were taken from the work of Aurenche et al. The corrected values are used to recompute the photon spectra shown in Figs. 1–4 of the original publication, which compare two-loop quark-matter photon rates with hadronic-matter rates in relativistic heavy-ion collisions at SPS, RHIC, and LHC energies.

## Key numbers

- The corrected \(p_T\) range for which quark-matter two-loop photon emission outshines hadronic emission is \(p_T < 0.5\) GeV/\(c\) at SPS energies.
- The corrected \(p_T\) range for which quark-matter two-loop photon emission outshines hadronic emission is \(p_T < 1\) GeV/\(c\) at RHIC energies.
- The corrected \(p_T\) range for which quark-matter two-loop photon emission outshines hadronic emission is \(p_T < 2\) GeV/\(c\) at LHC energies.

## Context

This erratum corrects a numerical error in the original paper by Srivastava (1999), which used two-loop photon rates from Aurenche et al. The error reduced the transverse-momentum window where quark-matter emission dominates, but the qualitative conclusion, that two-loop rates can make the quark-gluon plasma a stronger photon source than hadronic matter, still holds. This work enables more reliable comparisons of direct photon spectra from heavy-ion collisions at SPS, RHIC, and LHC, and it underscores the importance of precise two-loop calculations in thermal photon phenomenology.
