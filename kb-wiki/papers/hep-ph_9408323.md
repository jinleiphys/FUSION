# Threshold Pion Photo- and Electroproduction in Chiral Perturbation Theory

> I present some recent results on neutral pion photo- and electroproduction in the threshold region and discuss the low energy theorem for $E_{0+}$ and some novel ones for the $P$-waves. I also study the two-pion production process $γN \to ππN$ from which complementary information can be gained.

## Key claim

The paper argues that the traditional low-energy theorem (LET) for the electric dipole amplitude \(E_{0+}\) in neutral pion photoproduction is actually only a "low-energy guess" (LEG), because a novel contribution from the triangle diagram (a Goldstone boson loop) enters at next-to-leading order in the quark mass expansion, invalidating the assumptions used in the original derivation.

## Method

The author performs a systematic one-loop calculation of threshold pion photo- and electroproduction using baryon chiral perturbation theory (CHPT) in the heavy-mass formulation, working in the isospin limit with SU(2) flavor and including terms up to \({\cal O}(q^4)\). The effective Lagrangian includes pion-nucleon interactions up to \({\cal O}(q^4)\), with counterterms determined either by fitting to total and differential photocross sections or by resonance saturation (e.g., \(\Delta\), \(\rho\), \(\omega\) exchange). The calculation yields the multipoles \(E_{0+}\) and the \(P\)-waves (\(P_1, P_2, P_3\)) for neutral pions, and also extends to the two-pion production process \(\gamma N \to \pi\pi N\).

## Key numbers

* \(E_{0+}\) at threshold for \(\gamma p \to \pi^0 p\) is given by \(E_{0+} = -\frac{e g_{\pi N}}{8 \pi m} \mu \left\lbrace 1 - \left[ \frac{1}{2} (3 + \kappa_p) + \left(\frac{m}{4 F_\pi}\right)^2 \right] \mu + {\cal O}(\mu^2) \right\rbrace\), where \(\mu = M_\pi/m \approx 1/7\) and \(\kappa_p = 3.71\) is the anomalous magnetic moment of the proton.
* The triangle diagram contributes a term \(\delta A_1 = (e g_{\pi N} / 32 F_\pi^2) \mu\) to the Lorentz invariant amplitude \(A_1\), which enters at next-to-leading order in the quark mass expansion.
* The \(P\)-waves \(P_{1,2}\) are proportional to \(|\vec q|\) (pion three-momentum) at leading order, while \(P_3\) is essentially given by a single counterterm \(b = 4b_{14} + g_A b_{22}\) plus a small Born term.

## Context

This work extends the systematic CHPT analysis of threshold pion photoproduction, challenging the original low-energy theorem derived in the 1970s (e.g., by de Baenst, 1970) which was based on chiral symmetry and "harmless" assumptions. The new data from Mainz (1994) on \(\pi^0\) photoproduction and electroproduction (with unprecedented accuracy) motivated this reanalysis. The results enable future predictions for electroproduction observables and for the two-pion production process \(\gamma N \to \pi\pi N\) near threshold, which is being measured at Mainz.
