# Two-photon exchange effects in $e^+e^- \rightarrow π^+π^-$ and time-like pion electromagnetic form factor

> The two-photon-exchange (TPE) effects in the process $e^+e^- \rightarrow π^+π^-$ at large momentum transfer are discussed within the perturbative QCD (pQCD). The contributions from the twist-2 and twist-3 distribution amplitudes (DAs) of pion are considered in the estimation. Different with the results under the one-photon-exchange (OPE) approximation, the TPE effects result in an asymmetry of the differential cross section on the scattering angle. The precise measurement of this asymmetry by the further experiment is an precise test of pQCD at large momentum transfer. The time-like electromagnetic form factor of pion at the leading order of pQCD is re-discussed and the comparison of our results with those in the references are presented.

## Key claim

Two-photon-exchange (TPE) effects in $e^+e^- \rightarrow \pi^+\pi^-$ at large momentum transfer produce an asymmetry in the differential cross section as a function of scattering angle, with the magnitude of the TPE contributions to the cross section reaching about 10-20% at small angle, contrary to the expectation that TPE corrections in such processes are negligible.

## Method

The authors performed perturbative QCD (pQCD) calculations of the two-photon-exchange amplitudes for $e^+e^- \rightarrow \pi^+\pi^-$ at leading order in $\alpha_s$, including contributions from both twist-2 and twist-3 pion distribution amplitudes (DAs). They computed the interference between the one-photon-exchange and two-photon-exchange amplitudes to obtain the angle-dependent differential cross section, using Sudakov factors and threshold resummation in $b$-space, with numerical integration performed via Mathematica's NIntegrate and checked with the Vegas algorithm in the Cuba package.

## Key numbers

- The magnitude $Q^2|\tilde{F}_\pi(Q^2,\theta)|$ of the TPE general form factor is about 10-20% of $Q^2|F_\pi(Q^2)|$ at small $\theta$, indicating the absolute TPE contributions are not small.
- The pion decay constant used is $f_\pi = 0.131$ GeV.
- The chiral scale is taken as $\mu_\pi = 1.3$ GeV.
- The shape parameter in the threshold resummation factor $S_t(x)$ is $c = 0.4$.
- The Gegenbauer coefficient for the twist-2 DA is $a_2 = 0.2$.
- The number of flavors is $n_f = 3$ and $\Lambda = 0.2$ GeV in the Sudakov factor.
- Numerical results for $Q^2|\tilde{F}_\pi(Q^2,\theta)|$ are presented at $Q^2 = 20$ and $50$ GeV$^2$ and at angles $\theta = (1/9, 2/9, 1/3, 4/9)\pi$.

## Context

This work extends pQCD studies of two-photon-exchange effects from the space-like region (elastic $ep$ scattering, where TPE was invoked to resolve the discrepancy between polarized and Rosenbluth methods, e.g., Blunden (2003), Kondratyuk (2005), and the OLYMPUS experiment (2017)) to the time-like annihilation process $e^+e^- \rightarrow \pi^+\pi^-$. The authors also clarify discrepancies in the twist-3 contributions to the time-like pion form factor compared to earlier treatments by Raha (2010), Hao-Chung Hu (2013), and Shang-Chen (2015), noting a sign difference in the $t_2$ term and a factor difference in the $t_3$ term. The predicted asymmetry enables a new experimental test of pQCD factorization at large momentum transfer through precise measurement of the angle-dependent differential cross section.
