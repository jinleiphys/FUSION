# eta meson production in nucleon-nucleon collisions

> New experimental data are presented on the energy dependence of the total cross sections for the np -> d eta and quasi-two-body pp -> pp eta reactions, where the final diproton is detected at very low excitation energy. Differential cross sections of pp -> pp eta away from threshold show the influence of higher partial waves and a partial wave decomposition is attempted.

## Key claim

New experimental data on the energy dependence of total cross sections for the np → dη and quasi-two-body pp → ppη reactions are presented, but the analysis of the np → dη data is very preliminary and the quasi-free assumption used to extract the cross sections may account for less than half of the observed cross section at slightly lower energies.

## Method

The np → dη total cross section was extracted from dp → d p X data at 2.27 GeV using the missing-mass technique, assuming quasi-free production with a spectator proton. The pp → ppη reaction was studied at excess energies Q = 40 and 72 MeV using CELSIUS-WASA data from the two-photon decay branch, with a partial wave decomposition up to D-waves in the incident pp system and constant amplitudes multiplied by kinematic factors and the pp final state interaction.

## Key numbers

- The ratio of the dη production cross section to phase space drops by about a factor of four over 10 MeV in the ηd excitation energy.
- The pp → ppη reaction was investigated at Q = 40 and 72 MeV.
- The ANKE spectrometer measures the quasi-two-body pp → {pp}_Sη reaction with a diproton excitation energy cut below 3 MeV and cosθ_η > 0.95.
- The pp → {pp}_Sη cross section was measured at Q ≈ 55 MeV.
- The η′ signal in pp → ppX at 2.57 GeV shows approximately 500 expected events.

## Context

This work extends studies of η-meson production in nucleon-nucleon collisions, building on earlier CELSIUS experiments (Calen 1997, Bilger 2002) that investigated the ηd interaction. The possibility of η-nucleus quasi-bound states was first suggested by Haider and Liu (1986), and the author (Wilkin 1993) argued that pd → ³He η data might signal the formation of η³He. The new results enable further investigation of the ηd and ηpp final state interactions, with the quasi-two-body pp → {pp}_Sη reaction offering a cleaner system to study the η{pp}_S relative momentum dependence.
