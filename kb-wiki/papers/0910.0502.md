# Nucleon-Nucleon effective field theory with dibaryon fields

> We report on a calculation of the nucleon-nucleon scattering amplitudes in the $^1S_0$ and $^3S_1$-$^3D_1$ channels at next-to-next to leading order using a recently proposed non-relativistic chiral effective theory, which includes dibaryon field as fundamental degrees of freedom.

## Key claim

The paper reports that a chiral effective field theory including dibaryon fields achieves a good description of nucleon-nucleon scattering data in the 1S0 channel up to about 35 MeV center-of-mass energy at next-to-next-to-leading order (N2LO), but fails to describe the 3S1-3D1 channel beyond about 15 MeV, with the N2LO calculation actually comparing worse to data than the NLO one.

## Method

The authors calculate nucleon-nucleon scattering amplitudes in the 1S0 and 3S1-3D1 channels at N2LO using a non-relativistic chiral effective theory that includes isovector and isoscalar dibaryon fields as fundamental degrees of freedom. The calculation is organized in a sequence of effective theories: for momenta of order the pion mass (pNNEFT) and for momenta much smaller than the pion mass (pionless NNEFT), with matching performed using dimensional regularization and minimal subtraction. Phase shifts are computed from diagrams involving dibaryon propagators, pion exchanges, and dibaryon-nucleon vertices, with parameters fit to Nijmegen phase shift data in the 0-5 MeV range for the pionless theory and 5-20 MeV range for pNNEFT.

## Key numbers

* 1S0 channel: dibaryon residual mass delta_m_s = -1.51 MeV (pionless region), -2.25 MeV (NLO pNNEFT), -10.5 MeV (N2LO pNNEFT)
* 1S0 channel: dibaryon-nucleon coupling A_s = 0.0243 MeV^{-1} (pionless region), 0.0302 MeV^{-1} (NLO pNNEFT), 0.0251 MeV^{-1} (N2LO pNNEFT)
* 3S1-3D1 channel: dibaryon residual mass delta_m_v = 7.68 MeV (pionless region), 13.4 MeV (NLO pNNEFT), 45.3 MeV (N2LO pNNEFT)
* 3S1-3D1 channel: dibaryon-nucleon coupling A_v = 0.0265 MeV^{-1} (pionless region), 0.0359 MeV^{-1} (NLO pNNEFT), 0.0110 MeV^{-1} (N2LO pNNEFT)
* 3S1-3D1 channel: ratio B_v'/A_v = 12.4e-6 MeV^{-2} (NLO pNNEFT)
* Good description of 1S0 channel data up to about 35 MeV center-of-mass energy
* 3S1-3D1 channel description fails beyond about 15 MeV

## Context

This work extends the dibaryon effective field theory approach previously proposed by Soto (2007) from NLO to N2LO, and is motivated by the need to check convergence after the KSW approach (Kaplan, Savage, and Wise, 1998) showed good NLO results but bad convergence in the 3S1 channel at N2LO (Fleming, 1999). The numerical results for phase shifts and mixing angle are similar to the KSW scheme, but the authors note their final expressions are simpler. The failure in the 3S1-3D1 channel is attributed to the iteration of the one-pion-exchange potential giving a very large contribution at N2LO, and the paper enables further investigation of whether this convergence problem can be resolved by alternative resummation schemes or higher-order calculations.
