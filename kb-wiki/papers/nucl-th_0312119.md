# Photoproduction of pentaquark cascades from nucleons

> The cross sections for production of pentaquark $Ξ^+_5$ from the reaction $γp\to K^0K^0Ξ^+_5$ and $Ξ^{--}_5$ from the reaction $γn\to K^+K^+Ξ^{--}_5$ are evaluated in a hadronic model that includes their couplings to both $Σ\bar K$ and $Σ\bar K^*$. With these coupling constants determined from the empirical $πNN(1710)$ and $ρNN(1710)$ coupling constants by assuming that $Ξ^+_5$, $Ξ^{--}_5$, and N(1710) belong to the same antidecuplet of spin 1/2 and positive parity, and using form factors at strong interaction vertices similar to those for pentaquark $Θ^+$ production in photonucleon reactions, we obtain a cross section of about 0.03-0.6 nb for the reaction $γp\to K^0K^0Ξ^+_5$ and about 0.1-0.6 nb for the reaction $γn\to K^+K^+Ξ^{--}_5$ at photon energy $E_γ=4.5$ GeV, depending on the value of the coupling constant $g_{K^*ΣΞ_5}$.

## Key claim

The cross sections for photoproduction of pentaquark cascades Ξ⁺₅ and Ξ⁻⁻₅ from nucleons are evaluated in a hadronic model, yielding values of 0.03–0.6 nb and 0.1–0.6 nb respectively at photon energy Eγ = 4.5 GeV, but these values depend on the sign and magnitude of the coupling constant gK*ΣΞ₅, which is not fixed by SU(3) symmetry.

## Method

The authors use a hadronic model based on SU(3) flavor-invariant Lagrangians extended to include interactions of pentaquark Ξ₅ with Σ and K or K* mesons. Coupling constants for the Ξ₅ vertices are determined from the empirical πNN(1710) and ρNN(1710) coupling constants by assuming Ξ⁺₅, Ξ⁻⁻₅, and N(1710) belong to the same antidecuplet of spin 1/2 and positive parity. Form factors with a cutoff parameter Λ = 1.7 GeV are applied at strong interaction vertices, and cross sections are computed for the reactions γp → K⁰K⁰Ξ⁺₅ and γn → K⁺K⁺Ξ⁻⁻₅ as functions of photon energy.

## Key numbers

- Cross section for γp → K⁰K⁰Ξ⁺₅: 0.03–0.6 nb at photon energy Eγ = 4.5 GeV, depending on the value of gK*ΣΞ₅
- Cross section for γn → K⁺K⁺Ξ⁻⁻₅: 0.1–0.6 nb at photon energy Eγ = 4.5 GeV, depending on the value of gK*ΣΞ₅
- Coupling constants used: gKΣΞ₅ = 2.16, gK*ΣΞ₅ = ±1.27 or 0 (from gKNΘ = 3.06 and gK*NΘ = 1.8)
- Cutoff parameter for form factors: Λ = 1.7 GeV, determined from fitting charmed hadron production data
- Empirical coupling constants used for SU(3) relations: gπNN = 13.5, gρNN = 3.25, with α = D/(D+F) = 0.64

## Context

This work extends the same hadronic model previously applied to pentaquark Θ⁺ photoproduction (Liu, Ko, and collaborators, 2003) to the production of the heavier pentaquark cascades Ξ⁺₅ and Ξ⁻⁻₅, which belong to the same antidecuplet as Θ⁺. The predicted cross sections are about an order of magnitude smaller than those for Θ⁺ production in photonucleon reactions. The study enables experimental searches for these pentaquark states at photon facilities and provides a framework for testing whether the parity of Ξ₅ is positive or negative, since negative parity would lead to much smaller cross sections.
