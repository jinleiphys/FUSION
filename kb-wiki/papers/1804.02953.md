# B $\rightarrow f_2(1270)$ form factors with light-cone sum rules

> We construct the quark-antiquark chiral odd distribution amplitudes including twist-four mass contributions for tensor mesons. We also give quark-antiquark-gluon distribution amplitudes, where we calculate the input parameters with QCD sum rules. With the help of equations of motion we determine the twist-three and twist-four distribution amplitudes including SU(3) breaking terms. We use QCD light-come sum rules to derive the form factors for the decay B $\rightarrow f_2(1270)$ with vector, axial-vector and tensor currents. We also give the $q^2$ dependence of the form factors.

## Key claim

The authors provide the first calculation of the chiral odd quark-antiquark distribution amplitudes for tensor mesons including twist-four mass contributions, and use these to derive the form factors for the decay B → f₂(1270) via light-cone sum rules, finding that the meson mass terms have a noticeable impact on the sum rules and should be taken into account in future studies.

## Method

The authors construct chiral odd quark-antiquark distribution amplitudes (DAs) including twist-four mass contributions for the tensor meson f₂(1270), and also give quark-antiquark-gluon DAs with input parameters calculated using QCD sum rules. Using equations of motion, they determine twist-three and twist-four DAs including SU(3) breaking terms. They then apply QCD light-cone sum rules (LCSR) to derive the form factors for the B → f₂(1270) decay with vector, axial-vector and tensor currents, and provide the q² dependence of the form factors fitted to a three-parameter form.

## Key numbers

- f₂(1270) mass: m_f₂ = 1.275 GeV; B meson mass: m_B = 5.279 GeV
- Decay constants at scale μ = 1 GeV: f_f₂ = 0.101(10) GeV, f_f₂^T = 0.117(25) GeV
- Pole b-quark mass: m_b = 4.8(1) GeV
- Borel parameter window: M² = 4–8 GeV²; duality threshold: s₀ = 35.5 ± 2 GeV²
- Form factor values at q² = 0: Ṽ(0) = 0.30 ± 0.03, Ã₁(0) = 0.17 ± 0.01, Ã₂(0) = 0.11 ± 0.02, Ã₀(0) = 0.22 ± 0.02, T̃₁(0) = 0.11 ± 0.02, T̃₂(0) = 0.12 ± 0.01, T̃₃(0) = -0.02 ± 0.04
- Maximum q² for LCSR approximation: q²_max = (m_B - m_f₂)² = 16.07 GeV²; fit range limited to 0 ≤ q² ≤ 10 GeV²
- Three-particle DA parameters at 1 GeV: ξ₃ = 0.15(8), ω₃ = -0.2(3), ω̃₃ = 0.06(1)
- Effect of meson mass terms on form factors at q² = 0 is less than 30% for all form factors; for Ã₀(q²) the effect is less than 13% over the whole q² range

## Context

This work extends previous studies of tensor meson distribution amplitudes by Braun et al. (2016) and earlier work on chiral even DAs, and provides the first calculation of chiral odd DAs including twist-four mass contributions and SU(3) breaking terms. The results enable more precise standard model predictions for B → f₂(1270) decays, which are sensitive probes for new physics through the polarization of the final tensor meson. The authors compare their results with earlier LCSR calculations by Wang (2010) and a perturbative QCD approach, finding that their inclusion of higher twist contributions and three-particle DAs yields significantly improved precision, particularly for the form factor Ṽ(0).
