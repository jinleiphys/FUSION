# Direct quarkonium-plus-gluon production in DIS in the Color Glass Condensate

> We compute the differential cross-section for direct quarkonium production accompanied by a gluon in high-energy deep inelastic scattering (DIS) at small-$x$. We employ the Non-Relativistic QCD factorization framework, focusing on the $S$-wave contribution to the formation of the quarkonium, and including both color singlet and octet contributions. Our short distance coefficients for the production of the heavy quark pair are obtained within the Color Glass Condensate effective field theory. Our results pave the way towards the next-to-leading order computation of direct quarkonium in DIS, as well as the study of azimuthal correlations of direct quarkonium and jet.

## Key claim

The authors compute the differential cross-section for direct quarkonium production accompanied by a gluon in deep inelastic scattering at small-x, using the Color Glass Condensate effective theory combined with Non-Relativistic QCD factorization, but only for the S-wave contribution and at leading order, with the explicit caveat that the P-wave state computation is deferred to a future publication.

## Method

The authors employ covariant perturbation theory within the Color Glass Condensate effective field theory, using momentum-space Feynman rules with CGC effective vertices that resum multiple eikonal interactions of quarks and gluons with the background field of the nucleus. They compute the four diagrams for heavy quark pair plus gluon production in virtual photon-nucleus collisions, separating contributions where the gluon is emitted before or after the shockwave, and project the heavy quark pair onto S-wave states using spin projectors for both color singlet and color octet contributions within the Non-Relativistic QCD factorization framework.

## Key numbers

* The longitudinal momentum fractions of the quark, antiquark, and gluon with respect to the photon are defined as x_q = p_q^+/q^+, x_antiquark = p_antiquark^+/q^+, and x_g = p_g^+/q^+, with longitudinal momentum conservation giving q^+ = p^+ + p_g^+.
* The photon flux factors are given by f_L(W^2, Q^2) = alpha_em/(pi Q^2 W^2) (1-y) for longitudinal polarization and f_T(W^2, Q^2) = alpha_em/(2 pi Q^2 W^2) [1+(1-y)^2] for transverse polarization, where y = (W^2 + Q^2)/s.
* The spin projector for the 1S0 state at k=0 is given by Pi^{00}(p,0) = -1/sqrt(8 m_Q) gamma^5 (2 m_Q + slashed p), and for the 3S1 state by Pi^{1 J_z}(p,0) = -epsilon_rho^*(J_z)/sqrt(8 m_Q) gamma^rho (2 m_Q + slashed p).

## Context

This work extends the study of direct quarkonium production in DIS at small-x within the CGC effective theory, following the approach of Kang et al. (2023) for quarkonium alone, and complements studies within the transverse momentum dependent framework by Bacchetta et al. (2018) and Boer et al. (2020, 2023). The results enable the next-to-leading order computation of direct quarkonium in DIS and the study of azimuthal correlations of direct quarkonium plus jet production in the saturated regime, building on the heavy quark pair plus gluon calculation in the CGC by Caucal et al. (2021).
