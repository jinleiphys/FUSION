# Extension of the Chiral Perturbation Theory Meson Lagrangian to Order $P^6$

> We have derived the most general chirally invariant Lagrangian ${\cal L}_6$ for the meson sector at order $p^6$. The result provides an extension of the standard Gasser-Leutwyler Lagrangian ${\cal L}_4$ to one higher order, including as well all the odd intrinsic parity terms in the Lagrangian. The most difficult part of the derivation was developing a systematic strategy so as to get all of the independent terms and eliminate the redundant ones in an efficient way. The 'equation of motion' terms, which are redundant in the sense that they can be transformed away via field transformations, are separated out explicitly. The resulting Lagrangian has been separated into groupings of terms contributing to increasingly more complicated processes, so that one does not have to deal with the full result when calculating $p^6$ contributions to simple processes.

## Key claim

The authors derive the most general chirally invariant Lagrangian at order $p^6$ for the meson sector, extending the standard Gasser-Leutwyler Lagrangian at order $p^4$ to one higher order, but note that it appears extremely unlikely that all renormalized coefficients at order $p^6$ may be determined empirically.

## Method

The authors constructed the Lagrangian using basic building blocks consisting of the Goldstone boson matrix U, external field strength tensors, and covariant derivatives, all transformed to have the same chiral transformation properties. They developed a systematic strategy to generate all independent terms while eliminating redundant ones, using total derivative arguments, symmetrization of multiple covariant derivatives, and explicit separation of equation-of-motion terms that can be transformed away via field redefinitions. The resulting terms were organized into groupings by the minimal number of Goldstone boson fields and by complexity of the processes they contribute to.

## Key numbers

* 111 independent terms in the even intrinsic parity sector of the order p^6 Lagrangian
* 32 independent structures in the odd intrinsic parity sector of the order p^6 Lagrangian
* The lowest-order Lagrangian contains 2 free parameters (pion decay constant and scalar quark condensate)
* The order p^4 Lagrangian contains 10 independent structures with renormalized coefficients L_1 through L_10

## Context

This work extends the Gasser-Leutwyler Lagrangian (Gasser and Leutwyler, 1984, 1985) from order p^4 to order p^6, including both even and odd intrinsic parity sectors. The odd intrinsic parity sector result differs from previous determinations by Issler (1993) and Akhoury (1993), which were not in agreement with each other. The Lagrangian enables consistent two-loop calculations in chiral perturbation theory, such as those for gamma gamma -> pi^0 pi^0 reported by Bellucci (1994), and provides the framework for calculating leading-order corrections to processes originating in the Wess-Zumino term.
