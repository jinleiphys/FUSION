> A new constraint of clustering for the AMD calculation is proposed. This new constraint gives us large improvement in studying the cluster structure by AMD which sometimes meets difficulty in giving rise to some specific cluster configurations. The usefulness of this new constraint is verified by applying it to the the study of the third $K^Ï= 0^+$ band of $^{20}$Ne which has been discussed to have $2Îḟ$-$^{12}$C structure. ## Key claim

The authors propose a new "d-constraint" for AMD calculations that constrains the distance between the centers of mass of pre-defined quasi-clusters, which allows the construction of cluster wave functions that are difficult or impossible to obtain using the standard quadrupole deformation (ÎĠ) constraint alone. The claim is hedged by noting that the d-constraint does not guarantee the formation of the intended cluster structure, nucleons may rearrange into different configurations during energy variation.
