# Decoding the nature of $Z_{cs}(3985)$ and establishing the spectrum of charged heavy quarkoniumlike states in chiral effective field theory

> We study the newly observed charmoniumlike state $Z_{cs}(3985)$ in the framework of chiral effective field theory. The interaction kernel of the $\bar{D}_sD^\ast/\bar{D}^\ast_sD$ system is calculated up to the next-to-leading order with the explicit chiral dynamics. With the fitted parameters extracted from the $Z_c(3900)$ data as inputs, the mass, width and event distributions of the $Z_{cs}(3985)$ are very consistent with the experimental measurements. Our studies strongly support the $Z_{cs}(3985)$ as the partner of the $Z_c(3900)$ in the SU(3)$_f$ symmetry and the $\bar{D}_sD^\ast/\bar{D}^\ast_sD$ molecular resonance with the same dynamical origin as the other charged heavy quarkoniumlike states. We precisely predict the resonance parameters of the unobserved states in $\bar{D}_s^\ast D^\ast$, $B^\ast_s \bar{B}/B_s\bar{B}^\ast$ and $B_s^\ast\bar{B}^\ast$ systems, and establish a complete spectrum of the charged charmoniumlike and bottomoniumlike states with the $I(J^P)$ quantum numbers $1(1^+)$ and $\frac{1}{2}(1^+)$, respectively.

## Key claim
The paper argues that the newly observed $Z_{cs}(3985)$ is the SU(3)$_f$ partner of the $Z_c(3900)$ and a $\bar{D}_sD^\ast/\bar{D}^\ast_sD$ molecular resonance, but this conclusion depends on using parameters fitted from $Z_c(3900)$ data as inputs rather than fitting the $Z_{cs}$ data independently; the authors note that re-fitting the $Z_{cs}$ data directly gives very similar results but with slightly larger errors.

## Method
The authors calculate the interaction kernel of the $\bar{D}_sD^\ast/\bar{D}^\ast_sD$ system using chiral effective field theory up to next-to-leading order, including contact terms, one-eta-exchange, and two-kaon-exchange contributions. They solve the Lippmann-Schwinger equation for the production amplitude of $e^+e^-\to K^+(D_s^-D^{\ast0}+D_s^{\ast-}D^0)$ and fit the $K^+$ recoil-mass spectrum using parameters previously extracted from $Z_c(3900)$ data. The pole positions are obtained by analytical continuation of the Green's function to the unphysical Riemann sheet.

## Key numbers
- $Z_{cs}(3985)$ pole mass: $(3982.4^{+4.8}_{-3.4})$ MeV, width: $(11.8^{+5.5}_{-5.2})$ MeV (from the chiral EFT calculation using $Z_c$ parameters as inputs)
- Experimental values from BESIII: $M_{Z_{cs}}^{\text{pole}}=(3982.5_{-2.6}^{+1.8}\pm2.1)$ MeV, $\Gamma_{Z_{cs}}^{\text{pole}}=(12.8_{-4.4}^{+5.3}\pm3.0)$ MeV
- Predicted $Z_{cs}(4125)$ in $\bar{D}_s^\ast D^\ast$ system: mass $4124.2_{-3.7}^{+5.6}$ MeV, width $9.8^{+5.2}_{-4.8}$ MeV, $\Delta m=5.1_{-3.7}^{+5.6}$ MeV above threshold
- Predicted $Z_{bs}(10700)$ in $B^\ast_s \bar{B}/B_s\bar{B}^\ast$ system: mass $10701.9^{+3.9}_{-2.7}$ MeV, width $7.4^{+3.6}_{-4.4}$ MeV, $\Delta m=7.2^{+3.9}_{-2.7}$ MeV
- Predicted $Z_{bs}(10745)$ in $B^\ast_s \bar{B}^\ast$ system: mass $10747.0^{+4.3}_{-3.1}$ MeV, width $7.3^{+3.7}_{-4.6}$ MeV, $\Delta m=6.9^{+4.3}_{-3.1}$ MeV
- Fit quality: $\chi^2/\text{dof}\simeq0.67$ for NLO fit using $Z_c$ parameters; LO fit gave $\chi^2/\text{dof}=1.21$

## Context
This work extends the chiral effective field theory framework previously applied to the isovector $D^{(\ast)}\bar{D}^{(\ast)}$ and $B^{(\ast)}\bar{B}^{(\ast)}$ systems by Wang, Meng, and Zhu (2020) to the strange sector, specifically the $\bar{D}_sD^\ast/\bar{D}^\ast_sD$ system. The study confirms the SU(3)$_f$ symmetry relation between $Z_{cs}(3985)$ and $Z_c(3900)$ proposed by Meng and Zhu (2020), and provides predictions for three unobserved charged heavy quarkoniumlike states with strangeness. The results enable experimental searches for $Z_{cs}(4125)$, $Z_{bs}(10700)$, and $Z_{bs}(10745)$ at facilities such as BESIII and LHCb, and establish a complete spectrum of charged charmoniumlike and bottomoniumlike states with $I(J^P)=1(1^+)$ and $\frac{1}{2}(1^+)$ respectively.
