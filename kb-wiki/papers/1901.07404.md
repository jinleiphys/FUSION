# $Φ$ and $J/Ψ$ Mesons in Cold Nuclear Matter

> We present $Φ$- and $J/Ψ$--nuclear bound state energies and absorption widths for some selected nuclei, using potentials in the local density approximation computed from an effective Lagrangian approach combined with the quark-meson coupling model. Our results suggest that these mesons should form bound states with all the nuclei considered provided that these mesons are produced in nearly recoilless kinematics.

## Key claim

The paper suggests that both phi and J/Psi mesons should form bound states with all five nuclei studied (He-4, C-12, O-16, Au-197, Pb-208), but only if the mesons are produced in nearly recoilless kinematics; the phi meson bound states are sensitive to the cutoff parameter Lambda_K and may disappear when the absorptive potential is included, particularly for He-4.

## Method

The authors computed phi and J/Psi meson self-energies in nuclear matter using an effective Lagrangian approach with the quark-meson coupling (QMC) model, considering only the K-anti-K loop for the phi and the D-anti-D loop for the J/Psi. They then constructed complex optical potentials in the local density approximation using nuclear density distributions from the QMC model, and solved the Klein-Gordon equation to obtain bound state energies and absorption widths for five nuclei.

## Key numbers

- At normal nuclear matter density rho_0 = 0.15 fm^{-3}, the in-medium kaon mass decreases by 13%
- For the phi meson in Au-197 with Lambda_K = 4000 MeV: 1s state binding energy E = -25.0 MeV, half-width Gamma/2 = 15.5 MeV
- For the phi meson in Pb-208 with Lambda_K = 4000 MeV: 1s state binding energy E = -25.8 MeV, half-width Gamma/2 = 16.0 MeV
- For the J/Psi meson in Au-197 with Lambda_D = 6000 MeV: 1s state binding energy E = -19.09 MeV
- For the J/Psi meson in Pb-208 with Lambda_D = 6000 MeV: 1s state binding energy E = -19.67 MeV
- For the phi meson in He-4 with Lambda_K = 4000 MeV: 1s state binding energy E = -1.0 MeV, half-width Gamma/2 = 8.3 MeV
- For the J/Psi meson in He-4 with Lambda_D = 6000 MeV: 1s state binding energy E = -5.52 MeV

## Context

This work extends previous calculations by Cobos-Martinez et al. (2017) on phi meson mass shifts and decay widths in nuclear matter, and by Krein et al. on J/Psi nuclear bound states. The results are relevant for planned experiments at J-PARC and JLab (12 GeV upgrade) searching for vector meson-nucleus bound states, particularly using light and medium-heavy nuclei. The paper presents phi and J/Psi bound state results for Au-197 for the first time, and updates J/Psi results using only the D-anti-D loop contribution.
