## Method

The authors employ the Projected Hartree-Fock-Bogoliubov (PHFB) model with a pairing plus quadrupole-quadrupole (PPQQ) effective two-body interaction to generate wave functions for parent and daughter nuclei, then restore rotational symmetry via angular momentum projection. The nuclear transition matrix elements M2־½ for 0+ ג†’ 0+ transitions are calculated using the summation method, which avoids explicit summation over intermediate odd-odd nuclear states by assuming the Gamow-Teller operator commutes with the two-body interaction. The strength of the proton-neutron quadrupole-quadrupole interaction ֿ‡pn is fixed by reproducing the experimental excitation energy of the first 2+ state in each nucleus, and all other parameters are kept fixed throughout the calculation.
