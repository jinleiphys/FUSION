# The Reaction $πN \to ππN$ at Threshold

> I summarize the results of the complete one--loop chiral perturbation theory calculation performed recently. It is shown that it allows to accurately pin down the isospin two, S--wave $ππ$ scattering length $a_0^2$. On the other hand, interesting resonance physics makes a precise determination of $a_0^0$ very difficult.

## Key claim

A complete one-loop chiral perturbation theory calculation of the threshold reaction πN → ππN allows an accurate determination of the isospin-two S-wave ππ scattering length a₀², but a precise determination of a₀⁰ remains very difficult due to large theoretical uncertainties from resonance physics, particularly the Roper N*(1440) contribution.

## Method

The calculation was performed to order M_π² within heavy-nucleon chiral perturbation theory, including one-loop graphs with insertions from the leading-order πN and ππ Lagrangians, tree graphs with insertions from the next-to-leading order ππ Lagrangian (L_ππ^(4)), and tree graphs with insertions from the second- and third-order πN Lagrangians (L_πN^(2) and L_πN^(3)). The low-energy constants from L_πN^(2) were fixed by comparing to the empirical subthreshold expansion of elastic πN scattering amplitudes from Höhler, while the constants from L_πN^(3) were estimated via resonance exchange, with the Roper N*(1440) being the only relevant baryon excitation for the threshold amplitudes.

## Key numbers

- D₁ (threshold amplitude for isospin-two ππ final state): 2.65 ± 0.24 fm³ (theoretical), compared to experimental value 2.26 ± 0.12 fm³
- D₂ (threshold amplitude for isospin-zero ππ final state): -9.06 ± 1.05 fm³ (theoretical), compared to experimental value -9.05 ± 0.36 fm³
- Chiral expansion of D₁: 1.59 · (1.00 + 0.52 + 0.15) fm³, showing rapid convergence
- Chiral expansion of D₂: -4.76 · (1.00 + 0.42 + 0.48) fm³, showing large corrections at order M_π²
- Isospin-zero S-wave ππ scattering length a₀⁰ = 0.21 ± 0.07 (extracted from threshold ππN amplitudes)
- Isospin-two S-wave ππ scattering length a₀² = -0.031 ± 0.007 (extracted from threshold ππN amplitudes)
- Combination 2a₀⁰ - 5a₀² = 0.577 ± 0.144, consistent with the universal curve value 0.614 ± 0.028

## Context

This work extends the low-energy theorems for πN → ππN from Bernard, Kaiser, and Meißner (1994) by performing the complete one-loop calculation to order M_π², superseding the earlier tree-level approach based on the Olsson-Turner model (1968) which is only compatible with QCD for a particular symmetry-breaking scenario. The extracted scattering lengths agree with the one-loop chiral perturbation theory predictions of Gasser and Leutwyler (1984), but the large uncertainty in a₀⁰ suggests that Kℓ4 decays or pionic molecules may be better candidates for precisely determining this quantity.
