# The correlation between the $α$-cluster separation and the neutron S-factor in $^{12}$Be

> The reduced width amplitudes (RWA) and the spectroscopic factor (S-factor) of $α$-cluster and valence neutron in $^{12}$Be are calculated by the generator coordinates method (GCM) with the cluster model. By fixing the distance between the $α$-clusters' generated coordinates, we make a theoretical experiment to analyze the relationship between the $α$-clustering separation and the orbital occupation of the valence neutron in $^{12}$Be. The analysis of the results shows that the percentage of the $σ$ orbital occupation in $^{12}$Be is positively related to the clustering separation.

## Key claim
The paper claims a linear positive correlation between the α-cluster separation distance and the spectroscopic factor (S-factor) for the valence neutron in the ground state of ¹²Be, but this result is obtained from a "theoretical experiment" using manually fixed cluster distances in virtual wave functions, not from direct experimental measurement.

## Method
The authors used the generator coordinates method (GCM) combined with a cluster model to calculate wave functions of ¹²Be, ¹¹Be, ⁸He, and ⁴He. They manually fixed the distance between the two α-clusters' generated coordinates in the wave functions of ¹²Be and ¹¹Be to values of 1, 1.5, 2, 2.5, and 3 fm, then calculated the overlap function and spectroscopic factor (S-factor) for the valence neutron between ¹¹Be and ¹²Be at each fixed distance.

## Key numbers
- The α-cluster separation distances were fixed to d = 1, 1.5, 2, 2.5, and 3 fm for the theoretical experiment.
- The calculated binding energy of ¹²Be(0⁺) is -71.14 MeV, compared to the experimental value of -68.65 MeV.
- The calculated binding energy of ¹¹Be(1/2⁺) is -70.51 MeV, compared to the experimental value of -65.48 MeV.
- The calculated Q-value for ¹²Be → ¹¹Be + n is -0.63 MeV, compared to the experimental Q-value of -3.17 MeV.
- The calculated Q-value for ¹²Be → ⁸He + ⁴He is -8.91 MeV, compared to the experimental Q-value of -8.95 MeV.
- The Gaussian width parameter was set to b = 1.46 fm, corresponding to ν = 1/(2b²).

## Context
This work extends the authors' previous GCM study of α-cluster structure in ¹⁰Be and ¹²Be (Zhao, 2022) by investigating the relationship between cluster separation and valence neutron orbital occupation. It builds on the molecular orbit model of Ito (2012) using the generalized two-center cluster model, which showed that molecular orbitals of valence neutrons are highly sensitive to the configuration of the two alpha clusters. The predicted correlation between α-cluster separation and neutron S-factor may be experimentally probed using proton-induced knockout reactions as demonstrated by Yang (2021) and Li (2023).
