# On strangeness production in the reactions pp -> p Lambda^0 K^+ and pn -> n Lambda^0 K^+ near threshold

> The cross sections for the reactions pp -> p Lambda^0K^+ and pn -> n Lambda^0K^+ are calculated near threshold of the final states. The theoretical ratio of the cross sections R = sigma(pn -> n Lambda^0K^+)/ sigma(pp ->pΛ^0K^+) = 3 shows the enhancement of the pn interaction with respect to the pp interaction near threshold of the strangeness production N Lambda^0K^+. Such an enhancement is caused by the contribution of the np interaction in the isospin-singlet state, which is stronger than the $pn$ interaction in the isospin-triplet state. For the confirmation of this result we calculate the cross sections for the reactions pp -> pp pi^0, pi^0 p -> Lambda^0 K^+ and pi^-p -> Lambda^0 K^0 near threshold of the final states. The theoretical cross sections agree well with the experimental data.

## Key claim

The ratio of cross sections \(R = \sigma(pn \to n\Lambda^0K^+)/\sigma(pp \to p\Lambda^0K^+) \simeq 3\) near threshold is caused by the contribution of the \(pn\) interaction in the isospin-singlet state, which is stronger than the \(pn\) interaction in the isospin-triplet state; the vector-meson exchanges contribute only about 9% to the \(pp\) cross section and do not influence the isospin-singlet channel.

## Method

The authors calculated the cross sections for \(pp \to p\Lambda^0K^+\) and \(pn \to n\Lambda^0K^+\) near threshold using chiral Lagrangians with non-derivative meson-baryon couplings invariant under linear \(SU(3)\times SU(3)\) chiral symmetry, evaluated in the one-meson exchange approximation with pseudoscalar and scalar meson exchanges. They included initial-state \(pp\) rescattering in the \({}^{3}\mathrm{P}_0\) and \({}^{3}\mathrm{P}_1\) states and final-state interactions in the \(N\Lambda^0\) and \(NK^+\) channels, and separately treated the \(pn\) pair in isospin-triplet (\(I=1\)) and isospin-singlet (\(I=0\)) states. For validation, they also calculated cross sections for \(pp \to pp\pi^0\), \(\pi^0 p \to \Lambda^0 K^+\), and \(\pi^- p \to \Lambda^0 K^0\) near threshold.

## Key numbers

- Ratio \(R = \sigma(pn \to n\Lambda^0K^+)/\sigma(pp \to p\Lambda^0K^+) \simeq 3\) near threshold of the final \(N\Lambda^0K^+\) state.
- Cross section for \(pp \to p\Lambda^0K^+\): \(\sigma^{pp\to p\Lambda^0K^+}(\varepsilon) = 3.49\,\varepsilon^2\) nb (without vector mesons), agreeing with experimental data \(\sigma_{\exp} = 3.68\,\varepsilon^2\) nb (Coventry et al., 1998) within 6%; with \(\rho^0\) and \(\omega^0\) meson exchanges it becomes \(3.18\,\varepsilon^2\) nb, agreeing within 16%.
- Cross section for \((pn)_{I=1} \to n\Lambda^0K^+\): \(\sigma^{(pn)_{I=1}\to n\Lambda^0K^+}(\varepsilon) = 3.30\,\varepsilon^2\) nb, giving \(R^{(I=1)} \simeq 1\).
- Effective coupling constants: \(C^{(pp)_{{}^{3}\mathrm{P}_0}_{(p\Lambda^0)_{{}^{1}\mathrm{S}_0}K^+} = -2.28\times 10^{-6}\) MeV\(^{-3}\), \(C^{(pp)_{{}^{3}\mathrm{P}_1}_{(p\Lambda^0)_{{}^{3}\mathrm{S}_1}K^+} = -1.58\times 10^{-6}\) MeV\(^{-3}\); for the \(pn\) isospin-singlet channel \(C^{(pn)_{{}^{1}\mathrm{P}_1}_{(n\Lambda^0)_{{}^{3}\mathrm{S}_1}K^+} = -1.95\times 10^{-6}\) MeV\(^{-3}\).
- Proton kinetic energy at threshold: \(T_N = 335\) MeV, relative momentum \(p_0 = 861\) MeV.

## Context

This work extends the analysis of strangeness production in \(pp\) collisions by Ivanov et al. (2001) using chiral Lagrangians to the \(pn\) channel, and provides a theoretical explanation for the ratio \(R \sim 3-4\) inferred from COSY data on \(pd \to K^+ X N\) reactions. The result contradicts the resonance-based explanations of Kaiser and Weise (1997) and Kondratyuk and Titov (1999), which invoked \(\rho\)-meson exchange and \(N(1535)/N(1650)\) resonances; here those resonances are shown to be negligible for the \(pN \to N\Lambda^0K^+\) amplitudes. The calculation enables improved modeling of kaon production in nucleon-nucleon collisions near threshold and provides a baseline for future experiments at COSY or other facilities.
