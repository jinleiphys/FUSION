# Transverse Polarization of Hyperons Produced in Semi-Inclusive Deep Inelastic Scattering

> We study the transverse polarization of hyperons produced in semi-inclusive deep inelastic scattering, $ep\to eΛ^\uparrow X$, in the framework of the collinear twist-3 factorization. The cross section from the twist-3 distribution functions and the twist-3 quark fragmentation functions is computed in the leading order with respect to the QCD coupling constant. The constraint relations among the twist-3 FFs are taken into account to simplify the formula. The formula is relevant to large-$P_T$ hyperon production in the future Electron-Ion-Collider experiment.

## Key claim

The paper derives the leading-order collinear twist-3 cross section formula for transverse hyperon polarization in semi-inclusive deep inelastic scattering, but the result is restricted to the contribution from twist-3 quark fragmentation functions; the contribution from twist-3 purely gluonic fragmentation functions is deferred to a separate publication.

## Method

The authors computed the polarized cross section for \(ep\to e\Lambda^\uparrow X\) using collinear twist-3 factorization at leading order in the QCD coupling constant, working in the hadron frame. They separated the calculation into two contributions: one from the twist-3 distribution function \(E_F(x_1,x_2)\) in the unpolarized proton combined with the twist-2 transversity fragmentation function \(H_1(z)\), and the other from twist-3 quark fragmentation functions (intrinsic, kinematical, and dynamical) combined with the unpolarized parton distribution \(f_1(x)\). Constraint relations among the twist-3 fragmentation functions (the equation-of-motion relation and the Lorentz invariance relation) were used to simplify the final expression.

## Key numbers

* The hard-pole contribution to the cross section contains terms proportional to \(1/q_T\), \(1/q_T^2\), and \(1/q_T^3\), where \(q_T\) is the transverse momentum of the virtual photon (defined via \(q_T = \sqrt{-q_t^2}\)).
* The soft-gluon-pole contribution contains terms proportional to \(1/q_T\), \(1/q_T^2\), and \(1/q_T^4\).
* The delta function enforcing the on-shell condition for the unobserved parton is \(\delta\left( \frac{q_T^2}{Q^2} - \left(1-\frac{1}{\hat{x}}\right)\left(1-\frac{1}{\hat{z}}\right) \right)\), where \(\hat{x}=x_{bj}/x\) and \(\hat{z}=z_f/z\).
* The cross section involves the nucleon mass \(M_N\) and the hyperon mass \(M_h\) as explicit factors in the twist-3 distribution and fragmentation contributions, respectively.

## Context

This work extends the collinear twist-3 formalism previously applied to \(pp\to\Lambda^\uparrow X\) (Kanazawa et al., 2015; Koike et al., 2017, 2021) and to \(ep^\uparrow\to e\pi X\) (Eguchi et al., 2006; Koike et al., 2007, 2009) to the case of hyperon production in semi-inclusive deep inelastic scattering. The derived formula is intended for use at the future Electron-Ion Collider, where large-transverse-momentum hyperon production can be measured. The contribution from twist-3 purely gluonic fragmentation functions, which was shown to be necessary for gauge invariance in the \(pp\) case (Koike et al., 2021), is left for a separate publication (Ikarashi et al., 2022).
