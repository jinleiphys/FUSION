# Onset of $О·$-meson binding in the He isotopes

> The onset of binding $О·$(548) mesons in nuclei is studied in the He isotopes by doing precise $О·NNN$ and $О·NNNN$ few-body stochastic variational method calculations for two semi-realistic $NN$ potentials and two energy dependent $О·N$ potentials derived from coupled-channel models of the $N^{\ast}(1535)$ nucleon resonance. It is found that a minimal value of the real part of the $О·N$ scattering length $a_{О·N}$ close to 1 fm is required to bind $О·$ mesons in $^3$He, yielding then a few MeV $О·$ binding in $^4$He.
