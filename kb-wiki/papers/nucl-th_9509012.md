# Quark Cluster Model Study of Isospin-Two Dibaryons

> Based on a quark cluster model for the non-strange sector that reproduces reasonably well the nucleon-nucleon system and the excitation of the $Δ$ isobar, we generate a nucleon-$Δ$ interaction and present the predictions for the several isospin two channels. The only attractive channels are $0^+$ and $0^-$, but not attractive enough to generate a resonance. If a resonance is artificially generated and is required to have the observed experimental mass, then our model predicts a width that agrees with the experimental result.

# Quark Cluster Model Study of Isospin-Two Dibaryons

## Key claim

The quark cluster model with standard parameters derived from nucleon-nucleon scattering cannot generate any resonance in the isospin-two nucleon-delta channels with J ≤ 2; however, if the sigma meson mass is artificially reduced to 234 MeV to produce a resonance at the experimentally observed mass of 2065 MeV, the model predicts a width of 0.6 MeV that agrees with the experimental result from Bilger and Clement (1993).

## Method

The authors used a constituent quark model with confining potential, one-gluon exchange, one-pion exchange, and one-sigma exchange between quarks, with parameters fixed from nucleon-nucleon scattering and chiral symmetry requirements. They generated a nucleon-delta interaction via the Born-Oppenheimer approximation and solved the Lippmann-Schwinger equation to compute Fredholm determinants for energies below threshold, then calculated Argand diagrams and phase shifts using relativistic kinematics with an unstable delta including its width.

## Key numbers

- The proposed resonance from Bilger and Clement (1993) has a mass of 2065 MeV and a width of 0.51 MeV
- With sigma meson mass m_sigma = 234 MeV, the model produces a resonance mass of 2064.4 MeV and width of 0.6 MeV
- With m_sigma = 350 MeV, the resonance mass is 2170.4 MeV with width 127.6 MeV
- With m_sigma = 210 MeV, the resonance mass is 2029.5 MeV with width 0.0015 MeV
- The pi-NN threshold is at 2017 MeV
- The quark model parameters include: quark mass m_q = 313 MeV, confinement strength a_c = 57.96 MeV/fm^2, strong coupling alpha_s = 0.4, chiral coupling alpha_ch = 0.0288

## Context

This work extends the quark cluster model previously applied to nucleon-nucleon scattering (Fernandez, Valcarce, Straub, and Faessler, 1993) and the deuteron (Valcarce, Buchmann, Fernandez, and Faessler, 1994) to the isospin-two nucleon-delta system. It contradicts the predictions of Garcilazo and Mathelitsch (1986) and Gale and Duck (1968), who found attraction in the 2- channel using three-body Faddeev approaches, while this quark model finds the 2- channel repulsive. The results enable a quark-model interpretation of the narrow resonance observed in pionic double charge exchange reactions, suggesting it may be a nucleon-delta resonance in the isospin-two channel without requiring exotic decay channels.
