# First observation of the hyper superheavy hydrogen 6ОӣH

> Three candidate events of the neutron-rich hypernucleus 6ОӣH were uniquely identified in the FINUDA experiment at DAОҰNE, Frascati, by observing ПҖ+ mesons from the (K-stop,ПҖ+) production reaction on 6Li targets, in coincidence with ПҖ-mesons from 6ОӣH \rightarrow 6He+ПҖ- weak decay. Details of the experiment and the analysis of its data are reported, leading to an estimate of (2.9\pm2.0)\cdot10-6/K- stop for the 6ОӣH production rate times the two-body ПҖ- weak decay branching ratio. The 6ОӣH binding energy with respect to 5H + Оӣ was determined jointly from production and decay to be BОӣ = (4.0 \pm 1.1) MeV, assuming that 5H is unbound with respect to 3H + 2n by 1.7 MeV. The consequences of this assignment to Оӣ hypernuclear dynamics are briefly discussed.

## Context

This work extends earlier attempts to produce neutron-rich hypernuclei at KEK, where only upper limits were obtained for 9ОӣHe, 12ОӣBe, and 16ОӣC (Kubota et al.), and a previous FINUDA analysis of limited statistics gave only upper limits for 6ОӣH and 7ОӣH production. The observation of 6ОӣH confirms predictions by Dalitz and Levi Setti (1963) and Majling that the Оӣ hyperon can stabilize nuclear cores beyond the neutron drip line, and the measured binding energy falls between the conservative estimate of 4.2 MeV and the stronger binding of 5.8 MeV predicted by Akaishi et al. based on coherent ОӣN-ОЈN mixing. The results enable future studies of Оӣ hypernuclear dynamics and the role of ОӣN-ОЈN coupling in neutron-rich systems, with implications for strange dense stellar matter.
