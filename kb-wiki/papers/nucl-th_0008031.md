# Three-body analysis of incoherent $η$-photoproduction on the deuteron in the near threshold region

> A three-body calculation of the reaction $γd\toηnp$ in the energy region from threshold up to 30 MeV above has been performed. The primary goal of this study is to assess the importance of the three-body aspects in the hadronic sector of this reaction. Results are presented for the $η$-meson spectrum as well as for the total cross section. The three-body results differ significantly from those predicted by a simple rescattering model in which only first-order $ηN$- and $NN$-interactions in the final state are considered. The major features of the experimental data are well reproduced although right at threshold the rather large total cross section could not be explained.

## Key claim

A full three-body calculation of the reaction γd → ηnp, including hadronic final-state interactions to all orders, significantly improves agreement with experimental data compared to a first-order rescattering model, but still cannot explain the large total cross section observed right at threshold.

## Method

The authors solved coupled Faddeev-type integral equations for the γd → ηnp transition amplitude, using separable s-wave potentials for the ηN and NN interactions in the (J^π;T) = (0^-;1) channel. The electromagnetic interaction was treated perturbatively to first order, with the γN → S11(1535) helicity amplitude fitted to elementary γp → ηp data. The resulting one-dimensional momentum-space integral equations were solved by matrix inversion with contour deformation to handle logarithmic singularities.

## Key numbers

- The calculation covers the energy region from threshold up to 30 MeV above threshold.
- The ηN-resonance S11(1535) has hadronic widths Γ_{N*→πN} ≈ Γ_{N*→ηN} ≈ 75 MeV.
- The isovector part of the γN → S11(1535) helicity amplitude was extracted as A_{1/2}^{(v)} = 0.9 A_{1/2}^{(p)}.
- A calculation using a large ηN-scattering length a_{ηN} = (0.91 + i0.25) fm still could not describe the data sufficiently well.
- Attributing the threshold discrepancy to the coherent channel γd → ηd would require a coherent cross section of about 100 nb at E_γ = 635 MeV.

## Context

This work extends the authors' previous rescattering model (Fix and Arenhoevel, 1999) by including higher-order rescattering terms via a full three-body formalism, motivated by the proximity of virtual ηNN three-body poles found in their earlier study (Fix and Arenhoevel, 2000). The approach contrasts with pion photoproduction on the deuteron, where the small πN scattering length allows truncation after first-order rescattering (Laget, 1978). The results enable future experimental measurements of the η-meson spectrum to identify final-state interaction effects, and leave open the question of whether the coherent channel γd → ηd can resolve the remaining threshold discrepancy.
