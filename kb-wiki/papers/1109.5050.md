# On high energy exclusive leptoproduction of the rho meson up to twist 3

> The Regge inspired k_T-factorization scheme expresses the scattering amplitude for exclusive leptoproduction of a light vector meson rho in terms of two impact factors, one for the gamma* - rho transition and the other one for the nucleon to nucleon transition, with, at leading order, a two "reggeized" gluon exchange in the t-channel. We report here on a recent phenomenological study where we apply a previously developped scheme which consistently takes into account the twist 3 distribution amplitudes for transversely polarized rho meson. The computation of the gamma* to rho impact factor are performed within collinear factorization of QCD, up to the twist 3 level, based on the Collinear Factorization scheme applied to the amplitudes gamma*g to g rho . Our results are in good agreement with high energy experimental data for the ratios of helicity amplitudes.

# On high energy exclusive leptoproduction of the rho meson up to twist 3

## Key claim
The Regge-inspired kT-factorization scheme, combined with collinear factorization up to twist 3 for the gamma* to rho impact factor, yields predictions for helicity amplitude ratios that are in good agreement with high energy experimental data from H1 and ZEUS, provided that the Wandzura-Wilczek contribution dominates over genuine twist 3 contributions.

## Method
The authors compute the scattering amplitude for exclusive leptoproduction of a rho meson using a kT-factorization scheme with two reggeized gluon exchange in the t-channel. The gamma* to rho impact factor is calculated within collinear factorization of QCD up to twist 3, involving both two-body (quark-antiquark) and three-body (quark-antiquark-gluon) distribution amplitudes, while the nucleon-to-nucleon impact factor is modeled with a simple phenomenological dipole form containing two free parameters A and M. The calculation keeps only leading terms in the 1/Q expansion for both the twist 2 gamma*_L to rho_L and twist 3 gamma*_T to rho_T transitions.

## Key numbers
- The ratio T11/T00 is plotted as a function of Q2 and compared to H1 data, with the model using fixed lambda=0 and various values of M, and fixed M=1 GeV with various lambda values.
- The ratio T01/T00 is plotted as a function of |t| for Q2=3.3 GeV2 and Q2=8.6 GeV2, compared with H1 data.
- The proton impact factor model uses a soft scale parameter M (varied around 1 GeV) and a normalization parameter A.

## Context
This work extends the earlier computation of gamma*_L to rho_L and gamma*_T to rho_L impact factors by Ginzburg (1985) and the consistent twist 3 treatment of gamma*_T to rho_T by Anikin (2009). It applies the previously developed scheme to a phenomenological study of exclusive rho meson leptoproduction, confronting predictions with data from H1 (Aaron, 2009) and ZEUS (Chekanov, 2007). The approach enables future comparisons with other scattering amplitude ratios and anticipates that a future electron-ion collider (Boer, 2011) will allow detailed studies of meson electroproduction.
