# Dynamical model of $œï$ meson photoproduction on the nucleon and $^4He$

> We investigate $œï$ meson photoproduction on the nucleon and the \nuclide[4]{He} targets within a dynamical model approach based on a Hamiltonian which describes the production mechanisms by the Pomeron-exchange, meson-exchanges, $œï$ radiations, and nucleon resonance excitations mechanisms. The final $œïN$ interactions are included being described by the gluon-exchange, direct $œïN$ couplings, and the box-diagrams arising from the couplings with $œÄN$, $œÅN$, $KŒõ$, and $KŒ£$ channels. The parameters of the Hamiltonian are determined by the experimental data of $Œ≥p \to œïp$ from the CLAS Collaboration. The resulting Hamiltonian is then used to predict the coherent $œï$-meson production on the \nuclide[4]{He} targets by using the distorted-wave impulse approximation. For the proton target, the final $œïN$ rescattering effects, as required by the unitarity condition, are found to be very weak, which supports the earlier calculations in the literature. For the \nuclide[4]{He} targets, the predicted differential cross sections are in good agreement with the data obtained by the LEPS Collaboration. The role of each mechanism in this reaction is discussed and predictions for a wide range of scattering angles are presented, which can be tested in future experiments.

## Key claim

The authors construct a Hamiltonian-based dynamical model for $\phi$ meson photoproduction on the nucleon and $^4$He, finding that final-state $\phi N$ rescattering effects on the proton target are very weak (supporting earlier calculations), while the model's predictions for coherent production on $^4$He agree well with LEPS Collaboration data.

## Method

The model Hamiltonian includes Pomeron-exchange, meson-exchange ($\pi^0$, $\eta$, $a_0$, $f_0$, $f_1(1285)$), direct $\phi$ radiation, and nucleon resonance ($N^*$) excitation mechanisms for the production amplitude, plus a $\phi N$ final-state interaction described by gluon-exchange, direct $\phi N$ couplings, and box diagrams from $\phi \to K\bar{K}$ and $\phi \to \pi\rho$ decays coupling to $K\Lambda$, $K\Sigma$, $\pi N$, and $\rho N$ channels. Parameters are fitted to CLAS $\gamma p \to \phi p$ data, and the resulting Hamiltonian is applied to coherent $\phi$ production on $^4$He using the distorted-wave impulse approximation.

## Context

This work extends the ANL-Osaka Hamiltonian formulation (MSL06, KLNS19) to $\phi$ meson photoproduction, following earlier models for near-threshold vector meson production (TOY97, TOYM98, TLTS99, KN19, KN20). It provides predictions for coherent $\phi$ production on $^4$He targets that can be tested in future experiments, and enables further studies of $\phi$-nucleus bound states predicted by lattice QCD (NPLQCD-14).
