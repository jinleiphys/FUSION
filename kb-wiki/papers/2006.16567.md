# $ΛΛN$-$ΞNN$ $S$ wave resonance

> We use an existing model of the $ΛΛN - ΞNN$ three-body system based in two-body separable interactions to study the $(I,J^P)=(1/2,1/2^+)$ three-body channel. For the $ΛΛ$, $ΞN$, and $ΛΛ- ΞN$ amplitudes we have constructed separable potentials based on the most recent results of the HAL QCD Collaboration. They are characterized by the existence of a resonance just below or above the $ΞN$ threshold in the so-called $H$-dibaryon channel, $(i,j^p)=(0,0^+)$. A three-body resonance appears {2.3} MeV above the $Ξd$ threshold. We show that if the $ΛΛ- ΞN$ $H$-dibaryon channel is not considered, the $ΛΛN - ΞNN$ $S$ wave resonance disappears. Thus, the possible existence of a $ΛΛN - ΞNN$ resonance would be sensitive to the $ΛΛ- ΞN$ interaction. The existence or nonexistence of this resonance could be evidenced by measuring, for example, the $Ξd$ cross section.

# ΛΛN-ΞNN S wave resonance

## Key claim

A three-body resonance in the ΛΛN-ΞNN system with quantum numbers (I,J^P)=(1/2,1/2^+) appears 2.3 MeV above the Ξd threshold, but only if the ΛΛ-ΞN H-dibaryon channel (i,j^p)=(0,0^+) is included; if this channel is omitted, the three-body resonance disappears entirely.

## Method

The authors constructed separable potential models for the ΛΛ, ΞN, and coupled ΛΛ-ΞN two-body amplitudes, fitted to reproduce the phase shifts and inelasticities of the HAL QCD Collaboration's lattice QCD results (model t/a=12). They then solved the coupled-channel Faddeev-type integral equations for the ΛΛN-ΞNN three-body system in the (I,J^P)=(1/2,1/2^+) channel, using contour rotation of momentum variables into the complex plane to search for resonances above the Ξd threshold. The Ξd elastic scattering amplitude was computed and displayed as an Argand diagram and cross section.

## Key numbers

* Three-body resonance appears 2.3 MeV above the Ξd threshold.
* The ΞN and ΞNN thresholds are 25.6 MeV above the ΛΛ and ΛΛN thresholds, respectively (using physical baryon masses; the HAL QCD threshold is 32 MeV due to different lattice masses).
* At E=2.3 MeV, the Ξd elastic cross section is 55 mb with full coupling; dropping the ΛΛN channel changes this to 58 mb (a change of about 10%).
* The two-body ΛΛ-ΞN separable potential parameters: α=1.3465 fm⁻¹, β=1.1460 fm⁻¹, λ₁₁=-0.1390 fm⁻², λ₃₃=-0.3171 fm⁻², λ₁₃=0.0977 fm⁻².

## Context

This work extends the authors' previous study (Garcilazo and Valcarce, 2019) which found a ΛΛN-ΞNN resonance just below the Ξd threshold using Nijmegen ESC08c potentials, which gave no H-dibaryon resonance. It contrasts with the negative results for ΞNN bound states in the (1/2,1/2^+) channel found by Hiyama et al. (2020) using HAL QCD ΞN interactions with the AV8 NN potential and the Gaussian Expansion Method, and by Garcilazo and Valcarce (2019) using Nijmegen ESC08c potentials. The present work shows that the three-body resonance is driven by the H-dibaryon resonance in the ΛΛ-ΞN channel, analogous to how the nucleon-nucleon ¹D₂ Hoshizaki resonance arises from the NΔ system. The predicted resonance could be observed through the Ξd cross section in quasifree Ξ⁻ production via the (K⁻,K⁺) reaction on a deuteron target.
