# $őõőõN$-$őěNN$ $S$ wave resonance

> We use an existing model of the $őõőõN - őěNN$ three-body system based in two-body separable interactions to study the $(I,J^P)=(1/2,1/2^+)$ three-body channel. For the $őõőõ$, $őěN$, and $őõőõ- őěN$ amplitudes we have constructed separable potentials based on the most recent results of the HAL QCD Collaboration. They are characterized by the existence of a resonance just below or above the $őěN$ threshold in the so-called $H$-dibaryon channel, $(i,j^p)=(0,0^+)$. A three-body resonance appears {2.3} MeV above the $őěd$ threshold. We show that if the $őõőõ- őěN$ $H$-dibaryon channel is not considered, the $őõőõN - őěNN$ $S$ wave resonance disappears. Thus, the possible existence of a $őõőõN - őěNN$ resonance would be sensitive to the $őõőõ- őěN$ interaction. The existence or nonexistence of this resonance could be evidenced by measuring, for example, the $őěd$ cross section.

# őõőõN-őěNN S wave resonance

## Key claim

A three-body resonance in the őõőõN-őěNN system with quantum numbers (I,J^P)=(1/2,1/2^+) appears 2.3 MeV above the őěd threshold, but only if the őõőõ-őěN H-dibaryon channel (i,j^p)=(0,0^+) is included; if this channel is omitted, the three-body resonance disappears entirely.

## Method

The authors constructed separable potential models for the őõőõ, őěN, and coupled őõőõ-őěN two-body amplitudes, fitted to reproduce the phase shifts and inelasticities of the HAL QCD Collaboration's lattice QCD results (model t/a=12). They then solved the coupled-channel Faddeev-type integral equations for the őõőõN-őěNN three-body system in the (I,J^P)=(1/2,1/2^+) channel, using contour rotation of momentum variables into the complex plane to search for resonances above the őěd threshold. The őěd elastic scattering amplitude was computed and displayed as an Argand diagram and cross section.

## Key numbers

* Three-body resonance appears 2.3 MeV above the őěd threshold.
* The őěN and őěNN thresholds are 25.6 MeV above the őõőõ and őõőõN thresholds, respectively (using physical baryon masses; the HAL QCD threshold is 32 MeV due to different lattice masses).
* At E=2.3 MeV, the őěd elastic cross section is 55 mb with full coupling; dropping the őõőõN channel changes this to 58 mb (a change of about 10%).

## Context

This work extends the authors' previous study (Garcilazo and Valcarce, 2019) which found a őõőõN-őěNN resonance just below the őěd threshold using Nijmegen ESC08c potentials, which gave no H-dibaryon resonance. It contrasts with the negative results for őěNN bound states in the (1/2,1/2^+) channel found by Hiyama et al. (2020) using HAL QCD őěN interactions with the AV8 NN potential and the Gaussian Expansion Method, and by Garcilazo and Valcarce (2019) using Nijmegen ESC08c potentials. The present work shows that the three-body resonance is driven by the H-dibaryon resonance in the őõőõ-őěN channel, analogous to how the nucleon-nucleon ¬ĻD‚āā Hoshizaki resonance arises from the NőĒ system. The predicted resonance could be observed through the őěd cross section in quasifree őě‚ĀĽ production via the (K‚ĀĽ,K‚Āļ) reaction on a deuteron target.
