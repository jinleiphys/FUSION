# Quarkonium production in pNRQCD: the $P$-wave case

> We review our recent understanding of quarkonium production at $e^+e^-$ and hadronic colliders from the point of view of potential non-relativistic QCD, and apply it to $P$-wave charmonium and bottomonium inclusive production.

## Key claim

The paper argues that moving from NRQCD to potential NRQCD (pNRQCD) provides a further factorization of the long-distance matrix elements (LDMEs) that appear in quarkonium production cross sections, potentially reducing the number of non-perturbative parameters that must be fitted to data; this approach is applied specifically to P-wave charmonium and bottomonium production, but the results depend on the assumption that the condition \(mv^2 \ll \Lambda_{\rm QCD}\) (strongly coupled pNRQCD) holds and, for hadroproduction, on the large \(N_c\) limit.

## Method

The author reviews the construction of pNRQCD from NRQCD by integrating out gluon modes of energy or momentum larger than \(mv^2\), then matches the NRQCD LDMEs for inclusive P-wave quarkonium production onto pNRQCD at leading order in the heavy-quark relative velocity \(v\). For electromagnetic production \(e^+e^- \to \chi_{QJ}(nP)+\gamma\), the matching yields expressions for the production LDMEs in terms of the derivative of the P-wave radial wavefunction at the origin \(|R'(\mathbf{0})|^2\), the binding energy \(\varepsilon\), and chromoelectric field correlators \({\cal E}_1\) and \(i{\cal E}_2\). For hadroproduction \(pp \to \chi_{QJ}(nP)+X\) and \(pp \to h_Q(nP)+X\), the matching expresses the color-octet LDMEs in terms of a universal dimensionless chromoelectric correlator \({\cal E}\) and the same wavefunction factor, with the correlator fitted to LHC data on the ratio of \(\chi_{c2}(1P)\) to \(\chi_{c1}(1P)\) differential cross sections.

## Key numbers

*   For charmonium electromagnetic production at \(\sqrt{s}=10.6\) GeV: \(\sigma(e^+ e^- \to \chi_{c0}(1P) + \gamma) = 1.84 {}^{+0.25}_{-0.26} \pm 0.76\) fb.
*   For charmonium electromagnetic production at \(\sqrt{s}=10.6\) GeV: \(\sigma(e^+ e^- \to \chi_{c1}(1P) + \gamma) = 16.4 {}^{+0.2}_{-0.2} \pm 6.4\) fb.
*   For charmonium electromagnetic production at \(\sqrt{s}=10.6\) GeV: \(\sigma(e^+ e^- \to \chi_{c2}(1P) + \gamma) = 3.75 {}^{+0.67}_{-0.56} \pm 2.16\) fb.
*   Fitted chromoelectric correlator for electromagnetic production: \({\cal E}_1 = -0.20 {}^{+0.14}_{-0.14} \pm 0.90\) GeV\(^2\).
*   Fitted chromoelectric correlator for electromagnetic production: \(i{\cal E}_2 = 0.77 {}^{+0.98}_{-0.86} \pm 0.85\) GeV.
*   Fitted dimensionless chromoelectric correlator for hadroproduction from NLO short-distance coefficients: \({\cal E}|_{\rm NLO}(\Lambda=1.5\ \text{GeV}) = 1.17 \pm 0.05\).
*   Fitted dimensionless chromoelectric correlator for hadroproduction from LP+NLO short-distance coefficients: \({\cal E}|_{\rm LP+NLO}(\Lambda=1.5\ \text{GeV}) = 4.48 \pm 0.14\).
*   Combined value of the dimensionless chromoelectric correlator for hadroproduction: \({\cal E}(\Lambda=1.5\ \text{GeV}) = 2.8 \pm 1.7\).

## Context

This work extends the pNRQCD factorization program for quarkonium production, previously applied to S-wave states, to P-wave charmonium and bottomonium, building on the NRQCD factorization framework of Bodwin, Braaten, and Lepage (1994). It addresses long-standing difficulties in NRQCD where fits of color-octet LDMEs to different data sets yield inconsistent results, as noted by Chung et al. (2018) and Lansberg (2019). The pNRQCD approach enables predictions for bottomonium production using correlators fitted to charmonium data, providing a testable universality; the predicted ratio of \(\chi_{b2}(1P)\) to \(\chi_{b1}(1P)\) differential cross sections can be compared with LHCb (2014) and CMS (2014) data.
