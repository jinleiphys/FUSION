# Angular distribution of leptons from the decay of massive vector bosons

> We examine the transverse momentum $Q_\perp$ dependence of the helicity structure functions for massive vector bosons of mass $Q$ in hadron reactions. We demonstrate that large logarithmic terms of the form $\ln(Q/Q_\perp)$ in the helicity structure functions have the same origin as the logarithmic terms in the angular-integrated cross section and that they can be resummed to all orders in the strong coupling $α_s$, in the same way as the angular-integrated cross section. As a consequence of current conservation, the resummed part of the helicity structure functions preserves the Lam-Tung relation as a function of $Q_\perp$ to all orders in $α_s$.

## Key claim
The paper demonstrates that the large logarithmic terms of the form ln(Q/Q_perp) in the helicity structure functions W_T, W_L, and W_DeltaDelta for massive vector boson production have the same origin as those in the angular-integrated cross section and can be resummed to all orders in alpha_s, but only for these three structure functions; the fourth structure function W_Delta requires separate treatment and is not addressed in this work.

## Method
The authors analyze the Drell-Yan hadronic tensor in the limit Q_perp/Q → 0, extracting the singular contributions to each helicity structure function. They construct an asymptotic tensor that includes all singular terms while conserving the electromagnetic current, then show that the asymptotic parts of W_T, W_L, and W_DeltaDelta are proportional to the asymptotic part of the angular-integrated Drell-Yan transverse momentum distribution. They verify this explicitly at parton level for the q-qbar → gamma* g and q-g → gamma* q subprocesses at order alpha_s, demonstrating that the asymptotic tensor removes all divergent contributions as Q_perp/Q → 0.

## Key numbers
- The asymptotic part of W_L is proportional to (Q_perp^2/Q^2) * (W^Asym/2) in the Collins-Soper frame
- The asymptotic part of W_DeltaDelta is proportional to (1/2) * (Q_perp^2/Q^2) * (W^Asym/2) in the Collins-Soper frame
- For the q-qbar subprocess, the asymptotic term w_qbar^Asym/2 contains the factor 8π^2 α_s/(3 x_1 x_2) * (Q^2/Q_perp^2)
- The splitting functions used have C_F = 4/3 and T_R = 1/2
- The uncanceled finite term in W_L and W_DeltaDelta for the q-g subprocess is proportional to 4 z_2 T_R for unpolarized initial states

## Context
This work extends the Collins-Soper-Sterman resummation formalism (CSS, 1985) for the angular-integrated Drell-Yan cross section to the helicity structure functions that describe the lepton angular distribution. It builds on the general formalism for Drell-Yan angular distributions developed by Lam and Tung (1978) and addresses the Q_perp dependence of the Lam-Tung relation, which previous perturbative calculations at order α_s^2 (NLO) had shown to be approximately satisfied. The results enable refined tests of QCD through angular distribution measurements and reduced systematic uncertainties in W boson mass determinations at hadron colliders.
