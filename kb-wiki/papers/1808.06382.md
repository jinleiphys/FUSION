# Vector mesons and electromagnetic form factor of the $Λ$ hyperon

> The measured electromagnetic form factors of $Λ$ hyperon in the time-like region are significantly deviated from pQCD prediction. We attribute the non-vanishing cross section near threshold to be the contribution of below-threshold $ϕ$(2170) state, supporting its exotic structure. Above the threshold, we find significant role of a wide vector meson with the mass of around 2.34 GeV, which would be the same state present in $p\bar{p}$ annihilation reactions. As a result, we give a satisfactory description of the behavior of existing data without modifying pQCD expectation.

# Vector mesons and electromagnetic form factor of the Λ hyperon

## Key claim

The deviation of the measured Λ hyperon electromagnetic form factors in the time-like region from perturbative QCD predictions can be explained without modifying the pQCD expectation itself, by attributing the non-vanishing cross section near threshold to the below-threshold φ(2170) state and the behavior above threshold to a wide vector meson with mass around 2.34 GeV, using a Fano-type interference formalism.

## Method

The authors fit the world data on e+e- → ΛΛ cross sections using a Fano-type form factor that describes the coherent interference between a pQCD continuum background and two vector meson resonances. The φ(2170) resonance is fixed at its PDG mass of 2.188 GeV and width of 83.0 MeV, while the second resonance X(2340) has its mass and width as free parameters. The fit uses 16 data points from BaBar and BESIII with five free parameters.

## Key numbers

- The fit yields χ²/d.o.f. = 7.7/11 = 0.7 for 16 data points and five free parameters
- The φ(2170) resonance mass is fixed at 2.188 ± 0.010 GeV with width 83.0 ± 12.0 MeV from PDG
- The X(2340) resonance mass is 2.338 ± 0.046 ± 0.030 GeV
- The X(2340) resonance width is 257 ± 159 ± 41 MeV
- The pQCD normalization parameter A_Λ = 1.19 ± 0.46 ± 0.09, compared to A_p ≈ 5.0 for e+e- → pp
- The Fano parameter q₁⁰ for φ(2170) is 6.32 ± 2.22 ± 2.95
- The Fano parameter q₂⁰ for X(2340) is -2.53 ± 0.93 ± 0.35
- The QCD scale parameter Λ_QCD is set to 0.3 GeV

## Context

This work extends the analysis of vector meson contributions in baryon electromagnetic form factors previously applied to e+e- → pp by Lorenz et al. (2015), where structures around 2.125 GeV and 2.43 GeV were observed. The authors note that simple fits using Breit-Wigner shapes with effective pole terms (Lorenz 2015) cannot explain the near-threshold enhancement in ΛΛ production, whereas the Fano-type formalism provides a natural explanation. The results support the interpretation of φ(2170) as a ΛΛ molecular state, consistent with proposals by Zhao et al. (2013) and Dong et al. (2017). The X(2340) state may correspond to the ω(2290) seen in pp → ΛΛ partial wave analysis by Bugg et al. (2004), or to ω(2330) or ω(2205) listed by PDG. Future measurements by BESIII and Belle-II with higher precision could clarify the nature of these states and enable combined analyses of e+e- → ΛΛ and e+e- → pp.
