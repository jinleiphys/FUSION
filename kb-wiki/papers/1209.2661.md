# Threshold neutral pion photoproduction off the tri-nucleon to O(q^4)

> We calculate electromagnetic neutral pion production off tri-nucleon bound states (3H, 3He) at threshold in chiral nuclear effective field theory to fourth order in the standard heavy baryon counting. We show that the fourth order two-nucleon corrections to the S-wave multipoles at threshold are very small. This implies that a precise measurement of the S-wave cross section for neutral pion production off 3He allows for a stringent test of the chiral perturbation theory prediction for the S-wave electric multipole E_{0+}^{pi0 n}.

## Key claim

The fourth-order two-nucleon corrections to the S-wave multipoles at threshold are very small, implying that a precise measurement of the S-wave cross section for neutral pion production off 3He allows for a stringent test of the chiral perturbation theory prediction for the S-wave electric multipole E_{0+}^{pi0 n}, provided the calculation is complete to fourth order in the standard heavy baryon counting.

## Method

The authors calculated electromagnetic neutral pion production off 3H and 3He at threshold using chiral nuclear effective field theory to fourth order (next-to-next-to-leading order) in the heavy baryon expansion. They evaluated one-body contributions using chiral 3N wave functions from the N2LO interaction with five different cutoff combinations, and computed two-nucleon contributions including boost corrections, static corrections, and recoil corrections using Monte Carlo integration with the VEGAS algorithm.

## Key numbers

- The kinematical factor K_{1N} for converting from the 1N to the 3N system is approximately 1.092
- The prefactor for leading two-nucleon contributions K_{2N} is approximately 0.135 fm x 10^{-3}/M_{pi+}
- The form factor F_T^{(a)} - F_T^{(b)} for 3He is -29.3(2)(1) fm^{-1} and for 3H is -29.7(2)(1) fm^{-1}
- The form factor F_L^{(a)} - F_L^{(b)} for 3He is -22.9(2)(1) fm^{-1} and for 3H is -23.2(1)(1) fm^{-1}
- The prefactor for fourth-order two-nucleon contributions K_{2N}^{q^4} is approximately -0.0104 fm^3 x 10^{-3}/M_{pi+}
- The single-nucleon form factor F_T^{S+V} for 3He is 0.017(13)(3) and for 3H is 1.493(25)(3)
- The single-nucleon form factor F_T^{S-V} for 3He is 1.480(26)(3) and for 3H is 0.012(13)(3)

## Context

This work extends the previous calculation by Lenkewitz (2011) from third order to fourth order in the chiral expansion, including all next-to-next-to-leading order contributions. It follows the approach developed for the deuteron by Beane (1995, 1997) and Krebs (2004), and enables future experimental measurements of neutral pion photoproduction off 3He to test the chiral perturbation theory prediction that the neutron S-wave multipole E_{0+}^{pi0 n} is larger in magnitude than the corresponding proton multipole.
