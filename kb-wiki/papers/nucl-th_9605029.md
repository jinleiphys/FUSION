# Meson Transition Form Factors From A QCD Model Field Theory

## Key claim

The model field theory generates finite-size $\bar{q}q$ meson modes and produces form factors and coupling constants for the $\gamma^* \pi^0 \gamma$, $\rho\pi\pi$ and $\gamma\pi\rho$ interactions that are consistent with dynamical chiral symmetry breaking, gauge invariance, quark confinement and perturbative QCD, but the paper does not present explicit numerical results for these quantities in the truncated text.

## Method

The approach implements dressing of the vertices and propagators within a model field theory framework that respects dynamical chiral symmetry breaking, gauge invariance, quark confinement, and perturbative QCD constraints. The form factors and coupling constants for the $\gamma^* \pi^0 \gamma$, $\rho\pi\pi$ and $\gamma\pi\rho$ interactions are generated from the finite-size $\bar{q}q$ meson modes produced by the model.
