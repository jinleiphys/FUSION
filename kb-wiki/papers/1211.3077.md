# Holographic Pomeron and Entropy

> In dipole-dipole scattering at large rapidity χ={\rm ln}(s/s_0), the induced instanton on the string worldsheet carries entropy S_k=2(α_{Pk}-1)χwith α_{Pk}-1 the pomeron intercept for a dipole source of N-ality k. This stringy entropy is neither coherent nor thermal. We argue that it is released promptly over a time t_R\approx (b / χ)^3 / (4 α') with α'/2 the pomeron slope and b the impact parameter. This stringy entropy may explain the 3/2 jump in the total charged multiplicities at about 10 participants reported over a wide range of collider energies by PHOBOS. We predict the charged multiplicities in pp, pA and AA collisions at LHC.

## Key claim

The pomeron exchange in dipole-dipole scattering at large rapidity carries a stringy entropy that is neither coherent nor thermal, and this entropy may explain the 3/2 jump in total charged multiplicities at about 10 participants reported by PHOBOS, provided the impact parameter is large enough that the Unruh temperature remains below the Hagedorn temperature.

## Method

The authors model high-energy dipole-dipole scattering using a holographic description where the pomeron is a closed string exchange in 5 dimensions, with the string worldsheet subject to a longitudinal electric field that encodes the rapidity. They compute the semiclassical string instanton solutions and their on-shell action, then derive the entropy from the free energy via the Unruh temperature on the string worldsheet. The entropy is related to the wee-dipole multiplicity and the pomeron intercept for each N-ality k, and the formation time for entropy release is estimated from string diffusion in Rindler space.

## Key numbers

- Pomeron intercept for N-ality k=1: (α_P1 - 1) ≈ 0.15, giving entropy per unit rapidity S_1/χ ≈ 1/3
- For pp collisions: typical number of dipoles A_pp Q_S^2 ≈ 12 with Q_S^2 ≈ 1/2 GeV^2, yielding S_pp/χ ≈ 4
- Asymptotic energy scaling of pp entropy: S_pp ≈ (s/s_0)^0.228 ln(s/s_0)
- Predicted charged multiplicities at LHC: N_ch,pp ∼ 54, N_ch,pPb ∼ 320, N_ch,PbPb ∼ 16800 at √s = 2.76 TeV; N_ch,pp ∼ 82, N_ch,pPb ∼ 470, N_ch,PbPb ∼ 23400 at √s = 7 TeV using ⟨A_PbPb⟩ = 191
- Release time estimate: t_R ∼ (25 fm)/χ^3 for typical impact parameter b ∼ 10√α' with α' = (0.1 fm)^2

## Context

This work extends the holographic pomeron description of Basar (2012) by adding entropy considerations from the string worldsheet instanton, and connects to the color glass condensate approach at weak coupling (Kharzeev 2001, Baier 2002) and black hole formation in holographic QCD (Shuryak 2005, Gubser 2008). The entropy mechanism enables predictions for charged multiplicities in pp, pA, and AA collisions at LHC energies, and suggests that the 3/2 jump observed by PHOBOS arises from the exchange of N-ality k=1 and k=2 strings when nucleons begin to overlap.
