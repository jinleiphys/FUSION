# Polarization in low energy kaon-hyperon interaction

> In this paper, we study the low energy kaon-hyperon interaction considering effective chiral Lagrangians that include kaons, $σ$ mesons, hyperons and the corresponding resonances. The scattering amplitudes are calculated and then we determine the angular distributions and polarizations.

## Key claim

The paper calculates angular distributions and polarizations for low-energy kaon-hyperon and antikaon-hyperon scattering using effective chiral Lagrangians, but the authors note that these observables will likely not be measured experimentally in the near future due to technical difficulties; instead, the results are intended to be used as input for a hydrodynamical model of hyperon polarization in high-energy collisions.

## Method

The authors compute scattering amplitudes for \(K\Lambda\), \(\overline K\Lambda\), \(K\Sigma\), and \(\overline K\Sigma\) interactions using effective nonlinear chiral Lagrangians that include kaons, \(\sigma\) mesons, hyperons, and spin-1/2 and spin-3/2 baryon resonances. The tree-level amplitudes are unitarized via the \(K\)-matrix method, and the \(S\)-wave (\(l=0\)) and \(P\)-wave (\(l=1\)) partial waves are retained as the dominant contributions at low energies. The polarization and angular distributions are then obtained from the spin-non-flip and spin-flip amplitudes \(f\) and \(g\) for each isospin channel.

## Key numbers

*   Kaon mass: \(m_K = 496\ \mathrm{MeV}\).
*   \(\Lambda\) hyperon mass: \(m_\Lambda = 1116\ \mathrm{MeV}\).
*   \(\Sigma\) hyperon mass: \(m_\Sigma = 1190\ \mathrm{MeV}\).
*   Coupling constant \(g_{\Lambda KN} = 11.50\) (dimensionless) for the \(K\Lambda\) interaction.
*   Coupling constant \(g_{\Sigma KN} = 6.90\) (dimensionless) for the \(K\Sigma\) interaction.
*   Coupling constant \(g_{\Sigma K\Xi} = 13.40\) (dimensionless) for the \(K\Sigma\) interaction.
*   The scalar \(\sigma\) meson exchange amplitude is parametrized as \(A_\sigma = a + bt\) with \(a = 1.05\ m_\pi^{-1}\) and \(b = -0.8\ m_\pi^{-3}\), where \(m_\pi = 140\ \mathrm{MeV}\).
*   The polarization and angular distribution results are presented graphically as functions of the incident momentum \(k\) and \(x = \cos\theta\) in the center-of-mass frame, with a gap of \(10\ \mathrm{MeV}\) between polarization lines.

## Context

This work extends a previously proposed model by Barros and collaborators (2010-2015) that described hyperon and antihyperon polarization in high-energy proton-nucleus and heavy-ion collisions by considering only final-state pion-hyperon interactions. The present paper calculates the kaon-hyperon and antikaon-hyperon interactions needed to improve that hydrodynamical model, following the same chiral Lagrangian approach previously applied to pion-hyperon scattering by Barros and collaborators (2015) and to pion-nucleon scattering by Mancini and collaborators (2005). The results enable future inclusion of kaon-hyperon final-state interactions in the hydrodynamical model, which could be tested against experimental data from high-energy collisions.
