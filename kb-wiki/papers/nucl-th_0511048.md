# Boson Realization of the su(3)-Algebra. IV

> On the basis of the Schwinger boson representation for the Elliott model developed in (III), the Holstein-Primakoff representation for the su(3)-algebra is presented. The basic idea is the same as that adopted in (II), and as an example, the case of the lowest approximation is shown.

## Key claim
The paper constructs a Holstein-Primakoff boson representation for the su(3)-algebra suitable for the Elliott model, but the treatment is explicitly valid only under the condition that the total boson number \(l_a+l_b\) is much larger than the fluctuation quantum numbers \(m_1+2m_2\), meaning the approximation holds for high angular momentum states.

## Method
Starting from the Schwinger boson representation of the su(3)-algebra developed in paper (III), the authors introduce a new boson space with operators \((\hat{\alpha}_+, \hat{\alpha}_+^*)\), \((\hat{\alpha}_0, \hat{\alpha}_0^*)\), \((\hat{\beta}_+, \hat{\beta}_+^*)\), and \((\hat{\beta}_0, \hat{\beta}_0^*)\). They map the original boson operators to these new bosons using square-root factors analogous to the Holstein-Primakoff mapping, then express the su(3) generators (angular momentum operators \(\hat{L}_\pm, \hat{L}_0\), quadrupole operators \(\hat{Q}_0\), \(\hat{C}_1\), \(\hat{C}_2\), and su(1,1) generators \(\hat{T}_\pm, \hat{T}_0\)) in terms of the new bosons. As the simplest approximation, they linearize these expressions in the boson operators and diagonalize the resulting forms.

## Key numbers
- The intrinsic state parameters are defined as \(l_a = (1/2)(L^0 + Q^0)\) and \(l_b = (1/2)(L^0 - Q^0)\), where \(L^0\) and \(Q^0\) are quantum numbers.
- In the linear approximation, the angular momentum raising operator becomes \(\bar{L}_+^A = \sqrt{2(l_a + l_b)}\,\hat{\gamma}_+^*\).
- The quadrupole operator eigenvalue in the linear approximation is \(Q^A = (l_a - l_b)(1 - 2(m_1 - m_2))\).
- The angular momentum magnitude eigenvalue is \(L^A = l_a + l_b - (m_1 + 2m_2)\).
- The condition for validity is \(l_a + l_b \gg m_1 + 2m_2\).

## Context
This paper (IV) is a direct continuation of the authors' series (I), (II), and (III) on boson realizations of the su(3)-algebra for the Elliott model of nuclear collective motion. It extends the Holstein-Primakoff representation approach previously applied to su(2) in paper (II) to the su(3) case. The linearized approximation obtained is described as equivalent to the random phase approximation (RPA) order, and the resulting boson states provide an approximate form of the formalism developed in paper (III).
