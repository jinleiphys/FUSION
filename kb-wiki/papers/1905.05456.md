## Context
This work extends the BnGa partial-wave-analysis program to the őõ(1405) region and directly contradicts the two-pole structure found in chiral unitary approaches by Oller and Meissner (2000), Jido et al. (2003), and many subsequent studies such as Cieply et al. (2009), Ikeda et al. (2012), and Mai et al. (2012). The result supports earlier single-pole interpretations from HADES data (Agakishiev et al., 2012) and the CLAS collaboration (Moriya et al., 2013), and is consistent with quark model expectations where őõ(1405) is the SU(3) singlet state. The analysis enables a forthcoming companion study (Hyperon II and III) of the full hyperon resonance spectrum.
