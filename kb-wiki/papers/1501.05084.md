# Strong $Σ_bNB$ and $Σ_cND$ coupling constants in QCD

> We study the strong interactions among the heavy bottom spin--1/2 $Σ_b$ baryon, nucleon and $B$ meson as well as the heavy charmed spin--1/2 $Σ_c$ baryon, nucleon and $D$ meson in the context of QCD sum rules. We calculate the corresponding strong coupling form factors defining these vertices by using a three point correlation function. We obtain the numerical values of the corresponding strong coupling constants via the most prominent structure entering the calculations.

## Key claim

The strong coupling constants \(g_{\Sigma_bNB}\) and \(g_{\Sigma_cND}\) are extracted from QCD sum rules at the unphysical point \(Q^2 = -m_{B[D]}^2\) using a three-point correlation function, with the results depending on the choice of the most prominent Dirac structure (the \(\not{q}\not{p}\gamma_5\) structure was selected based on pole dominance and OPE convergence criteria).

## Method

The authors computed the three-point correlation function for the \(\Sigma_b N B\) and \(\Sigma_c N D\) vertices in the framework of QCD sum rules. The OPE side was evaluated using heavy and light quark propagators, including perturbative and non-perturbative (condensate) contributions, while the hadronic side was expressed in terms of hadron masses, residues, and decay constants. A double Borel transformation was applied to suppress higher-state and continuum contributions, and the sum rule for the coupling form factor was obtained by matching the two sides for the \(\not{q}\not{p}\gamma_5\) Dirac structure.

## Key numbers

*   \(g_{\Sigma_bNB}(Q^2 = -m_B^2) = 12.96 \pm 3.49~\text{GeV}^{-1}\) (from the \(\not{q}\not{p}\gamma_5\) structure).
*   \(g_{\Sigma_cND}(Q^2 = -m_D^2) = 3.78 \pm 1.06~\text{GeV}^{-1}\) (from the \(\not{q}\not{p}\gamma_5\) structure).
*   Borel parameter windows: \(10~\text{GeV}^2 \leq M^2 \leq 20~\text{GeV}^2\) (for \(\Sigma_b\)) and \(2~\text{GeV}^2 \leq M^2 \leq 6~\text{GeV}^2\) (for \(\Sigma_c\)); \(1~\text{GeV}^2 \leq M'^2 \leq 3~\text{GeV}^2\) (for the nucleon channel).
*   Continuum threshold windows: \(34.9~\text{GeV}^2 \leq s_0 \leq 37.4~\text{GeV}^2\) (for \(\Sigma_b\)) and \(6.5~\text{GeV}^2 \leq s_0 \leq 7.6~\text{GeV}^2\) (for \(\Sigma_c\)); \(1.04~\text{GeV}^2 \leq s'_0 \leq 1.99~\text{GeV}^2\) (for the nucleon channel).

## Context

This work extends the authors' previous QCD sum-rule calculation of the \(\Lambda_b N B\) and \(\Lambda_c N D\) couplings (Azizi, 2014) to the \(\Sigma_b N B\) and \(\Sigma_c N D\) vertices. The results are intended for use in describing the interactions of \(B\) and \(D\) mesons with nucleons in nuclear medium, and in the bottom and charmed meson clouds description of the nucleon, which may help explain exotic events observed at LHC and heavy-ion collision experiments like PANDA at FAIR. Prior approximate predictions for \(g_{\Lambda_c N D}\) at zero momentum transfer were made by Navarra (year not given), and the charmed baryon couplings were also studied in light-cone QCD sum rules by Khodjamirian (year not given).
