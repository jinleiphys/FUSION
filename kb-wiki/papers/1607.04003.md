# Spectra and elliptic flow of (multi-)strange hadrons at RHIC and LHC within viscous hydrodynamics+hadron cascade hybrid model

> Using the (2+1)-dimensional ultrarelativistic viscous hydrodynamics+hadron cascade, VISHNU, hybrid model, we study the $p_{\rm T}$-spectra and elliptic flow of $Λ$, $Ξ$, and $Ω$ in Au+Au collisions at $\sqrt{s_{NN}}$=200 GeV and in Pb+Pb collisions at $\sqrt{s_{NN}}$=2.76 TeV. Comparing our model results with the data measurements, we find that the VISHNU model gives general descriptions of the measurements of these strange and multi-strange hadrons at several centrality classes at RHIC and LHC. Mass ordering of elliptic flow $v_{2}$ among $π$, $K$, $p$, $Λ$, $Ξ$, and $Ω$ are further investigated and discussed at the two collision systems. We find, at both RHIC and LHC, the $v_{2}$ mass ordering among $π$, $K$, $p$, and $Ω$ are fairly reproduced within the VISHNU hybrid model, and more improvements are needed to implement for well describing the $v_{2}$ mass ordering among $p$, $Λ$, and $Ξ$.

## Key claim
The VISHNU hybrid model generally describes the pT-spectra and elliptic flow of Λ, Ξ, and Ω at RHIC and LHC, but the mass ordering of elliptic flow among p, Λ, and Ξ is not fully reproduced, with the model under-predicting the elliptic flow of protons relative to Λ and Ξ at low pT.

## Method
The (2+1)-dimensional ultrarelativistic viscous hydrodynamics code VISH2+1 was coupled with the hadronic cascade model UrQMD, using a switching temperature of 165 MeV. Smooth initial entropy density profiles were generated from the MC-KLN model with reaction plane averaging, and the QGP specific shear viscosity was set to (η/s)QGP = 0.16. The model was run for Au+Au collisions at √sNN = 200 GeV and Pb+Pb collisions at √sNN = 2.76 TeV, and results were compared to STAR and ALICE measurements.

## Key numbers
- Switching temperature Tsw = 165 MeV for transition from hydrodynamics to hadronic cascade
- Initial hydrodynamic start time τ0 = 0.9 fm/c
- MC-KLN λ parameter tuned to 0.218 at RHIC and 0.138 at LHC
- QGP specific shear viscosity (η/s)QGP = 0.16
- Charged hadron multiplicity density dNch/dη = 687.4 ± 36.6 at RHIC and 1601 ± 60 at LHC for most central collisions
- VISHNU Λ spectra at pT < 2 GeV/c are about 40% lower than STAR Λ and about 20% lower than STAR anti-Λ at RHIC
- VISHNU Λ spectra are about 30% lower than ALICE measurements at LHC due to missing Σ0 and Σ(1385) feed-down contributions
- VISHNU generally describes elliptic flow for Λ, Ξ, and Ω at pT < 2 GeV/c within statistical error bars at LHC

## Context
This work extends previous VISHNU studies of π, K, and p (Song 2011, Song 2013) to strange and multi-strange hadrons, which are less affected by hadronic interactions and thus more sensitive to the QGP stage. The results are compared to STAR measurements at RHIC (Adams 2006, Abelev 2008, Adamczyk 2015) and ALICE measurements at LHC (Abelev 2013, Abelev 2014). The failure to describe the mass ordering among p, Λ, and Ξ suggests that improvements to UrQMD hadronic cross-sections or inclusion of initial flow may be needed, as previously discussed by Hirano (2007).
