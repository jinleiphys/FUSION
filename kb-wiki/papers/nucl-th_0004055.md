# Electromagnetic production of vector mesons at low energies

> We have investigated exclusive photoproduction of light vector mesons ($ω$, $ρ$ and $ϕ$) on the nucleon at low energies. In order to explore the questions concerning the so-called missing nucleon resonances, we first establish the predictions from a model based on the Pomeron and meson exchange mechanisms. We have also explored the contributions due to the mechanisms involving $s$- and $u$-channel intermediate nucleon state. Some discrepancies found at the energies near threshold and large scattering angles suggest a possibility of using this reaction to identify the nucleon resonances.

## Key claim
The paper suggests that discrepancies between their non-resonant model predictions and existing data for vector meson photoproduction at low energies and large scattering angles could be removed by including contributions from nucleon resonances, but this is presented as a possibility rather than a confirmed finding.

## Method
The authors constructed a model for exclusive photoproduction of ρ, ω, and φ mesons on the nucleon at low energies, including three mechanisms: Pomeron exchange, one-meson exchange (π, η, σ), and s- and u-channel intermediate nucleon terms. They calculated total and differential cross sections and compared them with existing experimental data to identify kinematic regions where the non-resonant background fails to describe the measurements.

## Key numbers
- Pomeron trajectory parameters: α_P(t) = 1.08 + α'_P t with α'_P = 0.25 GeV^{-2}
- Vector meson decay constants: f_ρ = 5.04, f_ω = 17.05, f_φ = 13.13
- Coupling constants β_u = β_d = 2.07 GeV^{-1}, β_s = 1.60 GeV^{-1}, μ_0^2 = 1.1 GeV^2
- Pseudoscalar coupling constants: g_{Vγπ} = 0.274 (ρ), 0.706 (ω), 0.042 (φ) in GeV^{-1}; g_{Vγη} = 0.062 (ω), 0.209 (φ) in GeV^{-1}
- σ meson mass M_σ = 0.5 GeV, g^2_{σNN}/4π = 8.0, g_{Vγσ} ≈ 3.0
- Nucleon coupling constants: (g_{ρNN}, κ_ρ) = (6.2, 2.0), (g_{ωNN}, κ_ω) = (7.0, 0)
- Form factor cutoff parameters: Λ_π = 0.7 GeV, Λ_{Vγπ} = 0.77 GeV, Λ_η = 1.0 GeV, Λ_{Vγη} = 0.9 GeV, Λ_σ = 1.0 GeV, Λ_{Vγσ} = 0.9 GeV, Λ_{VNN} = 0.8 GeV
- The model reproduces total cross section data up to E_γ = 10 GeV for ρ photoproduction
- Discrepancies are found at E_γ ≤ 2 GeV for ρ and ω photoproduction

## Context
This work extends the Pomeron exchange model of Donnachie and Landshoff (1984) and others, which successfully describes vector meson photoproduction at high energies, to low energies near threshold where meson exchange mechanisms become important. The authors build on the σ exchange mechanism introduced by Friman and Soyeur (1996) for ρ production and the s+u nucleon-term formalism from Sato and Lee (1996). The study aims to establish the non-resonant background needed to search for missing nucleon resonances predicted by quark models such as those of Zhao, Li, and Zou (1998) and the Regge theory approach of Laget (2000). The results enable future work incorporating nucleon resonance excitations to test QCD-inspired hadron structure models against new experimental data.
