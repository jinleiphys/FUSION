# Effects of charge symmetry breaking on form factors of the pion and kaon

> Effects of charge symmetry breaking associated with the $u$ and $d$ quark mass difference in the elastic form factors of the pion and kaon are presented. We use a confining version of the Nambu--Jona-Lasinio model. The pion and kaon are described as a dressed quark and antiquark bound states governed by the Bethe-Salpeter equation, and exhibit the properties of Goldstone bosons, with the pion mass difference given by $m_{π^{+}}^2 - m_{π^{0}}^2 \propto (m_u -m_d)^2$ as demanded by dynamical chiral symmetry breaking. We found significant charge symmetry breaking effects for realistic current quark mass ratios ($m_u/m_d \sim 0.5$) in the quark electromagnetic form factors of the pion and kaon. We also report the effects of charge symmetry breaking on the parton distribution functions, which are rather smaller than those found in the electromagnetic form factors.

## Key claim

Charge symmetry breaking (CSB) effects from the \(u\) and \(d\) quark mass difference are surprisingly large in the quark elastic form factors of the pion and kaon at high momentum transfer, reaching 8% in the pion and 15% in the kaon at \(Q^2 \simeq 10\) GeV\(^2\) for a realistic mass ratio \(m_u/m_d \sim 0.5\); however, CSB effects in the parton distribution functions are much smaller, at the few percent level.

## Method

The authors use a confining version of the Nambu-Jona-Lasinio (NJL) model, a chiral effective field theory of QCD, to describe the pion and kaon as dressed quark-antiquark bound states via the Bethe-Salpeter equation. The electromagnetic form factors are computed from the sum of two Feynman diagrams for the meson electromagnetic current, with the dressed quark-photon vertex and body form factors evaluated for varying \(m_u/m_d\) ratios while fixing the charged pion mass and decay constant at their physical values.

## Key numbers

- For \(m_u/m_d \simeq 0.5\), CSB effects in the quark sector radii of the pion: \([|r^u_{\pi^+}| - |r^d_{\pi^+}|] / [|r^u_{\pi^+}| + |r^d_{\pi^+}|] \simeq 0.7\%\).
- For \(m_u/m_d \simeq 0.5\), CSB effects in the quark sector radii of the kaon: \([|r^u_{K^+}| - |r^d_{K^0}|] / [|r^u_{K^+}| + |r^d_{K^0}|] \simeq 0.6\%\).
- At \(Q^2 \simeq 10\) GeV\(^2\) and \(m_u/m_d \simeq 0.5\), CSB in the pion quark form factor ratio reaches 8\%.
- At \(Q^2 \simeq 10\) GeV\(^2\) and \(m_u/m_d \simeq 0.5\), CSB in the kaon ratio \(F^u_{K^+}(Q^2)/F^d_{K^0}(Q^2)\) reaches 15\%.
- At the scale \(Q^2 = 5\) GeV\(^2\), CSB effects in the parton distribution functions are at the few percent level.
- For the pion ratio \(u_{\pi^+}(x)/\bar{d}_{\pi^+}(x)\) as \(x\rightarrow 1\), CSB reaches 3\%.
- For the kaon ratio \(u_{K^+}(x)/d_{K^0}(x)\) as \(x\rightarrow 1\), CSB reaches 1\%.

## Context

This work extends previous NJL model studies of hadron properties (Hutauruk 2018, Cloet 2014) by explicitly incorporating charge symmetry breaking from the \(u\) and \(d\) quark mass difference into the elastic form factors and parton distribution functions of the pion and kaon. The results provide predictions for CSB effects that are relevant for interpreting experiments such as parity-violating deep inelastic scattering on the deuteron and \(\pi^+/\pi^-\) production in semi-inclusive DIS at Jefferson Lab, and could be tested against future lattice QCD calculations of CSB in these systems.
