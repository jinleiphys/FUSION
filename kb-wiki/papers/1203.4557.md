# Proceedings of the 2nd Iberian Nuclear Astrophysics Meeting on Compact Stars

> This volume contains most of the links to the presentations delivered at this international workshop. This meeting was the second in the series following the previous I Encuentro Ibérico de Compstar, held at the University of Coimbra, Portugal in 2010. The main purpose of this meeting was to strengthen the scientific collaboration between the participants of the Iberian and the rest of the southern European branches of the European Nuclear Astrophysics network, formerly, COMPSTAR. This ESF (European Science Foundation) supported network has been crucial in helping to make a broader audience for the the most interesting and relevant research lines being developed currently in Nuclear Astrophysics, especially related to the physics of neutron stars. The program of the meeting was tailored to theoretical descriptions of the physics of neutron stars although some input from experimental observers and other condensed matter and optics areas of interest was also included.

# Proceedings of the 2nd Iberian Nuclear Astrophysics Meeting on Compact Stars

## Key claim
This volume indexes the presentations delivered at a workshop aimed at strengthening scientific collaboration among Iberian and southern European branches of the European Nuclear Astrophysics network (formerly COMPSTAR), with the program tailored to theoretical descriptions of neutron-star physics while also including input from experimental observers and condensed-matter/optics areas.

## Method
The meeting was held at the University of Salamanca, Spain on September 22-23, 2011, and the proceedings consist of an index of links to the presentations. The program covered theoretical descriptions of neutron-star physics, including magnetic fields in compact stars, nuclear structure and in-medium effects, equation of state from nuclear matter to quarks, crust evolution, computational simulations of collapsing dense objects, and observational phenomenology. Leading experts delivered specialized review talks on core-collapse supernova simulations and hadron-quark phase transitions.

## Key numbers
Review-type paper: no original numerics.

## Context
This meeting was the second in a series following the I Encuentro Ibérico de Compstar held at the University of Coimbra, Portugal in 2010. It was supported by the ESF (European Science Foundation) network COMPSTAR, which has been crucial for broadening the audience for research lines in Nuclear Astrophysics related to neutron stars. The proceedings enable access to presentations on topics including the symmetry energy, hyperonic three-body forces, magnetar X-ray spectra, and vortex-lattice interactions in pulsar glitches, with the goal of fostering new collaborations in this interdisciplinary area.
