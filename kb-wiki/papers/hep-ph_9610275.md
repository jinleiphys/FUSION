# Renormalization of the three flavor Lagrangian in heavy baryon chiral perturbation theory

> The complete renormalization of the generating functional for Green functions of quark currents between one--baryon states in three flavor heavy baryon chiral perturbation theory is performed to order q^3. As an example, we study the kaon loop induced divergences in neutral pion photoproduction off protons.

## Key claim

The paper performs the complete renormalization of the generating functional for Green functions of quark currents between one-baryon states in three-flavor heavy baryon chiral perturbation theory to order \(q^3\), extending the two-flavor work of Ecker (1994) to SU(3). The authors note that while the two-flavor case has nucleons in the fundamental representation of SU(2), the three-flavor case has baryons in the adjoint representation of SU(3), leading to a richer algebraic structure and many more allowed terms in the chiral Lagrangian.

## Method

The authors use the path integral approach to heavy baryon chiral perturbation theory, decomposing baryon fields into velocity-dependent light and heavy components and integrating out the heavy components. They expand the generating functional around the classical meson solution to one-loop order, then extract the divergent parts of the irreducible tadpole and self-energy functionals using heat kernel techniques in \(d\)-dimensional Euclidean space, with divergences appearing as simple poles in \(\epsilon = 4-d\). The counterterm Lagrangian at order \(q^3\) is constructed and the \(\beta\)-functions for the pertinent operators are tabulated.

## Key numbers

- The chiral dimension for processes with one baryon line is given by \(D = 2L + 1 + \sum_{d=4,6,\ldots} (d-2) N_d^M + \sum_{d=2,3,\ldots} (d-1) N_d^{MB} \ge 2L+1\), where \(L\) is the number of meson loops, \(N_d^M\) counts mesonic vertices of dimension \(d\), and \(N_d^{MB}\) counts meson-baryon vertices of dimension \(d\)
- Tree graphs start contributing at order \(q\) and \(L\)-loop graphs at order \(q^{(2L+1)}\)
- The inverse of the matrix \(C\) is expanded as \([C^{ab}]^{-1} = \delta^{ab}/(2m) - A^{ab}_{(1)}/(2m)^2 + \mathcal{O}(q^2)\)
- The first three heat kernel coefficients in the coincidence limit are \(h_0| = 1\), \(h_1| = -a\), and \(h_2| = \frac{1}{2}a^2 - \frac{1}{6}[d_\mu,[d_\mu,a]] + \frac{1}{12}(\gamma_{\mu\nu})^2\)

## Context

This work extends the two-flavor (pion-nucleon) renormalization program of Ecker (1994) to the three-flavor (kaon, pion, eta) sector, where previous calculations of baryon masses, magnetic moments, and hyperon decays were either finite to order \(q^3\) or only accounted for leading non-analytic pieces at a scale of 1 GeV. The only prior works performing renormalization at order \(q^3\) in SU(3) concerned kaon-nucleon interaction, kaon photo- and electroproduction, and strong and electromagnetic decays of the decuplet. The results enable systematic chiral-invariant renormalization of all two-baryon Green functions in three-flavor heavy baryon chiral perturbation theory to order \(q^3\), with the tabulated counterterms and \(\beta\)-functions serving as a check for future calculations such as neutral pion photoproduction off protons.
