# Properties of the light scalar mesons face the experimental data on the $ŽĢ\toŽĆ^0ŽĆ^0╬│$ decay and the $ŽĆŽĆ$ scattering

> The high-statistical KLOE data on the $ŽĢ\toŽĆ^0ŽĆ^0╬│$ decay are described simultaneously with the data on $ŽĆŽĆ$ scattering and the $ŽĆŽĆ\to K\bar K$ reaction. The description is carried out taking into account the chiral shielding of the putative $Žā(600)$ meson and it's mixing with the well-established $f_0(980)$ meson. It is shown that the data don't contradict the existence of the $Žā(600)$ meson and yield evidence in favor of the four-quark nature of the $Žā(600)$ and $f_0(980)$ mesons.

## Key claim

The high-statistical KLOE data on the $\phi\to\pi^0\pi^0\gamma$ decay do not contradict the existence of the putative $\sigma(600)$ meson and yield evidence in favor of the four-quark nature of both the $\sigma(600)$ and $f_0(980)$ mesons, provided that the chiral shielding of the $\sigma(600)$ and its mixing with the $f_0(980)$ are taken into account in a simultaneous description of the $\phi$ decay, $\pi\pi$ scattering, and $\pi\pi\to K\bar K$ reaction data.

## Method

The authors performed a simultaneous fit of the KLOE data on the $\phi\to\pi^0\pi^0\gamma$ decay mass spectrum, the $\pi\pi$ S-wave scattering phase shift $\delta^0_0$, and the $\pi\pi\to K\bar K$ reaction data up to 1.1 GeV. The analysis used the $K^+K^-$ loop model for the signal amplitude $\phi\to\gamma(f_0+\sigma)\to\gamma\pi^0\pi^0$, including the mixing of the $f_0(980)$ and $\sigma(600)$ mesons via a matrix of inverse propagators, and incorporated the chiral shielding background phase $\delta_B^{\pi\pi}$ parameterized as a polynomial in $p_\pi/m_\pi$ with a cut-off factor, along with a parameterized $K\bar K$ background phase $\delta_B^{K\bar K}$.

## Key numbers

* The $f_0(980)$ mass from Fit 1 is $984.1$ MeV, with coupling $g_{f_0K^+K^-}=4.3$ GeV and $g_{f_0\pi^+\pi^-}=-1.8$ GeV.
* The $\sigma(600)$ mass from Fit 1 is $461.9$ MeV, with width $\Gamma_\sigma=286.0$ MeV, coupling $g_{\sigma\pi^+\pi^-}=2.4$ GeV, and $g_{\sigma K^+K^-}=0.55$ GeV.
* The total $\chi^2$ per number of degrees of freedom for Fit 1 is $40.0/48$, corresponding to a confidence level of $79\%$.
* The $\pi\pi$ scattering length $a^0_0$ is fixed to $0.22\ m_\pi^{-1}$ in Fits 3 and 4, and obtained as $0.209\ m_\pi^{-1}$ in Fit 1.
* The inelasticity $\eta^0_0$ at $m=1010$ MeV is $0.48$ in Fit 1 and $0.41$ in Fit 4.
* The Adler zero in the $\pi\pi\to\pi\pi$ amplitude appears at $(178\ \text{MeV})^2$ in Fit 1.

## Context

This work extends the earlier simultaneous analysis of the $\phi\to\pi^0\pi^0\gamma$ decay and $\pi\pi$ scattering phase by Achasov and Kiselev (2003) by incorporating the chiral shielding of the $\sigma(600)$ meson, as originally proposed by Achasov and collaborators (1994) in the context of the linear sigma model. The analysis uses the high-statistics KLOE data (2004) and the four-quark model of Jaffe (1977) as a motivating scenario for the scalar meson couplings. The results enable further tests of the four-quark nature of light scalar mesons and provide constraints for future experiments on $\phi$ radiative decays and $\pi\pi$ scattering.
