# Resonances in Exotic $^7$He Nucleus within the No-Core Shell Model

> We present results of calculations of $n{-}{^6\rm He}$ elastic scattering phase shifts and resonances in ${^7\rm He}$. The calculations utilize the SS-HORSE method combined with ab initio no-core shell model calculations of the ${^7\rm He}$ and ${^6\rm He}$ nuclei with Daejeon16 and the JISP16 $NN$ interactions.

## Key claim

The paper claims that the SS-HORSE method combined with no-core shell model (NCSM) calculations can predict neutron-⁶He elastic scattering phase shifts and ⁷He resonance parameters using only two-nucleon (NN) interactions (Daejeon16 and JISP16), but the results for the 1/2⁻ resonance are sensitive to the choice of interaction and contradict the hypothesis of a low-lying narrow resonance suggested by one experiment (Meister).

## Method

The authors performed ab initio NCSM calculations of ⁶He and ⁷He eigenenergies with Daejeon16 and JISP16 NN interactions, using basis spaces up to Nmax=16–17 and oscillator energies ħΩ from 8 to 50 MeV. They then applied the SS-HORSE method, which uses the NCSM eigenenergies to compute single-channel S-matrix elements via Eq. (2), parameterized the effective range function with a Padé approximant, and located S-matrix poles in the complex energy plane to extract resonance energies and widths. The fit was performed on selected NCSM energies that satisfy convergence criteria (monotonic ħΩ dependence and consistent phase shifts across different Nmax).

## Key numbers

- 3/2⁻ resonance in ⁷He with Daejeon16: Er = 0.27(1) MeV, Γ = 0.12(1) MeV; with JISP16: Er = 0.70(2) MeV, Γ = 0.60(2) MeV.
- 1/2⁻ resonance in ⁷He with Daejeon16: Er = 2.7(1) MeV, Γ = 4.2(1) MeV; with JISP16: Er = 2.8(1) MeV, Γ = 5.02(2) MeV.
- 5/2⁻ resonance in ⁷He with Daejeon16: Er = 3.65(2) MeV, Γ = 1.37(1) MeV; with JISP16: Er = 4.37(4) MeV, Γ = 1.55(2) MeV.
- 3/2⁻ scattering length with Daejeon16: a₁ = −170(10) fm³; with JISP16: a₁ = −66(2) fm³.
- 1/2⁺ scattering length with Daejeon16: a₀ = 2.1(2) fm; with JISP16: a₀ = 3.2(5) fm.
- Experimental 3/2⁻ resonance from Cao compilation: Er = 0.430(3) MeV, Γ = 0.182(5) MeV.

## Context

This work extends the SS-HORSE method, previously applied to n-α and p-α scattering in ⁵He and ⁵Li (Shirokov et al., 2016–2019), to the A=7 system with a ⁶He target. It provides ab initio predictions for ⁷He resonances that can be compared with Gamow shell model (GSM) calculations (Papadimitriou et al., 2013) and NCSM with continuum (NCSMC) calculations (Navratil et al., 2010–2016) using SRG-evolved N³LO chiral forces. The results enable future studies of neutron-rich helium isotopes and tests of NN interactions in continuum states, while highlighting the need for three-nucleon forces or improved interactions to resolve discrepancies in the 1/2⁻ resonance width.
