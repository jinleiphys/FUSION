# Soft gluon resummation in the quark-vector boson vertex at high energy: On-mass-shell case

> Resummation of the soft gluon radiative corrections for the quark-vector boson vertex is performed within the path-integral (world-line) approach. The leading order expression for the vacuum averaged Wilson integral for an arbitrary gauge field is found in n-dimensional space-time. The cusp anomalous dimension of the color non-singlet Sudakov form factor of on-mass-shell quark is calculated in an arbitrary covariant gauge in the one-loop order, and the leading double-logarithmic asymptotical behavior is obtained from the corresponding evolution equation.

## Key claim

The paper claims that the cusp anomalous dimension for the color non-singlet quark form factor has a negative sign in the leading logarithmic approximation, leading to enhancement rather than suppression of the form factor at large momentum transfer, in contrast to the color singlet case. This result corrects an erroneous sign in the gauge-dependent term of the anomalous dimension reported in prior work by Sityf (year not given in text).

## Method

The author applies the path-integral (world-line) approach to compute the vacuum averaged Wilson integral for the quark-vector boson vertex, representing the color non-singlet Sudakov form factor of an on-mass-shell quark. The calculation is performed in n-dimensional space-time in an arbitrary covariant gauge, using dimensional regularization with n = 4 - 2ε to regulate infrared divergences. The cusp anomalous dimension is extracted from the renormalization group evolution equation for the form factor at one-loop order.

## Key numbers

- The one-loop cusp anomalous dimension for the color non-singlet quark form factor is: Γ_{cusp}^{qqg}[α_s(μ²); χ; ξ] = (α_s/π)[(C_F - C_A/2) χ coth χ + (C_A/4)ξ - C_F] + O(α_s²), where χ is the hyperbolic angle related to the scattering angle and ξ is the gauge parameter.
- In the large scattering angle limit (χ → ∞), the leading asymptotic anomalous dimension is: Γ_{cusp}^{qqg}[α_s] = (α_s/π)(C_F - C_A/2) + O(α_s²), which is negative for SU(3) color group.
- The leading double-logarithmic behavior of the form factor is: F^{qqg}[Q²]/F^{qqg}[Q₀²] = exp[(2/β₀ N_c) ln(Q²/Λ_{QCD}²) ln(ln(Q²/Λ_{QCD}²)/ln(Q₀²/Λ_{QCD}²)) + NLO].
- The one-loop beta function coefficient is β₀ = (11/3)N_c - (2/3)n_f.

## Context

This work extends the path-integral formulation of Sudakov resummation, previously applied to the color singlet quark-photon vertex by Kron (year not given) and Stef (year not given), to the color non-singlet quark-gluon vertex. The result corrects an erroneous sign in the gauge-dependent term of the cusp anomalous dimension reported by Sityf (year not given) in the leading logarithmic approximation. The findings enable further study of the infrared behavior of QCD, chiral symmetry breaking, and confinement, and are relevant for evaluating electroweak Sudakov corrections at future TeV-scale colliders.
