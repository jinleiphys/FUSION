# Possible $Λ_cΛ_c$ molecular bound state

> Possible $Λ_cΛ_c$ hadronic molecule is investigated in the one-pion-exchange potential model. In the study with this model, the heavier meson exchange effects are encoded into a phenomenological cutoff parameter and couplings to the nearby $Σ_cΣ_c$, $Σ_cΣ_c^*$, and $Σ_c^*Σ_c^*$ channels are essential. From the numerical results, we find that a molecular bound state of two $Λ_c$'s is possible, where the tensor force plays a crucial role, although the binding energies are sensitive to the cutoff parameter.

## Key claim

A molecular bound state of two Λc baryons is possible in a one-pion-exchange potential model, but only when couplings to ΣcΣc, ΣcΣc*, and Σc*Σc* channels are included; the binding energies are highly sensitive to the phenomenological cutoff parameter Λπ, and the tensor force from S-D wave mixing is essential.

## Method

The authors solved coupled-channel Schrödinger equations using a variational method for the ΛcΛc system with quantum numbers I(JP)=0(0+), including five coupled channels: ΛcΛc(1S0), ΣcΣc(1S0), Σc*Σc*(1S0), Σc*Σc*(5D0), and ΣcΣc*(5D0). They derived non-relativistic one-pion-exchange potentials from chiral Lagrangians with heavy-quark spin symmetry relations, introduced a monopole form factor with a common cutoff Λπ at each vertex, and treated Λπ as a free parameter scanned from 1.0 to 1.5 GeV.

## Key numbers

* Binding energy of the ΛcΛc system ranges from 3.39 MeV (Λπ = 1.0 GeV) to 177.07 MeV (Λπ = 1.5 GeV) in the full five-channel calculation.
* Root-mean-square radius of the bound state ranges from 2.0 fm (Λπ = 1.0 GeV) down to 0.5 fm (Λπ = 1.5 GeV).
* With only the ΣcΣc*(5D0) D-wave channel included (and no Σc*Σc*(5D0)), binding energy is 0.07 MeV at Λπ = 1.0 GeV and 114.25 MeV at Λπ = 1.5 GeV.
* The ΛcΛc(1S0) channel probability in the full calculation is 97.4% at Λπ = 1.0 GeV and 79.0% at Λπ = 1.5 GeV; the total D-wave probability rises from 2.2% to 13.7% over the same range.
* Hadron masses used: mπ = 137.27 MeV, mΛc = 2286.46 MeV, mΣc = 2453.56 MeV, mΣc* = 2517.97 MeV.

## Context

This work extends the authors' previous study of the ΛcN system (Liu 2011) using the same one-pion-exchange potential approach, and contrasts with a contemporaneous study by Lee (2011) that omitted Σc* contributions and D-wave channels, leading to different conclusions. The results suggest that a ΛcΛc molecular bound state could be searched for at facilities such as GSI, J-PARC, RHIC, or Belle, and that future work should incorporate intermediate-range scalar meson exchange, short-range vector meson exchange, and the ΞccN channel to better constrain the binding energy.
