# Polarization observables in the $e^+ e^- \rightarrow \barО› О›$ reaction

> Cross-section, vector-polarization, and tensor-polarization distributions are calculated for the reactions $e^+ e^- \rightarrow \bar{p}p$ and $e^+ e^- \rightarrow \barО› О›$. Each reaction requires six characteristic functions that are bilinear in the, possibly complex, electromagnetic form factors, denoted $G_E(P^2)$ and $G_M(P^2)$, of $p$ and $О›$. For the hyperon reaction also the joint-decay distributions of $О›$ and $\barО›$ are calculated. Their knowledge allow a complete determination of the hyperon electromagnetic form factors, without measuring hyperon spins. We explain how this is done in practice. For some tensor-polarization components our results are in conflict with previously repeatedly published distributions.

## Key claim

The paper claims that for the reaction \(e^+ e^- \rightarrow \bar{\Lambda} \Lambda\), the non-diagonal tensor-polarization components \(A_{xy}=A_{yx}\) vanish, leaving \(A_{xz}=A_{zx}\) as the only non-vanishing non-diagonal components, which contradicts previously published results by Gakh and Tomasi-Gustafsson (2007) and Buttimore and Jennings (2008) that suggested a non-vanishing value for \(A_{xy}\).

## Method

The author calculates cross-section, vector-polarization, and tensor-polarization distributions for \(e^+ e^- \rightarrow \bar{p}p\) and \(e^+ e^- \rightarrow \bar{\Lambda} \Lambda\) using bilinear combinations of the complex electromagnetic form factors \(G_E(P^2)\) and \(G_M(P^2)\). For the hyperon reaction, the joint-decay distributions of \(\Lambda \rightarrow p\pi^-\) and \(\bar{\Lambda} \rightarrow \bar{p}\pi^+\) are computed using the folding method of Czyz et al. (1997) in the covariant version developed by FГ¤ldt (2015), with proper counting of intermediate states.

## Key numbers

- The unpolarized differential cross-section is given by \(\frac{d\sigma}{d\Omega} = \frac{\alpha^2}{4s^2}\frac{p}{k}\left( D_c - D_s \sin^2(\theta)\right)\), where \(D_c = 2s|G_M|^2\) and \(D_s = s|G_M|^2 - 4M^2|G_E|^2\).
- The vector-polarization component \(P_y\) is proportional to \(-2Ms\sqrt{s}\sin(2\theta)\text{Im}(G_MG_E^\star)\).
- The non-vanishing non-diagonal tensor-polarization component is \(A_{xz} = A_{zx} = 2Ms\sqrt{s}\sin(2\theta)\text{Re}(G_MG_E^\star)/\mathcal{P}^{00}\).
- The diagonal tensor-polarization components are: \(\mathcal{P}^{55}_{yy} = -s D_s \sin^2(\theta)\), \(\mathcal{P}^{55}_{xx} = s(D_c - D_s)\sin^2(\theta)\), and \(\mathcal{P}^{55}_{zz} = s[D_s\sin^2(\theta) + D_c\cos^2(\theta)]\).

## Context
