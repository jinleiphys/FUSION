# Charge Radii of Hyperons

> The momentum projected SU(3) Chiral Color Dielectric Model (CCDM) is employed to calculate the charge root mean square radii and charge distributions of hyperons. We compare our result with Skyrme, MIT bag and Cloudy Bag model results. The charge distribution of $Λ$ in CCDM is similar to that of Skyrme Model prediction.

# Charge Radii of Hyperons

## Key claim
The momentum-projected SU(3) Chiral Color Dielectric Model (CCDM) predicts charge radii for hyperons that are generally larger than those from the MIT bag model and Cloudy Bag Model, with the charge distribution of the Λ hyperon being similar to the Skyrme model prediction. The neutron charge radius is found to be -0.284 fm, which is close to the experimental value, though the model does not reproduce the Skyrme model value of -0.55 fm.

## Method
The authors employ the SU(3) Chiral Color Dielectric Model with a non-scaling version where confinement is identical for strange and non-strange baryons, similar to the MIT bag model. The Peierls-Yoccoz momentum projection technique is applied to remove spurious center-of-mass motion from the mean-field solution. The total electromagnetic current includes both quark and meson contributions, with the charge radius extracted from the second-order expansion of the charge density matrix element in momentum transfer.

## Key numbers
- Proton charge radius in CCDM: 0.814 fm (compared to experimental value 0.82 fm)
- Neutron charge radius in CCDM: -0.284 fm (experimental value -0.35 fm)
- Λ charge radius in CCDM: 0.287 fm (Skyrme model: 0.33 fm, MIT bag: 0.16 fm, CBM: 0.008 fm)
- Σ⁺ charge radius in CCDM: 0.841 fm (Skyrme: 0.98 fm, MIT bag: 0.75 fm, CBM: 0.75 fm)
- Σ⁻ charge radius in CCDM: -0.737 fm (Skyrme: -0.87 fm, MIT bag: -0.71 fm, CBM: -0.76 fm)
- Ξ⁰ charge radius in CCDM: 0.554 fm (Skyrme: 0.47 fm, MIT bag: 0.23 fm, CBM: 0.15 fm)
- Ξ⁻ charge radius in CCDM: -0.593 fm (Skyrme: -0.51 fm, MIT bag: -0.69 fm, CBM: -0.72 fm)
- Ω⁻ charge radius in CCDM: -0.86 fm (Skyrme: -0.38 fm, MIT bag: -0.67 fm, CBM: -0.75 fm)
- Model parameters: m_u = m_d = 105 MeV, B^(1/4) = 94.5 MeV, m_GB = 1050 MeV, m_s = 318 MeV, f_φ = 93 MeV

## Context
This work extends the SU(3) Chiral Color Dielectric Model previously applied to baryon masses and magnetic moments (Sahu, 1993) to calculate charge radii of the full baryon octet and decuplet. The results are compared with predictions from the Skyrme model (Kunz, 1990), MIT bag model, and Cloudy Bag Model (Thomas, 1984). The finding that Λ and Σ⁰ have nearly identical charge radii and charge distributions in CCDM, and that neutral hyperons exhibit negative core charge density with positive surface charge density, provides testable predictions for future hyperon electromagnetic form factor measurements.
