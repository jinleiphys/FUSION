# Sudakov Resummations in Mueller-Navelet Dijet Production

> In high energy hadron-hadron collisions, dijet production with large rapidity separation proposed by Mueller and Navelet, is one of the most interesting processes which can help us to directly access the well-known Balitsky-Fadin-Kuraev-Lipatov evolution dynamics. The objective of this work is to study the Sudakov resummation of Mueller-Navelet jets. Through the one-loop calculation, Sudakov type logarithms are obtained for this process when the produced dijets are almost back-to-back. These results could play an important role in the phenomenological study of dijet correlations with large rapidity separation at the LHC.

## Key claim

Through a one-loop calculation in the Coulomb gauge, Sudakov-type double logarithms are identified in Mueller-Navelet dijet production when the produced dijets are almost back-to-back, but these logarithms are expected to be important primarily when the rapidity separation Y is not too large, while BFKL evolution dominates at asymptotically large Y.

## Method

The authors perform a one-loop calculation in the color dipole model using the Coulomb gauge, which treats projectile and target protons symmetrically. They compute real and virtual gluon emission diagrams, isolating Sudakov double logarithms from the soft-collinear region of phase space where the gluon transverse momentum lies between the dijet momentum imbalance q_perp and the hard scale P_perp. The Sudakov factor is obtained by adding real and virtual contributions, with the cancellation of ultraviolet divergences between virtual graphs and the cancellation of soft-collinear divergences between real and virtual graphs.

## Key numbers

- The Sudakov suppression factor for the quark-quark channel is S_qq→qq = -(α_s C_F)/(2π) ln²(P_perp² R_perp² / c₀²), where c₀ = 2e^{-γ_E}
- For the qg→qg channel: S_qg→qg = -(α_s (C_F + C_A))/(4π) ln²(P_perp² R_perp² / c₀²)
- For the gg→gg channel: S_gg→gg = -(α_s C_A)/(2π) ln²(P_perp² R_perp² / c₀²)
- The BFKL pomeron intercept at leading order is α_P - 1 = (4α_s N_c)/π ln 2
- The leading order cross section for the quark-quark channel contains a factor (N_c² - 1)/N_c² × α_s²/(k₁_perp² k₂_perp²) δ^(2)(k₁_perp + k₂_perp)

## Context

This work extends the Sudakov resummation techniques developed by Mueller, Xiao, and Yuan (2013) for Higgs and dijet production in pA collisions to the case of Mueller-Navelet dijet production in pp collisions. It complements the extensive next-to-leading order BFKL calculations by groups including Ciafaloni (1998), Bartels (2001-2002), Caporale (2011-2014), and Colferai (2010), which have achieved reasonable agreement with CMS data from 2013. The results enable simultaneous treatment of Sudakov resummation and BFKL evolution in phenomenological studies of dijet correlations at the LHC, particularly in kinematic regimes where both effects may be significant.
