# Revealing transversity GPDs through the photoproduction of a photon and a $ρ$ meson

> Photoproduction of a pair of particles with large invariant mass is a natural extension of collinear QCD factorization theorems which have been much studied for deeply virtual Compton scattering and deeply virtual meson production. We discuss the production of a photon and a meson, where the wide angle Compton scattering on a meson subprocess factorizes from generalized parton distribution. We calculate at dominant twist and leading order in $α_s$, the production cross-section of a transversely polarized $ρ$ meson which is sensitive to chiral-odd GPDs, and show that it may be measurable in near future JLab experiments.

## Key claim

The photoproduction of a transversely polarized ρ meson in association with a photon, at large invariant mass, provides access to chiral-odd transversity GPDs (H_T, E_T, H̃_T, Ẽ_T) which are otherwise inaccessible in deeply virtual Compton scattering and deeply virtual meson production at leading order, and the cross-section may be measurable in near-future JLab experiments.

## Method

The authors calculate the amplitude for the reaction γ + N → γ + ρ⁰ + N' using collinear QCD factorization, where a wide-angle Compton scattering subprocess γ + (q q̄) → γ + ρ factorizes from generalized parton distributions (GPDs). The calculation is performed at dominant twist and leading order in α_s, using the asymptotic form of the ρ-meson distribution amplitude and a model for transversity GPDs based on transversity PDFs. The differential cross-section is obtained by integrating over the parton momentum fraction x and the meson distribution amplitude variable z, with numerical integration for the x-dependence.

## Key numbers

* Differential cross-section dσ/(dt du' dM²_{γρ}) evaluated at |t| = |t|_min for S_{γN} = 20 GeV², shown as a function of -u' for M²_{γρ} = 3, 4, 5, 6 GeV² (units: pb·GeV⁻⁶)
* The hard scale M²_{γρ} = (p_ρ + k)² is the squared invariant mass of the final photon-ρ meson pair, with values 3, 4, 5, 6 GeV² used in the numerical results
* The skewness parameter ξ is given by ξ = τ/(2-τ) where τ = (M²_{γρ} - t)/(S_{γN} - M²)

## Context

This work extends the factorization approach used for deeply virtual Compton scattering (DVCS) and deeply virtual meson production to the photoproduction of a photon-meson pair, following the strategy initiated for π meson production in Pire, Szymanowski, and Wallon (2014). The process provides an alternative to quark mass effects (Polyakov and Schweitzer, 2015) or twist-3 distribution amplitude approaches (Liuti) for accessing transversity GPDs, which decouple from DVCS and deeply virtual meson production at leading order. The results enable experimental extraction of chiral-odd GPDs at JLab@12GeV through angular analysis of the π⁺π⁻ decay of the ρ meson.
