# Light S-wave pentaquarks on the light front

> We construct an explicit basis set for pentaquark states on a regular 4-simplex, that diagonalizes the Hamiltonian for light pentaquarks with confinement on the light front (LF). The ensuing eigenstates are free of the center of mass motion and satisfy exact Dirichlet boundary conditions. Hyperfine interactions in the form of color-spin or flavor-spin are shown to lift the degeneracy of the 16 pentastates, with a spectrum that compares fairly with some of the empirical nucleon excited states. The quark PDF for the light pentastates is discussed.

## Key claim

The authors construct an explicit basis set for pentaquark states on a regular 4-simplex that diagonalizes the Hamiltonian for light pentaquarks with confinement on the light front, producing eigenstates free of center-of-mass motion that satisfy exact Dirichlet boundary conditions. The resulting spectrum compares fairly with some empirical nucleon excited states, but the authors note that the minimal pentastate mass from their unshifted Hamiltonian is about 4.7 GeV, requiring a free constant shift to compare with experimental data.

## Method

The authors used Jacobi coordinates to eliminate center-of-mass motion from the light-front Hamiltonian for five-quark states, then constructed longitudinal eigenstates using Slater determinants of circular waves mapped onto a regular 4-simplex (pentachoron) with Dirichlet boundary conditions. Transverse eigenstates were constructed using generalized Laguerre polynomials as harmonic oscillator solutions. The full light-front Hamiltonian was diagonalized in this product basis with truncation cutoffs of 134 longitudinal states and 126 transverse states, and hyperfine interactions (color-spin and flavor-spin) were applied to lift degeneracies among the 16 pentastates.

## Key numbers

- Constituent quark mass: m_Q = 383 ± 39 MeV, tied to the vacuum condensate at zero momentum
- String tension: σ_T = (0.44 GeV)^2, fixed from the rho mass via σ_T = m_ρ^2/π
- Ground state mass minimal value occurs at einbein parameter a = 7.59
- The minimal pentastate mass from the unshifted LF Hamiltonian is about 4.7 GeV
- The quark PDF at large x_5 asymptotes as (1-x_5)^15
- For color-spin interaction, the Delta-nucleon mass splitting (1232-939 MeV) sets the coupling via M_Δ - M_N = 16V_1g^2/m_Q^2
- For flavor-spin interaction, C_χ = 21 MeV is used following Helminen (2000)
- The spin 5/2^- state is 300 MeV heavier than the empirical PDG value

## Context

This work extends the light-front approach to pentaquarks previously developed for baryons by Shuryak (2022), and complements variational analyses by Miesch (2025) that showed S- and P-shell pentaquark states mix with 3-quark states through meson clouds in the center-of-mass frame. Unlike the approach of Wiecki (2014) where center-of-mass motion is removed by projection with a Lagrange multiplier, the present construction yields an eigenbasis inherently free of CM motion. The results enable future studies of parton distribution functions in pentaquark states and their role in explaining baryon structure, including the d-u flavor asymmetry in nucleons.
