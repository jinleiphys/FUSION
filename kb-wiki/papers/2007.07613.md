# Inclusive Hadroproduction of $P$-Wave Heavy Quarkonia in Potential Nonrelativistic QCD

> We compute the color-singlet and color-octet nonrelativistic QCD (NRQCD) long-distance matrix elements for inclusive production of $P$-wave quarkonia in the framework of potential NRQCD. In this way, the color-octet NRQCD long-distance matrix element can be determined without relying on measured cross section data, which has not been possible so far. We obtain inclusive cross sections of $χ_{cJ}$ and $χ_{bJ}$ at the LHC, which are in good agreement with data. In principle, the formalism developed in this Letter can be applied to all inclusive production processes of heavy quarkonia.

# Inclusive Hadroproduction of $P$-Wave Heavy Quarkonia in Potential Nonrelativistic QCD

## Key claim
The color-octet NRQCD long-distance matrix element for $P$-wave quarkonium production can be determined from first principles using potential NRQCD, without relying on measured cross section data, under the assumption of strong coupling ($\Lambda_{\rm QCD} \gg mv^2$) and with the approximation that wave functions for excited gluonic states ($n \neq 0$) are the same as the quarkonium wave function ($n=0$), making an error of at most $O(1/N_c^2)$.

## Method
The authors compute the color-singlet and color-octet NRQCD long-distance matrix elements (LDMEs) for inclusive $P$-wave quarkonium production by expanding the NRQCD Hamiltonian in quantum-mechanical perturbation theory and matching to potential NRQCD (pNRQCD) in the strong coupling regime. The LDMEs are expressed in terms of quarkonium wave functions at the origin and a universal gluonic correlator $\mathcal{E}$, which is then fitted to $\chi_{cJ}$ production data from ATLAS at the LHC and used to predict $\chi_{bJ}$ feed-down fractions.

## Key numbers
- $|R^{(0)'}_{\chi_{c0}}(0)|^2 = 0.057$ GeV$^5$, obtained from comparing measured two-photon decay rates of $\chi_{c0}$ and $\chi_{c2}$ with pNRQCD expressions at leading order in $v$ and NLO in $\alpha_s$.
- $|R^{(0)'}_{\chi_{b0}(1P)}(0)|^2 = 1.47$ GeV$^5$, $|R^{(0)'}_{\chi_{b0}(2P)}(0)|^2 = 1.74$ GeV$^5$, $|R^{(0)'}_{\chi_{b0}(3P)}(0)|^2 = 1.92$ GeV$^5$, taken from potential-model calculations.
- Fitted value of the gluonic correlator in the $\overline{\rm MS}$ scheme: $\mathcal{E}(\Lambda = 1.5\ \text{GeV}) = 1.94 \pm 0.04$, obtained from $\chi_{cJ}$ production data at $\sqrt{s}=7$ TeV, $|y|<0.75$.
- Heavy quark masses: $m_c = 1.5$ GeV, $m_b = 4.75$ GeV.
- Uncertainties: 30% for $\chi_{cJ}$ cross sections and 10% for $\chi_{bJ}$ cross sections from unaccounted relativistic corrections of relative order $v^2$.

## Context
This work extends the application of potential NRQCD, previously successful for annihilation and exclusive electromagnetic production (Brambilla 2001, Brambilla 2002, Brambilla 2020), to inclusive production processes for the first time. It resolves the long-standing problem that color-octet LDMEs could only be determined phenomenologically from fits to cross section data, which led to inconsistent sets of LDMEs and contradicting predictions, particularly for quarkonium polarization (Chung 2018). The formalism enables first-principles predictions for $\chi_{bJ}(nP)$ cross sections without fitting color-octet LDMEs on bottomonium production data, and can be extended to other heavy quarkonium production processes including $J/\psi$ and $\Upsilon(nS)$ states.
