# Canonical interpretations of the newly observed $Ξ_c(2923)^0$, $Ξ_c(2939)^0$ and $Ξ_c(2965)^0$ resonances

> Three neutral resonances $Ξ_c(2923)^0$, $Ξ_c(2939)^0$ and $Ξ_c(2965)^0$ have been observed in the $Λ_c^+ K^-$ mass spectrum by the LHCb Collaboration. Given the $Ξ_c$ and $Ξ_c^\prime$ mass spectra predicted by the constituent quark models, these three resonances are tentatively treated as the $λ-$mode $Ξ_c(2S)$, $Ξ_c^\prime(2S)$, $Ξ_c^{\prime *}(2S)$, and $Ξ_c^\prime(1P)$ states, and their strong decay behaviors are calculated within the $^3P_0$ model. Our results indicate that the $Ξ_c(2923)^0$ and $Ξ_c(2939)^0$ can be clarified into the $J^P=3/2^-$ and $5/2^-$ $Ξ_c^\prime(1P)$ states respectively, and the $Ξ_c(2965)^0$ can be regarded as the $J^P=1/2^+$ $Ξ_c^\prime(2S)$ state. Also, we suggest that the previous observed $Ξ_c(2930)$ may be the overlap of two structures $Ξ_c(2923)$ and $Ξ_c(2939)$, and the $Ξ_c(2970)$ should be the same state as $Ξ_c(2965)$. Other theoretical information on the missing $Ξ_c(2S)$, $Ξ_c^{\prime *}(2S)$, and $Ξ_c^\prime(1P)$ states may be helpful for future experimental searches.

## Key claim

The paper argues that the three newly observed resonances can be assigned to specific conventional quark-model states: the Ξ_c(2923)^0 and Ξ_c(2939)^0 are identified as J^P=3/2^- and 5/2^- Ξ_c'(1P) states respectively, while the Ξ_c(2965)^0 is identified as a J^P=1/2^+ Ξ_c'(2S) state; this assignment is conditional on the calculated strong decay widths within the ^3P_0 model agreeing with the experimental data, and the paper notes that the Ξ_c(2S) assignment is excluded because it cannot decay to Λ_c K̅.

## Method

The strong decay widths of the three resonances were calculated under various assignments (Ξ_c(2S), Ξ_c'(2S), Ξ_c'*(2S), and five Ξ_c'(1P) states) using the ^3P_0 quark-pair-creation model. The model parameters (harmonic oscillator parameter α_ρ, quark masses, and overall pair-creation strength γ) were taken from previous successful studies of singly heavy baryons. The calculated total widths and partial decay modes were compared with the experimental masses and widths from LHCb to determine the most likely assignments.

## Context

This work extends the interpretation of the low-lying Ξ_c and Ξ_c' spectra using the ^3P_0 model, following previous quark-model studies by Ebert (2011), Chen (2014, 2016), and others. The paper contradicts the QCD sum rule analysis by Yang (2020), which suggested all three resonances are P-wave Ξ_c' baryons. The results enable future experimental searches for the missing Ξ_c(2S), Ξ_c'*(2S), and other Ξ_c'(1P) states, and suggest that the previously observed Ξ_c(2930) (from BaBar) is an overlap of the two new narrow structures, while Ξ_c(2970) (from Belle) is the same state as Ξ_c(2965).
