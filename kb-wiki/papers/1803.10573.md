> We present an analysis of $őě^-$ baryon production in subthreshold proton--nucleus ($p+A$) collisions in the framework of a BUU type transport model. We propose a new mechanism for $őě$ production in the collision of a secondary $őõ$ or $ő£$ hyperon and a nucleon from the target nucleus.
