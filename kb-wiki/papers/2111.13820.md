# Hidden charm mesons in nuclear matter and nuclei

> Recent results for the $η_c$- and $J/ψ$-nucleus bound state energies for various nuclei are presented. The attractive potentials for the $η_c$ and $J/ψ$ mesons in the nuclear medium originate, respectively, from the in-medium enhanced $DD^{*}$ and $D\bar{D}$ loops in the $η_c$ and $J/ψ$ self energies. Our results suggest that the $η_c$ and $J/ψ$ mesons should form bound states with all the nuclei considered.

## Key claim
The authors claim that both the ηc and J/ψ mesons should form bound states with all nuclei considered (4He, 16O, 40Ca, 208Pb), but the precise bound-state energies depend sensitively on the cutoff parameter ΛD used in the vertex form factors, which introduces a significant uncertainty.

## Method
The authors compute the ηc and J/ψ self-energies in nuclear matter using effective Lagrangians at the hadronic level, considering only the DD* loop for ηc and the D\bar{D} loop for J/ψ. The in-medium D and D* meson masses are calculated within the quark-meson coupling (QMC) model, and the meson-nucleus potentials are obtained via a local density approximation using nuclear density distributions from the QMC model. Bound-state energies are then found by solving the Klein-Gordon equation for each nucleus.

## Key numbers
- ηc-4He 1s bound-state energy: -1.49 MeV (ΛD = 1500 MeV) to -8.55 MeV (ΛD = 3000 MeV)
- ηc-208Pb 1s bound-state energy: -12.99 MeV (ΛD = 1500 MeV) to -24.12 MeV (ΛD = 3000 MeV)
- J/ψ-4He 1s bound-state energy: not bound (n) for ΛD = 1500 and 2000 MeV; -0.01 MeV (ΛD = 2500 MeV); -0.02 MeV (ΛD = 3000 MeV)
- J/ψ-208Pb 1s bound-state energy: -3.28 MeV (ΛD = 1500 MeV) to -7.08 MeV (ΛD = 3000 MeV)
- Coupling constants used: gηc = 3.24, gJ/ψ = 7.64
- Saturation density of symmetric nuclear matter: ρ0 = 0.15 fm^{-3}
- Cutoff parameter ΛD varied in the range 1500-3000 MeV

## Context
This work extends previous studies by the same group on J/ψ-nucleus bound states (Cobos-Martinez et al., 2020) to include the ηc meson, using the same QMC model framework for in-medium D meson masses. The approach is also related to earlier work on φ meson binding in nuclei (Cobos-Martinez et al., 2020) and has been extended to bottomonium (Zeminiani et al., 2020). The results suggest that experimental searches for charmonium-nucleus bound states could be feasible at JLab with the 12 GeV upgrade, particularly for medium and heavy nuclei like 208Pb.
