# Deformed Boson Scheme Stressing Even-Odd Boson Number Difference. I

> An idea of the deformed boson scheme developed by the present authors is applied to the case of boson-pair coherent state. In this state, even and odd boson number difference is stressed and various concrete examples of the boson-pair coherent states are shown. Concerning some of them, mutual relations are investigated in connection to the su(1,1)-algebra. A formulation in terms of the MYT boson mapping is also performed.

## Key claim

The paper systematically constructs deformed boson-pair coherent states that stress the even-odd boson number difference, showing that the conventional boson coherent state gives poorer results than certain deformed variants in numerical analyses, but the new states do not generally satisfy the Poisson bracket relation of the su(1,1)-algebra exactly.

## Method

The authors decompose the conventional exponential boson coherent state into hyperbolic cosine and sine forms, which respectively contain only even-boson-number and odd-boson-number components. They then apply a deformed boson scheme where the creation operator is multiplied by a function of the number operator, and derive the expectation values of the su(1,1)-algebra generators for both the even and odd deformed states, together with the conditions for canonical variables.

## Key numbers

* The conventional boson coherent state gives expectation values \((\tau_0)_{cr^0} = |c|^2/2 + 1/4\) (Eq. 2-11).
* For the even-boson-number state \(\ket{ch^0}\), \((\tau_0)_{ch^0} = |\gamma|^2/2 \cdot \tanh |\gamma|^2 + 1/4\) (Eq. 3-5a).
* For the odd-boson-number state \(\ket{sh^0}\), \((\tau_0)_{sh^0} = |\gamma|^2/2 \cdot \coth |\gamma|^2 + 1/4\) (Eq. 3-5b).
* The expectation value of the boson number for \(\ket{ch^0}\) is \(2c^*c\) and for \(\ket{sh^0}\) is \(2c^*c+1\) (text after Eq. 3-8).
* The approximate forms for the functions relating \(\gamma\) and \(c\) are \(F_{ch^0}(|\gamma|^2) = G_{ch^0}(2|c|^2) + 2|c|^2\) and \(F_{sh^0}(|\gamma|^2) = G_{sh^0}(2|c|^2) + (2|c|^2+2)\) (Eqs. 3-10a, 3-10b).
* The approximate exponential forms are \(G_{ch^0}(2|c|^2) \sim \exp[-(2/3)\cdot 2|c|^2 - (2/15)\cdot (2|c|^2)^2]\) and \(G_{sh^0}(2|c|^2) \sim \exp[-(2/5)\cdot 2|c|^2 - (2/175)\cdot (2|c|^2)^2]\) (Eqs. 3-11a, 3-11b).
* The inequalities for the even state are \(|\tau_\pm(0)| < |(\tau_\pm)_{ch^0}| < |\tau_\pm(1/4)|\) (Eq. 3-16a).
* The inequalities for the odd state are \(|\tau_\pm(1/2)| < |(\tau_\pm)_{sh^0}| < |\tau_\pm(3/4)|\) (Eq. 3-16b).

## Context

This work extends the deformed boson scheme previously developed by the same authors (Kuriyama et al., Refs. two, three, four, five) and follows the multiboson coherent state idea of Penson and Solomon (Ref. six). The paper systematically classifies boson-pair coherent states that stress even-odd number difference, enabling future application of the time-dependent variational method to Hamiltonians containing terms like \({\hat c}^{*4}\), \({\hat c}^{*3}{\hat c}\), and \({\hat c}^4\), where the effect of even-odd boson number difference can be investigated.
