# The Degrees of Freedom in Hot Quenched QCD

> In quenched QCD, on lattices with $\nt=4$, the absence of a pole in the pseudoscalar meson channel for $T>T_c$ is demonstrated. A set of effective 4-fermi couplings is extracted. It is observed that this coupling is small in the vector channel for all $T>T_c$, but not in the pseudoscalar channel. The temperature dependence of hadronic parameters below \tc{} is found to be small. NOTE: Latex source, no figures, needs espcrc2.sty from hep-lat macros.

## Key claim
In quenched QCD on lattices with Nτ=4, the pseudoscalar meson channel shows no pole above Tc, ruling out a pion state to 10σ, but the vector channel coupling is small for all T>Tc while the pseudoscalar channel coupling is not; the temperature dependence of hadronic parameters below Tc is small.

## Method
The study uses lattice QCD simulations with staggered fermions on Nτ=4 lattices with spatial sizes up to 32, at β values corresponding to T=0.75Tc, 1.1Tc, 1.5Tc, and 2Tc. Screening masses and susceptibilities are computed in the π, σ, ρ, and A channels for quark masses 0.01 to 0.15, with extrapolations to the chiral limit. Effective 4-fermi couplings are extracted by inverting ladder-sum relations for the susceptibilities.

## Key numbers
- Pion screening mass in the chiral limit at β=5.5 (T=0.75Tc): μπa = 0.005 ± 0.002
- Rho screening mass in the chiral limit at β=5.5: μρa = 1.47 ± 0.02
- Chiral condensate at T=0.75Tc: ⟨ψ̄ψ⟩a³ = 0.227 ± 0.006 (N_z=16) and 0.231 ± 0.005 (N_z=32)
- Pion decay constant at T=0.75Tc: fπa = 0.185 ± 0.007 (N_z=16) and 0.187 ± 0.002 (N_z=32)
- Slope Aπ from μπ²a² = Aπ mq a + Bπ: Aπ = 6.6 ± 0.2 (N_z=16) and 6.60 ± 0.04 (N_z=32)
- Fit constant B from 1/2χ = Aμ sinh μ + B at T=0.75Tc: B = 0.0008 ± 0.0004 (N_z=16) and 0.0008 ± 0.0003 (N_z=32)
- Fit constant B at β=5.75 (T=1.1Tc): B = 0.041 ± 0.003 (12²×24 lattice) and 0.043 ± 0.004 (16²×32 lattice), ruling out a pion pole to 10σ
- Effective 4-fermi coupling gγ5 T² at β=5.75 on 4×8²×16 lattice: 1.19 ± 0.03
- Effective 4-fermi coupling gγμ T² at β=5.75 on 4×8²×16 lattice: 0.21 ± 0.03
- Effective 4-fermi coupling gγ5 T² at β=6.05 on 4×8²×16 lattice: 0.38 ± 0.02
- Effective 4-fermi coupling gγμ T² at β=6.05 on 4×8²×16 lattice: -0.02 ± 0.03

## Context
This work extends earlier quenched QCD screening mass measurements (Author, year not given in text) and zero-temperature results (Fukugita, year not given in text) to finite temperature, demonstrating the absence of mesonic poles above Tc. It provides effective 4-fermi couplings that can serve as an effective theory for low-energy processes in the plasma phase, such as dimuon production rates, and enables lattice estimates of such processes by Wick-rotating spatial correlation functions.
