# Pentaquark baryons in SU(3) quark model

> We study the SU(3) group structure of pentaquark baryons which are made of four quarks and one antiquark. The pentaquark baryons form {1}, {8}, {10}, {10}-bar, {27}, and {35} multiplets in SU(3) quark model. First, the flavor wave functions of all the pentaquark baryons are constructed in SU(3) quark model and then the flavor SU(3) symmetry relations for the interactions of the pentaquarks with three-quark baryons and pentaquark baryons are obtained.

## Key claim

The paper constructs the complete SU(3) flavor wave functions for all pentaquark multiplets (singlet, octet, decuplet, antidecuplet, 27-plet, and 35-plet) in the quark model, but the results are purely group-theoretical and do not impose any dynamics on the quarks, meaning the wave functions are general regardless of the specific diquark-diquark-antiquark picture used for construction.

## Method

The authors start with quark and antiquark operators in SU(3) flavor space, first forming diquark states from two quarks (giving 6-plet and anti-triplet representations), then combining two diquarks to form four-quark states in various SU(3) representations (3, anti-6, 15_1, and 15_2), and finally coupling each four-quark state with an antiquark to obtain the six pentaquark multiplets. The flavor wave functions are identified by matching SU(3) tensor components to physical baryon states using hypercharge and isospin quantum numbers, and SU(3) symmetric interaction Lagrangians are constructed for pentaquark couplings to three-quark baryons and meson octets.

## Key numbers

- Pentaquark baryons form six SU(3) multiplets: **1**, **8**, **10**, **10-bar**, **27**, and **35**
- The antidecuplet contains a state with hypercharge Y=2 and isospin I=0 identified as Theta^+(1540), and a state with Y=-1 and I=3/2 identified as Xi(1862)
- The 27-plet contains isovector Theta states (Y=2, I=1): Theta_1^(++), Theta_1^+, Theta_1^0
- The 35-plet contains isotensor Theta states (Y=2, I=2): Theta_2^(+++), Theta_2^(++), Theta_2^+, Theta_2^0, Theta_2^-
- Gell-Mann-Okubo mass relations are found for the 27-plet, and equal spacing rules apply not only for decuplet and antidecuplet but also for 27-plet and 35-plet

## Context

This work extends earlier SU(3) studies of pentaquark antidecuplet wave functions by groups such as Close and Dudek (2004) and Ko et al. (2003-2004), and provides the complete set of wave functions for all six pentaquark multiplets that were previously only partially constructed. The results enable future studies of representation mixing induced by SU(3) symmetry breaking in quark potential models, and provide the necessary input for constructing chiral Lagrangians and analyzing production mechanisms for pentaquark states beyond the antidecuplet, particularly the isovector Theta in the 27-plet and isotensor Theta in the 35-plet that could be searched for in experiments.
