# $ρ$-Meson Form Factors in the Point Form

> We present a calculation of the electromagnetic form factors of the $ρ^+$ meson. Our formalism is based on the point-form of relativistic quantum mechanics. Electron-$ρ$-meson scattering is formulated as a coupled-channel problem for a Bakamjian-Thomas mass operator, such that the dynamics of the exchanged photon is taken explicitly into account. The $ρ$-meson current is extracted from on-shell matrix elements of the optical potential of the scattering process. As a consequence of the violation of cluster separability in the Bakamjian-Thomas framework, our current includes additional, unphysical contributions, which can be separated from the physical ones uniquely. Our results for the form factors are in good agreement with other approaches.

## Key claim

The authors calculate the electromagnetic form factors of the ρ⁺ meson using a point-form relativistic quantum mechanics approach, but the extracted current includes additional unphysical contributions due to the violation of cluster separability in the Bakamjian-Thomas framework; these spurious contributions can be uniquely separated from the physical ones by taking the limit of large total invariant mass squared \(s \to \infty\).

## Method

The authors formulate elastic electron-ρ-meson scattering as a coupled two-channel problem for a Bakamjian-Thomas mass operator, with channels \(e q \bar q\) and \(e q \bar q \gamma^*\) coupled through vertex operators derived from QED. The confining quark-antiquark interaction is modeled by an instantaneous harmonic-oscillator potential. The electromagnetic ρ-meson current is extracted from on-shell matrix elements of the one-γ*-exchange optical potential after a Feshbach reduction, and the physical form factors \(F_1\), \(F_2\), and \(G_M\) are obtained by taking the limit \(s \to \infty\) to eliminate spurious contributions arising from the violation of cluster separability.

## Key numbers

- Quark mass \(m_q = 0.34\) GeV and oscillator length \(a = 0.312\) GeV, fixed by the ρ-meson ground state mass \(m_\rho = 0.77\) GeV and the first two radial excitations of the vector-meson spectrum.
- Predicted magnetic dipole moment \(\mu_\rho = 2.2\) in units of \(|e|/2m_\rho\).
- Predicted electric quadrupole moment \(Q_\rho = -0.47\) in units of \(|e|/m_\rho^2\).

## Context

This work extends the authors' previously developed point-form coupled-channel formalism from the spin-0 pion case to the spin-1 ρ-meson, finding remarkable similarities with the covariant light-front approach of Carbonell et al. (1998). The results enable future applications of the formalism to deuteron form factors, electroweak form factors of heavy-light mesons, nucleon form factors with pion-cloud effects, baryon transition form factors such as N-Δ transitions, and meson-baryon vertices.
