# Microscopic calculations for solar nuclear reactions

> We have studied the 4He(3He,gamma)7Be, 3He(3He,2p)4He, and 7Be(p,gamma)8B reactions of the solar p-p chain, using microscopic cluster models. Among other results, we showed that the 6Li+p channel has a nontrivial effect on the 7Be-producing reaction, that the existence of a resonance in 6Be close to the 3He+3He threshold is rather unlikely, and that the correlations between some properties of 7Be/8B and the low-energy cross section of 7Be(p,gamma)8B might help one to constrain the value of the S_17(0) astrophysical S-factor.

## Key claim
The paper shows that the inclusion of the 6Li+p channel has an appreciable effect on the 4He(3He,γ)7Be reaction, but a more sophisticated 6Li state than the simple α+d model used is needed for a clear picture; the currently accepted S34(0) value appears in conflict with the recommended quadrupole moment of 7Li.

## Method
The authors used microscopic cluster models with wave functions built from antisymmetrized cluster internal states and high-precision variational treatment of intercluster relative motion. For 4He(3He,γ)7Be, they included the 6Li+p channel; for 3He(3He,2p)4He, they searched for narrow resonances in 6Be using rigorous 2-body and 3-body resonance treatments; for 7Be(p,γ)8B, they computed correlations between the zero-energy S-factor and properties of 7Be and 8B.

## Key numbers
- For 4He(3He,γ)7Be: the zero-energy astrophysical S-factor S34(0) is discussed; the currently accepted value is in conflict with the recommended quadrupole moment of 7Li, but no explicit S34(0) number is given in the text.
- For 3He(3He,2p)4He: no narrow resonances were found in 6Be close to the 3He+3He threshold; the only remaining possibility for such a state would come from s- and d-state interplay not present in the model.
- For 7Be(p,γ)8B: the model cannot produce a low value for S17(0); the correlations shown in Figure 1 involve the 8B point-nucleon radius r(8B) (in fm), the r2(8B)−r2(7Be) value (in fm2), the 8B quadrupole moment Q8 (in e fm2), and the Δ=E(8Li)−E(8B) Coulomb displacement energy (in MeV), but no explicit numerical values for these quantities are given in the text.

## Context
This work extends microscopic cluster-model calculations for solar p-p chain reactions, building on prior studies by Bahcall (solar models) and Adelberger (uncertainties in nuclear inputs). It specifically addresses the correlation between quadrupole moments and S34(0) previously used by Kajino, and corrects an error in the Coulomb-displacement energy used in an earlier study by the same group (Csoto 2000). The results suggest that fully consistent A-body dynamical models will be needed for further progress, and that the 7Be(p,γ)8B S-factor remains an open question.
