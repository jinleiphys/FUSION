# Chiral dynamics of the S11(1535) and S11(1650) resonances revisited

> We analyze s-wave pion-nucleon scattering in a unitarized chiral effective Lagrangian including all dimension two contact terms. We find that both the S11(1535) and the S11(1650) are dynamically generated, but the S31(1620) is not. We further discuss the structure of these dynamically generated resonances.

## Key claim
Both the S11(1535) and S11(1650) resonances are dynamically generated by coupled-channel dynamics in a unitarized chiral effective Lagrangian, even when the scattering data are fitted only up to sqrt(s) = 1.56 GeV, but the S31(1620) resonance is not generated by the same dynamics.

## Method
The authors analyzed s-wave pion-nucleon scattering using a coupled-channel Bethe-Salpeter equation (BSE) that includes all local contact terms from the first- and second-order chiral effective Lagrangian, going beyond the leading-order Weinberg-Tomozawa interaction. They considered six meson-baryon channels with strangeness zero and charge +1 (p pi0, n pi+, p eta, Lambda K+, Sigma0 K+, Sigma+ K0) and fitted the 14 low-energy constants plus three subtraction constants to the real and imaginary parts of the S11 and S31 partial wave amplitudes from the SAID program up to a center-of-mass energy of 1.56 GeV, without using the on-shell approximation for the BSE.

## Key numbers
- S11(1535) pole position: W = (1.506 - 0.140 i) GeV on the (222-111) Riemann sheet
- S11(1650) pole position: W = (1.692 - 0.046 i) GeV on the (222-111) Riemann sheet; W = (1.682 - 0.042 i) GeV on the (2222-11) Riemann sheet
- Best fit chi-squared per degree of freedom: chi2_dof = 1.23
- S-wave scattering lengths from the fit: a_{1/2} = 145.8 x 10^{-3} / M_{pi+}, a_{3/2} = -91.6 x 10^{-3} / M_{pi+}
- Fit range: center-of-mass energies up to W = 1.56 GeV
- For the S11(1535), the coupling ordering is: |g_{Lambda K+}|^2 > |g_{p eta}|^2 > |g_{Sigma+ K0}|^2 approx |g_{n pi+}|^2 > |g_{Sigma0 K+}|^2 approx |g_{p pi0}|^2
- For the S11(1650), the coupling ordering is: |g_{Sigma+ K0}|^2 > |g_{p eta}|^2 > |g_{Sigma0 K+}|^2 approx |g_{n pi+}|^2 > |g_{p pi0}|^2 >> |g_{Lambda K+}|^2

## Context
This work extends earlier unitarized chiral perturbation theory analyses of s-wave pion-nucleon scattering, particularly the studies by Kaiser (1995) which first suggested the S11(1535) might be dynamically generated, and by Nieves (2001) which fitted S11 phase shifts and cross sections up to about 2 GeV. The authors go beyond previous approaches by including all dimension-two contact terms in the chiral potential and solving the full Bethe-Salpeter equation without the on-shell approximation. The results enable future applications to meson photoproduction in a gauge-invariant chiral unitary framework, as the authors note this is an important step toward describing kaon photoproduction from coupled-channel unitarized chiral perturbation theory.
