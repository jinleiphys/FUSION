# Symmetry broken and restored coupled-cluster theory. II. Global gauge symmetry and particle number

> We have recently extended many-body perturbation theory and coupled-cluster theory performed on top of a Slater determinant breaking rotational symmetry to allow for the restoration of the angular momentum at any truncation order [T. Duguet, J. Phys. G: Nucl. Part. Phys. 42 (2015) 025107]. Following a similar route, we presently extend Bogoliubov many-body perturbation theory and Bogoliubov coupled cluster theory performed on top of a Bogoliubov reference state breaking global gauge symmetry to allow for the restoration of the particle number at any truncation order. Eventually, formalisms can be merged to handle $SU(2)$ and $U(1)$ symmetries at the same time. Several further extensions of the newly proposed many-body formalisms can be foreseen in the mid-term future. The long-term goal relates to the ab initio description of near-degenerate finite quantum systems with an open-shell character.

## Key claim

The paper extends Bogoliubov many-body perturbation theory (BMBPT) and Bogoliubov coupled cluster (BCC) theory, which are formulated on a reference state that breaks global gauge symmetry, to allow for exact particle-number restoration at any truncation order, provided the formalism is applied to a finite quantum system such as the atomic nucleus.

## Method

The authors reformulate the many-body formalism on the basis of a Bogoliubov reference state using Bogoliubov algebra, adapting the approach previously developed for SU(2) symmetry restoration in Paper I (Duguet, 2015). They introduce off-diagonal time-dependent kernels of the norm, Hamiltonian, particle-number, and grand potential operators, expressed as Fourier series over the irreducible representations of the U(1) group. The ground-state energy is extracted from the large-time limit of the ratio of the energy kernel to the norm kernel, which yields the eigenvalue for the targeted particle number at any gauge angle.

## Key numbers

- The volume of the U(1) group is given as \( v_{U(1)} \equiv \int_{0}^{2\pi} d\varphi = 2\pi \).
- The orthogonality relation for irreducible representations is \( \frac{1}{2}\int_{0}^{2\pi} d\varphi \, e^{-iA\varphi} e^{+iA'\varphi} = \delta_{AA'} \).
- The gauge rotation operator is \( S(\varphi) = e^{iA\varphi} \) with \( \varphi \in [0,2\pi] \).

## Context

This work extends the symmetry-restoration framework developed for angular momentum (SU(2) group) in Paper I (Duguet, 2015) to the U(1) group associated with particle-number conservation. It generalizes standard single-reference Bogoliubov many-body perturbation theory and Bogoliubov coupled cluster theory, which were recently formulated and applied by Stolarczyk and Monkhorst, Signoracci (2014), and Henderson (2014), by incorporating exact particle-number projection. The formalism can be merged with the SU(2) treatment to handle both symmetries simultaneously, enabling future ab initio descriptions of near-degenerate finite quantum systems with an open-shell character.
