# Possible molecular states from interactions of charmed baryons

> In this work, we perform a systematic study of possible molecular states composed of two charmed baryons including hidden-charm systems $Λ_c\barΛ_c$, $Σ_c^{(*)}\barΣ_c^{(*)}$, and $Λ_c\barΣ_c^{(*)}$, and corresponding double-charm systems $Λ_cΛ_c$, $Σ_c^{(*)}Σ_c^{(*)}$, and $Λ_cΣ_c^{(*)}$. With the help of the heavy quark chiral effective Lagrangians, the interactions are described with $π$, $ρ$, $η$, $ω$, $ϕ$, and $σ$ exchanges. The potential kernels are constructed, and inserted into the quasipotential Bethe-Salpeter equation. The bound states from the interactions considered is studied by searching for the poles of the scattering amplitude. The results suggest that strong attractions exist in both hidden-charm and double-charm systems considered in the current work, and bound states can be produced in most of the systems. More experiment studies about these molecular states are suggested though the nucleon-nucleon collison at LHC and nucleon-antinucleon collison at $\rm \bar{P}ANDA$.

## Key claim

The paper systematically predicts that strong attractions exist in both hidden-charm and double-charm systems of two charmed baryons, and bound molecular states can be produced in most of the systems considered, but the existence of these states depends on the value of a single free parameter α introduced for regularization, meaning the predictions are conditional on the choice of this parameter.

## Method

The authors constructed potential kernels for the interactions of charmed baryons using a one-boson-exchange model with π, ρ, η, ω, φ, and σ meson exchanges, based on heavy quark chiral effective Lagrangians. They then inserted these potentials into the quasipotential Bethe-Salpeter equation (qBSE) and searched for bound states by finding poles of the scattering amplitude for S-wave systems with various isospin and spin-parity quantum numbers.

## Key numbers

- For the Λ_c Λ_c double-charm system, bound states with binding energies smaller than 30 MeV are produced in a larger range of the parameter α.
- For the hidden-charm Σ_c Σ_c system with isospin I=0, bound states are produced at an α value below 0, and binding energies increase rapidly to a value larger than 30 MeV at an α value of about 0.7.
- For the hidden-charm Σ_c Σ_c system with I=2, bound states appear at a small α value, and binding energies increase to 30 MeV at an α value of about 0.7.
- For the hidden-charm Λ_c Σ_c system, binding energies increase to 30 MeV at an α value of about 1.
- For the double-charm Λ_c Σ_c system, binding energies reach 30 MeV at an α value of about 2.
- The mass of the broad σ meson is chosen as 500 MeV.

## Context

This work extends previous studies of molecular states composed of charmed baryons, particularly building on the authors' own prior work on Λ_c Λ_c molecular states (Song 2022) and on studies of hidden-charm pentaquarks (He 2019). It also relates to earlier theoretical calculations suggesting strong attraction between Λ_c and Λ_c baryons via σ and ω exchanges (Lee 2011, Simonov 2011). The results enable future experimental searches for these predicted molecular states in nucleon-nucleon collisions at LHC and nucleon-antinucleon collisions at PANDA.
