# Phase and equation of state of finite density QC$_2$D at lower temperature

> We investigate the phase structure and the equation of state (EoS) for dense two-color QCD at low temperatures, $T = 40$ MeV ($32^4$ lattice) and $T = 80$ MeV ($16^4$ lattice). A rich phase structure below the pseudo-critical temperature $T_c$ as a function of quark chemical potential $μ$ has been revealed. By performing $T = 40$ MeV simulations, essentially similar results to the previous ones at $T = 80$ MeV are obtained, but several finer understandings are achieved. Breaking of the conformal bound is also confirmed thanks to smaller statistical errors. This talk is mainly based on Refs.~\cite{Iida:2022hyy, Iida:2024irv}. It also includes related studies and subsequent developments that were not mentioned in the original papers.

## Key claim

The authors confirm that the conformal bound (speed of sound squared equal to 1/3) is violated in dense two-color QCD at low temperatures, with smaller statistical errors at T = 40 MeV providing clearer evidence than earlier work at T = 80 MeV. The violation occurs in both the BEC and BCS phases of the superfluid regime.

## Method

The authors performed lattice Monte Carlo simulations using the RHMC algorithm with the Iwasaki gauge action and naive Wilson fermions for two-color QCD at β = 0.80 and κ = 0.159. They generated gauge configurations on 32^4 (T = 40 MeV), 16^4 (T = 80 MeV), and 32^3 × 8 (T = 160 MeV) lattices, introducing a diquark source term j and taking the j → 0 limit. The phase structure was determined using the Polyakov loop, diquark condensate, and quark number density, while the equation of state was computed from the trace anomaly and pressure using the nonperturbative β-function.

## Key numbers

- T = 40 MeV on a 32^4 lattice and T = 80 MeV on a 16^4 lattice
- Pseudo-scalar meson mass m_PS ≈ 738 MeV at μ = 0
- Critical chemical potential at T = 40 MeV: μ/m_PS = 0.47 from fitting
- Nonperturbative β-function values: a dβ/da = -0.352, a dκ/da = 0.0282 at β = 0.80, κ = 0.159
- Pion decay constant from ChPT fits: F = 51.1(5) MeV from pressure fit, F = 56.7(7) MeV from energy density fit
- Earlier result for comparison: F = 60.8(1.6) MeV from Astrakhantsev (2020)
- Upper bound from hydrodynamics: c_s^2/c^2 < 0.781 from Hippert (2024)

## Context

This work extends the authors' previous studies (Iida 2022, Iida 2024) on two-color QCD at T = 80 MeV to a lower temperature of T = 40 MeV, revealing that the hadronic-matter phase shrinks and the diquark condensate shows quadratic scaling in the BCS phase as predicted by weak-coupling analysis (Schäfer 1999, Hanada 2011, Kanazawa 2013). The breaking of the conformal bound was independently confirmed in three-color QCD with isospin chemical potential (Brandt 2022, Abbott 2023, Abbott 2024). These results enable comparisons with neutron star phenomenology and analytical studies of the pairing gap's effect on the speed of sound (Kojo 2014, Leonhardt 2019, Braun 2022) and the negativity of the trace anomaly (Fukushima 2024).
