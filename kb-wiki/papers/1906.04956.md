# Possible Molecular States Composed of Doubly Charmed Baryons with Couple-channel Effect

> We systematically investigate the possible molecular states composed of (1) two spin-$\frac{3}{2}$ doubly charmed baryons, and (2) a pair of spin-$\frac{3}{2}$ and spin-$\frac{1}{2}$ doubly charmed baryons. The one-boson-exchange (OBE) model is used to describe the potential between two baryons. The channel mixing effect is considered for the systems with the same quantum number $(I(J^P))$ but different total spin ($S$) and orbital angular momenta ($L$). We also study the channel mixing effect among the systems composed of various doubly charmed baryons if they have the same quantum number. Many of the systems are good candidates of molecular states.

## Key claim

The authors systematically investigate possible molecular states composed of two doubly charmed baryons (both spin-3/2, or one spin-3/2 and one spin-1/2) using the one-boson-exchange model, finding that many systems are good candidates for molecular states, but the existence and binding energies depend strongly on the cutoff parameter Λ, which cannot be determined without experimental data.

## Method

The one-boson-exchange (OBE) model was used to describe the potential between two doubly charmed baryons, with coupling constants derived from nucleon coupling constants via quark model relations. The Schrödinger equation was solved numerically including channel mixing effects between states with the same quantum numbers (I, J^P) but different total spin S and orbital angular momenta L, considering S-wave, D-wave, and G-wave mixing where applicable. A form factor with adjustable cutoff parameter Λ (ranging from 0.8 to 2.6 GeV) was introduced at each baryon-baryon-meson vertex to account for the baryon structure.

## Key numbers

- Mass of Ξ_{cc} doubly charmed baryon: 3621.40 ± 0.72(stat) ± 0.27(syst) ± 0.14(Λ_c^+) MeV (from LHCb, 2017)
- [Ξ_{cc}^*Ξ_{cc}^*]_{J=0}^{I=1} system: binding energy 2.37-15.93 MeV with cutoff Λ = 2.0-2.4 GeV, root-mean-square radius 2.15-1.17 fm
- [Ξ_{cc}^*Ξ_{cc}^*]_{J=1}^{I=0} system: binding energy 25.28-39.72 MeV with cutoff Λ = 0.80-0.84 GeV, root-mean-square radius 0.99-0.84 fm
- [Ξ_{cc}^*Ω_{cc}^*]_{J=1}^{I=1/2} system: binding energy 1.07-20.89 MeV with cutoff Λ = 1.8-2.2 GeV
- [Ω_{cc}^*Ω_{cc}^*]_{J=2}^{I=0} system: binding energy 2.71-13.03 MeV with cutoff Λ = 2.4-2.6 GeV
- [Ξ_{cc}^*Ξ_{cc}^*]_{J=3}^{I=0} system: binding energy 2.65-8.87 MeV with cutoff Λ = 1.2-1.4 GeV
- No bound state found for [Ξ_{cc}^*Ξ_{cc}^*]_{J=2}^{I=1} system even with channel mixing and varying cutoff

## Context

This work extends previous studies of molecular states composed of doubly charmed baryons, specifically following the formalism of Meng (2017) who investigated deuteron-like bound states of two spin-1/2 doubly charmed baryons using SU(3) flavor symmetry. The authors extend this to spin-3/2 baryons (Ξ_{cc}^* and Ω_{cc}^*) and mixed spin-3/2 and spin-1/2 systems, including channel mixing among BB, B^*B, and B^*B^* systems. The results provide predictions for possible molecular states that could be searched for experimentally, complementing earlier work by Chen (2017, 2018) on systems containing Ξ_{cc} with charmed mesons or baryons, and by Froemel (2004) and Meng (2017) on Ξ_{cc}-nucleon systems.
