# Magnetic moments of the spin-${3\over 2}$ doubly heavy baryons

> In this work, we investigate the chiral corrections to the magnetic moments of the spin-$3\over 2$ doubly charmed baryons systematically up to next-to-next-to-leading order with the heavy baryon chiral perturbation theory. The numerical results are given up to next-to-leading order: $μ_{Ξ^{*++}_{cc}}=1.72μ_{N}$, $μ_{Ξ^{*+}_{cc}}=-0.09μ_{N}$, $μ_{Ω^{*+}_{cc}}=0.99μ_{N}$. As a by-product, we have also calculated the magnetic moments of the spin-$3\over 2$ doubly bottom baryons and charmed bottom baryons: $μ_{Ξ^{*0}_{bb}}=0.63μ_{N}$, $μ_{Ξ^{*-}_{bb}}=-0.79μ_{N}$, $μ_{Ω^{*-}_{bb}}=0.12μ_{N}$, $μ_{Ξ^{*+}_{bc}}=1.12μ_{N}$, $μ_{Ξ^{*0}_{bc}}=-0.40μ_{N}$, $μ_{Ω^{*0}_{bc}}=0.56μ_{N}$.

## Key claim

The authors compute chiral corrections to the magnetic moments of spin-3/2 doubly heavy baryons using heavy baryon chiral perturbation theory (HBChPT) up to next-to-next-to-leading order analytically, but only present numerical results up to next-to-leading order because the higher-order low-energy constants cannot be fixed without experimental data.

## Method

The authors construct the chiral Lagrangians for spin-3/2 doubly charmed baryons in HBChPT up to O(p^4), then calculate the magnetic moments order by order using the standard power counting scheme. Since no experimental data exist for these baryons, the low-energy constants (LECs) are estimated using the naive quark model with constituent quark masses from Lichtenberg (1976) and Karliner (2014). The numerical results are presented for two values of the mass splitting between spin-1/2 and spin-3/2 doubly charmed baryons, delta = -100 MeV and delta = -70 MeV.

## Key numbers

* Magnetic moment of Xi*++_cc = 1.72 mu_N (for delta = -100 MeV) or 1.59 mu_N (for delta = -70 MeV)
* Magnetic moment of Xi*+_cc = -0.09 mu_N (for delta = -100 MeV) or 0.02 mu_N (for delta = -70 MeV)
* Magnetic moment of Omega*+_cc = 0.99 mu_N (for delta = -100 MeV) or 1.00 mu_N (for delta = -70 MeV)
* Magnetic moment of Xi*0_bb = 0.63 mu_N (for delta = -40 MeV) or 0.58 mu_N (for delta = -20 MeV)
* Magnetic moment of Xi*-_bb = -0.79 mu_N (for delta = -40 MeV) or -0.75 mu_N (for delta = -20 MeV)
* Magnetic moment of Omega*-_bb = 0.12 mu_N (for both delta = -40 MeV and delta = -20 MeV)
* Magnetic moment of Xi*+_bc = 1.12 mu_N (for delta = -60 MeV) or 1.07 mu_N (for delta = -40 MeV)
* Magnetic moment of Xi*0_bc = -0.40 mu_N (for delta = -60 MeV) or -0.35 mu_N (for delta = -40 MeV)
* Magnetic moment of Omega*0_bc = 0.56 mu_N (for both delta = -60 MeV and delta = -40 MeV)
* Quark model estimates (Set A): Xi*++_cc = 2.61 mu_N, Xi*+_cc = -0.18 mu_N, Omega*+_cc = 0.17 mu_N (from constituent quark masses m_u=m_d=336 MeV, m_s=540 MeV, m_c=1660 MeV, m_b=4700 MeV)

## Context

This work extends previous HBChPT calculations of magnetic moments for octet, decuplet, and spin-1/2 doubly charmed baryons (Jenkins 1992, Li 2016, Li 2017) to the spin-3/2 doubly charmed sector. The results provide predictions that can be compared with future lattice QCD simulations and experimental measurements once the spin-3/2 doubly charmed baryons are observed. The analytical expressions up to next-to-next-to-leading order may also serve as a framework for chiral extrapolation of lattice data.
