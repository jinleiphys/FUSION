# Hidden-charm pentaquark states in a mass splitting model

> Assuming that the $P_c(4312)^+$ is a $I(J^P)=\frac12(\frac32^-)$ compact pentaquark, we study the mass spectrum of its S-wave hidden-charm partner states in a color-magnetic interaction model. Combining the information from their decays obtained in a simple rearrangement scheme, one finds that the quantum numbers of $P_c(4457)^+$, $ P_c(4440)^+$, and $P_c(4337)^+$ can be assigned to be $I(J^P)=\frac12(\frac32^-)$, $\frac12(\frac12^-)$, and $\frac12(\frac12^-)$, respectively, while both $P_{cs}(4338)^0$ and $P_{cs}(4459)^0$ can be interpreted as $I(J^P)=0(\frac12^-)$ $udsc\bar{c}$ compact states. Based on the numerical results, we also find narrow pentaquarks in $ssnc\bar{c}$ ($n=u,d$) and $sssc\bar{c}$ systems. The decay properties of the studied pentaquarks and the searching channels for them can be tested in future experiments.

## Key claim

Assuming the \(P_c(4312)^+\) is a compact pentaquark with \(I(J^P)=\frac12(\frac32^-)\) rather than a \(\Sigma_c\bar{D}\) molecule, the authors assign quantum numbers to other observed pentaquarks and predict narrow states in strangeness sectors, but note that the spin of \(P_c(4312)^+\) has not been determined experimentally and their assignment is a "different possibility from the molecule picture."

## Method

The authors use a chromomagnetic interaction (CMI) model to compute mass spectra of S-wave \(qqqc\bar{c}\) (\(q=u,d,s\)) compact pentaquarks, taking the \(P_c(4312)^+\) as a reference state to compensate for effective quark mass uncertainties. They construct wave functions that include both color-octet \((qqq)_{8_c}(c\bar{c})_{8_c}\) and color-singlet \((qqq)_{1_c}(c\bar{c})_{1_c}\) components, then calculate rearrangement decay widths into meson-baryon and charmonium channels using a simple constant-amplitude scheme, fixing the decay constant \(\alpha\) from the measured width of \(P_c(4312)^+\).

## Key numbers

- \(P_c(4312)^+\) mass: \(4311.9 \pm 0.7^{+6.8}_{-0.6}\) MeV, width \(9.8 \pm 2.7^{+3.7}_{-4.5}\) MeV (LHCb 2019).
- \(P_c(4440)^+\) mass: \(4440 \pm 1.3^{+4.1}_{-4.7}\) MeV, width \(20.6 \pm 4.9^{+8.7}_{-10.2}\) MeV.
- \(P_c(4457)^+\) mass: \(4457.3 \pm 0.6^{+4.1}_{-1.7}\) MeV, width \(6.4 \pm 2.0^{+5.7}_{-1.9}\) MeV.
- \(P_{cs}(4338)^0\) mass: \(4338.2 \pm 0.7 \pm 0.4\) MeV, width \(7.0 \pm 1.2 \pm 1.3\) MeV.
- \(P_{cs}(4459)^0\) mass: \(4458.8 \pm 2.9^{+4.7}_{-1.1}\) MeV, width \(17.3 \pm 6.5^{+8.0}_{-5.7}\) MeV.
- The model assigns \(P_c(4457)^+\) as \(I(J^P)=\frac12(\frac32^-)\), \(P_c(4440)^+\) as \(\frac12(\frac12^-)\), \(P_c(4337)^+\) as \(\frac12(\frac12^-)\), and both \(P_{cs}(4338)^0\) and \(P_{cs}(4459)^0\) as \(I(J^P)=0(\frac12^-)\).
- The decay constant \(\alpha = 4647.94\) MeV is determined from the width of \(P_c(4312)^+\).
- Effective coupling parameters extracted from ground hadrons include \(C_{nn}=18.3\) MeV, \(C_{ns}=12.0\) MeV, \(C_{cn}=4.0\) MeV, \(C_{cs}=4.4\) MeV, \(C_{c\bar{c}}=5.3\) MeV, \(C_{c\bar{s}}=6.7\) MeV, \(C_{c\bar{n}}=6.6\) MeV, \(C_{ss}=5.7\) MeV.
- Mass gap \(\Delta_{sn} = 90.6\) MeV between strange and light quarks.

## Context

This work extends the authors' previous CMI model studies of compact pentaquarks (Wu et al. 2017, Cheng et al. 2019) by including color-singlet \((qqq)_{1_c}(c\bar{c})_{1_c}\) components in the wave functions, which allows calculation of charmonium decay channels. It differs from the molecule interpretation favored by most studies (e.g., Chen et al. 2019, Guo et al. 2019) and from cusp-effect explanations (Nakamura et al. 2021). The results enable future experimental searches for predicted narrow pentaquarks in \(ssnc\bar{c}\) and \(sssc\bar{c}\) systems, and provide testable decay channels for the observed states.
