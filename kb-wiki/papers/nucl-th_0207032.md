# Note on the Deformed Boson Scheme in Four Kinds of Boson Operators

> The deformed boson scheme in four kinds of boson operators, which was recently proposed by the present authors, is supplemented by the T-type deformation closely related with the su(1,1)-algebra. Two subjects are discussed in relation to the S-type deformation closely related with the su(2)-algebra.

## Key claim

The paper supplements the authors' previously proposed deformed boson scheme for four kinds of boson operators by introducing a T-type deformation based on the su(1,1)-algebra, and shows that for specific choices of deformation functions the resulting operators take forms analogous to q-deformed algebras or Holstein-Primakoff representations, but with different behavior for boson transfer between subsystems compared to the previously studied S-type deformation.

## Method

The authors define deformed raising operators for two coupled bosonic systems (labeled (+) and (-)) using functions d_S, e_S for the S-type (su(2)-based) deformation and d_T, e_T for the T-type (su(1,1)-based) deformation, with the simplifying choice f_S=g_S=f_T=g_T=1. They then examine two specific cases: first, exponential (q-deformed) forms for the deformation functions, and second, square-root forms that yield Holstein-Primakoff-type representations for the operators.

## Key numbers

- For the S-type q-deformation, the parameter q_S is a positive parameter appearing in the relations d_S(I+1/2)^{-1}d_S(I) = (q_S)^I and e_S(J)^{-1}e_S(J+1/2) = (q_S)^J
- For the T-type q-deformation, the parameter q_T is a positive parameter appearing in d_T(K-1/2)^{-1}d_T(K) = (q_T)^{-(K-1/2)} and e_T(L)^{-1}e_T(L-1/2) = (q_T)^{-(L-1/2)}
- For the S-type Holstein-Primakoff deformation, the parameter Z_S is a positive parameter (assumed large) in d_S(I+1/2)^{-1}d_S(I) = sqrt(1-2I/Z_S) and e_S(J)^{-1}e_S(J+1/2) = sqrt(1-2J/Z_S)
- For the T-type Holstein-Primakoff deformation, the parameter Z_T is a positive parameter in d_T(K-1/2)^{-1}d_T(K) = sqrt(1+(2K-1)/(Z_T+1)) and e_T(L)^{-1}e_T(L-1/2) = sqrt(1+(2L-1)/(Z_T+1))

## Context

This note extends the deformed boson scheme for four kinds of boson operators introduced by the same authors in their previous paper (referred to as (I)), which itself built on a series of three earlier papers by the group (Kuriyama et al., 2001-2002). The work connects to the q-deformed su(2) and su(1,1) algebras in two kinds of boson operators studied by the authors in a prior paper (Kuriyama et al., 2001), and to the q-deformation approach of Penson and Solomon (1999). The results enable further investigation of many-body systems involving su(2) and su(1,1) algebraic structures, particularly for describing pairing correlations, pairing rotation, and pairing vibration in two-level shell models.
