# Superdeformed Bands of Odd Nuclei in A=190 Region in the Quasiparticle Picture

> We study the properties of the superdeformed (SD) bands of 195Pb and 193Hg by the cranked Hartree-Fock-Bogoliubov method. Our calculations reproduce the flat behavior of the dynamical moment of inertia of two of the SD bands of 195Pb measured recently. We discuss possible configuration assignments for the observed bands 3 and 4 of 195Pb. We also calculate the two interacting SD bands of 193Hg. Our analysis confirms the superiority of a density-dependent pairing force over a seniority pairing interaction.

# Superdeformed Bands of Odd Nuclei in A=190 Region in the Quasiparticle Picture

## Key claim
The flat (nearly constant) dynamical moment of inertia observed in the two lowest superdeformed bands of 195Pb can be reproduced by cranked Hartree-Fock-Bogoliubov calculations and is correlated with the curvature of the neutron intruder quasiparticle routhian, but the configuration assignments for bands 3 and 4 remain ambiguous because the moments of inertia, quadrupole moments, and magnetic moments of the [624]9/2 and [512]5/2 bands are too similar to distinguish them.

## Method
The authors performed cranked Hartree-Fock-Bogoliubov (HFB) calculations using the SkM* Skyrme force parametrization for the particle-hole channel and a zero-range, surface-peaked density-dependent pairing force for the pairing channel, with the Lipkin-Nogami prescription to correct for finite particle number. Different superdeformed bands in the odd-N nuclei 195Pb and 193Hg were described by self-consistently creating the appropriate quasiparticle on the even vacuum, and the bands were followed versus rotational frequency using continuity properties of parity and signature.

## Key numbers
- The dynamical moment of inertia of the [752]5/2 bands in 195Pb displays a small variation for ℏω ≥ 0.24 MeV.
- The charge quadrupole moments of the [512]5/2 and [624]9/2 bands in 195Pb differ by approximately 0.3 b.
- The quadrupole moment of the [752]5/2, α=+1/2 intruder band in 195Pb is 19.96 eb at I = 32.5 (ℏω = 0.314 MeV).
- The quadrupole moment of the [752]5/2, α=−1/2 intruder band in 195Pb is 19.47 eb at I = 31.5 (ℏω = 0.290 MeV).
- The magnetic moments of the [512]5/2 and [624]9/2 bands in 195Pb are approximately 12.4 eℏ/2Mpc at I ≈ 31.
- The calculated relative excitation energies E_rel for the seven SD bands in 195Pb at I = 32.5 range from 0.000 MeV (reference [752]5/2, α=−1/2) to 0.368 MeV ([642]3/2, α=−1/2).
- In 193Hg, the band crossing between the [752]5/2 and [512]5/2 bands is calculated at ℏω ≈ 0.15 MeV, while the observed crossing is at ℏω ≈ 0.25 MeV, a discrepancy of Δℏω ≈ 0.1 MeV.

## Context
This work extends earlier cranked HFB studies of superdeformed bands in even nuclei in the A=190 region (Bo96, Te95) to the odd-N neighbors 195Pb and 193Hg. The flat dynamical moment of inertia in the two lowest bands of 195Pb was first reported by Farris et al. (1995), and the present calculations confirm that this behavior arises from the intruder [752]5/2 quasiparticle routhian curvature. The study also improves upon earlier calculations for 193Hg by Heenen et al. (1995) that used a seniority pairing interaction, which failed to reproduce the observed band interaction; the density-dependent pairing force used here successfully produces the interaction, though the crossing frequency is still off by about 0.1 MeV. The results enable future work to refine effective interactions to better reproduce single-particle energies in the superdeformed well.
