# Note on the Minimum Weight States in the su(2)-Algebraic Many-Fermion Model --- Extension of the Role of the Auxiliary su(2)-Algebra ---

> The role of the auxiliary su(2)-algebra proposed by the present authors in the latest paper is extended. Its aim is to determine the minimum weight states completely. With the aid of scalar operators for the auxiliary algebra, new aspects are added to the minimum weight states in the aligned scheme discussed in the latest paper. Some simple examples are shown.

## Key claim

The paper extends the authors' previous work (Tsue et al., 2011) by showing that the minimum weight states of the su(2)-algebraic many-fermion model, previously restricted to the "aligned scheme," can be generalized through the introduction of scalar operators for the auxiliary su(2)-algebra (R-spin), though the general proof of orthogonality for the extended set of states may be impossible.

## Method

The authors construct scalar operators for the auxiliary R-spin algebra from fermion creation operators, specifically the operators S*_λ and S*_μν defined as bilinear combinations of fermion creation operators. They then derive the corresponding "counterpart" operators via a transformation that ensures the resulting states are minimum weight states for both the R-spin and S-spin algebras. The method is illustrated by constructing explicit minimum weight states for two-fermion and four-fermion systems, showing how the scalar operators generate states beyond the aligned scheme.

## Key numbers

- The total number of single-particle states is 4Ω₀, where Ω₀ is an integer or half-integer.
- The seniority number is given by n = 2(p+q+r), where p, q, r are non-negative integers.
- The relation p+q+r+s = Ω₀ holds for the minimum weight states, generalizing the earlier result r+s = Ω₀ from the aligned scheme.
- For the two-fermion case with (r=0, q=1, p=0), the state S*_μν|0⟩ is proportional to (1/2)Ω₀(2Ω₀+1)(c*_μ c*_ν̄ - c*_μ̄ c*_ν)|0⟩.
- For the four-fermion case with (r=0, q=2, p=0), the state S*_μ₂ν₂ S*_μ₁ν₁|0⟩ is proportional to (1/4)Ω₀(Ω₀-1)(2Ω₀+1)(2Ω₀-1) S*_μ₂ν₂ S*_μ₁ν₁|0⟩.

## Context

This work extends the authors' previous paper (Tsue et al., 2011, referred to as (I)) which introduced the auxiliary su(2)-algebra (R-spin) for constructing minimum weight states of the S-spin in the many-fermion model. The earlier work only covered the "aligned scheme" where the minimum weight states are simple products of fermion creation operators. The present paper generalizes this by showing that scalar operators for the R-spin can generate additional minimum weight states, as demonstrated explicitly for two-fermion and four-fermion cases. This enables a more complete classification of minimum weight states in the su(2)-algebraic many-fermion model, though the general orthogonalization problem remains open.
