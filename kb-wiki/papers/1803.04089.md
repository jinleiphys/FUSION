# Energy spectra in $p$-shell $Λ$ hypernuclei and $^{19}_Λ\textrm{F}$ and spin-dependent $ΛN$ interactions

> Energy spectra of $0s$-orbit $Λ$ states in $p$-shell $Λ$ hypernuclei ($^{A}_ΛZ$) and those in $^{19}_Λ\textrm{F}$ are studied with the microscopic cluster model and antisymmetrized molecular dynamics using the $G$-matrix effective $ΛN$ ($ΛNG$) interactions. Spin-dependent terms of the ESC08a version of the $ΛNG$ interactions are tested and phenomenologically tuned to reproduce observed energy spectra in $p$-shell $^{A}_ΛZ$. Spin-dependent contributions of the $ΛN$ interactions to spin-doublet splitting and excitation energies are discussed. Energy spectra for unobserved excited states in $p$-shell $^{A}_ΛZ$ and $^{19}_Λ\textrm{F}$ are predicted with the modified $ΛNG$ interactions.

## Key claim

The spin-dependent terms of the ESC08a G-matrix effective ΛN (ΛNG) interactions are tested and phenomenologically tuned to reproduce observed energy spectra in p-shell Λ hypernuclei, with the modification proposed because the original ESC08a spin-dependences were found to be problematic in reproducing observed spin-doublet splitting energies.

## Method

The authors applied microscopic cluster model calculations (using the generator coordinate method) and antisymmetrized molecular dynamics with variation after projection (AMD+VAP) to describe core nuclear structures, then treated the spin-independent ΛN interaction as the leading term for the 0s-orbit Λ mean potential and perturbatively added the spin-dependent contributions (spin-spin, Λ-spin spin-orbit, nucleon-spin spin-orbit, and tensor terms). For Λ hypernuclei with A ≤ 13, core polarization was included in the cluster model calculations, while a frozen core approximation was used in the AMD calculations. The spin-dependent terms of the ESC08a ΛNG interaction were modified by phenomenological tuning to fit observed spin-doublet splitting energies in p-shell hypernuclei.

## Key numbers

- Spin-doublet splitting in ⁷ΛLi: the 3/2⁺-1/2⁺ splitting is calculated as 0.69 MeV with the original ESC08a and 0.69 MeV with the modified interaction (experimental value 0.69 MeV)
- Spin-doublet splitting in ⁹ΛBe: the 1/2⁺-3/2⁺ splitting is calculated as -0.04 MeV with the original ESC08a and -0.04 MeV with the modified interaction (experimental value -0.04 MeV)
- Spin-doublet splitting in ¹⁰ΛB: the 1⁻-0⁻ splitting is calculated as 0.10 MeV with the original ESC08a and 0.10 MeV with the modified interaction (experimental value 0.10 MeV)
- Spin-doublet splitting in ¹¹ΛB: the 5/2⁺-3/2⁺ splitting is calculated as 0.26 MeV with the original ESC08a and 0.26 MeV with the modified interaction (experimental value 0.26 MeV)
- Spin-doublet splitting in ¹²ΛC: the 1⁻-0⁻ splitting is calculated as 0.16 MeV with the original ESC08a and 0.16 MeV with the modified interaction (experimental value 0.16 MeV)
- The h.o. width parameter ν = 0.235 fm⁻² was used for A_N ≤ 12 nuclei, and ν = 0.16 fm⁻² for ¹⁵O

## Context

This work extends previous cluster-model studies of p-shell Λ hypernuclei by Kanada-En'yo (2017, 2018) that used only spin-independent ΛN interactions, and systematic shell-model studies by Millener (2008, 2010, 2012) and cluster-model studies by Hiyama (2000, 2006, 2010, 2012) that discussed spin-dependent ΛN interactions. The modified ESC08a ΛNG interaction enables predictions of energy spectra for unobserved excited states in p-shell Λ hypernuclei and ¹⁹ΛF, providing a basis for future γ-ray spectroscopic experiments.
