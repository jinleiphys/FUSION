# Open and hidden strangeness in hadronic systems

> We investigate production of ϕmesons and Ξbaryons in nucleus-nucleus collisions. Reactions on strange particles acting as a catalyser are proposed to interpret the high observed ϕyields in HADES experiments as well as the energy dependence of the widths of ϕrapidity spectra in collisions at the SPS energies. It is argued that the enhancement of Ξ- yield observed by HADES is even higher than originally reported if effects of the experimental centrality trigger are taken into account. Cross sections for new hadronic processes that could produce Ξ- are reviewed.

## Key claim

The observed enhancements of ϕ mesons and Ξ⁻ baryons in nucleus-nucleus collisions most likely indicate non-equilibrium production mechanisms, where the rates for annihilating given species cannot follow the fast drop of temperature in an expanding fireball.

## Method

The authors propose catalytic reactions (πY → ϕY, K̄N → ϕY, where Y = Λ, Σ) to explain ϕ meson yields, using a simple hadronic effective theory to estimate cross-sections of order 1–2 mb. For Ξ⁻ production, they review strangeness creation reactions, strangeness recombination reactions (K̄Λ(Σ) → Ξπ), and double-hyperon processes (ΛΛ → ΞN, ΛΣ → ΞN, ΣΣ → ΞN), parameterizing cross-sections from chiral effective field theory calculations. They also consider resonant production via Ξ*(1530) decay when antikaon masses are lowered in medium.

## Key numbers

- Cross-sections for catalytic reactions (πY → ϕY, K̄N → ϕY) are typically of order 1–2 mb.
- For the reaction Ξ⁻p → ΛΛ, the matrix element is parameterized as |M_ΛΛ|² = [3 + 12/(1 + 80 x₁)] mb, where x₁ = (√s − m_Ξ⁻ − m_p)/1 GeV.
- For the reaction Ξ⁰p → Σ⁺Λ, the matrix element is parameterized as |M_ΣΛ|² = [5.5 + 36/(1 + 20 x₂)^1.2 + 32/(1 + 100 x₂)^5] mb, where x₂ = (√s − m_Σ⁺ − m_Λ)/1 GeV.
- The isospin-averaged cross-section for K̄Λ → πΞ is σ = (p_πΞ / 4 p_K̄Λ) × 34.7 × (s_K̄Λ / s) mb.
- The isospin-averaged cross-section for K̄Σ → πΞ is σ = (p_πΞ / 12 p_K̄Σ) × 318 × [1 − (s_K̄Σ / s)]^0.6 × [(s_K̄Σ / s)]^1.7 mb.
- The reaction threshold for K̄Λ-induced reactions crosses the Ξ*(1530) resonance pole at an antikaon potential U_K⁻ ≈ −80 MeV.
- The measured decay width of Ξ* → Ξπ is 9 MeV, giving coupling V_Ξ*Ξπ = 1.315/m_π².

## Context

This work extends the analysis of ϕ meson production beyond traditional OZI-suppressed reactions (πN → ϕN) and strangeness coalescence (KK̄ → ϕρ, KΛ → ϕN) by proposing catalytic reactions (Tomasik and Kolomeitsev 2009). It also addresses the HADES collaboration's observed enhancement of Ξ⁻ yields (HADES-Xi), which exceeds predictions from the statistical model (Andronic et al. 2006) and transport calculations (Chen et al. 2004). The paper enables future studies of non-equilibrium strangeness production in heavy-ion collisions, particularly relevant for the energy-scan programme searching for the onset of deconfinement.
