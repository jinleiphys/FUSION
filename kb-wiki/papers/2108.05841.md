# Relations between strong decay widths of the $P_c$ pentaquarks in the SU(4) flavor-spin model

> In a previous work we have studied the isospin 1/2 lowest positive and negative parity states of the pentaquark $uudc\overline{c}$, in a constituent quark model with a linear confinement and an SU(4) flavor-spin hyperfine interaction and we compared the results with the $P^+_c(4312)$, $P^+_c(4440)$ and $P^+_c(4457)$ pentaquarks observed at LHCb in 2019. Here we extend the previous work by calculating ratios of decay rates of the $P_c$ pentaquarks to $J/Ψ$ and $η_c$ and similarly the ratio of decay rates to $Λ_c {\bar D}^*$ and $Λ_c \bar D$. Our predictions are based on the SU(4)$\times$SU(2) structure of compact pentaquarks.

## Key claim

The SU(4) flavor-spin model predicts that the ratios of strong decay widths for both the lowest positive-parity and negative-parity compact pentaquark states are equal to 1/3 for decays to η_c p versus J/ψ p, and also 1/3 for decays to Λ_c D̄ versus Λ_c D̄*, under the assumption that kinematical differences between final channels are neglected.

## Method

The authors calculated the flavor-spin overlap between the pentaquark wave functions (obtained from a constituent quark model with linear confinement and SU(4) flavor-spin hyperfine interaction) and the flavor-spin wave functions of the baryon-meson decay channels. The decay width ratios were derived from the squared ratios of these overlaps, using the SU(4)×SU(2) symmetry structure of compact pentaquarks with flavor content uudc c̄, for the lowest positive parity state P_c(1/2^+) and the lowest negative parity state P_c(1/2^-).

## Key numbers

- The calculated mass of P_c(1/2^+) is 4273 MeV, tentatively assigned to the observed P_c^+(4312) resonance
- The calculated mass of P_c(1/2^-) is 4487 MeV, close to the P_c^+(4440) and P_c^+(4457) resonances
- The flavor-spin overlap O^FS for P_c(1/2^+) decaying to Λ_c + D̄* is 0.5303 (largest overlap for positive parity)
- The flavor-spin overlap O^FS for P_c(1/2^-) decaying to p + J/ψ is 0.3062 (largest overlap for negative parity)
- The ratio Γ(P_c → η_c p) / Γ(P_c → J/ψ p) = 1/3 for both P_c(1/2^+) and P_c(1/2^-)
- The ratio Γ(P_c(1/2^+) → Λ_c D̄) / Γ(P_c(1/2^+) → Λ_c D̄*) = 1/3
- Quark masses used: m_{u,d} = 340 MeV, m_c = 1350 MeV
- Calculated baryon masses: m_N = 960 MeV, m_{Λ_c} = 2180 MeV, m_{Σ_c} = 2434 MeV

## Context

This work extends the author's previous spectrum calculations (Stancu, 2019) of the uudc c̄ pentaquark using the SU(4) flavor-spin model, which itself extends the meson exchange model between quarks (Glozman, 1995; Glozman, 1996) to include heavy meson exchange. The results are compared with the molecular scenario calculations of Voloshin (2019), where the ratio Γ(P_c → η_c p) / Γ(P_c → J/ψ p) was predicted to be the inverse of the value found here. The work enables future estimates of decay width ratios for excited pentaquark states with J^P up to 5/2^+ and motivates further experimental determination of pentaquark parities.
