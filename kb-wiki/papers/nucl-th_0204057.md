# Nonmesonic weak decay of light hypernuclei with coherent $Σ$ mixing

> Nonmesonic weak decays of the A=4, and 5 hypernuclei are studied. The short range parts of the hyperon-nucleon weak interactions are described by the direct quark (DQ) weak transition potential, while the longer range interactions are given by the $π$ and $K$ meson exchange processes. Virtual $Σ$ mixings of the coherent type are found to give significant effects on the decay rates of $^4_Λ{\rm He}$. A large violation of the $ΔI = 1/2$ rule is predicted in the J=0 transition amplitudes.

## Key claim

Virtual Σ mixing of the coherent type gives significant effects on the nonmesonic decay rates of ⁴_ΛHe, and a large violation of the ΔI = 1/2 rule is predicted in the J=0 transition amplitudes, though the ΔI = 1/2 rule is maintained at the Λ → Nπ and Σ → Nπ vertices used in the meson-exchange parts of the model.

## Method

The authors employ a quark-meson hybrid model for the weak YN → NN transition potential: long- and medium-range parts are described by one-pion exchange (OPE) and one-kaon exchange (OKE) with soft form factors, while the short-range part is treated via a direct quark (DQ) transition potential derived from the nonrelativistic reduction of the effective four-quark weak Hamiltonian for |ΔS|=1. The model is applied to s-shell hypernuclei (A=4 and 5) using harmonic-oscillator shell-model wave functions with short-range correlations, and for A=4 systems the coherent Σ mixing is included by admixing a Σ+3N component with mixing amplitude β estimated from first-order perturbation theory.

## Key numbers

- For ⁵_ΛHe with the full π+K+DQ model: total decay rate = 0.523 Γ_Λ, Γ_pn = 0.304 Γ_Λ, Γ_nn = 0.219 Γ_Λ, Γ_nn/Γ_pn = 0.720, asymmetry parameter α = -0.678.
- For ⁴_ΛHe with π+K+DQ and β = -0.1: total decay rate = 0.168 Γ_Λ, Γ_pn = 0.165 Γ_Λ, Γ_nn = 0.003 Γ_Λ, Γ_nn/Γ_pn = 0.017, α = -0.645.
- For ⁴_ΛHe, the calculated Σ⁺p → pp decay rate at β = 0.1 is Γ_pp = 0.0003 Γ_Λ.
- For ⁴_ΛH with π+K+DQ and β = -0.1: total decay rate = 0.168 Γ_Λ, Γ_pn = 0.034 Γ_Λ, Γ_nn = 0.134 Γ_Λ, Γ_nn/Γ_pn = 3.938.
- The mixing amplitude β for ⁴_ΛHe is estimated as β = -0.05 using the D2 potential and Gaussian wave function with b = 2.24 fm.
- The DQ process alone gives Γ_nn/Γ_pn = 1.216 for ⁵_ΛHe.

## Context

This work extends the authors' previous quark-meson hybrid model (Sasaki et al., Nucl. Phys. A) from nuclear matter to finite light hypernuclei, and specifically addresses the role of coherent Σ mixing in A=4 systems, which was previously considered in hypernuclear structure calculations by Hiyama et al. (Nucl. Phys. A) and Akaishi et al. (Phys. Rev. Lett.) for binding energy problems. The results contradict earlier meson-exchange-only approaches (e.g., Parreño et al., Phys. Rev. C; Dubach et al., Ann. Phys.) that found smaller Γ_nn/Γ_pn ratios, and the predicted large ΔI = 1/2 violation in J=0 amplitudes provides a testable signature for future experiments on A=4 hypernuclei, particularly through the κ ratio between Γ_nn(⁴_ΛHe) and Γ_pn(⁴_ΛH).
