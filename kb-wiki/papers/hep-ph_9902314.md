# Polarized proton-deuteron Drell-Yan processes and parton distributions

> We show in general that there are 108 structure functions in the proton-deuteron Drell-Yan processes. However, there exist only 22 finite ones after integrating the cross section over the virtual-photon transverse momentum \vec Q_T or after taking the limit Q_T->0. There are 11 new structure functions in comparison with the ones of the proton-proton reactions, and they are related to the tensor structure of the deuteron. Parton-model analyses indicate an important tensor structure function V_T^{UQ_0}, which can be measured by a quadrupole spin asymmetry. The Drell-Yan process has an advantage over lepton reactions in finding tensor polarized antiquark distributions. We hope that our studies will be realized in the next-generation RHIC-Spin project and other ones.

## Key claim

The paper shows that the polarized proton-deuteron Drell-Yan process contains 108 structure functions in general, but only 22 remain finite after integrating over the virtual-photon transverse momentum or taking the limit Q_T -> 0, with 11 of these being new compared to proton-proton reactions and related to the tensor structure of the deuteron.

## Method

The authors developed a general formalism for polarized proton-deuteron Drell-Yan processes using both a spin-density formalism (expressing structure functions via helicity amplitudes and Clebsch-Gordan coefficients) and a hadron-tensor formalism (finding possible Lorentz index expressions for the hadron tensor W^mu^nu). They then analyzed the structure functions in a naive parton model, expressing the finite structure functions in terms of unpolarized, longitudinally-polarized, transversity, and tensor-polarized parton distributions.

## Key numbers

- 108 structure functions exist in the general polarized proton-deuteron Drell-Yan processes
- 22 structure functions remain finite after integrating over Q_T or taking the limit Q_T -> 0
- 11 new structure functions appear compared to proton-proton reactions, associated with the tensor structure of the deuteron
- 60 new functions exist in the general case compared to proton-proton reactions
- 15 spin asymmetries can be investigated, including 7 quadrupole asymmetries (A_UQ0, A_TQ0, A_UQ1, A_LQ1, A_TQ1, A_UQ2, A_LQ2, A_TQ2)
- 4 finite structure functions remain after Q_T integration in the parton-model analysis: overline_W_T, overline_V_T^LL, overline_U_2,2^TT, and overline_V_T^UQ0

## Context

This work extends the formalism of polarized Drell-Yan processes from proton-proton reactions to proton-deuteron reactions, building on initial studies of spin-one structure functions in lepton-deuteron scattering that identified the b1, b2, b3, and b4 structure functions. The paper provides the theoretical foundation for future experimental searches for tensor polarized antiquark distributions at facilities like RHIC, and enables investigation of antiquark flavor asymmetries such as Delta u/Delta d and Delta_T u/Delta_T d by combining pd Drell-Yan data with pp data, following the approach of the unpolarized case studied by Kumano (1998).
