# Addition theorems for spin spherical harmonics. II Results

> Based on the results of part I, we obtain the general form of the addition theorem for spin spherical harmonics and give explicit results in the cases involving one spin-$s'$ and one spin-$s$ spherical harmonics with $s',s=1/2$, 1, 3/2, and $|s'-s|=0$, 1. We obtain also a fully general addition theorem for one scalar and one tensor spherical harmonic of arbitrary rank. A variety of bilocal sums of ordinary and spin spherical harmonics are given in explicit form, including a general explicit expression for bilocal spherical harmonics.

## Key claim

The paper derives the general form of addition theorems for spin spherical harmonics, providing explicit results for spins up to 3/2 and for the case of one scalar and one tensor spherical harmonic of arbitrary rank, but the explicit algebraic expressions become too lengthy for spins greater than 2 and are omitted for brevity.

## Method

The author uses matrix elements of angular-momentum projector operators with tensor products of the position vector and angular-momentum operator, previously obtained in part I of the paper, to construct bilocal sums of ordinary spherical harmonics. These sums are then recast into addition theorems for spin spherical harmonics by applying the Wigner-Eckart theorem and using the explicit forms of spin spherical harmonics for integer and half-integer spins. Explicit results are given for spins s',s = 1/2, 1, 3/2 with |s'-s| = 0, 1, and for tensor spherical harmonics of rank 1, 2, and 3.

## Key numbers

- For vector spherical harmonics: the addition theorem yields expressions involving P'_ℓ(x) with prefactors (2ℓ+1)/(4π√(ℓ(ℓ+1))) for the case ℓ_n = ℓ, and √(2ℓ+1)/(4π√(ℓ_1+ℓ+1)) for the case ℓ_n = ℓ_1
- For rank-2 tensor spherical harmonics: the result for ℓ_n = ℓ involves prefactor (√(3/2)/(4π)) × (2ℓ+1)/√(ℓ(ℓ+1))/√((2ℓ-1)(2ℓ+3))
- For rank-3 tensor spherical harmonics: the result for ℓ_n = ℓ involves prefactor (i/(2π)) × (√10/3) × √((2ℓ-3)!/(2ℓ+4)!) × (2ℓ+1)^(3/2)
- Bilocal sums of spherical harmonics weighted by ℓ_z^t are given explicitly for t ≤ 5, with the t=3 result containing terms proportional to P'''_ℓ(x), P''_ℓ(x), and P'_ℓ(x)
- The local case (verpp = verp) reduces to the Clebsch-Gordan series for spherical harmonics, with the result proportional to CG coefficient ⟨ℓ,0; n+t,0 | ℓ_n,0⟩

## Context

This paper extends the results of part I (Bouzas, 2010) on angular-momentum projector matrix elements to explicit addition theorems for spin spherical harmonics. The work is motivated by partial-wave analysis of scattering processes with particles of different spins, where two-body unitarity requires inclusion of intermediate states with various spins. The results generalize earlier work on vector spherical harmonics found in textbooks such as Jackson (Classical Electrodynamics) and Varshalovich (1988) on quantum theory of angular momentum. The addition theorems enable systematic partial-wave expansions of S-matrix elements between states involving particles with different spins, and are expected to be applicable in quantum scattering and multipolar expansions of electromagnetic radiation fields.
