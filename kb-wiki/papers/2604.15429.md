## Context
This work extends analytic regularization (Speer 1968, Bollini 1964) by applying the analytic continuation of kinetic operators systematically at the level of the action, building on a provisional strategy from Kroll (1966) for modifying vertices to maintain Ward identities. The approach differs from dimensional regularization (t Hooft and Veltman 1972) by keeping spacetime dimensions fixed at 4, avoiding ambiguities with Œ≥‚Åµ and preserving supersymmetry at leading order, and from denominator regularization (Horowitz 2022) by ensuring the theory is finite before evaluating Feynman diagrams.
