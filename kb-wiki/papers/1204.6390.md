## Context

This work extends the molecular hadron interpretation to the charmed baryon sector, following the approach of one-boson exchange models used for other molecular candidates. The authors propose that X_c(3250)^0 is the first observed D*_0(2400)N molecular state, analogous to earlier molecular interpretations of XYZ states in the charmonium sector. The study enables future experimental searches for the predicted isoscalar partner Y_c(3250)^+ and for other decay channels such as X_c(3250)^0 Ôćĺ ╬Ť_c^+¤Ç^- at facilities like LHCb, Belle II, and SuperB.
