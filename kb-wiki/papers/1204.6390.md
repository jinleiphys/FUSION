# New structure around 3250 MeV in the baryonic B decay and the $D^*_0(2400)N$ molecular hadron

> In this work, we first propose the isovector $nD^{*}_0(2400)^0$ molecular state to explain the enhancement structure around 3250 MeV ($X_c(3250)^0$) in the $Σ_c^{++}π^-π^-$ invariant mass spectrum newly observed by the BaBar Collaboration. Under this molecular state configuration, both the analysis of the mass spectrum and the study of its dominant decay channel can well depict its resonance parameters measured by BaBar. Our investigation also shows that the isovector $nD^{*}_0(2400)^0$ molecular state can decay into $Σ_c^{++}π^-π^-$, which is consistent with the experimental observation. These studies provide the direct support to the isovector $nD^{*}_0(2400)^0$ molecular state assignment to $X_c(3250)^0$.

## Key claim

The paper proposes that the enhancement structure X_c(3250)^0 observed by BaBar in the Σ_c^{++}π^-π^- invariant mass spectrum can be explained as an isovector nD*_0(2400)^0 molecular state, but this assignment depends on the cutoff parameter Λ being around 1.23 GeV in the one-boson exchange model to reproduce the experimental binding energy of -13 MeV.

## Method

The authors constructed an effective potential for the nD*_0(2400)^0 system using one-boson exchange with ρ, ω, and σ meson exchanges, with coupling constants taken from the literature. They solved the Schrödinger equation in coordinate space to find bound state solutions and used the covariant spectator theory to calculate the vertex function and decay width for X_c(3250)^0 → nDπ via the intermediate D*_0(2400)^0 resonance. The decay width was computed by integrating over the three-body phase space with the Breit-Wigner distribution of the D*_0(2400)^0.

## Key numbers

- Mass of X_c(3250)^0: M = 3245 ± 20 MeV, width Γ = 108 ± 6 MeV (BaBar measurement)
- Binding energy of X_c(3250)^0: approximately -13 MeV, from M_D0*(2400)^0 = 2318 MeV and M_n = 940 MeV giving threshold at 3258 MeV
- Cutoff Λ = 1.23 GeV reproduces the -13 MeV binding energy
- Decay width of X_c(3250)^0 → nDπ: 105 ± 16 MeV at Λ = 1.23 GeV, compared to BaBar total width 108 ± 6 MeV
- Width of D*_0(2400)^0: Γ = 267 ± 40 MeV
- Mass of exchanged σ meson: 660 MeV

## Context

This work extends the molecular hadron interpretation to the charmed baryon sector, following the approach of one-boson exchange models used for other molecular candidates. The authors propose that X_c(3250)^0 is the first observed D*_0(2400)N molecular state, analogous to earlier molecular interpretations of XYZ states in the charmonium sector. The study enables future experimental searches for the predicted isoscalar partner Y_c(3250)^+ and for other decay channels such as X_c(3250)^0 → Λ_c^+π^- at facilities like LHCb, Belle II, and SuperB.
